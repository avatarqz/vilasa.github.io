# 【标题】第21期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1995【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 巴利圣典中的案例史（一）：《沙门果经》中的假设案例史——或如何确保赢得辩论
作者：乔伊·曼内（Joy Manne）  
摘要：本文将当代心理学中的“案例史”概念引入巴利圣典研究，聚焦《长部·沙门果经》40-98段的假设案例史（HCH）。该案例史以“居士、居士之子或其他族群转生者”为通用主体，描述了从对如来生信、出家修行，到持守戒律、守护根门、断除五盖、证入四禅，再到开发神变神通（如天眼通、宿命通），最终证得漏尽解脱的完整修行历程。研究对比了该案例史在《长部》《中部》《增支部》中的不同版本，指出其核心功能是在辩论场景中彰显佛陀教法的优越性，通过“普适性修行路径+确定性解脱结果”的叙事结构说服对手。文章还分析了该案例史的文本特征、与其他佛教心理案例史（如佛陀案例史、弟子真实案例史）的差异，探讨了其在教义传播与辩论中的工具性价值，同时提出该案例史虽包含超验描述，但本质是对修行阶段的系统化呈现，为理解早期佛教的修行观与辩论传统提供了新视角。  
关键词：巴利圣典；案例史；沙门果经；辩论传统；修行路径  
Title：Case Histories from the Pali Canon I: The Samannaphala Sutta Hypothetical Case History or How to be Sure to Win a Debate  
Author：Joy Manne  
Abstract：This paper introduces the concept of "case history" from contemporary psychology into the study of the Pali Canon, focusing on the Hypothetical Case History (HCH) in paragraphs 40-98 of the Samannaphala Sutta in the Digha Nikaya. Taking "a householder, the son of a householder, or someone reborn in another group" as the universal subject, this case history describes a complete practice journey: from generating faith in the Tathagata and renouncing the world, to observing precepts, guarding the sense faculties, eliminating the five hindrances, attaining the four jhanas, developing spiritual powers (such as the divine eye and knowledge of past lives), and finally achieving the destruction of asavas and liberation. The study compares different versions of this case history in the Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, and Anguttara Nikaya, pointing out that its core function is to demonstrate the superiority of the Buddha's teaching in debate scenarios through a narrative structure of "universal practice path + certain liberation result" to persuade opponents. The paper also analyzes the textual characteristics of this case history, its differences from other Buddhist psychological case histories (such as the Buddha's case history and the real case histories of disciples), explores its instrumental value in doctrinal dissemination and debates, and proposes that although the case history contains transcendental descriptions, it is essentially a systematic presentation of practice stages, providing a new perspective for understanding the practice view and debate tradition of early Buddhism.  
Keywords：Pali Canon；case history；Samannaphala Sutta；debate tradition；practice path

# 论文2. 巴利圣典中的案例史（二）：预流果、一来果、不还果、阿罗汉果——四果案例史——或精神物质主义与对切实成果的需求
作者：乔伊·曼内（Joy Manne）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利圣典中“四果”（预流果、一来果、不还果、阿罗汉果）构成的假设案例史，探讨其作为跨生死修行阶段体系的形成与功能。该案例史以“轮回转生”与“业力”思想为理论基础，将解脱过程划分为递进式阶段：预流果断除三结、一来果贪嗔痴减弱、不还果断除五下分结、阿罗汉果断尽一切烦恼。研究梳理了四果在四部尼柯耶中的不同表述（标准版、简略版、含果版）及细分阶段（如预流果的七种类型、不还果的五种类型），分析了各阶段的核心成就、修行方法与优势。文章指出，四果案例史的形成源于佛教对“可衡量修行成果”的需求，既为修行者提供明确目标，也通过“多生修行仍可解脱”的设定缓解了即时解脱的压力。同时，该案例史在文本中被灵活运用，既用于论证教法有效性，也存在被工具化的情况（如作为身份赋予、奖励或解决教义争议的手段），反映了早期佛教对修行阶段系统化与实用化的双重追求。  
关键词：四果；预流果；一来果；不还果；阿罗汉果；案例史；修行阶段  
Title：Case Histories from the Pali Canon II: Sotapanna, Sakadagamin, Anagamin, Arahat - The Four Stages Case History or Spiritual Materialism and the need for Tangible Results  
Author：Joy Manne  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the hypothetical case history composed of the "Four Fruits" (Sotapanna, Sakadagamin, Anagamin, Arahat) in the Pali Canon, exploring its formation and function as a trans-life practice stage system. Based on the ideas of "samsara" and "karma", this case history divides the liberation process into progressive stages: Sotapanna eliminates the three fetters, Sakadagamin weakens passion, hatred and delusion, Anagamin eliminates the five lower fetters, and Arahat eliminates all defilements. The study sorts out different expressions of the Four Fruits in the four Nikayas (standard version, brief version, version with fruits) and their subdivisions (such as the seven types of Sotapanna and five types of Anagamin), analyzing the core achievements, practice methods and advantages of each stage. The paper points out that the formation of the Four Stages case history stems from Buddhism's need for "measurable practice results", which not only provides clear goals for practitioners but also relieves the pressure of immediate liberation through the setting that "liberation can still be achieved through multiple lifetimes of practice". At the same time, this case history is flexibly used in texts, both to demonstrate the effectiveness of the teaching and to be instrumentalized (such as being used as a means of identity conferral, reward, or resolving doctrinal disputes), reflecting the dual pursuit of systematization and pragmatism of practice stages in early Buddhism.  
Keywords：Four Fruits；Sotapanna；Sakadagamin；Anagamin；Arahat；case history；practice stages

# 论文3. 《妙吉祥疏》与《解惑疏》的结语
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文整理并研究了巴利文注释书《妙吉祥疏》（Sumangalavilasini，《长部》注释）与《解惑疏》（Kankhavitarani，《波罗提木叉》注释）中被巴利圣典协会（PTS） editions遗漏的“结语”（nigamana）部分。该结语基于Chatthasangayana版本整理，包含文本编纂背景、作者信息、文本篇幅等关键文献学信息。《妙吉祥疏》的结语明确其由佛音尊者（Buddhaghosa）主导编纂，与《清净道论》共同构成140个“篇章单元”（bhanavara），强调其对《长部》教义阐释的权威性；《解惑疏》的结语则提及该注释为回应律藏中的疑问而作，篇幅达22个篇章单元，作者归属与佛音尊者相关传承。文章指出，这些结语是研究巴利注释书编纂史、文本流传及佛音尊者著作体系的重要史料，补充了现有版本的文献缺口，为巴利文献学研究提供了关键佐证。  
关键词：巴利注释书；妙吉祥疏；解惑疏；结语；佛音尊者；文献学  
Title：The Nigamanas of the Sumangalavilasini and the Kankhavitarani  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper collates and studies the "nigamana" (conclusion) sections of the Pali commentaries Sumangalavilasini (commentary on the Digha Nikaya) and Kankhavitarani (commentary on the Patimokkha) that were omitted from the Pali Text Society (PTS) editions. The nigamana is collated based on the Chatthasangayana version, containing key philological information such as textual compilation background, author information, and textual length. The nigamana of Sumangalavilasini clearly states that it was compiled under the leadership of Buddhaghosa, forming 140 "bhanavaras" (chapter units) together with the Visuddhimagga, emphasizing its authority in interpreting the doctrines of the Digha Nikaya; the nigamana of Kankhavitarani mentions that this commentary was written to address doubts in the Vinaya, with a length of 22 bhanavaras, and its author is associated with the tradition of Buddhaghosa. The paper points out that these nigamanas are important historical materials for studying the compilation history of Pali commentaries, textual transmission, and Buddhaghosa's literary system, supplementing the literary gap of existing editions and providing key evidence for Pali philological research.  
Keywords：Pali commentaries；Sumangalavilasini；Kankhavitarani；nigamana；Buddhaghosa；philology

# 论文4. 阿希里耶·维杰亚松达拉拉马亚图书馆的缅文与暹罗文巴利手稿目录
作者：杰奎琳·菲利奥扎（Jacqueline Filliozat）  
摘要：本文详细整理了斯里兰卡康提阿希里耶·维杰亚松达拉拉马亚图书馆（Vijayasundararamaya Asgiriya）收藏的缅文与暹罗文巴利手稿目录，该馆藏是斯里兰卡重要的南传佛教文献遗存。目录涵盖两类核心手稿：缅文手稿《阿毗达磨词汇灯》（Abhidhanappadipika）与暹罗文手稿《护经集》（Paritta）。《阿毗达磨词汇灯》为巴利语词典类文献，以中号缅文书写，间有小字缅文释义，共33叶贝叶经，内容包含词汇分类与释义，可与1865年僧伽罗语版及1898年缅文版比对；《护经集》包含《七护经》《十二护经》《吉祥经》《宝经》等15部护佑类经文，以暹罗卡姆文（Kham）书写，夹杂僧伽罗语转录与暹罗文注释，共31叶贝叶经，记录了经文的开篇、结尾及相关仪轨说明。文章逐一记录了手稿的形制（尺寸、行数、书写字体）、内容构成、版本关联及保存状况，为研究巴利文献在东南亚的传播（缅、泰、斯三国的文本交流）、书写传统及护经类文献的传承提供了详实的一手资料。  
关键词：巴利手稿；缅文手稿；暹罗文手稿；图书馆目录；文献收藏；南传佛教  
Title：Catalogue of the Pali Manuscript Collection in Burmese & Siamese Characters Kept in the Library of Vijayasundararamaya Asgiriya  
Author：Jacqueline Filliozat  
Abstract：This paper details the catalogue of Pali manuscripts in Burmese and Siamese characters collected by the Vijayasundararamaya Asgiriya Library in Kandy, Sri Lanka, which is an important relic of Theravada Buddhist literature in Sri Lanka. The catalogue covers two core manuscripts: the Burmese manuscript Abhidhanappadipika and the Siamese manuscript Paritta. Abhidhanappadipika is a Pali dictionary-style document, written in medium-sized Burmese with small-sized Burmese interpretations interspersed, totaling 33 palm-leaf folios, including vocabulary classification and explanations, which can be compared with the 1865 Sinhalese version and the 1898 Burmese version; Paritta includes 15 protective sutras such as the Sattaparitta, Dvadasaparitta, Mangalasutta, and Ratanasutta, written in Siamese Kham script with Sinhalese transcriptions and Siamese commentaries interspersed, totaling 31 palm-leaf folios, recording the opening, closing, and relevant ritual instructions of the sutras. The paper records the form (size, number of lines, writing script), content composition, version correlation, and preservation status of each manuscript one by one, providing detailed first-hand data for studying the dissemination of Pali literature in Southeast Asia (textual exchange between Myanmar, Thailand, and Sri Lanka), writing traditions, and the inheritance of protective sutra literature.  
Keywords：Pali manuscripts；Burmese manuscripts；Siamese manuscripts；library catalogue；literature collection；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文5. 巴利律藏中的僧人：祭司还是婚礼宾客？
作者：理查德·冈布里奇（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利律藏中对僧人行仪的规范，探讨早期佛教僧人在社会仪式中的角色定位问题。通过梳理《律藏》中关于僧人参与世俗活动的禁令与许可，作者指出，律藏明确禁止僧人担任祭祀、主持婚礼等涉及世俗仪式的角色，核心原因是避免僧人陷入“邪命”（micchajiva）与世俗利益绑定。文章对比了婆罗门祭司的社会功能与佛教僧人的修行宗旨，强调佛教僧人的核心职责是修行与传法，而非参与世俗仪式提供宗教服务。研究还结合律藏中“不参与世俗庆典”“不接受不当供养”等具体规范，分析了早期佛教对僧团“出世性”的坚守，以及这种坚守对僧团独立性与教法纯粹性的保障作用，为理解南传佛教僧团的社会角色与修行伦理提供了律藏文本依据。  
关键词：巴利律藏；僧人；社会角色；邪命；修行伦理；世俗仪式  
Title：The Monk in the Pali Vinaya: Priest or Wedding Guest?  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the norms for monastic conduct in the Pali Vinaya, exploring the role positioning of early Buddhist monks in social rituals. By sorting out the prohibitions and permissions for monks to participate in secular activities in the Vinaya, the author points out that the Vinaya explicitly prohibits monks from acting as priests, presiding over weddings, and other roles involving secular rituals, with the core reason of avoiding monks falling into "micchajiva" (wrongful livelihood) and being bound to secular interests. The paper compares the social functions of Brahmin priests with the practice purpose of Buddhist monks, emphasizing that the core responsibility of Buddhist monks is practice and propagating the Dhamma, rather than participating in secular rituals to provide religious services. The study also combines specific norms in the Vinaya such as "not participating in secular celebrations" and "not accepting improper alms", analyzes the adherence of early Buddhism to the "world-transcending nature" of the sangha, and the role of this adherence in ensuring the independence of the sangha and the purity of the teaching, providing a Vinaya textual basis for understanding the social role and practice ethics of the Theravada Buddhist sangha.  
Keywords：Pali Vinaya；monk；social role；micchajiva；practice ethics；secular rituals

# 论文6. 已知最古老的巴利语文本（5-6世纪）：1995年剑桥骠国室利差呾罗金质巴利语文本研讨会成果
作者：贾尼斯·斯塔加特（Janice Stargardt）  
摘要：本文汇总了1995年4月剑桥大学举办的“骠国室利差呾罗金质巴利语文本研讨会”的核心成果，聚焦5-6世纪缅甸骠国（Pyu）室利差呾罗（Sri Ksetra）遗址出土的金质巴利语文本——这是目前已知最古老的巴利语文本实物。研究团队通过考古学、文献学、语言学多学科交叉研究，确认该文本以金箔为载体，刻有巴利语佛号、祈愿文及简短教义片段，书写字体为早期骠国文字与巴利文混合体。文章详细介绍了文本的出土背景、形制特征、语言分析结果及年代测定依据，探讨了该文本反映的5-6世纪巴利语在东南亚的传播状况、骠国佛教的发展水平，以及骠国与印度、斯里兰卡佛教文化圈的交流关联。该文本的发现填补了早期巴利语传播史的考古空白，为证实南传佛教在东南亚的早期发展提供了关键实物证据。  
关键词：巴利语文本；骠国；室利差呾罗；金质文本；佛教传播；考古学  
Title：The Oldest Known Pali Texts, 5th-6th century; Results of the Cambridge Symposium on the Pyu Golden Pali Text from Sri Ksetra, 18-19 April 1995  
Author：Janice Stargardt  
Abstract：This paper summarizes the core achievements of the "Cambridge Symposium on the Pyu Golden Pali Text from Sri Ksetra" held at the University of Cambridge in April 1995, focusing on the golden Pali text unearthed from the 5th-6th century Pyu site of Sri Ksetra in Myanmar—the oldest known physical Pali text to date. Through interdisciplinary research combining archaeology, philology, and linguistics, the research team confirmed that the text is carrier on gold foil, inscribed with Pali Buddha names, prayers, and short doctrinal fragments, with a writing script that is a mixture of early Pyu script and Pali. The paper details the excavation background, form characteristics, linguistic analysis results, and chronological basis of the text, exploring the dissemination of Pali in Southeast Asia in the 5th-6th century, the development level of Buddhism in the Pyu kingdom, and the cultural exchange between the Pyu kingdom and the Buddhist cultural circles of India and Sri Lanka reflected by the text. The discovery of this text fills the archaeological gap in the early dissemination history of Pali and provides key physical evidence for confirming the early development of Theravada Buddhism in Southeast Asia.  
Keywords：Pali texts；Pyu kingdom；Sri Ksetra；golden text；Budd
