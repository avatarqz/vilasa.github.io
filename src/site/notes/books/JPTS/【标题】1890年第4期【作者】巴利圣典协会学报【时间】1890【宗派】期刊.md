# 【标题】1890年第4期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1890【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  《本生经》索引
作者：W·H·D·劳斯（W. H. D. Rouse）  
摘要：本文系统编制《本生经》的详细索引，包含三个核心部分。第一部分按“尼帕塔（Nipatas，篇章）”排序，列出17个已出版篇章及2个哥本哈根手稿未出版篇章的编号范围、对应卷次与页码；第二部分按“瓦加（Vaggas，小节）”排序，标注每小节在全系列中的位置、所属篇章及包含的《本生经》编号；第三部分按《本生经》编号排序，逐一列出每部经的标题、对应卷次与页码，同时补充哥本哈根手稿中未出版篇目（以“W”标记）。索引参考韦斯特加德《哥本哈根皇家图书馆东方手稿目录》及福斯博尔校勘本（截至第五卷第192页），为《本生经》的检索与研究提供精准工具。  
关键词：《本生经》；索引编制；尼帕塔；瓦加；篇章编号；文献检索  
Title：Index to the Jataka  
Author：W. H. D. Rouse  
Abstract：This paper systematically compiles a detailed index to the Jataka Tales, consisting of three core parts. The first part is arranged by "Nipatas (Books)", listing the number range, corresponding volumes and page numbers of 17 published books and 2 unpublished books from the Copenhagen manuscript; the second part is arranged by "Vaggas (Sections)", indicating the position of each section in the entire series, the affiliated book and the numbers of the Jataka Tales it contains; the third part is arranged by the numbers of the Jataka Tales, listing the title, corresponding volume and page number of each tale one by one, and supplementing the unpublished entries from the Copenhagen manuscript (marked with "W"). The index refers to Westergaard's Catalog of Oriental Manuscripts in the Royal Library of Copenhagen and Fausböll's edition (up to Vol. V, p. 192), providing an accurate tool for the retrieval and research of the Jataka Tales.  
Keywords：Jataka Tales；Index Compilation；Nipatas；Vaggas；Tale Numbers；Literature Retrieval

# 论文2.  《清净道论》内容摘要
作者：J·E·卡彭特教授（Prof. J. E. Carpenter, M.A., M.R.A.S.）  
摘要：本文基于僧伽罗语手稿（S°）与曼德勒缅甸语手稿（Bm），整理 Buddhaghosa 所著《清净道论》（Visuddhi-Magga）的内容摘要，按“戒、定、慧”三学框架分为23章。戒学部分含“戒的阐释”“头陀行阐释”2章；定学部分含11章，涵盖禅修基础、十种遍作、不净观、念住、四梵住等禅修方法；慧学部分含10章，聚焦五蕴、十二处、十八界、十二因缘、四圣谛等法义阐释，以及七种清净境界的修证路径。摘要精准呈现著作的章节结构与核心主题，为《清净道论》的研读提供纲要性指引。  
关键词：《清净道论》；Buddhaghosa；内容摘要；戒定慧三学；禅修方法；南传佛教  
Title：Visuddhi-Magga. Abstract of Contents  
Author：Prof. J. E. Carpenter, M.A., M.R.A.S.  
Abstract：Based on the Sinhalese manuscript (S°) and the Mandalay Burmese manuscript (Bm), this paper compiles an abstract of the contents of Buddhaghosa's Visuddhi-Magga, divided into 23 chapters according to the framework of "Sila, Samadhi, Panna". The Sila section includes 2 chapters: "Exposition of Sila" and "Exposition of the Dhutanga"; the Samadhi section includes 11 chapters, covering meditation basics, ten Kasinas, Asubha meditation, Satipatthana, Brahmavihara and other meditation methods; the Panna section includes 10 chapters, focusing on the explanation of Dharma such as the Five Khandhas, Twelve Ayatanas, Eighteen Dhatus, Twelve Nidanas, Four Noble Truths, and the practice path of seven purities. The abstract accurately presents the chapter structure and core themes of the work, providing an outline guide for the study of the Visuddhi-Magga.  
Keywords：Visuddhi-Magga；Buddhaghosa；Content Abstract；Sila, Samadhi, Panna；Meditation Methods；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文3.  《善法集》（Saddhamma Samgahoe）校勘本
作者：内迪马莱·萨达南达（Nedimale Saddhananda）（锡兰卡卢塔拉阿育王寺）  
摘要：本文校勘出版《善法集》，该著作由 Dhammakittyabhidhanatthera 为促进佛法传承与三藏典籍研读而著，后因抄写疏漏出现文本混乱，经校勘者比对多方手稿整理完善。全书共11章，核心叙述四次佛教结集的历史：第一次结集由摩诃迦叶主导，集结经律论三藏；第二次结集于吠舍离，纠正跋耆比丘的十种邪说；第三次结集由摩哂陀尊者推动，净化僧团并派遣僧众至各地弘扬佛法；第四次结集在兰卡完成，将口传典籍书面化。同时详述 Buddhaghosa 尊者整理三藏注疏的历程，以及佛法在斯里兰卡的传承与发展，兼具史料价值与文献校勘意义。  
关键词：《善法集》；佛教结集；佛法传承；文本校勘；南传佛教史；三藏典籍  
Title：Saddhamma Samgahoe  
Author：Nedimale Saddhananda (of the Asokarama Viharaya at Kalutara in Ceylon)  
Abstract：This paper collates and publishes the Saddhamma Samgahoe, a work compiled by Dhammakittyabhidhanatthera to promote the transmission of Buddhism and the study of the Tipitaka. Later, the text became confused due to copying omissions, and was sorted out and improved by the collator after comparing multiple manuscripts. The book consists of 11 chapters, focusing on the history of the four Buddhist Councils: the first Council, led by Mahakassapa, compiled the Tipitaka (Vinaya, Sutta, Abhidhamma); the second Council in Vesali corrected the ten heresies of the Vajjiputtaka bhikkhus; the third Council, promoted by Ven. Moggaliputta Tissa, purified the Sangha and sent monks to propagate Buddhism in various places; the fourth Council was completed in Ceylon, converting oral scriptures into written form. It also details the process of Ven. Buddhaghosa sorting out the commentaries on the Tipitaka, as well as the transmission and development of Buddhism in Sri Lanka, with both historical and textual collation significance.  
Keywords：Saddhamma Samgahoe；Buddhist Councils；Buddhism Transmission；Textual Collation；Theravada Buddhist History；Tipitaka Texts

# 论文4.  《优陀那》（Udana）1885年版校勘注释
作者：E·温迪施（E. Windisch）  
摘要：本文基于印度事务部图书馆珍藏的曼德勒缅甸语手稿（M），与P·施泰因塔尔博士的《优陀那》1885年版（Ed.）进行比对校勘，梳理文本差异与校正建议。校勘范围涵盖经文段落、偈颂、词汇用法等，纠正原版拼写错误（如短元音“i”与长元音“ī”混淆）、补充遗漏文本（如重复表述、关键修饰词）、厘清异文（如地名“Pataliyam”与“Pavayam”、词汇“huhunka°”与“humhumka°”），同时标注手稿中独特的标点用法与文本重复特征。注释聚焦影响语义与格律的核心差异，为《优陀那》的精准解读与学术研究提供校勘依据。  
关键词：《优陀那》；文本校勘；缅甸语手稿；异文比对；南传佛教文献；词汇校正  
Title：Notes on the Edition of the Udana (P.T.S. 1885)  
Author：E. Windisch  
Abstract：Based on the rare Mandalay Burmese manuscript (M) in the Library of the India Office, this paper collates and compares it with Dr. P. Steinthal's 1885 edition of the Udana (Ed.), sorting out textual differences and correction suggestions. The collation covers scripture passages, gathas, vocabulary usage, etc., correcting spelling errors in the original edition (such as confusion between short vowel "i" and long vowel "ī"), supplementing missing texts (such as repeated expressions and key modifiers), clarifying variant readings (such as place names "Pataliyam" and "Pavayam", vocabulary "huhunka°" and "humhumka°"), and marking the unique punctuation usage and textual repetition characteristics in the manuscript. The notes focus on core differences affecting semantics and meter, providing collation basis for the accurate interpretation and academic research of the Udana.  
Keywords：Udana；Textual Collation；Burmese Manuscript；Variant Reading Comparison；Theravada Buddhist Literature；Vocabulary Correction

# 论文5.  巴利圣典协会出版物列表（1882-1890）
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文以年份排序和字母排序两种方式，梳理巴利圣典协会1882-1890年的出版物信息。按年份统计，9年间共出版28卷，合计5979页，每年出版物包含期刊与专题典籍（如《增支部》《相应部》《长部》等尼柯耶、注疏及单篇文献）；按字母排序列出每部文本的出版年份与编者，标注期刊中收录的文本（以星号标记），并说明1882-1883年部分出版物已绝版或库存稀少。列表全面呈现协会早期出版成果，为研究者检索巴利语佛教文献提供权威参考。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；出版物列表；出版年份；编者；文本类型；文献检索  
Title：Issues of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the publication information of the Pali Text Society from 1882 to 1890 in two ways: chronological order and alphabetical order. According to chronological statistics, 28 volumes have been published in 9 years, totaling 5,979 pages. Annual publications include journals and special texts (such as Nikayas like Anguttara, Samyutta, Digha, commentaries and single texts); listed alphabetically are the publication year and editor of each text, marking texts included in the journal (marked with an asterisk), and indicating that some publications from 1882 to 1883 are out of print or in short supply. The list comprehensively presents the society's early publication achievements, providing an authoritative reference for researchers to retrieve Pali Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Publication List；Publication Year；Editor；Text Type；Literature Retrieval
