# 【标题】第24期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1998【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 牛津大学博德利图书馆巴利文手稿收藏调查报告
作者：杰奎琳·菲利奥扎（Jacqueline Filliozat）  
摘要：本文基于1986年对牛津大学博德利图书馆的考察，系统整理了馆内收藏的161部巴利文手稿的核心信息。这些手稿分属9个收藏系列，涵盖缅甸文、暹罗卡姆文、僧伽罗文等书写形式，载体包括贝叶、象牙板、金属板、漆布等，抄写时间跨度从17世纪末至20世纪初。研究重点梳理了手稿的分类、藏品编号、内容构成（以《迦玛瓦卡》仪式文本和阿毗达摩纲要类文献为主）、保存状况及来源背景，纠正了此前学界认为该馆仅存34部巴利手稿的认知偏差。文章还附列了手稿中涉及的巴利文本索引、编年目录及捐赠者/售卖者名单，为巴利文献的收藏史与传播研究提供了详实的一手资料。  
关键词：博德利图书馆；巴利文手稿；收藏调查；佛教文献；书写载体  
Title：Survey of the Pali manuscript collection in the Bodleian Library, Oxford  
Author：Jacqueline Filliozat  
Abstract：Based on a survey of the Bodleian Library, Oxford in 1986, this paper systematically sorts out the core information of 161 Pali manuscripts collected in the library. These manuscripts belong to 9 collection series, covering writing forms such as Burmese, Siamese Kham, and Sinhalese, with carriers including palm leaves, ivory plates, metal plates, lacquered cloth, etc., and their copying time spans from the late 17th century to the early 20th century. The study focuses on sorting out the classification, collection numbers, content composition (mainly Kammavaca ritual texts and Abhidhamma compendium documents), preservation status, and source background of the manuscripts, correcting the previous academic misunderstanding that the library only holds 34 Pali manuscripts. The paper also attaches an index of Pali texts involved in the manuscripts, a chronological catalog, and a list of donors/vendors, providing detailed first-hand data for the research on the collection history and dissemination of Pali literature.  
Keywords：Bodleian Library；Pali manuscripts；collection survey；Buddhist literature；writing carriers

# 论文2. 《阿毗达摩俱舍论疏》中的弥兰陀王考
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文聚焦《阿毗达摩俱舍论疏》中引用的弥兰陀王与那先比丘的对话片段，对相关文本与名称问题展开考证。学界此前基于汉译本关注该引文，但梵文原典中国王名称作“迦陵伽王”，与巴利文《弥兰陀问经》中的“弥兰陀王”存在差异。通过比对藏译本、汉译本（真谛、玄奘译本）及相关平行文献（如《杂宝藏经》中“难陀王与那先比丘对话”），作者论证梵文“迦陵伽王”应为抄写讹误，原名称应与弥兰陀王同源。研究还梳理了弥兰陀文献的多元传承系统，指出除巴利文与汉译《那先比丘经》外，还存在（根本）说一切有部的梵文传本，印证了弥兰陀相关叙事在古代佛教世界的广泛传播。  
关键词：弥兰陀王；阿毗达摩俱舍论疏；那先比丘；佛教文献；文本考证  
Title：A Note on King Milinda in the Abhidharmakosabhasya  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the quoted dialogue between King Milinda and Ven. Nagasena in the Abhidharmakosabhasya, conducting textual research on relevant texts and naming issues. Previous academic attention to this quotation was based on Chinese translations, but the king's name in the Sanskrit original is "King Kalinga", which differs from "King Milinda" in the Pali Milindapañha. By comparing the Tibetan translation, Chinese translations (Paramartha's and Xuanzang's versions), and related parallel documents (such as the "Dialogue between King Nanda and Ven. Nagasena" in the Za bao zang jing), the author argues that "King Kalinga" in the Sanskrit text is a scribal error, and the original name should be cognate with Milinda. The study also sorts out the diverse transmission systems of Milinda-related literature, pointing out that in addition to the Pali text and the Chinese-translated Nagasenabhiksu-sutra, there was also a Sanskrit recension of the (Mula)Sarvastivadin school, confirming the wide dissemination of Milinda-related narratives in the ancient Buddhist world.  
Keywords：King Milinda；Abhidharmakosabhasya；Nagasena；Buddhist literature；textual research

# 论文3. 《佛教本生故事索引》补编
作者：莱斯利·格雷（Leslie Grey）  
摘要：本文是1994年出版的《佛教本生故事索引》的补编，基于耶路撒冷希伯来大学图书馆与海法大学以色列民间故事档案馆的文献，补充了约80%与犹太文化相关的平行故事资料，同时收录了1994年后新发现的其他文化背景的民间故事文献。补编按本生故事条目分类，每条目下标注相关参考文献、民间故事类型（AT分类法）及母题索引（TMI），涵盖《佛陀生平》《商人与报恩》《聪明的妻子》等多个经典本生主题。作者指出，佛教本生故事与世界各民族民间故事存在广泛的主题关联，补编旨在为后续研究者提供更全面的跨文化比较素材，推动本生故事传播与影响研究。  
关键词：佛教本生故事；索引补编；民间故事；跨文化比较；文献整理  
Title：Supplement to The Concordance of the Buddhist Birth Stories  
Author：Leslie Grey  
Abstract：This paper is a supplement to The Concordance of the Buddhist Birth Stories published in 1994. Based on documents from the Library of the Hebrew University of Jerusalem and the Israel Folktale Archives at the University of Haifa, it supplements approximately 80% of parallel story materials related to Jewish culture, and also includes folk tale documents from other cultural backgrounds discovered after 1994. The supplement is classified by Jataka entries, with each entry marked with relevant references, folk tale types (Aarne-Thompson classification), and motif indexes (TMI), covering multiple classic Jataka themes such as "The Life of the Buddha", "The Merchant and Reciprocity", and "The Clever Wife". The author points out that Buddhist Jataka tales have extensive thematic connections with folk tales of various ethnic groups around the world, and the supplement aims to provide more comprehensive cross-cultural comparison materials for subsequent researchers, promoting the research on the dissemination and influence of Jataka tales.  
Keywords：Buddhist Jataka tales；concordance supplement；folk tales；cross-cultural comparison；literature collation

# 论文4. 《法句经》第60颂与“逾缮那”长度考
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文针对《法句经》第60颂中“若人以邪行，行逾百逾缮”的记载，聚焦“逾缮那”（yojana）这一古印度长度单位的具体度量标准展开考证。通过梳理巴利文注释（如《法句经注》）、梵文平行文献、古代碑铭及旅行记中的相关记载，作者对比了不同时期、不同地区对“逾缮那”的长度界定——从传统佛教文献中的“四拘卢舍”（krosa）换算，到近现代考古与文献考证得出的约7-10公里不等的结论。研究结合经文语境，指出“逾缮那”在《法句经》中兼具实际长度与象征意义，既反映古代印度的度量制度，也通过“百逾缮”的表述强调邪行的深远危害，为理解经文语义与古代印度社会文化提供了计量学视角。  
关键词：法句经；逾缮那；长度单位；巴利文献；语义考证  
Title：A Note on Dhammapada 60 and the Length of the Yojana  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：Focusing on the record "If one acts wrongly, one travels a hundred yojanas" in Dhammapada 60, this paper conducts textual research on the specific measurement standard of the ancient Indian length unit "yojana". By sorting out relevant records in Pali commentaries (such as Dhammapada-atthakatha), Sanskrit parallel documents, ancient inscriptions, and travelogues, the author compares the definitions of the length of "yojana" in different periods and regions—from the conversion of "four krosas" in traditional Buddhist literature to the conclusion of approximately 7-10 kilometers derived from modern archaeological and textual research. Combining the context of the sutra, the study points out that "yojana" in the Dhammapada has both practical length and symbolic significance, reflecting the measurement system of ancient India and emphasizing the far-reaching harm of wrong actions through the expression "a hundred yojanas", providing a metrological perspective for understanding the semantic meaning of the sutra and ancient Indian social culture.  
Keywords：Dhammapada；yojana；length unit；Pali literature；semantic research

# 论文5. 美国国会图书馆缅甸文手稿增补目录
作者：威廉·普鲁伊特（William Pruitt）  
摘要：本文补充整理了美国国会图书馆收藏的缅甸文手稿新增藏品信息，涵盖此前未被编目或新入藏的多部巴利文相关手稿。这些手稿包括《迦玛瓦卡》仪式文本、律藏片段、本生故事选录等，抄写时间集中于19世纪至20世纪初，书写载体以贝叶和纸张为主，部分附有精美的漆绘装饰。作者按图书馆藏品编号逐一记录手稿的内容、形制、保存状况、来源背景及相关文献学信息，修正了早期编目中的错误，补充了文本识别与版本比对的关键细节，为缅甸文巴利手稿的海外收藏研究与文献检索提供了重要参考。  
关键词：美国国会图书馆；缅甸文手稿；增补目录；巴利文献；收藏整理  
Title：Additions to the Burmese Manuscripts in the Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.  
Author：William Pruitt  
Abstract：This paper supplements and sorts out the information of newly added Burmese manuscript collections in the Library of Congress, covering multiple Pali-related manuscripts that were previously uncataloged or newly acquired. These manuscripts include Kammavaca ritual texts, Vinaya fragments, Jataka selections, etc., with copying times concentrated from the 19th century to the early 20th century. The writing carriers are mainly palm leaves and paper, and some are accompanied by exquisite lacquer paintings. The author records the content, form, preservation status, source background, and relevant philological information of each manuscript one by one according to the library's collection number, correcting errors in early cataloging, supplementing key details of text identification and version comparison, and providing important references for the research on overseas collections and literature retrieval of Burmese-Pali manuscripts.  
Keywords：Library of Congress；Burmese manuscripts；supplementary catalog；Pali literature；collection collation

# 论文6. 《吉祥经》与《孔雀经》研究资料考
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文系统梳理了与《吉祥经》（Mangala-sutta）和《孔雀经》（Mora-sutta）相关的核心研究资料，涵盖巴利文原典、注释文献、平行文本及近现代学术研究成果。研究聚焦两部经文的文本传承——《吉祥经》以列举吉祥之事为核心，在南传佛教中广泛流传且存在多个版本；《孔雀经》通过孔雀喻阐释修行要义，其文本在不同佛教流派中略有差异。作者整理了经文的梵文、藏文、傣文等平行译本，对比了巴利文注释（如《清净道论》引用）与后世研究的解读分歧，指出两部经文的核心价值在于其对佛教伦理观与修行观的凝练表达，相关资料为经文的文本批判、教义阐释及跨文化传播研究提供了全面支撑。  
关键词：吉祥经；孔雀经；巴利圣典；文本传承；研究资料  
Title：Sources for the Study of the Mangala- and Mora-suttas  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the core research materials related to the Mangala-sutta and Mora-sutta, including Pali originals, commentarial documents, parallel texts, and modern academic research results. The study focuses on the textual transmission of the two sutras—the Mangala-sutta, which centers on enumerating auspicious things, is widely circulated in Theravada Buddhism with multiple versions; the Mora-sutta explains the essence of practice through the metaphor of a peacock, and its text varies slightly among different Buddhist schools. The author sorts out parallel translations of the sutras in Sanskrit, Tibetan, Tai, etc., compares the interpretive differences between Pali commentaries (such as citations in the Visuddhimagga) and later studies, and points out that the core value of the two sutras lies in their concise expression of Buddhist ethics and practice views. Relevant materials provide comprehensive support for the textual criticism, doctrinal interpretation, and cross-cultural transmission research of the sutras.  
Keywords：Mangala-sutta；Mora-sutta；Pali Canon；textual transmission；research sources

# 论文7. 赞不可赞之佛陀
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文探讨了佛教文献中“赞佛”（buddha-praise）传统的核心内涵与表达形式，聚焦“赞不可赞”这一悖论性命题——佛陀的功德与境界超越语言描述，却仍成为佛教文学的重要主题。通过分析巴利文《佛陀赞》《大颂》等经典赞佛文本，以及碑铭、手稿中的赞佛诗偈，作者梳理了赞佛文学的文体特征：既包含对佛陀身相、功德、教法的具体称颂，也常以“不可说”“无分别”等表述指向超越语言的终极境界。研究指出，赞佛不仅是宗教虔诚的表达，更是通过语言修行引导信徒体悟佛陀境界的重要方式，体现了佛教文学“以言表意”与“超越语言”的辩证统一。  
关键词：赞佛文学；佛陀；巴利文献；宗教表达；语言修行  
Title：Praises of the Buddha beyond Praise  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper explores the core connotation and expression forms of the "buddha-praise" tradition in Buddhist literature, focusing on the paradoxical proposition of "praising the unpraisable"—the Buddha's merits and realms transcend linguistic description, yet they still become an important theme in Buddhist literature. By analyzing classic Buddha-praising texts such as the Buddha-vandana and Mahagatha in Pali, as well as Buddha-praising verses in inscriptions and manuscripts, the author sorts out the stylistic characteristics of Buddha-praising literature: it not only includes specific praises of the Buddha's physical features, merits, and teachings, but also often points to the ultimate realm beyond language through expressions such as "inexpressible" and "non-discriminating". The study points out that praising the Buddha is not only an expression of religious piety, but also an important way to guide believers to realize the Buddha's realm through linguistic practice, reflecting the dialectical unity of "expressing meaning through words" and "transcending language" in Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Buddha-praising literature；Buddha；Pali literature；religious expression；linguistic practice
