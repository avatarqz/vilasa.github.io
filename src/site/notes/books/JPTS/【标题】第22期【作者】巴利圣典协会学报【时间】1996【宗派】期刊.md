# 【标题】第22期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1996【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 词典编纂、巴利语与巴利语词典编纂（1995年I.B.霍纳讲座）
作者：玛格丽特·科恩（Margaret Cone）  
摘要：本文围绕巴利圣典协会新《巴利语-英语词典》的编纂工作展开，追溯巴利语词典编纂的历史脉络。从印度早期词汇表《尼犍陀》、耶斯卡《尼鲁卡塔》的词典编纂雏形，到兰那地区摩伽拉那的《阿毗达磨词汇灯》，再到罗伯特·凯撒·柴尔德斯的首部巴利语词典、里斯·戴维斯与威廉·施泰德编纂的《巴利语-英语词典》及哥本哈根《批判巴利语词典》，梳理了各阶段词典的特点与传承。作者结合自身编纂经验，阐述新词典的编纂原则：以巴利圣典协会出版文本为核心依据，补充后期文献与语法著作，采用简洁的梵语平行词标注，优先呈现文本语境中的核心含义，通过完整引文示例词义用法，兼顾实用性与学术严谨性，同时坦诚词典编纂面临的文献局限性与语言难题。  
关键词：巴利语；词典编纂；巴利语词典；文献学；语言学  
Title：Lexicography, Pali and Pali lexicography (The I. B. Horner Lecture 1995)  
Author：Margaret Cone  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the compilation of the new Pali-English Dictionary of the Pali Text Society, tracing the historical context of Pali lexicography. From the embryonic form of lexicography in early Indian word-lists such as the Nighantu and Yaska's Nirukta, to Moggallana's Abhidhanappadipika in the Lan Na region, then to Robert Caesar Childers' first Pali dictionary, Rhys Davids and William Stede's Pali-English Dictionary, and the Copenhagen Critical Pali Dictionary, it sorts out the characteristics and inheritance of dictionaries at each stage. Combining her own compilation experience, the author elaborates on the editorial principles of the new dictionary: taking the texts published by the Pali Text Society as the core basis, supplementing later literature and grammatical works, using concise Sanskrit parallel word annotations, giving priority to presenting the core meaning in the textual context, exemplifying word usage through complete citations, balancing practicality and academic rigor, while frankly addressing the literary limitations and linguistic difficulties faced in dictionary compilation.  
Keywords：Pali；Lexicography；Pali dictionary；Philology；Linguistics

# 论文2. 佛教工坊拾遗：泰国北部的抄写者与手稿
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文基于对泰国南邦府莱欣寺（Vat Lai Hin）收藏的约140部巴利语手稿的考察，系统梳理了兰那地区15至18世纪巴利手稿的特征与传承。手稿涵盖律藏、本生经、注释文献等，最古老者可追溯至1471年，其抄写与捐赠活动集中于16至17世纪，以克萨拉帕纳长老（Maha Kesarapanna）等人为核心推动者。研究重点分析了手稿的页码标注体系（字母序列与数字结合）、文本分册方式、开篇祈福公式（如“礼敬佛陀”变体）、题跋中的宗教祈愿（如愿佛法住世五千年、祈愿生于弥勒佛时代），以及捐赠与抄写者的协作模式，揭示了兰那地区手稿在形制规范、宗教功能与文化传承中的独特价值，补充了南传佛教手稿研究的区域案例。  
关键词：兰那；巴利手稿；抄写者；题跋；佛教文献传承  
Title：Chips from Buddhist Workshops: Scribes and Manuscripts from Northern Thailand  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：Based on a survey of approximately 140 Pali manuscripts collected at Vat Lai Hin in Lampang Province, Thailand, this paper systematically sorts out the characteristics and inheritance of Pali manuscripts in the Lan Na region from the 15th to the 18th centuries. The manuscripts cover the Vinaya-Pitaka, Jatakas, commentarial literature, etc., with the oldest dating back to 1471. Their copying and donation activities were concentrated in the 16th to 17th centuries, centered on figures such as Ven. Maha Kesarapanna. The study focuses on analyzing the manuscript's pagination system (combination of alphabetical sequences and numbers), text division methods, opening blessing formulas (such as variants of "Namo Tassa Bhagavato"), religious aspirations in colophons (such as wishing the Dhamma to last for five thousand years, praying to be reborn in the era of Metteyya Buddha), and the collaborative model between donors and scribes, revealing the unique value of Lan Na manuscripts in formal norms, religious functions, and cultural inheritance, supplementing regional cases for the study of Theravada Buddhist manuscripts.  
Keywords：Lan Na；Pali manuscripts；scribes；colophons；Buddhist literature transmission

# 论文3. 一则对印度医学史具重要意义的巴利圣典段落
作者：吉纳达萨·利耶纳拉特纳（Jinadasa Liyanaratne）  
摘要：本文聚焦《长部·梵网经》与《长部·沙门果经》中关于“邪命”的记载，其中列举的20项“畜生般的技艺”多涉及古印度医学实践，为印度医学史研究提供了关键文本证据。通过比对阿育吠陀经典（如《妙闻本集》）与巴利注释（如《善见律毗婆沙》），逐一考证了“安抚仪式”“咒术修行”“壮阳术”“漱口”“沐浴疗法”“催吐”“泻下”“耳鼻疗法”“眼科疗法”“儿科疗法”等术语的医学内涵，指出这些技艺涵盖阿育吠陀八分支中的体疗、儿科、眼科、外科等核心领域。研究认为，佛陀否定的是将这些医学技艺作为谋生手段，而非医学本身，且经文记载印证了阿育吠陀核心疗法在佛陀时代已成型，佛教徒后续对印度医学发展亦有重要贡献。  
关键词：巴利圣典；印度医学史；阿育吠陀；邪命；医学术语  
Title：A Pali Canonical Passage of Importance for the History of Indian Medicine  
Author：Jinadasa Liyanaratne  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the records of "wrongful livelihood" in the Brahmajala-sutta and Samannaphala-sutta of the Digha Nikaya, among which the 20 listed "beastly sciences" mostly involve ancient Indian medical practices, providing key textual evidence for the study of the history of Indian medicine. By comparing Ayurvedic classics (such as the Susrutasamhita) with Pali commentaries (such as the Sumangalavilasini), the author verifies the medical connotations of terms such as "propitiatory rites", "mantra practice", "virilification", "acamana", "bathing therapy", "vamana", "virecana", "ear-nose therapy", "ophthalmic therapy", and "paediatric therapy" one by one, pointing out that these techniques cover core fields of Ayurveda's eight branches including kayacikitsa (general medicine), kaumarabhrtya (paediatrics), salakya (ophthalmology), and salya (surgery). The study argues that what the Buddha rejected was using these medical techniques as a means of livelihood, not medicine itself, and the textual records confirm that the core therapies of Ayurveda had taken shape during the Buddha's time, with Buddhists making important contributions to the development of Indian medicine thereafter.  
Keywords：Pali Canon；history of Indian medicine；Ayurveda；micchajiva；medical terms

# 论文4. 律藏术语研究（第一至三部分）
作者：伊迪丝·诺洛（Edith Nolot）  
摘要：本文为“律藏术语研究”系列的开篇三部分，聚焦巴利律藏中的核心术语，旨在系统梳理僧团程序、法律争议及相关规范的术语体系。第一部分解析“僧伽羯磨”（samgha-kamma），即僧团的四种正式程序（咨询、动议、二重程序、四重程序），明确其有效性条件（法定人数、界址合规、步骤完整等）与适用场景；第二部分探讨“争议”（adhikarana）的四大类别（教义争议、谴责争议、犯戒争议、职责争议）及七种解决规则（在场裁决、无罪裁决、癫狂裁决、认罪裁决、多数裁决、顽固错误裁决、掩盖争议）；第三部分围绕术语的语言平行性展开，对比梵语、佛教混合梵语中的对应术语，厘清巴利律藏术语的独特性与传承关系，为律藏文献的精准解读提供术语学支撑。  
关键词：巴利律藏；术语研究；僧伽羯磨；争议解决；佛教戒律  
Title：Studies in Vinaya Technical Terms I-III  
Author：Edith Nolot  
Abstract：This paper is the first three parts of the "Studies in Vinaya Technical Terms" series, focusing on core terms in the Pali Vinaya, aiming to systematically sort out the terminology system of monastic procedures, legal disputes, and related norms. The first part analyzes "samgha-kamma", i.e., the four formal procedures of the sangha (apalokana, natti, natti-dutiya, natti-catuttha), clarifying their validity conditions (quorum compliance, proper boundary, complete steps, etc.) and application scenarios; the second part discusses the four categories of "adhikarana" (doctrinal disputes, censure disputes, offence disputes, duty disputes) and seven settlement rules (sammukha-vinaya, sati-vinaya, amilha-vinaya, patinatta-karana, yebhuyyasika, tassa-papiyyasika, tina-vatharaka); the third part focuses on the linguistic parallelism of terms, comparing corresponding terms in Sanskrit and Buddhist Hybrid Sanskrit, clarifying the uniqueness and inheritance of Pali Vinaya terms, and providing terminological support for the accurate interpretation of Vinaya literature.  
Keywords：Pali Vinaya；term study；samgha-kamma；dispute settlement；Buddhist precepts

# 论文5. 《桑布达偈》与后期上座部佛性论
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文聚焦《桑布达偈》（Sambuddhe verses）及后期上座部佛教的佛性论思想，梳理了相关文本的传承与教义发展。《桑布达偈》作为赞颂佛陀的核心文献，其偈颂在巴利注释、碑铭及东南亚佛教文献中多有引用，强调佛陀的圆满功德与超越性特质。研究通过比对不同时期的文本版本与注释，分析了后期上座部佛性论的核心议题：佛陀的本具性与圆满性、众生潜在佛性的表述、佛性与解脱道的关联等，指出后期上座部佛性论既延续了早期佛教对佛陀功德的称颂，又吸收了区域文化元素，形成了兼具传统性与本土化的教义体系，补充了学界对上座部佛性思想发展脉络的认知。  
关键词：桑布达偈；上座部；佛性论；巴利文献；教义传承  
Title：The Sambuddhe verses and later Theravadin Buddhology  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Sambuddhe verses and the Buddhology of later Theravada Buddhism, sorting out the transmission and doctrinal development of relevant texts. As a core text praising the Buddha, the Sambuddhe verses are frequently quoted in Pali commentaries, inscriptions, and Southeast Asian Buddhist literature, emphasizing the Buddha's perfect merits and transcendent qualities. By comparing textual versions and commentaries from different periods, the study analyzes the core issues of later Theravada Buddhology: the inherent nature and perfection of the Buddha, the expression of sentient beings' potential Buddhahood, the connection between Buddhahood and the path to liberation, etc. It points out that later Theravada Buddhology not only inherits the praise of the Buddha's merits in early Buddhism but also absorbs regional cultural elements, forming a doctrinal system with both traditionality and localization, supplementing academic understanding of the development context of Theravada Buddhahood thought.  
Keywords：Sambuddhe verses；Theravada；Buddhology；Pali literature；doctrinal transmission
