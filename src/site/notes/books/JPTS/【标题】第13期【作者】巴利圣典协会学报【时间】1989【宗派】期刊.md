# 【标题】第13期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1989【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1. 美国国会图书馆藏缅甸巴利语手稿叙录
作者：威廉·普鲁伊特（William Pruitt）  
摘要：本文系统整理了美国国会图书馆收藏的缅甸巴利语手稿，涵盖1905年“罗克希尔赠品”及1985年新增馆藏，共计127部手稿（编号Burmese-Pali 1-127）。手稿内容包括巴利三藏及注疏、本生故事、律藏文献、阿毗达磨论著、仪式文本（如Kammavaca）等，部分含缅甸语逐词翻译（nissaya）与注疏（atthakatha）。研究按编号逐一记录手稿的索书号、标题、语言类型、叶数、作者、成书年代及文献出处，校正了此前目录中的编号混乱与描述误差，补充了手稿材质（棕榈叶、金属叶、象牙叶等）与书写字体信息。该叙录为缅甸巴利语文献的跨国检索与版本比对提供了权威参考，揭示了18-19世纪缅甸佛教文献的传承与传播脉络。  
关键词：美国国会图书馆；缅甸；巴利语手稿；文献叙录；南传佛教；手稿收藏  
Title：Burmese Manuscripts in the Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.  
Author：William Pruitt  
Abstract：This paper systematically organizes the Burmese Pali manuscripts collected by the Library of Congress, including the "Rockhill gifts" from 1905 and new acquisitions from 1985, totaling 127 manuscripts (numbered Burmese-Pali 1-127). The manuscripts cover the Pali Tipitaka and its commentaries, Jataka stories, Vinaya texts, Abhidhamma treatises, ritual texts (such as Kammavaca), etc., with some containing word-by-word Burmese translations (nissaya) and commentaries (atthakatha). The study records each manuscript's call number, title, language type, number of leaves, author, composition date, and literary source by number, corrects the numbering confusion and descriptive errors in previous catalogs, and supplements information on manuscript materials (palm leaves, metal leaves, ivory leaves, etc.) and writing scripts. This catalog provides an authoritative reference for transnational retrieval and version comparison of Burmese Pali literature, revealing the inheritance and dissemination context of Burmese Buddhist literature in the 18th-19th centuries.  
Keywords：Library of Congress；Myanmar；Pali manuscripts；literature catalog；Theravada Buddhism；manuscript collection

# 论文2. 巴利语语法学家研究I： Buddhaghosa对语法及语法学家的引述
作者：奥莱·霍尔滕·平德（Ole Holten Pind）  
摘要：本文聚焦 Buddhaghosa著作中对语法及语法学家的引述，核心探究其语法知识来源与应用逻辑。研究选取《清净道论》《善见律毗婆沙》等权威著作中的案例，分析 Buddhaghosa对巴利语词汇词源、句法规则、构词法的阐释，发现其语法体系主要承袭梵语巴尼尼（Panini）语法传统，同时融入南传佛教注疏特有的术语体系（如“bhavanapumsaka”指代副词）。通过比对巴尼尼语法经论与 Buddhaghosa的引用，证实其频繁借鉴《梵语语法八章》（Ashtadhyayi）及相关注疏，尤其在格位用法、后缀功能、词汇语义分析方面与梵语语法高度契合。本文揭示了南传佛教语法研究与梵语语法传统的深层关联，为理解巴利语注疏的语言学基础提供了关键视角。  
关键词：Buddhaghosa；巴利语语法；巴尼尼语法；南传佛教注疏；梵语语法；语法术语  
Title：Studies in the Pali Grammarians I: Buddhaghosa's References to Grammar and Grammarians  
Author：Ole Holten Pind  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the references to grammar and grammarians in Buddhaghosa's works, core exploring the source and application logic of his grammatical knowledge. Selecting cases from authoritative works such as the Visuddhimagga and Samantapasadika, the study analyzes Buddhaghosa's explanations of Pali etymology, syntactic rules, and word formation, finding that his grammatical system mainly inherits the Sanskrit Paninian grammatical tradition, while integrating the unique terminology system of Theravada Buddhist commentaries (e.g., "bhavanapumsaka" referring to adverbs). By comparing the Paninian grammatical sutras and commentaries with Buddhaghosa's citations, it is confirmed that he frequently draws on the Ashtadhyayi and related commentaries, especially in case usage, suffix functions, and lexical semantic analysis, which are highly consistent with Sanskrit grammar. This paper reveals the deep connection between Theravada Buddhist grammatical research and the Sanskrit grammatical tradition, providing a key perspective for understanding the linguistic basis of Pali commentaries.  
Keywords：Buddhaghosa；Pali grammar；Paninian grammar；Theravada Buddhist commentaries；Sanskrit grammar；grammatical terminology

# 论文3. 佛塔崇拜与现存巴利语律藏
作者：格雷戈里·肖彭（Gregory Schopen）  
摘要：本文针对现存巴利语律藏未收录佛塔相关戒律这一特殊现象展开考证，质疑此前学界“律藏编纂较早未纳入”“佛塔崇拜属居士事务”等观点。通过分析12世纪斯里兰卡《摩诃帕拉卡马巴胡僧团法典》、Buddhaghosa《清净道论》及律藏《分别解脱戒》中的相关记载，发现古代南传佛教僧团实际承担佛塔相关职责（如佛塔院落维护、供养护法等），且律藏注疏中多次提及“犍度瓦塔”（Khandhakavatta）包含佛塔相关义务。结合其他宗派律藏（如说一切有部、大众部）均有佛塔戒律的对比，推测巴利语律藏原应包含此类内容，后因传承过程中的散佚或系统性删除而缺失。本文通过考古铭文与文献互证，指出佛塔崇拜是早期僧团生活的重要组成部分，巴利语律藏的相关缺失需结合历史传播语境重新解读。  
关键词：佛塔崇拜；巴利语律藏；南传佛教；僧团戒律；犍度；文献传承  
Title：The Stupa Cult and the Extant Pali Vinaya  
Author：Gregory Schopen  
Abstract：This paper investigates the special phenomenon that the extant Pali Vinaya does not include precepts related to stupas, questioning previous academic views such as "the Vinaya was compiled early and not included" and "stupa worship is the affair of laypeople". By analyzing relevant records in the 12th-century Sri Lankan Mahaparakramabahu Katikavata, Buddhaghosa's Visuddhimagga, and the Suttavibhanga of the Vinaya, it is found that ancient Theravada Buddhist Sanghas actually undertook stupa-related duties (such as maintaining stupa courtyards and offering protection). Moreover, the Vinaya commentaries repeatedly mention that the "Khandhakavatta" includes stupa-related obligations. Comparing with other Vinayas (such as Sarvastivada and Mahasanghika) which all contain stupa precepts, it is speculated that the Pali Vinaya should originally have included such content, which was later lost due to omission or systematic deletion during transmission. Through the mutual verification of archaeological inscriptions and documents, this paper points out that stupa worship was an important part of early Sangha life, and the relevant absence in the Pali Vinaya needs to be reinterpreted in the context of historical dissemination.  
Keywords：stupa cult；Pali Vinaya；Theravada Buddhism；Sangha precepts；Khandhaka；literary transmission

# 论文4. 巴特那《法句经》（第一部分：文本）
作者：玛格丽特·科恩（Margaret Cone）  
摘要：本文整理并校勘了藏于印度巴特那贾亚斯瓦尔研究所的《法句经》手稿（简称Patna Dharmapada），该手稿由拉胡拉·桑克里蒂亚纳于西藏Ngor寺拍摄，书写于12世纪后半叶，字体属原始孟加拉文。研究基于手稿照片进行逐句转写，校正了此前N.S.舒克拉与G.罗斯版本中的误读，补充了残缺音节与异文标注，完整呈现手稿的分节结构（含Jama、Apramada、Brahmana等18个章节）。通过与巴利语《法句经》、犍陀罗《法句经》及梵语佛教文献比对，揭示该手稿的混合语言特征与文本独特性，部分偈颂与现存版本存在显著差异，为早期《法句经》的文本演变与传播研究提供了珍贵的原始文献依据。  
关键词：巴特那《法句经》；巴利语手稿；文本校勘；南传佛教；法句经；文本传承  
Title：Patna Dharmapada (Part I: Text)  
Author：Margaret Cone  
Abstract：This paper organizes and collates the Dharmapada manuscript (referred to as Patna Dharmapada) collected by the K.P. Jayaswal Research Institute in Patna, India. The manuscript was photographed by Rahula Saikrtyayana at Ngor Monastery in Tibet, written in the second half of the 12th century, and its script belongs to Proto-Bengali. Based on the manuscript photos, the study transcribes each sentence, corrects the misreadings in the previous versions by N.S. Shukla and G. Roth, supplements the missing syllables and variant annotations, and fully presents the section structure of the manuscript (including 18 chapters such as Jama, Apramada, Brahmana). By comparing with the Pali Dhammapada, Gandhari Dharmapada, and Sanskrit Buddhist literature, it reveals the mixed linguistic characteristics and textual uniqueness of this manuscript. Some verses are significantly different from the existing versions, providing precious original documentary evidence for the textual evolution and dissemination of the early Dharmapada.  
Keywords：Patna Dharmapada；Pali manuscript；textual collation；Theravada Buddhism；Dhammapada；textual inheritance

# 论文5. 巴利语词典学研究VI：六个巴利语词汇的词源考证
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录、释义有误或词源存疑的六个巴利语词汇展开专项研究。包括“asitta”（受诅咒的）应源自*asapta，而非PED推测的“asita+visa”；“ghacca”（杀戮）源于词根han-的未来被动分词gharya，属动作名词用法；“niruttipatha”（言说方式）为复合词，指语言使用的约定俗成规则；“mattigha”（弑母者）对应梵语matr-ghna，体现复合词中matr-的语音演变；“gedhalrodha”（灌木丛）应释为植被相关，而非PED误释的“洞穴”。研究通过文本语境分析、梵语-巴利语词形比对及方言佐证，纠正了词典疏漏，补充了词汇的语义演变脉络，为巴利语词汇研究与词典修订提供了精准参考。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源考证；词汇释义；PED；文本语境  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies VI: Six Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper conducts a special study on six Pali words that are unincluded, have incorrect meanings, or questionable etymologies in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED). These include "asitta" (cursed), which should be derived from *asapta rather than "asita+visa" as speculated by PED；"ghacca" (killing), derived from the future passive participle gharya of the root han-, belonging to the use of action nouns；"niruttipatha" (way of speaking), a compound word referring to the conventional rules of language use；"mattigha" (matricide), corresponding to Sanskrit matr-ghna, reflecting the phonetic evolution of matr- in compound words；"gedhalrodha" (thicket), which should be explained as vegetation-related rather than "cave" as mistakenly interpreted by PED. Through textual context analysis, Sanskrit-Pali word form comparison, and dialect evidence, the study corrects dictionary omissions, supplements the semantic evolution of words, and provides accurate references for Pali vocabulary research and dictionary revision.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；word definition；PED；textual context
