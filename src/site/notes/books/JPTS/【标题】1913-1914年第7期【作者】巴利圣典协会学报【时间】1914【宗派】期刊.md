# 【标题】1913-1914年第7期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1914【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  阿努律陀的《名色分别论》
作者：A.P. 佛陀达多（A. P. Buddhadatta）  
摘要：本文编辑整理了南传佛教阿毗达磨文献《名色分别论》（Namarupapariccheda），该文献由摩诃毗诃罗传承的三藏持者、具德阿努律陀长老创作，是南传佛教重要的阿毗达磨纲要著作之一。编辑过程中，作者参考了缅甸语与僧伽罗语手稿及相关注释，纠正了文本中大量讹误与不通顺之处，以罗马字母转写出版。文献核心围绕“名”（精神现象）与“色”（物质现象）的分类、特性、相互关系及相关修行观展开，系统阐释了心识、心所法、色法的构成与运作，为阿毗达磨名色理论研究提供了权威且精准的文本依据。  
关键词：《名色分别论》；阿努律陀；阿毗达磨；名色理论；南传佛教文献校勘  
Title：The Namarupapariccheda of Anuruddha  
Author：A. P. Buddhadatta  
Abstract：This paper edits and collates the Theravada Buddhist Abhidhamma text "Namarupapariccheda" (Distinction of Name and Form), which was composed by the virtuous Elder Anuruddha, a holder of the Tripitaka from the Mahavihara tradition and a master of the Dhamma. During the editing process, the author referenced Burmese and Sinhalese manuscripts and related commentaries, corrected numerous errors and ungrammatical expressions in the text, and published it in Roman script transcription. The core of the text focuses on the classification, characteristics, interrelationships, and related practice perspectives of "nama" (mental phenomena) and "rupa" (material phenomena), systematically explaining the composition and operation of consciousness, mental factors, and material phenomena, providing an authoritative and accurate textual basis for the study of the Abhidhamma theory of nama and rupa.  
Keywords：Namarupapariccheda；Anuruddha；Abhidhamma；Theory of Nama and Rupa；Textual Collation of Theravada Buddhist Literature

# 论文2.  佛教教义中的若干要点
作者：雷迪长老（Ledi Sadaw）；翻译：S.Z. 昂（S. Z. Aung）；编辑：里斯·戴维斯夫人（Mrs. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文节选自雷迪长老关于《双对论》（Yamaka）的论文附录，聚焦南传佛教教义的核心哲学问题展开深入阐释。内容涵盖佛陀的三方面全知、“根”（mūla）及其同义词的核心意义、名言的世俗谛与胜义谛双重内涵、五蕴的分类依据、元素（dhatu）的特性、苦谛的多层含义、爱欲与无明作为苦之根源的关系、涅槃的三种形态、意识与认知的分类及运作、善不善无记的界定、法的本质、根门（indriya）的控制功能等。通过逻辑论证与譬喻阐释，澄清了对因果缘起、无我、无常等核心教义的误解，凸显了阿毗达磨教义的思辨深度与实践价值。  
关键词：南传佛教；阿毗达磨；因果缘起；无我；涅槃；意识理论  
Title：Some Points in Buddhist Doctrine  
Author：Ledi Sadaw；Translator: S. Z. Aung；Editor: Mrs. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is selected from the appendix of Elder Ledi Sadaw's dissertation on the "Yamaka" (Pair Treatise), focusing on in-depth explanations of core philosophical issues in Theravada Buddhist doctrine. The content includes the Buddha's three aspects of omniscience, the core meaning of "mūla" (root) and its synonyms, the dual connotations of names in conventional truth and ultimate truth, the classification basis of the five aggregates, the characteristics of elements (dhatu), the multi-layered meaning of the truth of suffering, the relationship between craving and ignorance as the roots of suffering, the three forms of nibbana, the classification and operation of consciousness and cognition, the definition of wholesome, unwholesome, and indeterminate phenomena, the nature of dhamma, and the controlling function of indriya (sense faculties). Through logical arguments and metaphorical explanations, it clarifies misunderstandings about core doctrines such as dependent origination, anatta (non-self), and anicca (impermanence), highlighting the speculative depth and practical value of Abhidhamma doctrine.  
Keywords：Theravada Buddhism；Abhidhamma；Paticcasamuppada (Dependent Origination)；Anatta (Non-self)；Nibbana；Consciousness Theory

# 论文3.  《人施设论》注疏（摘自考伽罗摩的《五论注疏》，归于觉音）
作者：觉音（Buddhaghosa，归于）；编辑：格奥尔格·兰茨贝格博士（Dr. Georg Landsberg）、里斯·戴维斯夫人（Mrs. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文是《阿毗达磨藏》第四部《人施设论》（Puggala-Pannatti）的首部欧洲语编辑本，依据僧伽罗语棕榈叶手稿、纸质手稿及仰光缅甸文字体版本整理校勘而成。注疏围绕“施设”（pannatti）的本质展开，系统阐释了六种施设的分类（蕴施设、处施设、界施设、谛施设、根施设、人设施设），重点解析了人设施设的内涵、分类及相关哲学争议。通过对“有学”“无学”“凡夫”“不动法者”“动法者”等佛教人格类型的界定，结合因果缘起与无我理论，澄清了对“人”的实有执见，为理解南传佛教的人格观、认识论提供了关键文献支撑。  
关键词：《人施设论》；觉音；阿毗达磨；施设；南传佛教人格观  
Title：Puggala-Pannatti-Atthakatha (from the Pancappakarana-Atthakatha, attributed to Buddhaghosa)  
Author：Attributed to Buddhaghosa；Editors: Dr. Georg Landsberg, Mrs. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the first European edition of the commentary on the "Puggala-Pannatti" (Designation of Persons), the fourth book of the Abhidhamma Pitaka, collated and edited based on Sinhalese palm-leaf manuscripts, paper manuscripts, and the Rangoon Burmese script edition. The commentary focuses on the nature of "pannatti" (designation), systematically explaining the classification of six types of pannatti (aggregate designation, base designation, element designation, truth designation, faculty designation, person designation), with an emphasis on analyzing the connotation, classification, and related philosophical controversies of person designation. By defining Buddhist personality types such as "sekha" (trainee), "asekkha" (non-trainee), "puthujjana" (ordinary person), "akuppadhamma" (immovable phenomenon), and "kuppadhamma" (movable phenomenon), combined with the theories of dependent origination and non-self, it clarifies the substantialist view of "person," providing key textual support for understanding the personality view and epistemology of Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Puggala-Pannatti；Buddhaghosa；Abhidhamma；Pannatti (Designation)；Theravada Buddhist Personality View

# 论文4.  《双对论》论文补正（雷迪长老著）
作者：雷迪长老（Ledi Sadaw）；补正：雷迪长老、S.Z. 昂（S. Z. Aung）、译者  
摘要：本文是对1913年出版的雷迪长老《双对论》相关论文的补充与勘误，包含原文遗漏的词句、拼写错误修正、语法调整及语义补正等内容。补正内容覆盖文本的核心哲学论述部分，涉及因果关系、心识分类、善不善法界定、涅槃特性等关键教义阐释，确保了原论文文本的准确性与可读性，为相关研究提供了修正后的可靠依据。  
关键词：《双对论》；雷迪长老；文本补正；南传佛教；阿毗达磨文献  
Title：Additions and Corrections to a Dissertation on the Yamaka by Ledi Sadaw  
Author：Ledi Sadaw；Revisers: Ledi Sadaw, S. Z. Aung, Translator  
Abstract：This paper is the additions and corrections to Elder Ledi Sadaw's dissertation on the "Yamaka" published in 1913, including omitted words and sentences, spelling error corrections, grammatical adjustments, and semantic rectifications in the original text. The corrected content covers the core philosophical discussions of the text, involving the interpretation of key doctrines such as causal relationships, consciousness classification, the definition of wholesome and unwholesome phenomena, and the characteristics of nibbana, ensuring the accuracy and readability of the original dissertation and providing a revised and reliable basis for related research.  
Keywords：Yamaka；Ledi Sadaw；Textual Additions and Corrections；Theravada Buddhism；Abhidhamma Literature
