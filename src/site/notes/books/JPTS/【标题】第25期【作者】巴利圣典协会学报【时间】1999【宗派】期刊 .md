# 【标题】第25期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1999【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 律藏术语研究（第四至十部分）
作者：伊迪丝·诺洛（Edith Nolot）  
摘要：本文是“律藏术语研究”系列的第四至十部分，旨在纪念爱德华·伯克哈德（1902-1998）。研究聚焦巴利律藏中的七类纪律程序（谴责、依赖、驱逐、和解召唤、三类暂停）、相关术语（驱逐、恢复）、刑罚类型（逐出、惩罚、宣告程序、拒受布施、最高惩罚）及附录中“顽固错误”裁决的相关内容。通过对比巴利文原典与梵文、藏文等平行文献，详细分析各类术语的语义、适用场景、程序规范及历史演变，厘清不同纪律措施的适用条件、执行流程与撤销规则，补充修正此前系列研究中关于“tassa-papiyyasika”术语的解读，为律藏文献的深度研究提供精准的术语释义与文本佐证。  
关键词：巴利律藏；术语研究；纪律程序；刑罚类型；佛教戒律  
Title：Studies in Vinaya Technical Terms IV-X  
Author：Edith Nolot  
Abstract：This paper is the fourth to tenth parts of the "Studies in Vinaya Technical Terms" series, dedicated to the memory of Edouard Burckard (1902-1998). It focuses on seven types of disciplinary procedures in the Pali Vinaya (blame, dependence, banishment, summons to reconciliation, threefold suspension), related terms (dismissal, restoration), penalty types (expulsion, punishment, proclamation procedure, boycott of alms, maximal punishment), and the content of the "obstinate wrongness" verdict in the appendix. By comparing the Pali original texts with parallel documents in Sanskrit, Tibetan, etc., it detailedly analyzes the semantics, application scenarios, procedural norms, and historical evolution of various terms, clarifies the applicable conditions, implementation processes, and revocation rules of different disciplinary measures, supplements and corrects the interpretation of the term "tassa-papiyyasika" in previous series studies, and provides accurate term definitions and textual evidence for in-depth research on Vinaya literature.  
Keywords：Pali Vinaya；term study；disciplinary procedures；penalty types；Buddhist precepts

# 论文2. 《分别善恶业报经》中的六十四种世界毁灭
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文聚焦达萨巴拉希米特拉在《分别善恶业报经》中引用的上座部教义，围绕“六十四种世界毁灭”展开研究。该文本仅存藏语译本，散文部分与《清净道论》等巴利文献相近，韵文部分则在后世巴利著作中多有引用但未明确归属。研究通过翻译藏语译本中的相关散文与韵文，对比《阿毗达摩毗婆沙论》《俱舍论》《法句经注》等文献中的平行内容，揭示六十四种毁灭的构成（五十六次火毁灭、七次水毁灭、一次风毁灭）及序列规律，指出其与说一切有部宇宙观的共通性，为理解早期佛教宇宙论提供跨文本比较视角。  
关键词：六十四种毁灭；分别善恶业报经；上座部；宇宙论；佛教文献  
Title：The Sixty-four Destructions according to the Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Sthavira tenets cited by Dasabalasrimitra in his Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya, centering on the research of "the sixty-four destructions of the world". This text only survives in a Tibetan translation; the prose part is similar to Pali documents such as the Visuddhimagga, while the verse part is frequently quoted in later Pali works without clear attribution. By translating the relevant prose and verses in the Tibetan translation and comparing parallel contents in documents such as the Abhidharmavibhasa, Abhidharmakosa, and Dhammapada-atthakatha, the study reveals the composition (fifty-six destructions by fire, seven by water, one by wind) and sequence rules of the sixty-four destructions, points out its commonalities with the Vaibhasika cosmology, and provides an intertextual comparative perspective for understanding early Buddhist cosmology.  
Keywords：sixty-four destructions；Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya；Sthavira；cosmology；Buddhist literature

# 论文3. 巴利尼柯耶中的中阴身与高级烦恼
作者：G.A. 索马拉特纳（G.A. Somaratne）  
摘要：本文探讨巴利尼柯耶中“中般涅槃者”（antaraparinibbayin）类不还果圣者及其对存在的渴望与中阴身相关问题，对上座部正统教义的核心观点提出质疑。通过梳理四种圣者（预流果、一来果、不还果、阿罗汉果）的分类体系，重点分析不还果圣者的十种细分类型，尤其是中般涅槃者的特质与所缚烦恼。研究指出传统“十烦恼”列表存在缺陷，未包含“存在欲”这一关键烦恼，而部分尼柯耶文献隐含中阴身概念，中般涅槃者虽无再生烦恼却仍有存在烦恼，其涅槃过程需依托中阴状态，进而修正传统烦恼列表，为理解佛教解脱道阶位与生死流转提供新的文本依据。  
关键词：中阴身；不还果；中般涅槃者；烦恼；巴利尼柯耶  
Title：Intermediate Existence and the Higher Fetters in the Pali Nikayas  
Author：G.A. Somaratne  
Abstract：This paper discusses the antaraparinibbayin non-returners in the Pali Nikayas, their desire for existence, and issues related to the intermediate existence, casting doubt on a major point of Theravada orthodoxy. By sorting out the classification system of the four noble persons (stream-enterer, once-returner, non-returner, arahant), it focuses on analyzing the ten subdivisions of non-returners, especially the characteristics and bound fetters of antaraparinibbayins. The study points out that the traditional list of "ten fetters" is flawed as it does not include the key fetter of "desire for existence", while some Nikaya texts implicitly contain the concept of intermediate existence. Antaraparinibbayins, though free from rebirth fetters, still have existence fetters, and their nirvana process must rely on the intermediate state. It further revises the traditional list of fetters, providing new textual evidence for understanding the stages of the Buddhist path to liberation and the cycle of birth and death.  
Keywords：intermediate existence；non-returner；antaraparinibbayin；fetters；Pali Nikayas

# 论文4. 关于《甘露藏》及相关文献的评述
作者：纯子松村（Junko Matsumura）  
摘要：本文基于作者1992年出版的博士论文，概述《甘露藏》（Rasavahini）第五、六卷（《 Migapotakavagga》与《Uttaroliya-vagga》）的校勘成果，并补充相关研究进展。《甘露藏》是上座部佛教流行的后经典文献，作者韦德哈长老归属森林僧团，其成书年代经考证应为13世纪后半叶。研究梳理《甘露藏》与《千故事集》（Sahassavatthuppakarana）、僧伽罗语译本《Saddharmalankaraya》等相关文献的源流关系，分析文本的版本系统与校勘原则，指出僧伽罗语印刷本存在的问题，强调以平行文献佐证校勘的重要性，同时介绍文献在斯里兰卡及东南亚的传播情况，为该文献的进一步整理与研究提供基础框架。  
关键词：甘露藏；韦德哈长老；文献校勘；上座部后经典文献；僧伽罗语译本  
Title：Remarks on the Rasavahini and the Related Literature  
Author：Junko Matsumura  
Abstract：Based on the author's doctoral thesis published in 1992, this paper outlines the textual emendation achievements of the fifth and sixth volumes of the Rasavahini (Migapotakavagga and Uttaroliya-vagga) and supplements relevant research progress. The Rasavahini is a popular post-canonical literature in Theravada Buddhism; its author, Vedeha Thera, belonged to the Forest Fraternity, and its compilation date is verified to be the second half of the 13th century. The study sorts out the source relationship between the Rasavahini and related literatures such as the Sahassavatthuppakarana and the Sinhalese translation Saddharmalankaraya, analyzes the text's version system and editorial principles, points out the problems existing in Sinhalese printed editions, emphasizes the importance of using parallel documents to support emendation, and introduces the dissemination of the literature in Sri Lanka and Southeast Asia, providing a basic framework for the further collation and research of this document.  
Keywords：Rasavahini；Vedeha Thera；textual emendation；Theravada post-canonical literature；Sinhalese translation

# 论文5. 巴利圣典协会学报（第九至二十四卷）索引
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文为《巴利圣典协会学报》第九至二十四卷（1981-1997年出版）编制索引，是对1973年出版的第一至八卷索引的补充。索引按作者姓氏排序，收录各卷发表的论文、译文、书评等各类文献条目，明确标注每篇文献的发表卷次与页码，涵盖巴利语文学、律藏研究、术语释义、文献校勘、佛教哲学等多个研究领域。该索引为研究者快速检索学报往期成果提供便捷工具，助力巴利学相关主题的系统性研究与文献梳理。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会学报；索引；巴利学研究；文献检索；学术成果整理  
Title：An Index to JPTS Volumes IX-XXIV  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper compiles an index for Volumes IX to XXIV of the Journal of the Pali Text Society (published from 1981 to 1997), supplementing the index of Volumes I to VIII published in 1973. Arranged by authors' surnames, the index includes entries of various literatures such as papers, translations, and book reviews published in each volume, clearly marking the publication volume and page number of each document, covering multiple research fields including Pali literature, Vinaya studies, term interpretation, textual emendation, and Buddhist philosophy. This index provides a convenient tool for researchers to quickly retrieve past achievements of the journal, facilitating systematic research and literature sorting on Pali studies-related topics.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；index；Pali studies；literature retrieval；academic achievement collation
