# 【标题】第14期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1990【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1. 泰国北部三十部巴利语手稿的题跋研究
作者：哈拉尔德·亨迪乌斯（Harald Hundius）  
摘要：本文聚焦泰国北部三十部巴利语手稿的题跋，以1972-1974年拍摄的微缩胶片为研究基础，涵盖15至19世纪的文献遗存。题跋以北部泰语撰写，附于巴利语正文之后，记录了手稿抄写的时间、地点、抄写者、资助者、创作目的及个人祈愿等关键信息。研究梳理了兰那王国时期巴利语传统的历史脉络，分析了题跋作为历史文献的价值——包含语言演变证据、社会宗教关系记录、 manuscript制作的功德观与社会协作模式，同时考证了“Daa Sooy”等历史地名及古鲁·帕·卡icana等宗教人物的影响。本文通过转写、音系标注与翻译三种形式呈现题跋文本，为兰那文学遗产保护与南传佛教文献研究提供了基础资料。  
关键词：泰国北部；巴利语手稿；题跋；兰那传统；南传佛教；文献保护  
Title：The Colophons of Thirty Pali Manuscripts from Northern Thailand  
Author：Harald Hundius  
Abstract：This study focuses on the colophons of thirty Pali manuscripts from Northern Thailand, based on microfilms made between 1972 and 1974, covering literary relics from the 15th to the 19th centuries. Written in Northern Thai and appended to the Pali main texts, the colophons record key information such as the time, place, scribe, sponsor, purpose, and personal aspirations of the manuscript copying. The research sorts out the historical context of the Pali tradition during the Lanna Kingdom period, analyzes the value of colophons as historical documents—including evidence of linguistic evolution, records of social and religious relations, the merit concept and social cooperation model of manuscript production, and verifies the influence of historical place names such as "Daa Sooy" and religious figures like Gruu Paa Kafcana. This paper presents the colophon texts in three forms: transliteration, phonematic transcription, and translation, providing basic data for the preservation of Lanna literary heritage and the study of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Northern Thailand；Pali manuscripts；colophons；Lanna tradition；Theravada Buddhism；literature preservation

# 论文2. 巴利语语法学家研究II.1
作者：奥莱·霍尔滕·平德（Ole Holten Pind）  
摘要：本文延续对巴利语语法学家的系列研究，聚焦 Buddhaghosa、Mahanama、Buddhadatta 等学者的语法阐释，核心分析其对 Panini 语法体系的借鉴与适配。研究分为三部分：一是 Buddhaghosa 对 itthambhitakkhyana、ithambhitalakkhana 等句法范畴的解读，及其在《Samantapasadika》等注疏中的应用；二是《Paramatthajotika I-II》中的语法参考文献考证；三是后 Buddhaghosa 时代学者对巴利语语法的发展，包括 Mahanama 对绝对位格的阐释与 Buddhadatta 对“Buddha”一词的三种词源分析。本文揭示了南传佛教语法研究对梵语语法传统的继承与本土化改造，为理解巴利语注疏的语法依据提供了新视角。  
关键词：巴利语语法；Buddhaghosa；Panini 语法；南传佛教注疏；句法分析；词源考证  
Title：Studies in the Pali Grammarians II.1  
Author：Ole Holten Pind  
Abstract：This paper continues the series of studies on Pali grammarians, focusing on the grammatical interpretations of scholars such as Buddhaghosa, Mahanama, and Buddhadatta, with a core analysis of their borrowing and adaptation of the Paninian grammatical system. The research is divided into three parts: first, Buddhaghosa's interpretation of syntactic categories such as itthambhitakkhyana and ithambhitalakkhana, and their application in commentaries like the Samantapasadika；second, the textual research on grammatical references in the Paramatthajotika I-II；third, the development of Pali grammar by scholars in the post-Buddhaghosa era, including Mahanama's explanation of the locativus absolutus and Buddhadatta's three etymological analyses of the word "Buddha". This paper reveals the inheritance and localized transformation of the Sanskrit grammatical tradition in Theravada Buddhist grammatical research, providing a new perspective for understanding the grammatical basis of Pali commentaries.  
Keywords：Pali grammar；Buddhaghosa；Paninian grammar；Theravada Buddhist commentaries；syntactic analysis；etymological research

# 论文3. 巴利语词典学研究VIII：七个巴利语词汇的词源考证
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录或释义、词源有误的七个巴利语词汇展开专项考证。包括“aharita”（带来）实为古巴利语独立分词，纠正《巴利语批判词典》（CPD）将其归为“ahariya”变体的误判；“chandaso”（自愿地）与“chanda”（意愿）相关，而非“韵律、吠陀语”；“vac'-uggata”（口头习得的）源自“avagata”（习得），并非“发音优美”等。研究通过文本语境分析与语言演变溯源，补充了词汇的语义演变脉络与实际用法，修正了词典疏漏，为巴利语词汇研究与词典编纂提供了精准参考。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源考证；词汇释义；PED；CPD  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies VIII: Seven Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper conducts a special investigation on seven Pali words that are either unincluded or have incorrect meanings/etymologies in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED). These include "aharita" (having brought), which is actually an independent participle in Old Pali, correcting the misjudgment of the Critical Pali Dictionary (CPD) that classifies it as a variant of "ahariya"；"chandaso" (willingly), which is related to "chanda" (will) rather than "metre, Vedic language"；"vac'-uggata" (learnt orally), derived from "avagata" (learnt) instead of "well-intoned", etc. Through textual context analysis and the tracing of linguistic evolution, the study supplements the semantic evolution and practical usage of the words, corrects dictionary omissions, and provides accurate references for Pali vocabulary research and lexicography.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；word definition；PED；CPD

# 论文4. 《巴利圣典协会学报》第9-15期索引
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文编制了《巴利圣典协会学报》第9-15期（1981-1990年）的文献索引，按作者姓氏字母顺序编排，收录该时期发表的所有论文、校注、译文及书评。每条索引条目包含作者、文章标题、发表期号与页码，涵盖南传佛教文献校勘、巴利语语言学、阿毗达磨研究、律藏解读、佛教艺术与考古等多个研究领域。该索引为南传佛教研究者快速检索相关文献提供了高效工具，助力学术资源的整合与利用。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会学报；文献索引；南传佛教研究；巴利语研究；学术检索  
Title：An Index to JPTS Volumes IX-XV  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper compiles a literature index for Volumes IX-XV (1981-1990) of the Journal of the Pali Text Society, arranged alphabetically by the author's surname, including all papers, editions, translations, and book reviews published during this period. Each index entry includes the author, article title, issue number, and page number, covering multiple research fields such as Theravada Buddhist text criticism, Pali linguistics, Abhidhamma studies, Vinaya interpretation, and Buddhist art and archaeology. This index provides an efficient tool for Theravada Buddhist researchers to quickly retrieve relevant literature, facilitating the integration and utilization of academic resources.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；literature index；Theravada Buddhist studies；Pali studies；academic retrieval
