# 【标题】第19期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1993【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 韦尔科姆研究所藏缅甸与暹罗巴利语手稿叙录
作者：杰奎琳·菲利奥扎（Jacqueline Filliozat）  
摘要：本文系统叙录了伦敦韦尔科姆研究所收藏的106份缅甸与暹罗巴利语手稿，含140部已识别文本，涵盖戒律、经藏、论藏及注疏类文献。手稿载体包括棕榈叶、金属板、布料、象牙板等，抄写时间集中于18-19世纪，部分为欧洲馆藏孤本。叙录详细记录每份手稿的形制、尺寸、字体、装饰特征及内容概要，重点整理了《羯磨书》（Kammavaca）相关手稿的版本差异，以及《长部尼柯耶》《中部尼柯耶》等经典片段的留存状况。研究为南传佛教文献校勘、巴利语手稿物质文化研究提供了基础数据，其多元载体与装饰风格也反映了东南亚佛教文献的制作传统。  
关键词：韦尔科姆研究所；缅甸巴利语手稿；暹罗巴利语手稿；文献叙录；南传佛教  
Title：A Survey of the Burmese and Siamese Pali Manuscript Collections in the Wellcome Institute  
Author：Jacqueline Filliozat  
Abstract：This paper systematically catalogs 106 Burmese and Siamese Pali manuscripts in the collection of the Wellcome Institute in London, including 140 identified texts covering Vinaya, Sutta Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka, and commentarial literature. The manuscripts are written on various carriers such as palm leaves, metal plates, cloth, and ivory plates, dating mainly from the 18th to 19th centuries, with some being unique copies in European collections. The catalog records in detail the form, size, script, decorative features, and content summary of each manuscript, focusing on sorting out the textual variations of manuscripts related to the Kammavaca and the preservation status of fragments from canonical texts such as the Digha Nikaya and Majjhima Nikaya. The study provides basic data for the collation of Theravada Buddhist literature and the material cultural research of Pali manuscripts, and their diverse carriers and decorative styles also reflect the production traditions of Southeast Asian Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Wellcome Institute；Burmese Pali manuscripts；Siamese Pali manuscripts；literature catalog；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文2. 巴黎馆藏巴利语手稿中的《未来史》注疏
作者：杰奎琳·菲利奥扎（Jacqueline Filliozat）  
摘要：本文对巴黎国家图书馆及巴黎远东学院馆藏的12份与《未来史》（Anagatavamsa）相关的巴利语注疏手稿进行了校勘与分类研究。这些手稿包括《十菩萨赞》（Dasabodhisatta-uddesa）的四个版本、《甘露流注注疏》（Amatarasadhara-tika）的七个版本及《普贤注疏》（Samantabhaddha-tika）的一个版本，均为柬埔寨Mul字体抄写的棕榈叶手稿。研究梳理了各注疏的篇章结构、作者归属（如优波底沙）及文本传承关系，对比分析了不同版本对未来佛弥勒事迹、菩萨修行阶位等核心内容的阐释差异。通过与斯里兰卡、老挝相关文献的比对，揭示了《未来史》注疏在东南亚的传播脉络，为研究后期南传佛教弥勒信仰提供了关键文本证据。  
关键词：《未来史》；巴利语注疏；巴黎馆藏手稿；弥勒信仰；南传佛教文献  
Title：The Commentaries to the Anagatavamsa in the Pali Manuscripts of the Paris Collections  
Author：Jacqueline Filliozat  
Abstract：This paper collates and classifies 12 Pali commentarial manuscripts related to the Anagatavamsa in the collections of the Bibliothèque nationale de France and the École française d'Extrême-Orient in Paris. These manuscripts include four versions of the Dasabodhisatta-uddesa, seven versions of the Amatarasadhara-tika, and one version of the Samantabhaddha-tika, all palm-leaf manuscripts written in Khmer Mul script. The study sorts out the chapter structure, author attribution (such as Upatissa), and textual transmission relationship of each commentary, and comparatively analyzes the interpretive differences of core contents such as the deeds of Metteyya, the future Buddha, and the stages of bodhisattva practice among different versions. By comparing with relevant literature from Sri Lanka and Laos, it reveals the dissemination context of the Anagatavamsa commentaries in Southeast Asia, providing key textual evidence for the study of Metteyya belief in later Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Anagatavamsa；Pali commentaries；Paris collection manuscripts；Metteyya belief；Theravada Buddhist literature

# 论文3. 《戒律书》校勘补正
作者：提拉达摩比丘（Thiradhammo Bhikkhu）  
摘要：本文针对I.B.霍纳翻译的《戒律书》（The Book of the Discipline）前五卷中的翻译错误与文本疏漏进行校勘补正。补正内容包括词汇释义修正（如明确“gana”“sangha”的僧团人数界定）、句式结构调整（使戒律条文更符合巴利语原典语法）、注释偏差纠正（如修正对非出家众相关戒律的性质判定）及排版错误修订（如页码标注、注释编号顺序）。研究基于巴利语原典及南传佛教戒律传承传统，指出部分错误源于对巴利语多义词汇的误读与文化背景的隔阂，补正后的文本更贴合戒律原义，为英语世界的南传戒律研究提供了更可靠的译本参考。  
关键词：《戒律书》；校勘补正；巴利语戒律；南传佛教；翻译研究  
Title：Corrections to The Book of the Discipline  
Author：Thiradhammo Bhikkhu  
Abstract：This paper collates and corrects the translation errors and textual omissions in the first five volumes of The Book of the Discipline translated by I.B. Horner. The corrections include the revision of vocabulary interpretations (such as clarifying the definition of the number of monks in "gana" and "sangha"), the adjustment of sentence structures (to make the Vinaya provisions more in line with the grammar of the Pali original), the correction of annotation deviations (such as revising the determination of the nature of precepts related to laypeople), and the revision of typesetting errors (such as page numbering and the order of annotation numbers). Based on the Pali original and the transmission tradition of Theravada Buddhist Vinaya, the study points out that some errors stem from the misreading of polysemous Pali words and the lack of cultural background understanding. The corrected text is more in line with the original meaning of the Vinaya, providing a more reliable translation reference for Theravada Vinaya research in the English-speaking world.  
Keywords：The Book of the Discipline；textual correction；Pali Vinaya；Theravada Buddhism；translation research

# 论文4. 藏译南传佛教文献研究
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文系统梳理了藏文大藏经（甘珠尔、丹珠尔）中留存的南传佛教文献，包括甘珠尔中的14部独立文本（如《转法轮经》《本生因缘经》《慈经》等）、丹珠尔中的《解脱道论》第三章节选及各类文献中的南传教义引文。研究考证了这些文献的翻译背景——14部甘珠尔文本由斯里兰卡高僧阿难陀室利与藏族译师尼玛坚赞于14世纪初合作译出，部分丹珠尔引文源自印度论师对南传教义的援引。通过比对巴利语原典与藏译文本，分析了翻译过程中的语言适配（如梵语化表述调整）与教义阐释差异，揭示了南传佛教通过西藏与北传佛教的思想交流轨迹，补充了南传文献传播史的跨区域研究维度。  
关键词：藏译文献；南传佛教；甘珠尔；丹珠尔；文献传播  
Title：Theravadin Literature in Tibetan Translation  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the Theravada Buddhist literature preserved in the Tibetan Buddhist Canon (Kanjur and Tanjur), including 14 independent texts in the Kanjur (such as the Dharmacakrapravartana-sutra, Jataka-nidana, Maitri-sutra, etc.), an excerpt from Chapter 3 of the Vimuttimagga in the Tanjur, and citations of Theravada doctrines in various literatures. The study investigates the translation background of these documents—the 14 Kanjur texts were jointly translated by the Sri Lankan elder Anandasri and the Tibetan translator Ni ma rgyal mtshan in the early 14th century, and some citations in the Tanjur are derived from the quotation of Theravada doctrines by Indian masters. By comparing the Pali original with the Tibetan translations, it analyzes the linguistic adaptation (such as the adjustment of Sanskritized expressions) and doctrinal interpretation differences during the translation process, revealing the ideological exchange trajectory between Theravada Buddhism and Northern Buddhism through Tibet, and supplementing the cross-regional research dimension of Theravada literature dissemination history.  
Keywords：Tibetan translated literature；Theravada Buddhism；Kanjur；Tanjur；literature dissemination

# 论文5. 巴利语外部连声研究（以《经集》为重点）
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文以《经集》（Suttanipata）为核心语料，系统研究巴利语外部连声的规则与实际应用特征。外部连声指巴利语中词尾元音与后一词词首元音的缩合、省略或音变现象，与梵语连声存在本质差异——巴利语连声更具任意性，仅适用于句法紧密关联的词汇，允许元音分立，且常出现-i/-u音省略而非梵语式的-y/-v音化。研究分类整理了《经集》中-a、-ā、-i、-u等词尾元音与各类词首元音的连声组合形式，举例说明长元音在复辅音前的特殊变化，并指出部分连声形式为模仿梵语的超规范用法。附录提供了连声形式反向检索表，为巴利语初学者及文献校勘者提供实用工具，也为巴利语语言学史研究补充了基础数据。  
关键词：巴利语；外部连声；《经集》；语言学研究；连声规则  
Title：External Sandhi in Pali (with Special Reference to the Suttanipata)  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper takes the Suttanipata as the core corpus to systematically study the rules and practical application characteristics of external sandhi in Pali. External sandhi refers to the contraction, elision, or sound change of final vowels and initial vowels of subsequent words in Pali, which is fundamentally different from Sanskrit sandhi—Pali sandhi is more arbitrary, applicable only to syntactically closely related words, allowing vowel hiatus, and often showing elision of -i/-u sounds rather than the -y/-v sound change in Sanskrit. The study classifies and sorts out the sandhi combinations of final vowels such as -a, -ā, -i, -u with various initial vowels in the Suttanipata, illustrates the special changes of long vowels before consonant clusters with examples, and points out that some sandhi forms are hypercorrect usages imitating Sanskrit. The appendix provides a reverse lookup table of sandhi forms, offering a practical tool for Pali beginners and text collators, and supplementing basic data for the study of Pali linguistic history.  
Keywords：Pali；external sandhi；Suttanipata；linguistic research；sandhi rules
