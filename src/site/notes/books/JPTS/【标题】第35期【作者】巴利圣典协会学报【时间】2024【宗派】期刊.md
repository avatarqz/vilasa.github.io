# 期刊名字：【标题】第35期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】2024【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. K. R. 诺曼：1925-2020
作者：埃文德·卡尔斯（Eivind Kahrs）  
摘要：本文纪念著名巴利语和俗语学者肯尼斯·罗伊·诺曼教授（1925-2020）。诺曼教授一生致力于中印度雅利安语文献研究，曾任剑桥大学印度研究教授、巴利圣典协会会长及期刊主编，编纂《巴利三藏索引》《巴利语批判词典》等重要著作，翻译《长老偈》《长老尼偈》《经集》《法句经》等核心经典，其八卷《论文集》对巴利语和俗语研究影响深远。文章追溯其学术生涯、学术贡献及个人品格，展现他在该领域的卓越地位。  
关键词：K. R. 诺曼；巴利语研究；中印度雅利安语；巴利圣典协会；佛教文献翻译  
Title：K. R. Norman: 1925–2020  
Author：Eivind Kahrs  
Abstract：Professor Kenneth Roy Norman passed away peacefully on 5 November 2020 at the age of ninety-five. With his death, the world of Middle Indo-Aryan philology, also known as the study of Pali and Prakrit, has lost one of its most eminent scholars and one of its clearest voices ever. Roy Norman focused on Sanskrit and Middle Indo-Aryan dialects throughout his academic career at the University of Cambridge. He made immense contributions to Pali studies, including editing the Pāli Tipiṭakaṃ Concordance and A Critical Pāli Dictionary, translating major metrical texts such as the Theragāthā, Therīgāthā, Suttanipāta and Dhammapada, and publishing eight volumes of Collected Papers. This article traces his academic career, scholarly achievements and personal qualities, highlighting his outstanding status in the field.  
Keywords：K. R. Norman；Pali studies；Middle Indo-Aryan；Pali Text Society；Buddhist literature translation

# 论文2. 上座部佛教中的言语惯例（rūḷhi）：概括法则
作者：阿鲁纳·加马奇（Aruna Gamage）  
摘要：词语的语义演变是多种语言的常见现象，巴利语中部分术语的本义会被次要含义替代，形成“言语惯例（rūḷhi）”。本文聚焦上座部佛教文献中“语义扩展型”言语惯例，通过地名、圣地名称、宗教术语等案例，分析摩诃毗诃罗派注释家对这些惯例的阐释。研究表明，巴利语圣典中的诸多术语需结合注释文献还原其语义演变轨迹，言语惯例的阐释不仅具有语文学价值，更对佛教教义传承与修行实践具有重要意义。  
关键词：上座部佛教；言语惯例（rūḷhi）；语义演变；巴利语术语；佛教注释文献  
Title：Convention of Speech (rūḷhi) in Theriya Buddhism: The Law of Generalization  
Author：Aruna Gamage  
Abstract：Semantic development of words is a common phenomenon in many languages. Once a word’s secondary meaning has developed, its literal meaning may either become redundant or disappear. This article focuses on "semantic expansion" rūḷhi (convention of speech) in Theravada Buddhist literature, analyzing the exegeses of these conventions by Mahāvihāra school commentators through cases such as place names, shrine names and religious terms. The study shows that many terms in Pali scriptures require reference to commentarial literature to restore their semantic evolution trajectory. The interpretation of rūḷhi not only has philological value but also is of great significance for the inheritance of Buddhist doctrines and practice.  
Keywords：Theriya Buddhism；convention of speech (rūḷhi)；semantic development；Pali terms；Buddhist commentarial literature

# 论文3. 19世纪缅甸关于寺院边界的两项法律判定：Khaṅma kan charā tau 摩尼约塔的《寺院边界判定》及附录Aoṅmre rhve bhuṃcharā tau凯图达玛的《寺院边界判定》）
作者：佩特拉·基弗-普尔茨（Petra Kieffer-Pülz）、D. 克里斯蒂安·拉默茨（D. Christian Lammerts）  
摘要：本文研究缅甸19世纪两件关于寺院边界（sīmā）的法律判定文本，核心围绕“泼水边界”（udakukkhepasīmā）与“村庄边界”（gāmasīmā）的关联性争议展开。摩尼约塔的文本基于《巴利律藏》及其注释，反驳部分长老仅依据次级注释的判定观点，明确桥梁、平台等构造与边界的连接规则；凯图达玛的文本则回应摩尼约塔的观点，进一步阐释边界判定的法律依据。通过对这两份文本的校勘与翻译，揭示19世纪缅甸佛教界对寺院边界规则的学术争议与实践应用，为理解巴利语法律文献的传承提供关键参考。  
关键词：19世纪缅甸；寺院边界（sīmā）；法律判定；泼水边界；村庄边界；巴利律藏  
Title：Two Legal Judgments on Monastic Boundaries from Nineteenth-Century Burma: Khaṅma kan charā tau Maṇijota’s Sīmāvinicchaya, with an appendix on Aoṅmre rhve bhuṃcharā tau Ketudhamma’s Sīmāvinicchaya  
Author：Petra Kieffer-Pülz, D. Christian Lammerts  
Abstract：This article studies two 19th-century Burmese legal texts on monastic boundaries (sīmā), focusing on the dispute over the connection between the "water-splashing boundary" (udakukkhepasīmā) and the "village boundary" (gāmasīmā). Maṇijota’s text, based on the Pāli Vinaya and its commentaries, refutes the judgments of some elders that solely rely on secondary commentaries, clarifying the connection rules between structures such as bridges, platforms and boundaries. Ketudhamma’s text responds to Maṇijota’s views and further explains the legal basis for boundary determination. Through the emendation and translation of these two texts, this article reveals the academic disputes and practical applications of monastic boundary rules in the 19th-century Burmese Buddhist community, providing key references for understanding the transmission of Pali legal literature.  
Keywords：Nineteenth-Century Burma；monastic boundary (sīmā)；legal judgment；water-splashing boundary (udakukkhepasīmā)；village boundary (gāmasīmā)；Pāli Vinaya

# 论文4. 泰国公主布拉莱的刺绣《戒坛文》手稿
作者：皮拉·帕纳鲁特（Peera Panarut）、西尔普苏帕·江萨旺（Silpsupa Jaengsawang）  
摘要：本文研究泰国北部公主布拉莱（1847-1932）创作的两件刺绣《戒坛文》（kammavācā）手稿。两件手稿分别以兰那达摩文和高棉文刺绣于丝绸之上，内容为僧人受戒仪式所需的巴利语公式及仪式说明，一件捐赠给帕府的帕佛陀明寺，另一件作为皇家礼物赠予曼谷国王朱拉隆功。文章探讨手稿的创作背景、艺术特色、文本内容及社会功能，指出其不仅是佛教仪式文本的载体，更是融合书法、刺绣艺术的文化瑰宝，反映19世纪泰国北部与中央的政治文化关联及佛教手稿的多元呈现形式。  
关键词：泰国；布拉莱公主；刺绣手稿；戒坛文（kammavācā）；受戒仪式；佛教艺术  
Title：Princess Bualai’s Embroidered Kammavācā Manuscripts from Thailand  
Author：Peera Panarut, Silpsupa Jaengsawang  
Abstract：This article studies two embroidered Kammavācā manuscripts created by Princess Bualai (1847-1932) from Northern Thailand. The two manuscripts are embroidered on silk in Lan Na Dhamma script and Khom script respectively, containing Pali formulas and ritual instructions required for the Buddhist monk ordination ceremony. One was donated to Wat Phra Bat Ming Mueang monastery in Phrae, and the other was sent as a royal gift to King Chulalongkorn of Bangkok. The article explores the manuscripts’ creation background, artistic characteristics, textual content and social functions, pointing out that they are not only carriers of Buddhist ritual texts but also cultural treasures integrating calligraphy and embroidery art, reflecting the political and cultural connections between Northern and Central Thailand in the 19th century and the diverse forms of Buddhist manuscripts.  
Keywords：Thailand；Princess Bualai；embroidered manuscripts；Kammavācā；ordination ceremony；Buddhist art

# 论文5. 巴利语佛教中的两套记数系统
作者：哈维尔·施纳克（Javier Schnake）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语佛教文献中的两套记数系统，梳理其在巴利语文本中的分布、应用及历史演变。通过分析经典文献、注释文献及专门词典中的记数表述，揭示两套系统的计数规则、语义关联及文化背景。研究表明，这两套记数系统不仅服务于文本中的数量记录，更与佛教教义中的宇宙观、修行阶位等概念深度绑定，其传承与演变反映了巴利语佛教文化的跨地域交流与发展。  
关键词：巴利语；佛教；记数系统；文本研究；语义关联  
Title：Two Systems of Numerical Notation in Pali Buddhism  
Author：Javier Schnake  
Abstract：This article focuses on two systems of numerical notation in Pali Buddhist literature, sorting out their distribution, application and historical evolution in Pali texts. By analyzing numerical expressions in canonical literature, commentarial literature and specialized dictionaries, it reveals the counting rules, semantic connections and cultural backgrounds of the two systems. The study shows that these two numerical notation systems not only serve the recording of quantities in texts but are also deeply bound to concepts such as cosmology and practice stages in Buddhist doctrines. Their inheritance and evolution reflect the cross-regional exchange and development of Pali Buddhist culture.  
Keywords：Pali；Buddhism；numerical notation systems；textual study；semantic connection
