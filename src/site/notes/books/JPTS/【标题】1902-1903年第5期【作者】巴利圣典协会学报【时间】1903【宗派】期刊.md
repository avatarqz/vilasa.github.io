# 【标题】1902-1903年合刊【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1903【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  佛教文献目录（基于费城图书馆馆藏）
作者：阿尔伯特·J·埃德蒙兹（Albert J. Edmunds）  
摘要：本文基于费城公共图书馆馆藏、作者私人收藏及借阅文献，编撰了系统性的佛教文献目录。目录分为上座部（Hinayana）和大乘佛教（Mahayana）两大板块，上座部部分涵盖巴利三藏及欧洲译本、俗语与梵文典籍、汉译上座部及其他亚洲语种译本、巴利铭文与编年史等8个子类；大乘佛教部分包含尼泊尔九部核心典籍、各类大乘与密教文本、手稿与批评研究等内容。目录仅收录具有永久学术价值的文献，排除了早期研究不成熟的著作，详细标注了文献版本、出版信息及馆藏来源，为佛教研究提供了全面的文献检索参考。  
关键词：佛教文献目录；费城图书馆；上座部；大乘佛教；文献检索；巴利语经典  
Title：A Buddhist Bibliography; Based upon the Libraries of Philadelphia  
Author：Albert J. Edmunds  
Abstract：This paper compiles a systematic Buddhist bibliography based on collections from public libraries in Philadelphia, the author's private collection, and borrowed documents. The bibliography is divided into two major sections: Hinayana and Mahayana. The Hinayana section includes 8 subcategories such as the Pali Tipitaka and its European translations, Prakrit and Sanskrit texts, Chinese Hinayana and other Asian versions, Pali inscriptions and chronicles; the Mahayana section covers nine core Nepalese texts, various Mahayana and Tantric texts, manuscripts and critical studies. Only works of permanent academic value are included, excluding early immature studies, with detailed annotations on editions, publication information and collection sources, providing a comprehensive literature retrieval reference for Buddhist research.  
Keywords：Buddhist Bibliography；Libraries of Philadelphia；Hinayana；Mahayana；Literature Retrieval；Pali Canon

# 论文2.  柬埔寨手稿中的《大史》增广文本考
作者：埃德蒙·哈迪（Edmund Hardy）  
摘要：本文聚焦一份未被学界关注的柬埔寨手稿中的《大史》增广文本，该文本由名为摩伽拉那（Moggallana）的僧人编撰，与传统《大史》标题、章节数一致，但诗节数量约为传统版本的两倍（近5800节）。作者通过文本比对指出，增广文本大量借鉴《大史注疏》（Vamsatthapakasini），并吸纳《佛种姓史》《塔史》等文献内容，部分语句因表达笨拙近似巴利注疏的迂回风格。文章以具体诗节为例，论证该文本保留了部分优于现有版本的异文，可修正传统版本的讹误，同时其文本扩展特征为印度史诗的形成与发展研究提供了重要参照。  
关键词：《大史》；柬埔寨手稿；增广文本；摩伽拉那；文本比对；异文研究  
Title：Notes on the Enlarged Text of the Mahavamsa Extant in a Kambodjan M.S.  
Author：Edmund Hardy  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the enlarged text of the Mahavamsa in an overlooked Kambodjan manuscript, compiled by a monk named Moggallana. Consistent with the traditional Mahavamsa in title and number of chapters, the enlarged text contains approximately twice as many stanzas (nearly 5,800) as the traditional version. Through textual comparison, the author points out that the enlarged text extensively draws on the Mahavamsa Commentary (Vamsatthapakasini) and incorporates content from texts such as the Buddhavamsa and Thupavamsa, with some sentences resembling the convoluted style of Pali commentaries due to clumsy expression. Using specific stanzas as examples, the paper argues that the text preserves some variants superior to existing versions, which can correct errors in traditional editions, and its textual expansion characteristics provide important reference for the study of the formation and development of Indian epics.  
Keywords：Mahavamsa；Kambodjan Manuscript；Enlarged Text；Moggallana；Textual Comparison；Variant Research

# 论文3.  摩伽拉那的《 Saddalakkhana》与月官的《语法》
作者：R·奥托·弗兰克（R. Otto Franke）  
摘要：本文通过比对斯里兰卡高僧摩伽拉那（12世纪）的巴利语语法著作《Saddalakkhana》与月官（Candragomin）的梵语语法《Candra-Vyakarana》，证实月官语法是摩伽拉那语法的主要来源。研究发现，两部著作中有121条规则几乎完全一致，仅存在非实质性的用词或语序差异，且规则序列排列高度吻合。摩伽拉那虽借鉴月官语法，但结合巴利语特点进行了适应性调整，其语法中的术语及注释部分也与月官语法存在密切关联。该研究为巴利语语法的源流考证及梵语与巴利语语法的传承关系提供了关键证据。  
关键词：摩伽拉那；《Saddalakkhana》；月官；《Candra-Vyakarana》；巴利语语法；梵语语法  
Title：Moggallana's Saddalakkhana und das Candra-Vyakarana  
Author：R. Otto Franke  
Abstract：By comparing the Pali grammar work 《Saddalakkhana》 by the Sri Lankan monk Moggallana (12th century) with the Sanskrit grammar 《Candra-Vyakarana》 by Candragomin, this paper confirms that Candragomin's grammar is the main source of Moggallana's grammar. The study finds that 121 rules in the two works are almost identical, with only non-substantive differences in wording or word order, and the sequence of rules is highly consistent. Although Moggallana drew on Candragomin's grammar, he made adaptive adjustments according to the characteristics of Pali, and the terms and annotations in his grammar are also closely related to Candragomin's grammar. This research provides key evidence for the origin and development of Pali grammar and the inheritance relationship between Sanskrit and Pali grammar.  
Keywords：Moggallana；Saddalakkhana；Candragomin；Candra-Vyakarana；Pali Grammar；Sanskrit Grammar

# 论文4.  沃伦《佛教译文集》索引
作者：C·B·朗克尔（C. B. Runkle）  
摘要：本文为亨利·克拉克·沃伦的《佛教译文集》（Buddhism in Translations）编制了详细索引，便于研究者快速检索巴利三藏、《清净道论》等文献中被译介的段落。索引按律藏、经藏（长部、中部、相应部、增支部、小部）、《弥兰陀王问经》《清净道论》《阿毗达摩摄义论》等分类编排，每条索引均标注了原文出处（含经名、章节、诗节）与沃伦译文集的对应页码，覆盖了佛教核心教义中的缘起、涅槃、四念处等关键主题，解决了原书无索引导致的检索不便问题。  
关键词：《佛教译文集》；沃伦；索引；巴利三藏；文献检索；佛教教义  
Title：Index to Warren's 'Buddhism in Translations'  
Author：C. B. Runkle  
Abstract：This paper compiles a detailed index for Henry Clarke Warren's "Buddhism in Translations", facilitating researchers to quickly retrieve translated passages from the Pali Tipitaka, Visuddhi-magga and other documents. The index is arranged by categories such as Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka (Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya, Anguttara Nikaya, Khuddaka Nikaya), Milindapanha, Visuddhi-magga, Abhidhammattha-sangaha, etc. Each index entry indicates the original source (including text name, chapter, stanza) and the corresponding page number in Warren's translation collection, covering key themes in core Buddhist doctrines such as dependent origination, nirvana, and four foundations of mindfulness, solving the inconvenience of retrieval caused by the lack of an index in the original book.  
Keywords：Buddhism in Translations；Henry Clarke Warren；Index；Pali Tipitaka；Literature Retrieval；Buddhist Doctrines

# 论文5.  月官《词根表》与巴利语词根表的关系
作者：R·奥托·弗兰克（R. Otto Franke）  
摘要：本文探讨月官（Candragomin）梵语语法中的《词根表》（Dhatupatha）与现存三部巴利语词根表（《Dhatumaijisa》《Dhatupatha》《Dhatvatthadipani》）的关联。研究发现，月官词根表与巴利语词根表在部分词根释义上存在特殊对应，但整体重合比例有限，且不存在直接的借用关系。巴利语词根表的编撰者虽可能知晓月官的著作，但更主要借鉴了其他梵语词根表及巴利语文本，部分词根释义为巴利语独有的适应性调整。文章通过大量词根比对，厘清了两者的传承脉络，为巴利语词汇学与语法学的源流研究提供了重要参考。  
关键词：月官；《词根表》；巴利语词根表；词根比对；语言传承；词汇学  
Title：Das Verhältnis von Candra's Dhatupatha zu den Pali Dhatupathas  
Author：R. Otto Franke  
Abstract：This paper explores the relationship between the 《Dhatupatha》 (Root List) in Candragomin's Sanskrit grammar and the three existing Pali root lists (《Dhatumaijisa》, 《Dhatupatha》, 《Dhatvatthadipani》). The study finds that there are special correspondences in the explanations of some roots between Candragomin's root list and Pali root lists, but the overall overlap ratio is limited, and there is no direct borrowing relationship. Although the compilers of Pali root lists may have known Candragomin's works, they mainly drew on other Sanskrit root lists and Pali texts, with some root explanations being unique adaptive adjustments for Pali. Through extensive root comparison, the paper clarifies the inheritance context of the two, providing important reference for the research on the origin and development of Pali lexicology and grammar.  
Keywords：Candragomin；Dhatupatha；Pali Dhatupathas；Root Comparison；Language Inheritance；Lexicology

# 论文6.  《 Rupasiddhi》《 Saddalakkhana》《 Mahavutti》与《 Vutti》的关联研究
作者：R·奥托·弗兰克（R. Otto Franke）  
摘要：本文考察了布达哈皮亚（Buddhappiya）的《Rupasiddhi》与摩伽拉那的《Saddalakkhana》、注释书《Mahavutti》及《Vutti》的文献关联。研究发现，《Rupasiddhi》在语法规则、术语解释及示例引用上与《Saddalakkhana》存在诸多重合，证实布达哈皮亚可能部分借鉴了摩伽拉那的语法著作。同时，《Rupasiddhi》大量引用《Mahavutti》（大概率为一部失传的《迦旃延语法》注疏）与《Vutti》，其中《Vutti》的引文可与现存《迦旃延语法》注疏逐字对应，而《Mahavutti》的引文未见于现有文献。文章厘清了这些文献间的相互引用关系，为巴利语语法文献的传承谱系研究提供了关键线索。  
关键词：《Rupasiddhi》；《Saddalakkhana》；《Mahavutti》；《Vutti》；巴利语语法；文献关联  
Title：Rupasiddhi, Moggallana, Mahavutti und Vutti  
Author：R. Otto Franke  
Abstract：This paper investigates the literary connections between Buddhappiya's 《Rupasiddhi》, Moggallana's 《Saddalakkhana》, and the commentaries 《Mahavutti》 and 《Vutti》. The study finds that 《Rupasiddhi》 has many overlaps with 《Saddalakkhana》 in grammatical rules, term explanations and example citations, confirming that Buddhappiya may have partially drawn on Moggallana's grammatical work. At the same time, 《Rupasiddhi》 extensively quotes 《Mahavutti》 (probably a lost commentary on Kaccayana's grammar) and 《Vutti》. The citations from 《Vutti》 can be verbatim matched with the existing commentary on Kaccayana's grammar, while the citations from 《Mahavutti》 are not found in existing documents. The paper clarifies the mutual citation relationships among these documents, providing key clues for the research on the inheritance genealogy of Pali grammatical literature.  
Keywords：Rupasiddhi；Saddalakkhana；Mahavutti；Vutti；Pali Grammar；Literary Connection

# 论文7.  巴利圣典协会出版物列表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文按年份系统罗列巴利圣典协会1882-1903年的出版物，包括期刊、经藏校勘本（如《相应部》《中部》《长部》）、论藏文献（如《法集论》《分别论》）、注释著作等，共计43部文本、55卷，超16000页。同时预告1905-1907年计划出版的文献，包括《尼帝瑟》《大史》《长部》第三卷等。列表标注了每部出版物的名称、卷次及出版状态，明确订阅费用与过刊购买政策，全面呈现协会在巴利语佛教文献整理与传播中的核心成果。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；出版物列表；巴利语经典；文献整理；校勘本；出版计划  
Title：List of Publications of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically lists the publications of the Pali Text Society from 1882 to 1903 by year, including journals, collated editions of the Sutta Pitaka (such as 《Samyutta Nikaya》, 《Majjhima Nikaya》, 《Digha Nikaya》), Abhidharma texts (such as 《Dhamma Saṅgaṇi》, 《Vibhaṅga》), commentaries, etc., totaling 43 texts and 55 volumes, exceeding 16,000 pages. It also previews the literature planned for publication from 1905 to 1907, including 《Niddesa》, 《Mahavamsa》, the third volume of 《Digha Nikaya》, etc. The list indicates the name, volume and publication status of each publication, clarifies the subscription fee and back issue purchase policy, and comprehensively presents the core achievements of the Society in the collation and dissemination of Pali Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Publication List；Pali Canon；Literary Collation；Collated Editions；Publishing Plan
