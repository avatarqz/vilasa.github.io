# 论文1.  巴利圣典协会1906年度报告
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文概述巴利圣典协会成立二十五年来的核心成果与发展状况。协会已出版60卷文献（含期刊），共计超17000页，定价25几尼，远低于同类出版物价格。资金方面，依靠捐赠与旧刊销售收入实现稳定运营，剩余工作有望顺利完成。重点推进巴利语词典编撰项目，所需资金基本到位，将在知名学者协作下近期启动。作者因重病简要说明词典进展，强调该项目对佛教研究的重要性。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1906年度报告；巴利语词典；文献出版；资金状况  
Title：Report of the Society for the Year 1906  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper outlines the core achievements and development of the Pali Text Society over its twenty-five years of establishment. The Society has published 60 volumes of literature (including journals), totaling over 17,000 pages, priced at 25 guineas, much lower than similar publications. Financially, it has achieved stable operation relying on donations and sales revenue from back issues, and the remaining work is expected to be successfully completed. The focus is on advancing the Pali Dictionary compilation project, with the required funds basically in place, which will be launched soon in collaboration with renowned scholars. The author briefly explains the progress of the dictionary due to severe illness, emphasizing the importance of the project for Buddhist research.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1906 Annual Report；Pali Dictionary；Literary Publication；Financial Status

# 论文2.  一部新的羯磨文
作者：G. L. M. 克劳森（G. L. M. Clauson）  
摘要：本文介绍伊顿公学图书馆发现的一部新巴利语羯磨文（Kammavaca）手稿。该手稿与奥登堡教授校勘的《律藏》中记载的羯磨仪式公式同源，保留公元前5世纪吠舍离结集前的核心形态，采用特殊骨质叶片书写，部分文字因叶片破损受损。手稿内容整合《律藏》中多个传统公式，顺序与古律典记载相反，包含独特新增表述，与贝恩斯此前公布的缅甸羯磨文高度相似但存在细节差异。该文献为研究佛教戒律仪式的演变、地域传播及文本流变提供了重要实物证据。  
关键词：羯磨文；巴利语手稿；《律藏》；佛教戒律；文本校勘；仪式演变  
Title：A New Kammavaca  
Author：G. L. M. Clauson  
Abstract：This paper introduces a newly discovered Pali Kammavaca manuscript in the library of Eton College. The manuscript is homologous to the Kammavaca ritual formulas recorded in the Vinaya edited by Professor Oldenberg, preserving the core form before the Council of Vesali in the 5th century BCE. It is written on special bone-like leaves, with some characters damaged due to leaf breakage. The content integrates multiple traditional formulas from the Vinaya, with an order opposite to that recorded in ancient Vinaya texts, including unique additional expressions, and is highly similar to the Burmese Kammavaca published by Baynes previously but with detailed differences. This document provides important physical evidence for the study of the evolution, regional dissemination, and textual changes of Buddhist precept rituals.  
Keywords：Kammavaca；Pali Manuscript；Vinaya Pitaka；Buddhist Precepts；Textual Collation；Ritual Evolution

# 论文3.  佛教禅宗
作者：铃木大拙（Daisetz T. Suzuki）  
摘要：本文系统梳理佛教禅宗的历史渊源、核心教义与实践方法。禅宗起源于印度佛陀“拈花微笑”的典故，经二十八代祖师传承至菩提达摩，其于公元520年传入中国，历经慧可、僧璨等祖师传承，至六祖慧能形成南宗禅，成为禅宗正统。禅宗强调“不立文字，以心传心”，反对执着经教典籍，主张通过坐禅（禅那）实现内心觉悟，核心教义包括“自性是佛”“平常心是道”等。文中详述禅宗在中日两国的发展脉络，分析公案、参话头等修行方式的特点，探讨禅宗对中日文化、艺术及武士道精神的深远影响。  
关键词：禅宗；菩提达摩；慧能；坐禅；公案；中日佛教；教义传承  
Title：The Zen Sect of Buddhism  
Author：Daisetz T. Suzuki  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the historical origin, core doctrines, and practical methods of the Zen Sect of Buddhism. Zen originated from the Indian Buddha's allusion of "holding a flower and smiling," passed down through twenty-eight patriarchs to Bodhidharma, who introduced it to China in 520 CE. After inheritance by patriarchs such as Hui K'o and Seng Ts'an, it developed into the Southern School of Zen with Hui Neng, the Sixth Patriarch, becoming the orthodox of Zen. Zen emphasizes "transmitting outside the canonical teaching, not establishing words and letters, and transmitting from mind to mind," opposing attachment to Buddhist scriptures, advocating inner enlightenment through zazen (Dhyana), and its core doctrines include "one's own nature is Buddha" and "the ordinary mind is the path." The paper details the development context of Zen in China and Japan, analyzes the characteristics of practice methods such as ko-an and Hua-t'ou, and discusses the profound influence of Zen on Chinese and Japanese culture, art, and Bushido spirit.  
Keywords：Zen Sect；Bodhidharma；Hui Neng；Zazen；Ko-an；Sino-Japanese Buddhism；Doctrinal Inheritance

# 论文4.  汉译《本事经》集
作者：渡边海旭（K. Watanabe）  
摘要：本文研究玄奘于公元650年翻译的汉译《本事经》（Itivuttakas），该译本收录于《大正藏》，共七卷，分为“一法品”“二法品”“三法品”三部分，前两部分含多个子单元，每单元结尾附有总结偈颂，第三部分存在残缺且无结尾偈颂。通过文本比对发现，汉译本与巴利语《本事经》核心内容高度一致，但汉译本在结构编排、经文数量上有差异，收录65部经文（含部分巴利本未载篇目），缺失巴利本第四部分。汉译本对部分教义表述进行扩展，新增十项佛教教义忆念修行内容，反映说一切有部的思想特征，为研究佛教经典的跨语言传播与文本演变提供重要依据。  
关键词：汉译《本事经》；玄奘；巴利语经典；文本比对；说一切有部；经典传播  
Title：A Chinese Collection of Itivuttakas  
Author：K. Watanabe  
Abstract：This paper studies the Chinese translation of the Itivuttakas translated by Yuan Chwang in 650 CE, which is included in the Taisho Canon, totaling seven fascicles, divided into three parts: "Eka-dharma-khanda," "Dvi-dharma-khanda," and "Tri-dharma-khanda." The first two parts contain multiple sub-units, each ending with a summary gatha, while the third part is incomplete and lacks a concluding gatha. Textual comparison shows that the Chinese translation is highly consistent with the core content of the Pali Itivuttakas, but there are differences in structure and the number of sutras. The Chinese version includes 65 sutras (including some not recorded in the Pali version) and lacks the fourth part of the Pali version. The Chinese translation expands on some doctrinal expressions and adds ten items of Buddhist doctrine recollection practice, reflecting the ideological characteristics of the Sarvastivadin school, providing important basis for the study of the cross-linguistic dissemination and textual evolution of Buddhist scriptures.  
Keywords：Chinese Translation of Itivuttakas；Yuan Chwang；Pali Canon；Textual Comparison；Sarvastivadin School；Canonical Dissemination

# 论文5.  汉译《经集》相关考辨
作者：姉崎正治（M. Anesaki）  
摘要：本文通过阿含经文献研究，证实虽无完整汉译《经集》（Sutta-Nipata），但在汉文佛典中发现超过半数的《经集》单篇文本。核心发现是《经集》中的《义足论》（Atthaka-vagga）完整存于汉文文献中（南条文雄目录编号674），译为《义足》，成书于公元3世纪初，包含16篇文本，偈颂部分与巴利本高度一致，仅少数篇目顺序有差异。此外，《经集》中的《彼岸道品》（Parayana-vagga）在多部汉文文献中被引用至少十三次。这些发现为《经集》的文献源流、文本传播及跨语言演变研究提供了关键线索。  
关键词：《经集》；汉译佛典；《义足论》；文本考辨；文献传播；阿含经研究  
Title：Sutta-Nipata in Chinese  
Author：M. Anesaki  
Abstract：Through the study of Agama texts, this paper confirms that although there is no complete Chinese translation of the Sutta-Nipata, more than half of the individual texts of the Sutta-Nipata have been found in Chinese Buddhist scriptures. The core discovery is that the Atthaka-vagga in the Sutta-Nipata is completely preserved in Chinese literature (No. 674 in Nanjio's Catalogue), translated as "I-tsu," compiled in the early 3rd century CE, containing 16 texts. The gatha parts are highly consistent with the Pali version, with only slight differences in the order of a few texts. In addition, the Parayana-vagga in the Sutta-Nipata has been quoted at least thirteen times in various Chinese texts. These discoveries provide key clues for the study of the literary origin, textual dissemination, and cross-linguistic evolution of the Sutta-Nipata.  
Keywords：Sutta-Nipata；Chinese Buddhist Translations；Atthaka-vagga；Textual Research；Literary Dissemination；Agama Studies

# 论文6.  尼柯耶中的比喻：分类索引
作者：里斯·戴维斯夫人（Mrs. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文编制了巴利语《尼柯耶》（经藏）中比喻修辞的分类索引，涵盖简单比喻与复合比喻，基于已出版的巴利圣典协会校勘本编撰，暂未纳入未校勘文献及部分后期文献。索引收录自然现象、动植物、日常器物等多类比喻，系统统计各类比喻的使用频次，其中水相关比喻（114次）最多，火（58次）、土（41次）、空气（32次）次之；动物中大象、牛的比喻使用最频繁。通过分析比喻的文化背景与修辞功能，揭示古印度社会的自然环境、生产生活及佛教教义的表达逻辑，为比较文学研究与佛教文本解读提供基础工具。  
关键词：《尼柯耶》；比喻；分类索引；巴利语文学；修辞研究；佛教教义表达  
Title：Similes in the Nikayas, A Classified Index  
Author：Mrs. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper compiles a classified index of similes in the Pali Nikayas (Sutta Pitaka), covering simple and compound similes, compiled based on the published collated editions of the Pali Text Society, excluding uncollated texts and some later works. The index includes various types of similes such as natural phenomena, animals and plants, and daily utensils, systematically counting the frequency of each type of simile. Among them, water-related similes are the most frequent (114 times), followed by fire (58 times), earth (41 times), and air (32 times); among animals, similes of elephants and cows are the most frequently used. By analyzing the cultural background and rhetorical functions of the similes, it reveals the natural environment, production and life of ancient Indian society and the expression logic of Buddhist doctrines, providing a basic tool for comparative literature research and Buddhist text interpretation.  
Keywords：Nikayas；Similes；Classified Index；Pali Literature；Rhetorical Research；Buddhist Doctrinal Expression

# 论文7.  词典学札记：以H开头的词汇
作者：斯滕·科诺（Sten Konow）  
摘要：本文是为巴利语词典编撰准备的以H开头的词汇研究札记，收录ha、hari、hamsa、hattha、han等多个词根衍生的词汇，详细考证每个词汇的词性、词义演变、文献用例及语法形态。基于《律藏》《尼柯耶》《法句经》等核心巴利语经典，结合手稿异文与校勘成果，厘清词汇的语义层次与使用场景，补充此前词典未收录的义项与用例。该札记为巴利语词典的系统编撰提供了精准的词汇学基础，体现了对巴利语词汇的深度考据与梳理。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；H开头词汇；词义考证；语法形态；经典用例  
Title：Lexicographical Notes: Words Beginning with H  
Author：Sten Konow  
Abstract：This paper is a lexicographical note on words beginning with H prepared for the compilation of the Pali Dictionary. It includes words derived from multiple roots such as ha, hari, hamsa, hattha, and han, and detailedly researches the part of speech, semantic evolution, literary examples, and grammatical forms of each word. Based on core Pali classics such as the Vinaya Pitaka, Nikayas, and Dhammapada, combined with manuscript variants and collation results, it clarifies the semantic levels and usage scenarios of the words, supplementing meanings and examples not included in previous dictionaries. This note provides an accurate lexicographical foundation for the systematic compilation of the Pali Dictionary, reflecting in-depth research and sorting of Pali vocabulary.  
Keywords：Pali Language；Lexicography；Words Beginning with H；Semantic Research；Grammatical Form；Canonical Examples

# 论文8.  相关札记三则
作者：哈里纳特·德（Harinath De, M.A.）  
摘要：本文包含三则佛教文献相关札记。第一则通过觉音《清净道论》对“indriya”（根）的解释与波你尼语法著作的比对，证实波你尼早于觉音，反驳皮谢尔教授关于波你尼生活于公元6-7世纪的观点；第二则考证《本生经》中“lankaro”一词的含义，结合《清净道论》上下文，提出其意为“船帆”，修正考威尔教授“锚”的误释；第三则通过巴利语经典（《长老尼偈》《阿毗达磨义疏》等）中的比喻文本，解读寂天《入菩萨行论》注释中“海龟入轭”比喻的来源，追溯其在《中部尼柯耶》中的原始表述。  
关键词：波你尼；觉音；《清净道论》；词汇考证；比喻溯源；文献比对  
Title：Notes by Harinath De, M.A.  
Author：Harinath De, M.A.  
Abstract：This paper contains three notes related to Buddhist literature. The first compares the explanation of "indriya" (faculty) in Buddhaghosa's Visuddhimagga with Panini's grammatical works, confirming that Panini predates Buddhaghosa, refuting Professor Pischel's view that Panini lived in the 6th-7th centuries CE; the second researches the meaning of the word "lankaro" in the Jataka, proposing that it means "sail" based on the context of the Visuddhimagga, correcting Professor Cowell's misinterpretation of "anchor"; the third interprets the origin of the simile "tortoise entering the yoke" in the commentary on Santideva's Bodhicaryavatara through metaphorical texts in Pali classics (such as Therigatha and Atthasalini), tracing its original expression in the Majjhima Nikaya.  
Keywords：Panini；Buddhaghosa；Visuddhimagga；Vocabulary Research；Metaphor Tracing；Textual Comparison

# 论文9.  暹罗版《本事经》校勘
作者：贾斯汀·哈特利·穆尔（Justin Hartley Moore, A.M.）  
摘要：本文对暹罗国王版《本事经》（Iti-vuttaka）与温迪施教授校勘的巴利圣典协会版进行系统比对。暹罗版收录于39卷本《暹罗三藏》第25卷，采用暹罗文字书写，因文字壁垒未被西方学者广泛研究。校勘聚焦两部版本的文本异文，梳理112个经文单元中的用字差异、语句增减、偈颂顺序调整等情况，发现两者核心内容高度一致，但在虚词使用、段落划分、部分术语表述上存在差异。校勘结果为《本事经》的文本传承研究提供了重要的版本学依据，填补了暹罗版巴利三藏与西方校勘本的比对空白。  
关键词：《本事经》；暹罗版；文本校勘；温迪施校勘本；版本差异；巴利三藏  
Title：Collation of the Siamese Edition of the Iti-Vuttaka  
Author：Justin Hartley Moore, A.M.  
Abstract：This paper systematically compares the Siamese King's edition of the Iti-Vuttaka with the edition edited by Professor Windisch of the Pali Text Society. The Siamese edition is included in Volume 25 of the 39-volume Siamese Tipitaka, written in Siamese script, and has not been widely studied by Western scholars due to language barriers. The collation focuses on textual variants between the two editions, sorting out differences in word usage, additions and deletions of sentences, adjustments of gatha order, etc., in 112 sutra units, and finds that their core content is highly consistent, but there are differences in the use of function words, paragraph division, and some terminological expressions. The collation results provide important textual evidence for the study of the textual inheritance of the Iti-Vuttaka, filling the gap in the comparison between the Siamese version of the Pali Tipitaka and Western collated editions.  
Keywords：Iti-Vuttaka；Siamese Edition；Textual Collation；Windisch's Collated Edition；Version Differences；Pali Tipitaka

# 论文10.  《佛传》中的印刷错误更正
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文针对劳斯博士校勘的《佛传》（Jina-Carita）中出现的印刷错误进行更正。这些错误包括专有名词拼写错误（如Diparikara误作Dipaiikara、Suddhodana误作Suddhadana等）、词汇误用（如“mine of virtues”误作“mind of virtues”）、语法错误及语句遗漏等。作者指出部分错误此前已修正但仍未更正，遵循学术规范列出错误条目及对应正确表述，确保文献的准确性，为研究者提供可靠的文本依据。  
关键词：《佛传》；印刷错误；文本更正；专有名词；学术规范；文献校勘  
Title：Misprints in the Jina-Carita  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper corrects the misprints in the Jina-Carita edited by Dr. Rouse. These errors include misspellings of proper nouns (such as Diparikara misspelled as Dipaiikara, Suddhodana misspelled as Suddhadana), word misuse (such as "mine of virtues" miswritten as "mind of virtues"), grammatical errors, and sentence omissions. The author points out that some errors had been corrected previously but still not revised, and lists the error entries and corresponding correct expressions in accordance with academic standards to ensure the accuracy of the literature and provide a reliable textual basis for researchers.  
Keywords：Jina-Carita；Misprints；Textual Corrections；Proper Nouns；Academic Standards；Textual Collation

# 论文13.  1906年度财务报表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文公布巴利圣典协会1906年度财务收支明细。收入方面，年初银行余额439英镑13先令，加上年度订阅费128英镑5先令及捐赠收入，总收入696英镑12先令9便士；支出方面，包括印刷费、出版服务费、库存保险费、办公开支（打字、文具、邮资等），总支出146英镑9先令1便士；年末银行余额550英镑3先令8便士。报表清晰呈现协会的财务运营状况，确保资金使用透明，为捐赠者与订阅者提供财务监督依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1906年度；财务报表；收支明细；资金透明；财务监督  
Title：Statement of Account for 1906  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper publishes the detailed financial revenue and expenditure of the Pali Text Society for 1906. In terms of income, the bank balance at the beginning of the year was £439 13s, plus the annual subscription fee of £128 5s and donation income, the total income was £696 12s 9d; in terms of expenditure, it includes printing fees, publisher's charges, stock insurance fees, and office expenses (typing, stationery, postage, etc.), with a total expenditure of £146 9s 1d; the bank balance at the end of the year was £550 3s 8d. The statement clearly presents the financial operation status of the Society, ensuring transparent use of funds and providing a basis for financial supervision by donors and subscribers.
