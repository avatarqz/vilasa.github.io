# 【标题】第16期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1992【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 《Ruparipavibhaga》译文（形式与无形之物的分类）
作者：R.H.B. 埃克塞尔（R.H.B. Exell）  
摘要：本文翻译了10世纪佛教学者佛陀达多（Buddhadatta）的巴利语论著《Ruparipavibhaga》，该著作是南传阿毗达磨的简明摘要，属于“小指手册”（Let-than）系列。译文以“归敬佛陀”开篇，系统分类了“形式”与“无形之物”两类范畴：形式包括地、水、火、风四大元素及眼、耳等派生形式共28种，详细说明其内外、离合、粗细等属性及产生因缘；无形之物分为心识状态、心所法、涅槃三类，其中心识状态细分为善、恶、果报、无记四类共89种，心所法包含遍行、善、杂、恶四类共52种，涅槃则被定义为永恒、无死、安稳的终极境界。译文忠实呈现了南传佛教对身心现象的精细剖析，为阿毗达磨研究提供了可靠的英文译本参考。  
关键词：《Ruparipavibhaga》；巴利语译文；形式与无形；南传阿毗达磨；佛陀达多  
Title：Ruparipavibhaga Translation (The Classification of Forms and Formless Things)  
Author：R.H.B. Exell  
Abstract：This paper translates the Pali treatise Ruparipavibhaga by the 10th-century Buddhist scholar Buddhadatta, a concise summary of Theravada Abhidhamma belonging to the "Let-than" (Little-finger Manuals) series. Beginning with "Homage to the Buddha", the translation systematically classifies two categories: "forms" and "formless things". Forms include 28 types such as the four great elements (earth, water, fire, wind) and derived forms like eye and ear, detailing their attributes (internal/external, separable/inseparable, gross/subtle) and causal origins. Formless things are divided into three types: states of mind, mental properties, and Nibbana. States of mind are subdivided into 89 types under four categories (good, bad, resultant, inoperative); mental properties consist of 52 types under four categories (universal, good, miscellaneous, bad); Nibbana is defined as an eternal, deathless, and peaceful ultimate state. The translation faithfully presents the Theravada Buddhist's detailed analysis of physical and mental phenomena, providing a reliable English version reference for Abhidhamma research.  
Keywords：Ruparipavibhaga；Pali translation；forms and formless things；Theravada Abhidhamma；Buddhadatta

# 论文2. 柬埔寨、老挝和泰国巴利语手稿识别参考文献
作者：杰奎琳·菲利奥扎（Jacqueline Filliozat）  
摘要：本文基于作者十年间对欧洲主要馆藏千余部巴利语手稿的整理经验，汇编了柬埔寨、老挝和泰国巴利语手稿的核心识别参考资料。资料分为两部分：第一部分按字母顺序收录了1917-1989年间学者文献中提及的印度支那巴利语著作标题及题跋，涵盖经、律、论及注疏类文本；第二部分将金边佛教研究所版本中的文本目录按字母排序，包含文本开篇、结尾、偈颂及标志性段落等关键识别信息。该汇编填补了三国巴利语文献缺乏系统索引的空白，为图书馆、档案馆中未标名或无题跋手稿的分类与比对提供了实用工具。  
关键词：巴利语手稿；柬埔寨；老挝；泰国；文献识别  
Title：Documents useful for the identification of Pali manuscripts of Cambodia, Laos and Thailand  
Author：Jacqueline Filliozat  
Abstract：Based on the author's ten-year experience sorting over a thousand Pali manuscripts in major European collections, this paper compiles core reference materials for identifying Pali manuscripts from Cambodia, Laos, and Thailand. The materials are divided into two parts: the first part alphabetically collects titles and colophons of Indochinese Pali works mentioned in scholarly literature from 1917 to 1989, covering sutras, vinayas, abhidharmas, and commentaries; the second part sorts the table of contents from the publications of the Institut bouddhique de Phnom Penh alphabetically, including key identification information such as text beginnings, endings, stanzas, and significant passages. This compilation fills the gap of lacking systematic indexes for Pali literature from the three countries, providing a practical tool for classifying and comparing unnamed or colophon-less manuscripts in libraries and archives.  
Keywords：Pali manuscripts；Cambodia；Laos；Thailand；literature identification

# 论文3. 罪业的生起：apattisamutthana（南传律藏结构与历史研究）
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语律藏中的“罪业生起”（apattisamutthana）概念，弥补了《巴利语词典》（CPD）对该术语阐释的不足。研究梳理了罪业生起的分类演变：《小部》中最早将其分为身、口、意的六种组合形式，《附随律》（Parivara）扩展为13种分类并与波罗提木叉戒条对应，《Kankhavitarani》注释则详细说明其与语法规则的关联。通过比对《萨曼塔帕萨迪卡》（Samantapasadika）等注疏文献，揭示了罪业生起分类从理论建构到实践应用的发展轨迹，同时探讨了《弥兰陀王问经》中“世间责”与“教法责”的术语差异，为南传佛教教团法的历史演变研究提供了核心文本依据。  
关键词：罪业生起；apattisamutthana；南传律藏；巴利语佛教；教团法  
Title：The arising of an offence: apattisamutthana (A note on the structure and history of the Theravada-Vinaya)  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the concept of "apattisamutthana" (arising of an offence) in the Pali Vinaya, supplementing the insufficient explanation of this term in the Critical Pali Dictionary (CPD). The study sorts out the evolutionary classification of apattisamutthana: the Cullavagga first divides it into six combinations of body, speech, and mind; the Parivara expands it to 13 categories corresponding to the Patimokkha precepts; the Kankhavitarani commentary details its connection with grammatical rules. By comparing commentarial texts such as the Samantapasadika, it reveals the development trajectory of apattisamutthana classification from theoretical construction to practical application, and discusses the terminological differences between "lokavajja" (worldly blame) and "pannattivajja" (doctrinal blame) in the Milindapanha, providing core textual evidence for the historical evolution of Theravada Buddhist ecclesiastical law.  
Keywords：arising of an offence；apattisamutthana；Theravada Vinaya；Pali Buddhism；ecclesiastical law

# 论文4. 僧侣遇害案——南传律藏第三波罗夷戒相关故事研究
作者：劳伦斯·C.R. 米尔斯（Laurence C.R. Mills）  
摘要：本文探讨了南传律藏第三波罗夷戒（重罪戒）中的“僧侣遇害案”故事，该故事讲述佛陀教导不净观后，僧侣因厌弃身体引发自杀、互杀风潮，俗人弥伽兰迪卡（Migalandika）受雇杀害僧侣并因忏悔获得“救度”认可，最终导致六十余名僧侣死亡。研究对比了《萨曼塔帕萨迪卡》注释的阐释与法藏部律藏的平行记载，指出故事中存在的逻辑矛盾：佛陀作为全知者未预见不净观的负面影响、僧团未采取制止措施等。论文认为该故事可能是对历史事件的扭曲记录，其保留于律藏中反映了早期佛教对修行极端化风险的警示，同时为探讨南传律藏的叙事真实性提供了独特案例。  
关键词：波罗夷戒；僧侣遇害案；不净观；南传律藏；佛教叙事  
Title：The case of the murdered monks  
Author：Laurence C.R. Mills  
Abstract：This paper explores the story of "the murdered monks" related to the third Parajika (grave offence) precept in the Theravada Vinaya. The story recounts that after the Buddha taught asubhabhavana (meditation on the unattractiveness of the body), monks developed intense self-hatred, leading to a wave of suicide and mutual killing. A layman named Migalandika was hired to kill monks and gained recognition as "saving" them after repenting, eventually resulting in the death of over sixty monks. The study compares the interpretation of the Samantapasadika commentary with parallel records in the Dharmagupta Vinaya, pointing out logical contradictions in the story: the Buddha, as omniscient, did not foresee the negative impact of asubhabhavana, and the Sangha did not take preventive measures. The paper argues that the story may be a distorted record of historical events, and its preservation in the Vinaya reflects early Buddhism's warning against the risk of extreme practice, while providing a unique case for exploring the narrative authenticity of the Theravada Vinaya.  
Keywords：Parajika precept；murdered monks；asubhabhavana；Theravada Vinaya；Buddhist narrative

# 论文5. 巴利语词典学研究（九）——四个巴利语词汇的词源考
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录或释义、词源有误的四个巴利语词汇展开考证。其一“kinti”，词典仅释“何以”，实则可与愿望式搭配表“为使”，阿育王铭文及《弥兰陀王问经》中均有佐证；其二“kevala-kappa”，词典误释“完整劫”，结合注疏及梵语对应词“kalpa”，实则表“近乎全部”；其三“sakaya niruttiya”，并非“以自身方言”，而应指“佛陀教法的自身阐释”，可能为注释或方言转译；其四“hevam”，词典未明确语境差异，作为句首词时为独立形式，非“hi evam”缩写，是东部方言特征。研究纠正了词典疏漏，补充了巴利语词汇的用法细节。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源考证；kinti；kevala-kappa；sakaya niruttiya；hevam  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies IX: Four Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper conducts etymological research on four Pali words that are either unincluded or have incorrect meanings/etymologies in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED). First, "kinti": the dictionary only explains it as "how", but it can actually collocate with optative to mean "in order that", evidenced in Asokan inscriptions and the Milindapanha. Second, "kevala-kappa": the dictionary mistakenly interprets it as "a whole kappa", but combined with commentaries and the corresponding Sanskrit "kalpa", it actually means "(almost) entire". Third, "sakaya niruttiya": it does not mean "in one's own dialect", but rather "the inherent interpretation of the Buddha's teaching", possibly a commentary or dialect translation. Fourth, "hevam": the dictionary does not clarify contextual differences; when used as the first word of a clause, it is an independent form, not an abbreviation of "hi evam", and is a feature of Eastern dialects. The study corrects dictionary omissions and supplements details of Pali vocabulary usage.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；kinti；kevala-kappa；sakaya niruttiya；hevam

# 论文6. 巴利语律藏中僧侣的仪式义务与捐赠者角色
作者：格雷戈里·肖彭（Gregory Schopen）  
摘要：本文反驳了“早期佛教僧侣不参与世俗生命周期仪式”的观点，基于巴利语、梵语及混合俗语律藏文献，指出南传僧侣对优婆塞（upasaka）群体负有明确仪式义务。研究聚焦《雨安居犍度》（Vassupanayikakhandhaka），僧侣可因房屋落成、子女婚嫁、重病等世俗仪式中断雨安居，应请前往诵经祈福；同时，律藏明确记载僧侣、比丘尼、沙弥等可作为捐赠者修建僧寮、捐赠物资，并需其他僧侣参与其仪式。通过比对根本说一切有部、大众部律藏的平行记载及印度碑铭资料，证实早期佛教僧侣深度参与世俗仪式，且兼具捐赠者与仪式执行者双重角色，修正了对早期僧团社会功能的单一认知。  
关键词：南传律藏；僧侣；仪式义务；捐赠者角色；优婆塞  
Title：The ritual obligations and donor roles of monks in the Pali Vinaya  
Author：Gregory Schopen  
Abstract：This paper refutes the view that "early Buddhist monks had little involvement in lay life-cycle rituals". Based on Pali, Sanskrit, and Prakrit-cum-Sanskrit Vinaya texts, it points out that Theravada monks had clear ritual obligations to the upasaka (lay follower) community. Focusing on the Vassupanayikakhandhaka, the study notes that monks could interrupt the rain retreat to attend lay rituals such as house dedications, children's weddings, and serious illnesses upon invitation to recite sutras and bless. Meanwhile, the Vinaya explicitly records that monks, nuns, samaneras, etc., could act as donors to build monasteries and donate supplies, requiring other monks to participate in their rituals. By comparing parallel records in the Mulasarvastivada and Mahasanghika Vinayas and Indian epigraphic data, the paper confirms that early Buddhist monks were deeply involved in lay rituals and assumed dual roles as donors and ritual performers, revising the one-sided understanding of the social functions of early Sangha.  
Keywords：Theravada Vinaya；monks；ritual obligations；donor roles；upasaka

# 论文7. 声闻乘的护持文献（Raksa Literature）
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文系统研究声闻乘护持文献（Raksa Literature），将其定义为通过诵经、持咒实现疾病、灾厄防护的文本类型，核心分为四类：上座部的护经（paritta）、根本说一切有部的大部经（Mahasutras）、诸部派的吉祥偈（svastigatha）及五护持（Pancaraksa）文献。研究梳理了各类文献的传承脉络：护经在斯里兰卡、缅甸、泰国形成不同传本，包含《吉祥经》《宝经》等核心文本；大部经如《阿咤那抵经》（Atanatiya-sutra）通过藏译保存；吉祥偈以祝福祈愿为核心功能；五护持文献则融合咒语与护持仪轨。论文探讨了护持文献与 mantra、密法的关联，结合考古遗存指出其在早期佛教中的普及性，揭示了声闻乘护持实践的多元形态及跨派传播特征。  
关键词：声闻乘；护持文献；护经（paritta）；五护持（Pancaraksa）；佛教防护传统  
Title：The Raksa Literature of the Sravakayana  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper systematically studies the Raksa Literature of the Sravakayana, defining it as textual types that achieve protection against diseases and calamities through sutra recitation and mantra chanting. It is mainly divided into four categories: paritta of the Theravadins, Mahasutras of the Mulasarvastivadins, svastigatha of various schools, and Pancaraksa collections. The study sorts out the transmission context of various literatures: paritta formed different recensions in Sri Lanka, Burma, and Thailand, including core texts such as the Mangala-sutta and Ratana-sutta; Mahasutras such as the Atanatiya-sutra are preserved through Tibetan translations; svastigatha focuses on blessing and prayer functions; Pancaraksa literature integrates mantras and protection rituals. The paper explores the connection between Raksa literature and mantra, Tantra, points out its popularity in early Buddhism based on archaeological remains, and reveals the diverse forms and cross-school transmission characteristics of Sravakayana protection practices.  
Keywords：Sravakayana；Raksa Literature；paritta；Pancaraksa；Buddhist protection tradition
