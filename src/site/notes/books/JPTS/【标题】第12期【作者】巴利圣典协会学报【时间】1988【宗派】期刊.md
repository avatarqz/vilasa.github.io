# 【标题】第12期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1988【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1. 北寺注疏与《集学》：巴利语注疏中非大寺派的未署名文献来源
作者：森宗道（Sodo Mori）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语注疏中频繁出现的“某些人”“其他人”等未署名引文，考证其源自非大寺派（尤其是阿毗耶祇梨派、北寺派）的文献来源。通过梳理《清净道论》《长部注疏》等核心注疏及疏释（Tika）中的41处案例，识别出“阿毗耶祇梨派”“北寺派”“《集学》导师”等非大寺派标识，证实这些引文源自《集学》（Sarasamasa）与《北寺注疏》（Uttaraviharatthakatha）等失传文献。研究发现，非大寺派观点或被大寺派驳斥，或作为补充解释并行收录，反映两派在教义阐释上的差异与互补。本文通过文本比对与文献溯源，厘清了巴利语注疏的多元文献构成，揭示了南传佛教内部不同僧团的思想传承脉络。  
关键词：巴利语注疏；非大寺派；北寺注疏；《集学》；阿毗耶祇梨派；文献来源  
Title：Uttaraviharatthakatha and Sarasamasa: Some Unattributed Non-Mahaviharavasin Sources for the Pali Commentaries  
Author：Sodo Mori  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the unattributed quotations such as "some" and "others" frequently appearing in Pali commentaries, verifying their origins from non-Mahavihara fraternities, especially the Abhayagirivihara and Uttaravihara. By sorting out 41 cases from core commentaries such as the Visuddhimagga and Dighatthakatha as well as their Tikas, it identifies non-Mahavihara markers including "Abhayagirivasino", "Uttaraviharavasino" and "Sarasamasa-acariya", confirming that these quotations are derived from lost texts such as the Sarasamasa and Uttaraviharatthakatha. The study finds that non-Mahavihara views were either criticized by the Mahavihara or recorded in parallel as supplementary explanations, reflecting the differences and complementarities in doctrinal interpretation between the two fraternities. Through textual comparison and literary tracing, this paper clarifies the diverse literary composition of Pali commentaries and reveals the ideological transmission context of different Sanghas within Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Pali commentaries；non-Mahavihara fraternity；Uttaraviharatthakatha；Sarasamasa；Abhayagirivihara；literary sources

# 论文2. 巴利语词典学研究V：十二个巴利语词汇的词源考证
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录、释义有误或词源存疑的十二个巴利语词汇展开专项研究。包括“apilapati”（背诵）应源自“abhi-lapati”，而非PED推测的“a+pilavati”；“apatihirakata”（非指涉性的）与“sappatihirakata”（指涉性的）源自词根“prati+har-”，核心义为“与参照相关”；“patihara”（引荐）在语境中并非“计时敲击”，而是对访客的引荐方式；“paruta”（着衣的）是“parupati”的过去分词，而非“vr>ru”的语音演变结果等。研究通过文本语境分析、梵语-巴利语词形比对及语法规则验证，纠正了词典疏漏，补充了词汇的语义演变与实际用法，为巴利语词汇研究与词典修订提供了精准参考。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源考证；词汇释义；PED；文本语境  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies V: Twelve Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper conducts a special study on twelve Pali words that are unincluded, have incorrect meanings, or questionable etymologies in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED). These include "apilapati" (to recite), which should be derived from "abhi-lapati" rather than "a+pilavati" as speculated by PED；"apatihirakata" (non-referential) and "sappatihirakata" (referential), derived from the root "prati+har-" with the core meaning "related to reference"；"patihara" (introduction), which in context refers to the way of introducing visitors rather than "striking for timekeeping"；"paruta" (clothed), the past participle of "parupati" rather than the result of the phonetic evolution "vr>ru", etc. Through textual context analysis, Sanskrit-Pali word form comparison, and grammatical rule verification, the study corrects dictionary omissions, supplements the semantic evolution and practical usage of words, and provides accurate references for Pali vocabulary research and dictionary revision.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；word definition；PED；textual context

# 论文3. 《正法进路》英译与研究
作者：安·阿普尔比·黑兹尔伍德（Ann Appleby Hazlewood）  
摘要：本文首次完整英译了10世纪斯里兰卡巴利语劝诫诗《正法进路》（Saddhammopayana），该诗共629节，是现存唯一巴利语佛教书信体文献，作者为阿毗耶祇梨派的阿难大长老（Abhayagiri Kavi-cakravarti Ananda Maha-Thera）。诗歌以书信形式劝诫学友佛陀索玛（Buddhasoma），内容涵盖八难、十恶业及其果报、饿鬼与畜生道苦、十二种善业（布施、持戒、禅修等）及其福报，核心弘扬佛教伦理与解脱之道。译文基于1887年理查德·莫里斯的校勘本，参考僧伽罗语注疏与相关研究，还原了文本的教义内涵与文学特色。该诗对后世斯里兰卡佛教文献影响深远，其英译为跨文化佛教研究提供了重要文本依据。  
关键词：《正法进路》；巴利语诗歌；英译；南传佛教伦理；劝诫文献；书信体  
Title：Saddhammopayana Translation: The Gift-offering of the True Dhamma  
Author：Ann Appleby Hazlewood  
Abstract：This paper presents the first complete English translation of the 10th-century Sri Lankan Pali didactic poem Saddhammopayana ("The Gift-offering of the True Dhamma"), which consists of 629 verses and is the only surviving Buddhist epistle in Pali. Authored by Abhayagiri Kavi-cakravarti Ananda Maha-Thera of the Abhayagiri fraternity, the poem is written as a letter advising his fellow-student Buddhasoma, covering topics such as the eight inopportune births, the ten evil deeds and their karmic consequences, the sufferings of the peta and animal realms, the twelve meritorious actions (giving, good conduct, mental development, etc.) and their rewards, with the core of promoting Buddhist ethics and the path to liberation. Based on Richard Morris' 1887 edited version, the translation refers to Sinhala commentaries and related studies to restore the doctrinal connotation and literary characteristics of the text. The poem has a profound influence on later Sri Lankan Buddhist literature, and its English translation provides an important textual basis for cross-cultural Buddhist research.  
Keywords：Saddhammopayana；Pali poem；English translation；Theravada Buddhist ethics；didactic literature；epistle

# 论文4. 《清净道论》第九章二则札记
作者：理查德·冈布里奇（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文针对《清净道论》第九章的两处关键问题展开考证。其一，关于“puggala”（补特伽罗）的词源， Buddhaghosa将其释为“坠入名为pun的地狱者”，实则是对婆罗门教“putra”（儿子，拯救父亲脱离put地狱）词源的幽默讽刺，文本应校勘为“Put ti vuccati...”（有地狱名为Put）。其二，文本中出现的“abhasatha”（他说）是巴利语中罕见的未完成时形式，与梵语“abhasata”平行，印证了巴利语并非完全缺失未完成时范畴，且暗示Buddhaghosa可能熟悉梵语语法。两处考证结合文献校勘与语言学分析，为《清净道论》的文本研究与巴利语语法研究提供了新视角。  
关键词：《清净道论》；巴利语语法；词源考证；文本校勘；Buddhaghosa；补特伽罗  
Title：Two Notes on Visuddhimagga IX  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper investigates two key issues in Chapter IX of the Visuddhimagga. Firstly, regarding the etymology of "puggala" (individual), Buddhaghosa explains it as "those who slip into a hell called pun", which is actually a humorous satire on the Brahminical etymology of "putra" (son, who saves his father from a hell called put). The text should be emended to "Put ti vuccati..." (There is a hell called Put). Secondly, the form "abhasatha" (he said) appearing in the text is a rare imperfect form in Pali, parallel to Sanskrit "abhasata", confirming that Pali does not completely lack the imperfect tense category and suggesting that Buddhaghosa may have been familiar with Sanskrit grammar. The two investigations combine textual collation and linguistic analysis, providing new perspectives for the textual research of the Visuddhimagga and Pali grammatical studies.  
Keywords：Visuddhimagga；Pali grammar；etymological research；textual collation；Buddhaghosa；puggala

# 论文5. 《弥兰陀王问经》最古老有纪年手稿补记
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文补充报告了泰国北部棕榈叶手稿的新发现，该手稿是现存最古老的有纪年《弥兰陀王问经》抄本。通过参与清迈大学主导的泰国北部寺院手稿保护与微缩项目，在莱欣寺（Vat Lai Hin）图书馆新发现2个完整分册（phuk 8、9）及若干散叶，仅分册3、12、13仍缺失。新发现手稿补充了原文献的关键段落，结合1987年的初步调查，完善了该手稿的流传记录。文末补充了《弥兰陀王问经》的最新研究文献，包括校勘本、译本及专题论文，为该经典的版本学研究提供了更新的资料索引。  
关键词：《弥兰陀王问经》；巴利语手稿；有纪年手稿；泰国北部；版本学；文献补充  
Title：An Additional Note on the Oldest Dated Manuscript of the Milindapanha  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper supplements the report on the discovery of a new palm leaf manuscript in northern Thailand, which is the oldest dated manuscript of the Milindapanha extant. By participating in the palm leaf manuscript preservation and microfilming project led by Chiang Mai University, two complete fasciculi (phuk 8, 9) and several stray leaves were newly discovered in the library of Vat Lai Hin, with only fasciculi 3, 12, and 13 still missing. The newly discovered manuscript supplements key passages of the original text, and combined with the preliminary survey in 1987, improves the transmission record of this manuscript. At the end of the paper, the latest research literature on the Milindapanha is supplemented, including critical editions, translations, and monographic papers, providing an updated data index for the textual research of this classic.  
Keywords：Milindapanha；Pali manuscript；dated manuscript；northern Thailand；textual scholarship；literary supplement

# 论文6. 18世纪暹罗遣使锡兰赠书清单考述
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文对1756年暹罗国王遣使锡兰（康提王国）所赠97部巴利语典籍清单进行系统性考证。清单涵盖律藏、经藏、论藏注疏、语法书、历史文献等，包括《善见律毗婆沙》《集学》《清净道论疏》等核心文献，其中部分典籍为当时锡兰所缺失，旨在助力当地佛教文献的复兴。研究通过文献比对，厘清了清单中部分晦涩书名的对应文献，按律藏、经藏、论藏、非三藏文献等分类整理，并分析了暹罗与锡兰的佛教文化交流背景。清单反映了18世纪东南亚与南亚的巴利语文献传承状况，为南传佛教文献传播史研究提供了重要史料。  
关键词：18世纪；暹罗；锡兰；巴利语典籍；赠书清单；佛教文化交流  
Title：Remarks on a List of Books Sent to Ceylon from Siam in the 18th Century  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper conducts a systematic investigation on the list of 97 Pali texts sent by the King of Siam to Ceylon (Kandyan Kingdom) in 1756. The list covers Vinaya, Sutta, Abhidhamma commentaries, grammatical works, historical documents, etc., including core texts such as the Samantapasadika, Sarasamasa, and Visuddhimagga-mht. Some of these texts were missing in Ceylon at that time, aiming to assist the revival of local Buddhist literature. Through textual comparison, the study clarifies the corresponding documents of some obscure titles in the list, sorts them by category such as Vinaya, Sutta, Abhidhamma, and extra-canonical texts, and analyzes the background of Buddhist cultural exchanges between Siam and Ceylon. The list reflects the transmission status of Pali literature in Southeast Asia and South Asia in the 18th century, providing important historical materials for the study of the dissemination history of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：18th century；Siam；Ceylon；Pali texts；book list；Buddhist cultural exchange

# 论文7. 1756年暹罗大元帅致康提王室的巴利语书信
作者：素帕潘·那邦昌（Supaphan Na Bangchang）  
摘要：本文校勘并研究了1756年10月15日暹罗大元帅致锡兰康提王室的巴利语书信，该信是18世纪暹罗与锡兰佛教交流的核心史料。书信记录了暹罗国王响应锡兰请求，派遣僧人赴锡兰传戒、赠送97部巴利语典籍的经过，详述了双方使团互访、宗教仪式、礼物交换等细节，提及锡兰佛教复兴中的戒律传承、文献补全等问题。书信语言受泰语影响，包含部分特殊词汇与语法结构，反映了东南亚巴利语的地域特色。本文基于1916年丹隆亲王的校勘本进行重刊，补充了文献背景与内容摘要，为研究近代南传佛教跨国交流提供了原始文献依据。  
关键词：1756年；暹罗；锡兰；巴利语书信；佛教交流；大元帅  
Title：A Pali Letter sent by the Aggamahasenapati of Siam to the Royal Court at Kandy in 1756  
Author：Supaphan Na Bangchang  
Abstract：This paper collates and studies the Pali letter sent by the Aggamahasenapati of Siam to the royal court of Kandy, Ceylon on October 15, 1756, which is a core historical material for Buddhist exchanges between Siam and Ceylon in the 18th century. The letter records the King of Siam's response to Ceylon's request to send monks to Ceylon for ordination and present 97 Pali texts, detailing the mutual visits of envoys, religious rituals, gift exchanges and other details, and mentions issues such as precept transmission and literary completion in the revival of Ceylon Buddhism. Influenced by Thai, the language of the letter contains some special vocabulary and grammatical structures, reflecting the regional characteristics of Pali in Southeast Asia. Based on Prince Damrong's 1916 edited version, this paper republishes the letter with supplementary literary background and content summary, providing original documentary evidence for the study of modern transnational exchanges of Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：1756；Siam；Ceylon；Pali letter；Buddhist exchange；Aggamahasenapati
