# 【标题】1891-1893年合刊【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1893【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利圣典协会章程
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文阐述巴利圣典协会的成立宗旨、学术价值、出版规划及订阅捐赠规则。协会旨在整理出版欧洲各图书馆中未编辑的早期佛教文献，这些文献对民俗史、宗教史、语言史研究意义重大，是公元前400-250年间未受欧洲思想影响的古印度社会记录，也是研究佛教起源与早期发展的核心资料。协会计划出版约9000-10000页文献，已出版5000页，新增50名会员可实现年均1000页出版量。订阅费用为每年1几尼或六年5几尼，非巴利语使用者可捐赠支持，目前已获近400英镑捐赠。1894年订阅费已到期，协会要求订阅者提前支付，未付费者无法获取出版物。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；章程；佛教文献出版；订阅规则；学术价值；捐赠机制  
Title：Prospectus of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper expounds the founding purpose, academic value, publishing plan, and subscription and donation rules of the Pali Text Society. The society aims to collate and publish unedited early Buddhist literature in various libraries in Europe. These documents are of great significance for the study of folklore history, religious history, and linguistic history, serving as records of ancient Indian society from 400-250 BCE without European ideological influence, and core materials for researching the origin and early development of Buddhism. The society plans to publish approximately 9,000-10,000 pages of literature, with 5,000 pages already published, and the addition of 50 new members will enable an annual publication volume of 1,000 pages. The subscription fee is 1 guinea per year or 5 guineas for six years, and non-Pali speakers can support through donations, with nearly £400 already raised. Subscriptions for 1894 are due, and the society requires subscribers to pay in advance; those who fail to pay cannot obtain publications.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Prospectus；Buddhist Literature Publication；Subscription Rules；Academic Value；Donation Mechanism

# 论文2.  巴利圣典协会1891-1893年报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1891-1893年工作报告，核心说明协会出版进展与人员变动。主席因长期超负荷工作患病致协会工作停滞，理查德·莫里斯博士因病离世，其未完成的《增支部》校勘工作暂无人承接。莱昂·费尔继续推进《相应部》第四卷出版，罗伯特·查尔默斯接替特伦克纳完成《中部》及 Buddhaghosa 注疏《清净道论注》的校勘，爱德华·穆勒教授筹备《阿毗达磨义明灯》校勘，卡尔·诺伊曼在锡兰为协会获取多部手稿并承接《无碍解道》校勘。财务方面，报告列出收支明细，1893年末结余265英镑7先令3便士，主要支出用于印刷与编辑薪酬。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；工作报告；文献校勘；人员变动；财务收支；佛教文献出版  
Title：Report of the Pali Text Society (1891-1893)  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the work report of the Pali Text Society from 1891 to 1893, focusing on the society's publication progress and personnel changes. The Chairman fell ill due to long-term overwork, resulting in the suspension of the society's work. Dr. Richard Morris passed away from illness, and no one has yet undertaken the unfinished collation of the Anguttara Nikaya. Léon Feer continued to advance the publication of the fourth volume of the Samyutta Nikaya, Robert Chalmers succeeded Trenckner in completing the collation of the Majjhima Nikaya and Buddhaghosa's commentary Papancasudani, Professor Edward Müller prepared the collation of the Attha Salini, and Karl Neumann obtained multiple manuscripts for the society in Ceylon and undertook the collation of the Patisambhida. Financially, the report lists detailed income and expenditure, with a balance of £265 7s 3d at the end of 1893, and the main expenditures were for printing and editors' salaries.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Work Report；Textual Collation；Personnel Changes；Financial Revenue and Expenditure；Buddhist Literature Publication

# 论文3.  巴利圣典协会1890-1893年财务报表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文呈现巴利圣典协会1890年1月1日至1893年12月31日的财务收支情况。收入方面，1890年初始余额85英镑3先令8便士，投资收益250英镑，24份六年期订阅费126英镑，年度订阅费204英镑15先令，捐赠12英镑，银行利息7英镑17先令8便士，总收入685英镑16先令4便士。支出方面，汇兑损失1英镑4先令3便士，印刷费374英镑，编辑薪酬18英镑，出版商费用27英镑4先令10便士，1893年末结余265英镑7先令3便士。报表清晰反映协会财务运作状况，为出版规划提供资金参考。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；财务报表；收支明细；出版资金；订阅收入；捐赠收入  
Title：Accounts of the Pali Text Society (1890-1893)  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper presents the financial income and expenditure of the Pali Text Society from January 1, 1890 to December 31, 1893. In terms of income, the initial balance on January 1, 1890 was £85 3s 8d, investment proceeds were £250, 24 six-year subscriptions were £126, annual subscriptions were £204 15s, donations were £12, and bank interest was £7 17s 8d, with a total income of £85 16s 4d. In terms of expenditure, exchange loss was £1 4s 3d, printing costs were £374, editors' salaries were £18, and publisher's charges were £27 4s 10d, with a balance of £265 7s 3d at the end of 1893. The statement clearly reflects the society's financial operation and provides funding reference for publishing plans.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Financial Accounts；Income and Expenditure Details；Publishing Funds；Subscription Income；Donation Income

# 论文4.  巴利语词汇注释与考辨
作者：理查德·莫里斯牧师（Rev. R. Morris, M.A, LL.D.）  
摘要：本文对46个巴利语疑难词汇进行校勘与释义，涵盖词汇形态演变、语义辨析、语源追溯及文献用例考证。通过比对《Divyavadana》《三藏》《本生经》等典籍，纠正此前词典释义偏差（如“luha”意为“粗糙”而非“恶劣”），厘清易混淆词汇的语义边界（如“rabhasa”表“猛烈”、“aranavihari”表“无忧居”），补充未被词典收录的词汇用法（如“kalussiya”表“晦涩”），并结合梵语、俗语同源词推导词汇演变路径，为巴利语词典编纂与文献解读提供关键依据。  
关键词：巴利语；词汇考辨；语义校正；语源研究；文献用例；佛教语言  
Title：Notes and Queries  
Author：Rev. R. Morris, M.A, LL.D.  
Abstract：This paper collates and interprets 46 obscure Pali words, covering lexical morphological evolution, semantic discrimination, etymological tracing, and textual usage verification. By comparing texts such as the Divyavadana, Tipitaka, and Jataka Tales, it corrects previous dictionary interpretation deviations (e.g., "luha" means "coarse" rather than "bad"), clarifies the semantic boundaries of easily confused words (e.g., "rabhasa" means "violent," "aranavihari" means "living free from care"), supplements the usage of words not included in dictionaries (e.g., "kalussiya" means "obscurity"), and deduces the lexical evolution path by combining Sanskrit and Prakrit cognates, providing key basis for Pali dictionary compilation and textual interpretation.  
Keywords：Pali；Lexical Research；Semantic Correction；Etymological Study；Textual Usage；Buddhist Language

# 论文5.  Buddhaghosa《清净道论》目录
作者：亨利·C·沃伦（Henry C. Warren, Esq.）  
摘要：本文系统整理 Buddhaghosa 所著《清净道论》（Visuddhi-Magga）的详细目录，按“戒、定、慧”三部分类，共23章。戒学部分含“戒的阐释”“头陀行阐释”2章，详述戒律规范与苦行实践；定学部分含11章，涵盖禅修基础、十种遍作、不净观、念住等禅修方法及神通境界；慧学部分含10章，聚焦五蕴、十二处、十八界等法义阐释，以及见清净、度疑清净等七种清净境界的修证路径。目录精准呈现著作的逻辑架构与核心内容，为《清净道论》的研读与研究提供清晰指引。  
关键词：《清净道论》；Buddhaghosa；目录整理；戒定慧三学；禅修方法；南传佛教  
Title：Table of Contents of Buddhaghosa's Visuddhi-Magga  
Author：Henry C. Warren, Esq.  
Abstract：This paper systematically compiles the detailed table of contents of Buddhaghosa's Visuddhi-Magga, divided into three parts ("Sila, Samadhi, Panna") with 23 chapters in total. The Sila section includes 2 chapters: "Exposition of Sila" and "Exposition of the Dhutanga," detailing precept norms and ascetic practices; the Samadhi section includes 11 chapters, covering meditation basics, ten Kasinas, Asubha meditation, Satipatthana, and other meditation methods as well as supernatural states; the Panna section includes 10 chapters, focusing on the explanation of Dharma such as the Five Khandhas, Twelve Ayatanas, Eighteen Dhatus, and the practice path of seven purities including Ditthi Visuddhi and Kaikhavitarana Visuddhi. The table of contents accurately presents the logical structure and core content of the work, providing clear guidance for the study and research of the Visuddhi-Magga.  
Keywords：Visuddhi-Magga；Buddhaghosa；Catalogue Compilation；Sila, Samadhi, Panna；Meditation Methods；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文6.  三藏目录
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文梳理巴利语三藏（律藏、经藏、小部、论藏）的典籍清单及出版情况。律藏3部典籍已全部出版（1511页）；经藏4部尼柯耶已出版2075页，未出版2500页；小部22部典籍已出版1044页，未出版1135页；论藏7部典籍已出版451页，未出版1700页。目录明确标注每部典籍的印刷页数与未出版页数，为巴利三藏的整理出版规划提供量化参考。  
关键词：巴利三藏；律藏；经藏；小部；论藏；出版目录  
Title：List of the Pitakas  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the catalogues and publication status of the Pali Tipitaka (Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka, Khuddaka Nikaya, Abhidhamma Pitaka). All 3 texts of the Vinaya Pitaka have been published (1,511 pages); 4 Nikayas of the Sutta Pitaka have 2,075 pages published and 2,500 pages unpublished; 22 texts of the Khuddaka Nikaya have 1,044 pages published and 1,135 pages unpublished; 7 texts of the Abhidhamma Pitaka have 451 pages published and 1,700 pages unpublished. The catalogue clearly marks the printed pages and unpublished pages of each text, providing quantitative reference for the collation and publication planning of the Pali Tipitaka.  
Keywords：Pali Tipitaka；Vinaya Pitaka；Sutta Pitaka；Khuddaka Nikaya；Abhidhamma Pitaka；Publication Catalogue

# 论文7.  巴利圣典协会出版物列表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文以年份排序和字母排序两种方式，梳理巴利圣典协会1882-1893年的出版物信息。按年份统计，12年间共出版34部文本、34卷，合计7200页，涵盖三藏典籍、注疏、语法著作等；期刊中收录的文本以星号标记。按字母排序列出每部文本的出版年份与编者，清晰呈现协会的出版成果，为研究者检索相关文献提供便捷工具。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；出版物列表；文本编者；出版年份；文献检索；佛教文献  
Title：Issues of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the publication information of the Pali Text Society from 1882 to 1893 in two ways: chronological order and alphabetical order. According to chronological statistics, 34 texts and 34 volumes have been published in 12 years, totaling 7,200 pages, covering Tipitaka texts, commentaries, grammatical works, etc.; texts included in the journal are marked with an asterisk. Listed alphabetically are the publication year and editor of each text, clearly presenting the society's publication achievements and providing a convenient tool for researchers to retrieve relevant literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Publication List；Text Editors；Publication Year；Literature Retrieval；Buddhist Literature
