# 【标题】1888年第3期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1888【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利语专有名词辞典
作者：爱德华·穆勒教授（Professor Edward Müller）  
摘要：本文为系统梳理巴利语佛教文献中专有名词的专题辞典，涵盖人名、地名、典籍名、教派名、神名等核心类别。人名部分收录佛陀弟子、国王、婆罗门、比丘比丘尼等关键人物，如阿耆多翅舍钦婆罗、阿育王、摩哂陀等；地名涵盖印度、斯里兰卡等地的城镇、河流、山脉、园林，如王舍城、恒河、灵鹫山、祇树给孤独园等；典籍名包含三藏及注疏文献，如《增支部》《相应部》《清净道论》等；教派名涉及佛教部派及异端学派，如大众部、上座部、耆那教等。辞典对每个专有名词标注了出处文献（如《法句经》《本生经》）及相关语境，为巴利语佛教文献的研读与翻译提供权威参考。  
关键词：巴利语；专有名词；佛教辞典；南传佛教；文献研读；地名；人名；典籍名  
Title：A Glossary of Pali Proper Names  
Author：Professor Edward Müller  
Abstract：This paper is a thematic dictionary systematically sorting out proper names in Pali Buddhist literature, covering core categories such as personal names, place names, text titles, sect names and divine names. The personal name section includes key figures such as the Buddha's disciples, kings, brahmans, bhikkhus and bhikkhunis, such as Ajita Kesakambali, Asoka and Mahinda; place names cover cities, rivers, mountains and gardens in India, Sri Lanka and other places, such as Rajagaha, Ganges River, Gijjhakuta Mountain and Jetavana Vihara; text titles include Tipitaka and commentary literature, such as Anguttara Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya and Visuddhimagga; sect names involve Buddhist sects and heretical schools, such as Mahasamghika, Theravada and Jainism. Each proper name in the dictionary is annotated with the source literature (such as Dhammapada and Jataka) and relevant contexts, providing authoritative reference for the study and translation of Pali Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali；Proper Names；Buddhist Dictionary；Theravada Buddhism；Literature Study；Place Names；Personal Names；Text Titles

# 论文2.  大英博物馆巴利语手稿补充目录（1883年后新增）
作者：赫宁博士（Dr. Hoerning）  
摘要：本文为1883年巴利圣典协会发布的《大英博物馆巴利语手稿目录》的补充篇目，系统整理该馆后续新增的巴利语手稿藏品。手稿按律藏、经藏、论藏、宗教著作、语法词典类、杂项著作分类，涵盖《波罗提木叉注》《长部》《论事》等核心典籍，标注了每份手稿的叶数、文字类型（缅甸文、僧伽罗文等）、载体（棕榈叶、铜版、纸张等）及馆藏编号。补充目录丰富了巴利语原始文献的检索线索，为南传佛教文献校勘与学术研究提供重要史料支撑。  
关键词：大英博物馆；巴利语手稿；补充目录；文献收藏；南传佛教；手稿分类；馆藏编号  
Title：Supplementary List of the Pali MSS. in the British Museum  
Author：Dr. Hoerning  
Abstract：This paper is a supplementary volume to the "List of Pali MSS. in the British Museum" published by the Pali Text Society in 1883, systematically sorting out the subsequent newly added Pali manuscript collections of the museum. The manuscripts are classified into Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka, religious works, grammar and lexicography works, and miscellaneous works, covering core texts such as Pārijika Atthakathā, Digha Nikaya and Kathavatthu. Each manuscript is annotated with the number of leaves, character type (Burmese, Sinhalese, etc.), carrier (palm leaves, copper plates, paper, etc.) and collection number. The supplementary catalog enriches the retrieval clues of original Pali literature, providing important historical support for the collation and academic research of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：British Museum；Pali Manuscripts；Supplementary List；Literature Collection；Theravada Buddhism；Manuscript Classification；Collection Number

# 论文3.  巴利圣典协会捐赠者与会员名录
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文收录1888年巴利圣典协会的捐赠者名单与会员名录，捐赠者包含暹罗国王、印度事务大臣等机构及个人；会员名录按会费标准分为“五年五几尼会员”与“年度一几尼会员”，涵盖全球多所大学、学术机构及学者个人，标注了会员所属地区、机构及通讯地址。名录直观呈现协会的国际学术影响力与支持网络，为研究19世纪末南传佛教研究的学术共同体提供重要参考。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；捐赠者；会员名录；学术共同体；南传佛教研究；19世纪末  
Title：List of the Donors and Subscribers to the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper contains the list of donors and subscribers of the Pali Text Society in 1888. Donors include institutions and individuals such as the King of Siam and the Secretary of State for India; the subscriber list is divided into "Five Guineas for Six Years Subscribers" and "One Guinea per Year Subscribers" according to the subscription standard, covering universities, academic institutions and individual scholars around the world, with annotations of the subscribers' regions, institutions and correspondence addresses. The list intuitively presents the society's international academic influence and support network, providing important reference for the study of the academic community of Theravada Buddhist research in the late 19th century.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Donors；Subscriber List；Academic Community；Theravada Buddhist Research；Late 19th Century

# 论文4.  巴利圣典协会1888年财务报告
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1888年度财务收支明细报告，收入项包括会员会费（年度一几尼与五年五几尼）、储备基金利息等；支出项涵盖手稿购买、印刷费、编辑费用、邮寄装订费等。报告以精准数据呈现协会的财务运营状况，同时附斯里兰卡地区专项收支明细，为研究协会的学术出版规划与机构可持续发展提供量化依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；财务报告；收支明细；出版经费；斯里兰卡地区；机构运营  
Title：Accounts  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the detailed financial revenue and expenditure report of the Pali Text Society for 1888. Income items include membership dues (one guinea per year and five guineas for six years), interest on reserve funds, etc.; expenditure items include manuscript purchase, printing fees, editorial fees, mailing and binding fees, etc. The report presents the society's financial operation status with accurate data, and attaches the special revenue and expenditure details of the Sri Lanka region, providing quantitative basis for the study of the society's academic publication planning and institutional sustainable development.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Financial Report；Revenue and Expenditure Details；Publishing Funds；Sri Lanka Region；Institutional Operation

# 论文5.  巴利圣典协会已出版典籍清单
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文系统梳理巴利圣典协会成立以来至1888年已出版的全部巴利语典籍目录，按典籍名称排序，详细标注每部典籍的出版年份与编者。清单涵盖经藏、律藏、论藏及注疏、辞典等辅助文献，如《法句经注》《相应部》《阿毗达磨集论》等，其中标注星号的典籍为期刊收录篇目。清单全面呈现协会早期出版成果，为研究者检索相关文献提供权威指引。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；已出版典籍；文献目录；南传佛教；三藏典籍；注疏文献  
Title：Texts Already Published  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the catalog of all Pali texts published by the Pali Text Society from its establishment to 1888, arranged by text name, with detailed annotations of the publication year and editor of each text. The list covers Sutta Pitaka, Vinaya Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka as well as commentaries, dictionaries and other auxiliary literature, such as Dhammapada Atthakatha, Samyutta Nikaya and Abhidhammattha Samgaha, among which the texts marked with asterisks are included in the journal. The list comprehensively presents the society's early publication achievements, providing authoritative guidance for researchers to retrieve relevant literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Published Texts；Literature Catalog；Theravada Buddhism；Tipitaka Texts；Commentary Literature

# 论文6.  巴利圣典协会1888年年度报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1888年度工作报告，由主席T·W·里斯·戴维斯撰写。报告回顾协会第二届六年计划的首个年度进展，包括会员续订情况、典籍出版推进（如《相应部》第二部分、《长部》注疏等）、待出版典籍筹备（如《论事注》《无碍解道》等），并提及辞典编纂、手稿收集等学术工作。报告还呼吁新增会员以保障出版效率，为研究协会的学术规划与发展历程提供第一手资料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；年度报告；典籍出版；学术规划；会员发展；南传佛教文献  
Title：Report for 1888  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the 1888 annual work report of the Pali Text Society, written by the Chairman T. W. Rhys Davids. The report reviews the first year's progress of the society's second six-year plan, including the renewal of memberships, the advancement of text publication (such as Part II of Samyutta Nikaya and commentaries on Digha Nikaya), the preparation of texts to be published (such as Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha and Patisambhidamagga), and mentions academic work such as dictionary compilation and manuscript collection. The report also calls for new members to ensure publication efficiency, providing first-hand materials for the study of the society's academic planning and development process.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Annual Report；Text Publication；Academic Planning；Membership Development；Theravada Buddhist Literature

# 论文7.  巴利圣典协会章程
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会的核心运营章程，明确协会成立宗旨为整理出版欧洲各图书馆收藏的未刊巴利语早期佛教文献。章程详述文献的历史价值（涵盖民俗史、宗教史、语言学）、出版规模规划（约9000-10000页八开本）、会员订阅标准（年度一几尼或六年五几尼）、捐赠机制等，同时介绍管理委员会构成及英美、斯里兰卡地区负责人信息，为了解协会的组织架构与运营规则提供权威依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；章程；文献出版；会员制度；组织架构；学术宗旨  
Title：Prospectus of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the core operating charter of the Pali Text Society, clarifying the society's founding purpose as collating and publishing unpublished early Pali Buddhist literature collected in various libraries in Europe. The charter details the historical value of the literature (covering folk history, religious history and linguistics), publication scale planning (about 9,000-10,000 octavo pages), membership subscription standards (one guinea per year or five guineas for six years), donation mechanism, etc., and introduces the composition of the management committee and the information of responsible persons in Britain, the United States and Sri Lanka, providing authoritative basis for understanding the society's organizational structure and operating rules.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Charter；Literature Publication；Membership System；Organizational Structure；Academic Purpose
