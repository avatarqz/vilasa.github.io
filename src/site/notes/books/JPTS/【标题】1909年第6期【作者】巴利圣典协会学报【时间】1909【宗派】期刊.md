# 【标题】1909年第6期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1909【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利圣典协会1909年度报告
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文概述了巴利圣典协会1909年的核心工作成果与后续计划。完成了诺曼教授主编的《法句经注疏》第一卷剩余部分，其引言支持该注疏非觉音所著的观点，但最终定论需更多文本与更完善的词典佐证。期刊核心内容围绕协会词典编撰的筹备工作展开，次年将出版《长部》第三卷及《增支部》五卷索引，后续还将陆续推出《长部》《中部》等所有经典文本的索引。强调词典编撰的迫切性，现有资金可支持编撰一部篇幅为蔡尔德斯词典两倍、质量更优的工具书，由多位欧洲顶尖学者按字母分工编写，计划分四卷出版。同时提及巴伐利亚科学院从哈代基金拨款支持词典项目，恩斯特·库恩教授接替已故的哈代教授负责元音部分编撰，未来还将推出面向学生的简明词典，由仰光的杜瓦塞尔教授协助完成。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1909年度报告；巴利语词典；经典索引；文本校勘；学术计划  
Title：Report of the Society for the Year 1909  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper outlines the core achievements and subsequent plans of the Pali Text Society in 1909. It completed the remaining part of Volume I of the Dhammapada Commentary edited by Professor Norman, whose introduction supports the view that the commentary is not by Buddhaghosa, but a final conclusion requires more texts and a more comprehensive dictionary for verification. The core content of the journal focuses on the preparatory work for the compilation of the Society's dictionary. Next year, the third volume of the Digha Nikaya and the index to the five volumes of the Anguttara Nikaya will be published, and indexes for all canonical texts such as the Digha Nikaya and Majjhima Nikaya will be launched successively. It emphasizes the urgency of compiling the dictionary, with existing funds sufficient to support the compilation of a reference book twice the length of Childers's dictionary and of higher quality. Compiled by several top European scholars divided by letters, it is planned to be published in four volumes. At the same time, it mentions that the Bavarian Academy of Sciences has allocated funds from the Hardy Fund to support the dictionary project. Professor Ernst Kuhn has taken over from the late Professor Hardy to compile the vowel part. In the future, a concise dictionary for students will be launched, with the assistance of Professor Duroiselle from Rangoon.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1909 Annual Report；Pali Dictionary；Canonical Indexes；Textual Collation；Academic Plans

# 论文2.  以“S”开头的巴利语词汇研究
作者：斯滕·科诺博士（Dr. Sten Konow）；修订与增补：迪内斯·安德森教授（Professor Dines Andersen）  
摘要：本文是针对以“S”开头的巴利语词汇的专项研究，经迪内斯·安德森教授修订增补后发表。系统梳理了字母“S”（sa - kara）的多重功能，包括作为复合形容词和副词的前缀（表“与……一起”“相同”）、数词“一”、指示代词词根（表“那个”“他/她”）、肯定助词等用法。详细解析了“S”开头词汇的构词规则、语法变形及语义演变，涵盖“sva”（自身）、“cha”（仅在复合词中）等核心词根衍生的词汇，结合《法句经》《相应部》《中部》等经典文献中的实例，对“sa - upadisesa”“samyama”“sammoha”等术语的语义、用法及哲学心理内涵进行精准阐释，其中部分哲学与心理类词汇由里斯·戴维斯夫人补充论述。  
关键词：巴利语；“S”开头词汇；构词法；语义解析；经典文献佐证；术语研究  
Title：Pali Words Beginning with "S"  
Author：Dr. Sten Konow；Revised and Enlarged by Professor Dines Andersen  
Abstract：This paper is a special study on Pali words beginning with "S", published after revision and enlargement by Professor Dines Andersen. It systematically sorts out the multiple functions of the letter "S" (sa - kara), including serving as a prefix for compound adjectives and adverbs (meaning "with", "same as"), the numeral "one", the root of demonstrative pronouns (meaning "that", "he/she"), and an affirmative particle. It details the word - formation rules, grammatical variations, and semantic evolution of words beginning with "S", covering words derived from core roots such as "sva" (own) and "cha" (only in compounds). Combined with examples from canonical texts such as the Dhammapada, Samyutta Nikaya, and Majjhima Nikaya, it accurately interprets the semantics, usage, and philosophical - psychological connotations of terms such as "sa - upadisesa", "samyama", and "sammoha". Some philosophical and psychological terms are supplemented by Mrs. Rhys Davids.  
Keywords：Pali Language；Words Beginning with "S"；Word - Formation；Semantic Analysis；Canonical Text Evidence；Term Study

# 论文3.  卡尔马巴帕达的故事及其在印度文学中的演变
作者：渡边海旭（K. Watanabe）  
摘要：本文聚焦“卡尔马巴帕达的故事”这一核心主题，深入探讨其在印度文学中的传承与演变轨迹。通过文本比对与源流考证，梳理该故事在不同时期印度文学作品中的情节变异、角色塑造及主题深化，分析其与印度古代叙事传统、宗教文化的内在关联。揭示故事从早期雏形到成熟形态的发展脉络，探讨婆罗门教、佛教等宗教思想对其内容的影响，为研究印度文学的文本传承与文化交融提供典型案例。  
关键词：卡尔马巴帕达；印度文学；故事演变；文本传承；宗教文化影响  
Title：The Story of Kalmabapada, and Its Evolution in Indian Literature  
Author：K. Watanabe  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the core theme of "the Story of Kalmabapada" and delves into its inheritance and evolution trajectory in Indian literature. Through textual comparison and origin research, it sorts out the plot variations, character shaping, and theme deepening of the story in Indian literary works of different periods, and analyzes its internal connection with ancient Indian narrative traditions and religious culture. It reveals the development context of the story from its early embryonic form to its mature form, explores the influence of religious thoughts such as Brahmanism and Buddhism on its content, and provides a typical case for the study of textual inheritance and cultural integration in Indian literature.  
Keywords：Kalmabapada；Indian Literature；Story Evolution；Textual Inheritance；Influence of Religious Culture

# 论文4.  《长部》中的偈颂研究
作者：鲁道夫·奥托·弗兰克教授（Professor R. Otto Franke）  
摘要：本文是针对《长部》（Digha - Nikaya）中偈颂（Gathas）的专项研究。系统整理《长部》中的偈颂文本，考证其语言特征、韵律结构与文学风格，分析偈颂在经文叙事中的功能与意义——包括阐释教义、烘托氛围、强化记忆等。探讨偈颂与散文部分的关系，追溯其在巴利语佛教文献中的传承特点，为理解《长部》的文献构成、文学价值及佛教教义的传播方式提供重要支撑。  
关键词：《长部》；偈颂；巴利语佛教文献；韵律结构；文学功能；教义传播  
Title：Die Gathas des Digha - Nikaya  
Author：Professor R. Otto Franke  
Abstract：This paper is a special study on the Gathas in the Digha - Nikaya. It systematically sorts out the Gatha texts in the Digha - Nikaya, researches and verifies their linguistic characteristics, metrical structures, and literary styles, and analyzes the functions and significance of the Gathas in the narrative of the sutras—including explaining the Dharma, setting the atmosphere, and strengthening memory. It explores the relationship between the Gathas and the prose parts, traces their inheritance characteristics in Pali Buddhist literature, and provides important support for understanding the literary composition, literary value of the Digha - Nikaya and the propagation mode of Buddhist doctrines.  
Keywords：Digha - Nikaya；Gathas；Pali Buddhist Literature；Metrical Structure；Literary Function；Dharma Propagation

# 论文5.  巴利语书籍标题缩写规范
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文制定了巴利语书籍标题的统一缩写规范。针对巴利语佛教文献及相关研究著作的标题冗长、表述不一的问题，结合学术研究与文献引用的实际需求，对常用巴利语书籍标题进行标准化缩写处理，明确缩写规则与使用场景。该规范为巴利语学术文献的引用、索引编撰及学术交流提供了统一标准，提升了文献引用的准确性与便捷性。  
关键词：巴利语书籍；标题缩写；规范；学术引用；文献索引  
Title：Abbreviations of Titles of Pali Books  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper formulates unified abbreviation standards for the titles of Pali books. In response to the problems of lengthy and inconsistent expressions of titles of Pali Buddhist literature and related research works, combined with the actual needs of academic research and literature citation, it conducts standardized abbreviation processing for commonly used Pali book titles and clarifies the abbreviation rules and application scenarios. This standard provides a unified criterion for the citation of Pali academic literature, index compilation, and academic exchange, improving the accuracy and convenience of literature citation.  
Keywords：Pali Books；Title Abbreviations；Standards；Academic Citation；Literary Indexes

# 论文6.  巴利圣典协会出版物列表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文系统罗列了巴利圣典协会已出版的各类文献成果，包括巴利语原典、注疏、校勘本、研究论著等。按出版顺序详细记录每部出版物的书名、编者、出版年份、版本信息及核心内容概要，涵盖经藏、律藏、论藏相关文献及学术研究成果，全面呈现协会在巴利语佛教文献整理与传播方面的核心成就，为学界查阅相关文献提供了完整的参考目录。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；出版物列表；巴利语原典；文献整理；学术成果  
Title：Issues of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically lists various literary achievements published by the Pali Text Society, including Pali original texts, commentaries, collated editions, and research monographs. It details the title, editor, publication year, version information, and core content summary of each publication in the order of publication, covering literature related to the Sutta Pitaka, Vinaya Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka, and academic research results. It comprehensively presents the core achievements of the Society in the collation and dissemination of Pali Buddhist literature, providing a complete reference catalog for academic circles to retrieve relevant literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Publication List；Pali Original Texts；Literary Collation；Academic Achievements
