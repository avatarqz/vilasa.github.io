# 论文1. 兰斯·塞尔温·库辛斯（1942-2015）
作者：鲁柏·葛汀（Rupert Gethin）  
摘要：本文是对佛教学者兰斯·塞尔温·库辛斯的生平与学术贡献综述。库辛斯1942年生于英国赫特福德郡，先后求学于剑桥大学历史系与东方研究系，专攻梵语与中古印度语。他曾任职于曼彻斯特大学比较宗教学系，后在牛津大学教授巴利语、中古印度语及佛教课程，曾任英国佛教研究协会首任主席、巴利圣典协会会长。其研究涵盖巴利语圣典、阿毗达磨文献、早期佛教宗派史及禅修传统，发表多篇具影响力的论文，对巴利语口头文学、阿毗达磨知觉理论等领域有深入探索，同时积极推动禅修实践的传承与推广，2013年获曼谷摩诃朱拉隆功佛教大学荣誉博士学位。  
关键词：兰斯·塞尔温·库辛斯；佛教研究；巴利语学术；阿毗达磨；禅修传统  
Title：Lance Selwyn Cousins, 1942–2015  
Author：Rupert Gethin  
Abstract：This article is a review of the life and academic contributions of Buddhist scholar Lance Selwyn Cousins. Born in Hertfordshire, UK in 1942, Cousins studied at the University of Cambridge, first in the Department of History and later in Oriental Studies, specializing in Sanskrit and Middle Indian. He taught in the Department of Comparative Religion at the University of Manchester and later lectured on Pali, Middle Indian, and Buddhism at the University of Oxford. He served as the first president of the UK Association for Buddhist Studies and president of the Pali Text Society. His research covered Pali canonical texts, Abhidhamma literature, the history of early Buddhist schools, and meditation traditions. He published numerous influential articles, making in-depth explorations in fields such as Pali oral literature and Abhidhamma theory of perception, while actively promoting the inheritance and popularization of meditation practice. He was awarded an honorary doctorate in Buddhist Studies by Mahamakut Buddhist University in Bangkok in 2013.  
Keywords：Lance Selwyn Cousins；Buddhist Studies；Pali scholarship；Abhidhamma；meditation tradition

# 论文3. “对此有如下偈颂”：巴利语注释文献中hoti/honti/bhavanti c’ettha的用法
作者：佩特拉·基弗-普尔茨（Petra Kieffer-Pülz）  
摘要：巴利语注释文献的作者会使用特定术语与表达引入异议、回应、比较、引语及偈颂等内容，这些表达的使用并非固定不变，其分布可为文本断代提供依据，且有助于厘清文本传承问题与文本校勘。本文聚焦表达式hoti/honti/bhavanti c’ettha（字面意为“对此有/存在”），基于Chaṭṭhasaṅgāyana CD-ROM版本3及其他可检索文本 corpus，对其用法展开全面考察。研究发现，该表达式仅用于引入偈颂，不出现于 canonical 文本；在注释层（aṭṭhakathā layer）中极少使用，而在疏释层（ṭīkā layer）及更晚的巴利语文献中使用频次显著增加；其地域分布呈现斯里兰卡作者在12-13世纪占主导、缅甸作者自15世纪起增多的特点；所引入偈颂或为作者原创，或源自其他文本，功能多为总结前文、充当记忆口诀等。此外，本文还对比了梵语中对应表达式bhavati/bhavata+/bhavanti cītra的用法，指出巴利语中的该表达式可能受梵语影响传入。  
关键词：巴利语注释文献；hoti/honti/bhavanti c’ettha；偈颂引入；文本断代；文本校勘  
Title：“And there is this stanza in this connection”: The Usage of hoti/honti/bhavanti c’ ettha in Pāli Commentarial Literature  
Author：Petra Kieffer-Pülz  
Abstract：Authors of Pali commentarial literature use specific terms and expressions to introduce objections, replies, comparisons, quotations, stanzas, etc. The usage of these expressions is not static, and their distribution can provide evidence for text dating and help clarify text transmission issues and textual collation. This article focuses on the expression hoti/honti/bhavanti c’ ettha (literally "there is/are in this connection"), conducting a comprehensive investigation of its usage based on the corpus of texts from the Chaṭṭhasaṅgāyana CD-ROM version 3 and other searchable texts. The study finds that this expression is only used to introduce stanzas and does not appear in canonical texts; it is rarely used in the aṭṭhakathā layer but its frequency increases significantly in the ṭīkā layer and later Pali literature; its regional distribution shows that Sri Lankan authors dominated in the 12th-13th centuries, while Burmese authors increased from the 15th century onward; the stanzas introduced by it are either original works by the authors or borrowed from other texts, with functions mostly summarizing the preceding text, serving as mnemonics, etc. In addition, this article compares the usage of the corresponding Sanskrit expression bhavati/bhavata+/bhavanti cītra, pointing out that the expression in Pali may have been introduced under the influence of Sanskrit.  
Keywords：Pali commentarial literature；hoti/honti/bhavanti c’ ettha；stanza introduction；text dating；textual collation

# 论文4. 巴利语的h-未来时——兼论“历史”与“不规则”未来时形式
作者：托马斯·奥伯利斯（Thomas Oberlies）  
摘要：巴利语的h-未来时是一个复杂的语法问题，本文结合所有可验证形式、形成的中间阶段、时空分布、语音形态过程及历史拼写现象，提出符合巴利语形态学规律的解决方案。研究指出，梵语未来时后缀的差异化发展为h-未来时的形成奠定基础，最古老的h-未来时形式见于阿育王巴利语（Aśokan Prakrit），其元音特征显示原始发音可能介于a与i之间。巴利语中的h-未来时与s-未来时存在词源关联，属于“东方元素”，传承自恒河地区的通用语（koine gangetique）。文章还探讨了h-未来时在巴利语文献中的分布特征，指出其主要见于2、3人称单数及3人称复数，多出现于以长元音结尾的动词之后，且并非能产性语法范畴；同时分析了未来时与不定过去时的密切关联，以及语音演变中塞音弱化、历史拼写等因素对h-未来时形式的影响。  
关键词：巴利语；h-未来时；语法形态；语音演变；不定过去时  
Title：The h-Future of Pāli with Random Notes on “Historical” and “Irregular” Future Forms  
Author：Thomas Oberlies  
Abstract：The h-future of Pali is a complex grammatical problem. Combining all attested forms, intermediate stages of formation, spatio-temporal distribution, phonological and morphological processes, and the phenomenon of historical orthography, this article proposes a solution consistent with the laws of Pali morphology. The study points out that the differential development of Sanskrit future suffixes laid the foundation for the formation of the h-future. The oldest h-future forms are found in Aśokan Prakrit, and their vowel characteristics suggest that the original pronunciation may have been between a and i. The h-future in Pali is etymologically related to the s-future, belonging to an "eastern element" inherited from the koine gangetique of the Ganges region. The article also discusses the distribution characteristics of the h-future in Pali texts, noting that it mainly appears in the 2nd and 3rd person singular and 3rd person plural, mostly after verbs ending in long vowels, and is not a productive grammatical category; it also analyzes the close connection between the future tense and the aorist, as well as the influence of phonological changes such as stop weakening and historical orthography on the forms of the h-future.  
Keywords：Pali；h-future；grammatical morphology；phonological change；aorist

# 论文5. 巴利圣典协会该获诺贝尔奖？T.W.里斯·戴维斯致瑞典学院诺贝尔委员会书信（编辑版）
作者：尤金·丘尔廷（Eugin Ciurtin）（编辑）  
摘要：本文收录并编辑了巴利圣典协会创始人T.W.里斯·戴维斯致瑞典学院诺贝尔委员会的书信。作为著名东方学家与巴利语研究先驱，戴维斯在信中阐述了巴利圣典协会的创立宗旨、学术使命及多年来的学术成果，包括巴利语文献的罗马字转写出版、翻译、词典与语法著作编纂等。他强调协会的非营利性质及对巴利语研究的推动作用，希望通过书信向诺贝尔委员会展示相关学术工作的价值与意义。该书信为研究巴利圣典协会的早期发展、T.W.里斯·戴维斯的学术理念提供了珍贵的原始文献资料。

Title：A Nobel for the Pali Text Society? T.W. Rhys Davids Writes to the Nobel Commission of the Swedish Academy  
Author：Edited by Eugin Ciurtin  
Abstract：This article collects and edits the letter from T.W. Rhys Davids, founder of the Pali Text Society, to the Nobel Commission of the Swedish Academy. As a renowned orientalist and pioneer of Pali studies, Davids elaborated on the founding purpose, academic mission, and many years of academic achievements of the Pali Text Society in the letter, including the publication of Pali texts in roman characters, translations, compilation of dictionaries and grammatical works, etc. He emphasized the non-profit nature of the society and its role in promoting Pali studies, hoping to show the value and significance of relevant academic work to the Nobel Commission through the letter. This letter provides precious primary source materials for the study of the early development of the Pali Text Society and T.W. Rhys Davids' academic ideas.  
Keywords：T.W. Rhys Davids；Pali Text Society；Nobel Commission；letter；Pali studies

# 论文6. 作为人工构词的动词*addhabhavati
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语动词*addhabhavati，深入探讨其形态构成、语义特征及使用背景。研究指出，*addhabhavati并非自然演变形成的动词，而是人工构词的产物，其构词方式与语义指向均体现出特定的学术或文本语境需求。文章通过对比相关巴利语文献中的用法实例，分析该动词的构词理据、语法功能及与其他相关动词的语义关联，揭示其在巴利语词汇系统中的特殊地位，为巴利语词汇学与构词法研究提供了具体案例。  
关键词：巴利语；*addhabhavati；人工构词；词汇学；构词法  
Title：The Verb *addhabhavati as an Artificial Formation  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This article focuses on the Pali verb *addhabhavati, conducting an in-depth exploration of its morphological composition, semantic characteristics, and usage context. The study points out that *addhabhavati is not a naturally evolved verb but an artificial formation, and its word-formation method and semantic orientation reflect the needs of specific academic or textual contexts. By comparing usage examples in relevant Pali texts, the article analyzes the word-formation motivation, grammatical function of this verb, and its semantic connection with other related verbs, revealing its special status in the Pali vocabulary system and providing a specific case for Pali lexicology and word-formation research.  
Keywords：Pali；*addhabhavati；artificial formation；lexicology；word-formation

# 论文7. 一份保存至今的古老题跋：Ñāṇavaṃsa与Sobhaggasiri的三藏
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文对一份保存至今的古老巴利语题跋展开研究，该题跋与Ñāṇavaṃsa和Sobhaggasiri相关的三藏文献密切相关。通过对题跋文本的校勘、释读与语境分析，作者还原了其创作背景、核心内容及所涉三藏文献的传承信息，揭示了该题跋在文献学、历史学及佛教传承研究中的价值。题跋中包含的相关人物、时间与文献传承线索，为考证特定时期巴利语三藏的流传、抄写与保存情况提供了重要依据，补充了早期佛教文献传承研究的细节。  
关键词：巴利语题跋；Ñāṇavaṃsa；Sobhaggasiri；三藏文献；文献传承  
Title：An Old Colophon Preserved: The Tipiṭaka of Ñāṇavaṃsa and Sobhaggasiri  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This article conducts a study on an old preserved Pali colophon, which is closely related to the Tipiṭaka literature associated with Ñāṇavaṃsa and Sobhaggasiri. Through the collation, interpretation, and contextual analysis of the colophon text, the author restores its composition background, core content, and the inheritance information of the involved Tipiṭaka literature, revealing the value of this colophon in philological, historical, and Buddhist transmission research. The relevant figures, time, and literature transmission clues contained in the colophon provide important evidence for verifying the circulation, copying, and preservation of the Pali Tipiṭaka during a specific period, supplementing the details of early Buddhist literature transmission research.  
Keywords：Pali colophon；Ñāṇavaṃsa；Sobhaggasiri；Tipiṭaka literature；literature transmission

# 论文8. 关于《大业分别经》及其注释的札记
作者：鲁柏·葛汀（Rupert Gethin）  
摘要：《大业分别经》（Mahākammavibhaṅga-sutta）是巴利语圣典中的重要文献，其注释包含丰富的佛教伦理、业报理论等相关阐释。本文针对该经及其注释中的关键问题展开探讨，包括经文中业报分类的核心概念、注释对经文义理的延伸解读、相关术语的准确释义等。通过文本细究与义理分析，作者纠正了此前部分研究中的解读偏差，厘清了经注中关于业的性质、分类及果报机制的核心观点，为深入理解早期佛教的业报思想提供了精准的文本解读与理论梳理。  
关键词：《大业分别经》；经注；业报思想；巴利语圣典；佛教伦理  
Title：A Note on the Mahākammavibhaṅga-sutta and its Commentary  
Author：Rupert Gethin  
Abstract：The Mahākammavibhaṅga-sutta is an important text in the Pali canon, and its commentary contains rich interpretations related to Buddhist ethics, karma theory, etc. This article explores key issues in the sutta and its commentary, including the core concepts of karma classification in the sutta, the commentary's extended interpretation of the sutta's meaning, and the accurate definition of relevant terms. Through textual scrutiny and philosophical analysis, the author corrects misinterpretations in previous studies, clarifies the core views on the nature, classification, and fruition mechanism of karma in the sutta and its commentary, providing a precise textual interpretation and theoretical sorting for an in-depth understanding of the early Buddhist karma theory.  
Keywords：Mahākammavibhaṅga-sutta；commentary；karma theory；Pali canon；Buddhist ethics
