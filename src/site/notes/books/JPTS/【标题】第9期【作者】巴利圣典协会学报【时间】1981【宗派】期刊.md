# 【标题】第9期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1981【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  依据经典文献看上座部佛教与东印度的关联
作者：安德烈·巴罗（André Bareau）  
摘要：本文结合玄奘、义净的记载与巴利语、梵语及汉译佛典，探讨上座部佛教与东印度的历史关联。通过比对不同佛教部派的平行文献，发现早在公元前最后四个世纪，上座部已知晓乌卡拉（今奥里萨邦北部）、孙巴斯等东印度地区，相关记载仅见于巴利语经典，未见于其他部派文献。文中分析了上座部在东印度的传播轨迹、与其他部派的差异，指出东印度在阿育王征服后佛教传播有限，直到8世纪波罗王朝时期才逐渐兴盛，为研究上座部佛教的地域扩展与文献传承提供了核心依据。  
关键词：上座部佛教；东印度；经典文献；佛教传播；部派佛教  
Title：The Theravadins and East India According to the Canonical Texts  
Author：André Bareau  
Abstract：This paper explores the historical connection between Theravada Buddhism and East India by combining the records of Hsüan-tsang and I-ching with Pali, Sanskrit and Chinese Buddhist texts. By comparing parallel texts from different Buddhist schools, it is found that as early as the last four centuries B.C., the Theravada already knew East Indian regions such as Ukkala (northern Orissa today) and Sumbhas. Relevant records are only found in Pali classics, not in texts of other schools. The paper analyzes the spread trajectory of Theravada in East India and its differences from other schools, pointing out that Buddhism spread limitedly in East India after Asoka's conquest and only flourished gradually during the Pala Dynasty in the 8th century, providing core evidence for the study of the regional expansion and textual transmission of Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Theravadins；East India；canonical texts；Buddhist transmission；Buddhist sects

# 论文2.  印度尼西亚的佛乘：一种融合性的上座部佛教
作者：海因茨·贝歇特（Heinz Bechert）  
摘要：本文梳理印度尼西亚佛教的发展历程，指出其呈现独特的融合性特征。佛教于5世纪传入后，与印度教长期共存，后逐渐形成爪哇特色的密宗，14世纪后融入湿婆教形成 syncretism。20世纪佛教复兴后，出现以“佛乘”（Buddhayāna）为代表的融合性上座部佛教，整合巴利语三藏、梵语大乘经典与古爪哇语文献，将本初佛（Adibuddha）视为佛教的“神”以契合印尼建国五原则。文中详细分析了现代印尼佛教的经典体系、仪式实践（如《吉祥功德海》的应用）与组织架构，揭示其在本土传统与外来佛教融合中的创新与适应。  
关键词：印度尼西亚佛教；佛乘；上座部；宗教融合；本初佛  
Title：The Buddhayana of Indonesia: A Syncretistic Form of Theravada  
Author：Heinz Bechert  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the development history of Buddhism in Indonesia, pointing out that it presents a unique syncretic characteristic. After Buddhism was introduced in the 5th century, it coexisted with Hinduism for a long time, and later gradually formed a Javanese-style Vajrayana. After the 14th century, it merged with Saivism to form syncretism. After the Buddhist revival in the 20th century, a syncretic Theravada Buddhism represented by "Buddhayāna" emerged, integrating the Pali Tipitaka, Sanskrit Mahayana classics and Old Javanese literature, and regarding Adibuddha as the "God" of Buddhism to conform to Indonesia's Pancasila. The paper detailedly analyzes the canonical system, ritual practices (such as the application of Sanghyang Kamahayanan) and organizational structure of modern Indonesian Buddhism, revealing its innovation and adaptation in the integration of local traditions and foreign Buddhism.  
Keywords：Indonesian Buddhism；Buddhayāna；Theravada；religious syncretism；Adibuddha

# 论文3.  《发趣论》与上座部阿毗达磨的发展
作者：L.S. 库辛斯（L.S. Cousins）  
摘要：本文聚焦上座部阿毗达磨核心典籍《发趣论》（Patthana），探讨其对意识过程理论的建构与发展。通过分析“有分识”（bhavanga-vijñana）的概念演变，指出其与唯识宗“阿赖耶识”的理论关联，且早于后者形成系统。文中详细阐释了《发趣论》中的24种因缘关系、意识过程的阶段划分（如五门心路、意门心路）及业力机制，论证该论典奠定了上座部阿毗达磨的因果体系，其理论并非佛音尊者的创新，而是源自早期经典阿毗达磨传统，为研究上座部哲学的核心框架提供了关键文本依据。  
关键词：《发趣论》；上座部；阿毗达磨；有分识；意识过程  
Title：The Patthana and the Development of the Theravadin Abhidhamma  
Author：L.S. Cousins  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Patthana, a core text of Theravada Abhidhamma, exploring its construction and development of the theory of consciousness process. By analyzing the conceptual evolution of "bhavanga-vijñana", it points out its theoretical connection with the Yogacara "alaya-vijñana" and forms a system earlier than the latter. The paper detailedly explains the 24 types of conditional relations, the stage division of consciousness process (such as five-door process and mind-door process) and karmic mechanism in the Patthana, demonstrating that this text laid the causal system of Theravada Abhidhamma. Its theory is not an innovation of Buddhaghosa, but originates from the early canonical Abhidhamma tradition, providing key textual basis for the study of the core framework of Theravada philosophy.  
Keywords：Patthana；Theravadin；Abhidhamma；bhavanga-vijñana；consciousness process

# 论文4.  一种新的上座部礼拜仪式
作者：理查德·冈布里奇（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文记录了由斯里兰卡僧人帕纳杜勒·阿利亚达摩（Panadure Ariyadhamma）创制的新型上座部礼拜仪式——“二十八佛礼拜”（Atavisi Buddha Pijā）。该仪式突破传统，融合巴利语与僧伽罗语经文，强调信众全程参与吟唱，僧人与信众平坐礼拜佛陀像，增强了情感共鸣与互动性。文中详细描述了仪式流程、核心经文（如《慈经》的应用）、情感化的讲道风格及社会反响，分析其流行原因在于贴近信众需求，平衡了传统教义与现代宗教实践，为研究上座部仪式的当代演变提供了鲜活案例。  
关键词：上座部；礼拜仪式；二十八佛礼拜；斯里兰卡佛教；宗教实践  
Title：A New Theravadin Liturgy  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper records a new Theravadin liturgy created by the Sri Lankan monk Panadure Ariyadhamma——"Atavisi Buddha Pijā" (Worship of the Twenty-eight Buddhas). Breaking through tradition, this liturgy integrates Pali and Sinhala scriptures, emphasizing the congregation's full participation in chanting. Monks sit on an equal footing with the congregation to worship the Buddha image, enhancing emotional resonance and interaction. The paper detailedly describes the ritual process, core scriptures (such as the application of the Metta Sutta), emotional preaching style and social response, analyzing that its popularity lies in meeting the needs of believers, balancing traditional doctrines and modern religious practices, and providing a vivid case for the study of the contemporary evolution of Theravada rituals.  
Keywords：Theravadin；liturgy；Atavisi Buddha Pijā；Sri Lankan Buddhism；religious practice

# 论文5.  幽灵词“dvihitika”与早期佛教文献中的饥荒描述
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文考证了巴利语文献中的“dvihitika”一词，指出其实为“duhitika”的讹误，属于“幽灵词”（ghost word）。通过比对《律藏》《相应部》等经典及注疏，还原其本义为“艰难生存”，与早期佛教文献中对饥荒的描述相关。文中分析了该词在不同手稿传统（僧伽罗语、缅甸语）中的异文，以及与梵语文献中“cancā”“svetasthi”等饥荒相关术语的对应关系，纠正了传统注疏的误释，为解读早期佛教文献中的社会生活场景与语言演变提供了语言学依据。  
关键词：巴利语；幽灵词；dvihitika；饥荒描述；文献校勘  
Title：The Ghost Word Dvihitika and the Descriptions of Famines in Early Buddhist Literature  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper researches the Pali word "dvihitika", pointing out that it is actually a corruption of "duhitika" and belongs to a "ghost word". By comparing classics such as the Vinaya-pitaka and Samyutta-nikaya as well as their commentaries, it restores its original meaning as "difficult to survive", which is related to the descriptions of famines in early Buddhist literature. The paper analyzes the variant readings of this word in different manuscript traditions (Sinhalese and Burmese), as well as its corresponding relations with famine-related terms such as "cancā" and "svetasthi" in Sanskrit literature, correcting the misinterpretation of traditional commentaries and providing linguistic basis for interpreting social life scenes and language evolution in early Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali；ghost word；dvihitika；famine descriptions；textual collation

# 论文6.  巴利语注疏中的“keci”（某些）
作者：L.B. 霍纳（L.B. Horner）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语注疏中频繁出现的“keci”（意为“某些”“有人”）一词，探讨其在文献中的功能与意义。通过梳理《相应部注》《中部注》等注疏文献，发现“keci”常用于引述不同观点、异文或未署名学者的阐释，反映了注疏传统中的学术争议与多元传承。文中分析了“keci”所引观点的类型（如文本异读、教义阐释）、注疏家对这些观点的态度（认同、批判或存疑），揭示其为研究巴利语注疏的编纂过程、学术传承及部派差异提供了独特视角。  
关键词：巴利语注疏；keci；文献阐释；学术传承；部派佛教  
Title：Keci "Some" in the Pali Commentaries  
Author：L.B. Horner  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the frequently occurring Pali word "keci" (meaning "some" or "someone") in Pali commentaries, exploring its function and significance in the literature. By sorting out commentarial literature such as the Samyutta-nikaya-atthakatha and Majjhima-nikaya-atthakatha, it is found that "keci" is often used to quote different views, variant readings or interpretations of unsigned scholars, reflecting academic disputes and diverse inheritance in the commentarial tradition. The paper analyzes the types of views quoted by "keci" (such as textual variants and doctrinal interpretations) and the attitudes of commentators towards these views (approval, criticism or doubt), revealing that it provides a unique perspective for the study of the compilation process, academic inheritance and sectarian differences of Pali commentaries.  
Keywords：Pali commentaries；keci；textual interpretation；academic inheritance；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文7.  耆那教的“祖师本性”与佛教的“菩萨道”
作者：帕德马纳布·S. 贾尼（Padmanabh S. Jaini）  
摘要：本文对比耆那教“祖师”（Tirthankara）与佛教“佛陀”的概念共性，重点分析两者修行路径的差异。耆那教的“祖师本性”（Tirthankara-prakrti）强调通过苦行与破除业障获得全知（kevalajnana），无需漫长修行；而佛教菩萨道需经多劫修行六度，以大悲心救度众生。文中探讨了两者在预言传承、觉悟过程、教义核心（如非我、业力）的异同，指出耆那教无类似菩萨道的修行体系，其祖师与佛教阿罗汉、佛陀的本质区别，为比较研究印度两大解脱宗教提供了核心维度。  
关键词：耆那教；祖师；菩萨道；佛教；比较宗教  
Title：Tirthankara-Prakrti and the Bodhisattva Path  
Author：Padmanabh S. Jaini  
Abstract：This paper compares the conceptual commonalities between the Jaina "Tirthankara" and the Buddhist "Buddha", focusing on analyzing the differences between their practice paths. The Jaina "Tirthankara-prakrti" emphasizes achieving omniscience (kevalajnana) through asceticism and eliminating karmic obstacles, without the need for long-term practice; while the Buddhist Bodhisattva path requires practicing the six paramitas through countless kalpas to save all beings with great compassion. The paper explores the similarities and differences between the two in prophecy inheritance, enlightenment process and core doctrines (such as anatta and karma), pointing out that Jainism has no practice system similar to the Bodhisattva path, and the essential differences between its Tirthankaras and Buddhist arhats and Buddhas, providing a core dimension for the comparative study of India's two major liberation religions.  
Keywords：Jainism；Tirthankara；Bodhisattva path；Buddhism；comparative religion

# 论文8.  法显与斯里兰卡的佛教文献
作者：J.W. 德容（J.W. de Jong）  
摘要：本文考证了法显在斯里兰卡获取的佛教文献，纠正了传统认为其带回梵语文献的观点。通过分析法显翻译的《杂藏经》及带回的《摩诃僧祇律》《长阿含经》《杂阿含经》等文献的语言特征，指出这些文献可能为巴利语或混合语，而非纯粹梵语。文中梳理了这些文献的翻译与传承情况，探讨了法显时期斯里兰卡佛教的部派背景（如大众部、说一切有部的影响），为研究5世纪中斯佛教文化交流及文献传播提供了关键考证。  
关键词：法显；斯里兰卡；佛教文献；文献传播；中斯交流  
Title：Fa-hsien and Buddhist Texts in Ceylon  
Author：J.W. de Jong  
Abstract：This paper researches the Buddhist texts obtained by Fa-hsien in Ceylon, correcting the traditional view that he brought back Sanskrit texts. By analyzing the linguistic characteristics of the Tsa-tsang ching translated by Fa-hsien and the texts he brought back such as the Mahisasakavinaya, Dirghagama and Samyuktagama, it is pointed out that these texts may be in Pali or mixed languages, not pure Sanskrit. The paper sorts out the translation and inheritance of these texts, explores the sectarian background of Buddhism in Ceylon during Fa-hsien's period (such as the influence of Mahasanghika and Sarvastivada), providing key research for the study of Sino-Sri Lankan Buddhist cultural exchanges and textual transmission in the 5th century.  
Keywords：Fa-hsien；Ceylon；Buddhist texts；textual transmission；Sino-Sri Lankan exchange

# 论文9.  早期佛教的历史哲学
作者：戴维·J. 卡卢帕哈纳（David J. Kalupahana）  
摘要：本文探讨早期佛教的历史哲学特征，反驳印度文明缺乏历史书写的传统观点。通过分析《长部》《中部》等经典中的历史叙事（如佛陀生平、部派分裂），指出早期佛教虽不认同思辨性历史哲学（如绝对精神展开），但承认历史经验的价值，以“缘起”为核心解释历史现象的因果关联。文中对比西方历史哲学（思辨派与批判派），强调早期佛教的历史观兼具批判性与实用性，既拒绝形而上学预设，又重视历史知识对修行的指导意义，为理解佛教的时间观与历史意识提供了哲学维度。  
关键词：早期佛教；历史哲学；缘起；历史意识；因果观  
Title：The Philosophy of History in Early Buddhism  
Author：David J. Kalupahana  
Abstract：This paper explores the characteristics of the philosophy of history in early Buddhism, refuting the traditional view that Indian civilization lacks historical writing. By analyzing historical narratives (such as the Buddha's life and sectarian schism) in classics such as the Digha-nikaya and Majjhima-nikaya, it is pointed out that although early Buddhism does not recognize speculative philosophy of history (such as the unfolding of absolute spirit), it acknowledges the value of historical experience and explains the causal connection of historical phenomena with "pratityasamutpada" as the core. Comparing with Western philosophy of history (speculative and critical schools), the paper emphasizes that the historical view of early Buddhism is both critical and practical, rejecting metaphysical presuppositions while attaching importance to the guiding significance of historical knowledge for practice, providing a philosophical dimension for understanding Buddhism's view of time and historical consciousness.  
Keywords：early Buddhism；philosophy of history；pratityasamutpada；historical consciousness；causal view

# 论文10. 《相应部·尊敬经》及其大乘发展
作者：埃蒂安·拉莫特（Etienne Lamotte）  
摘要：本文解读《相应部·尊敬经》（Garava-sutta），分析其在早期上座部与大乘佛教中的不同阐释。早期上座部将经中佛陀“以法为师”解释为对佛法的尊崇，强调五净蕴（戒、定、慧、解脱、解脱知见）的修行；大乘则将“法”阐释为诸法实相（dharmata），主张“无生无灭”的空性思想，并扩展出本初佛、金刚座等宇宙论叙事。文中对比巴利语原典与汉译《杂阿含经》对应文本，梳理该经在大乘论典（如《大智度论》）中的发展，揭示上座部与大乘对“法”的核心分歧，为研究佛教教义的演变提供了关键文本案例。  
关键词：《尊敬经》；相应部；大乘佛教；诸法实相；教义演变  
Title：The Garava-sutta of the Samyutta-nikaya and Its Mahayanist Developments  
Author：Etienne Lamotte  
Abstract：This paper interprets the Garava-sutta of the Samyutta-nikaya, analyzing its different interpretations in early Theravada and Mahayana Buddhism. Early Theravada explained the Buddha's "taking the Dharma as teacher" in the sutta as respect for the Dharma, emphasizing the practice of the five pure skandhas (sila, samadhi, prajna, vimukti, vimuktijnanadarsana); Mahayana interpreted the "Dharma" as dharmata (true nature of all phenomena), advocating the sunyata thought of "anutpada and aniruddha", and expanding cosmic narratives such as Adibuddha and Vajrasana. Comparing the Pali original with the corresponding text in the Chinese translation of the Samyuktagama, the paper sorts out the development of this sutta in Mahayana treatises (such as the Mahaprajnaparamitopadesa), revealing the core divergence between Theravada and Mahayana on the "Dharma", providing a key textual case for the study of the evolution of Buddhist doctrines.  
Keywords：Garava-sutta；Samyutta-nikaya；Mahayana Buddhism；dharmata；doctrinal evolution

# 论文11.  佛教中的天神与至上神
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文通过解读《中部· Kannakatthala经》与《 Sangarava经》，探讨早期佛教对“天神”（deva）与“至上神”（adhideva）的认知。指出佛陀承认天神的存在，但认为其仍受业力与轮回束缚，并非永恒；“至上神”并非指超越的造物主，而是指修行成就卓越的圣者（如佛陀、阿罗汉）。文中纠正了对经文中“adhideva”的误译，分析其与“atideva”（殊胜天神）的语义关联，揭示早期佛教的神观既不否定世俗神祇，又强调圣者的超越性，为理解佛教的无神论倾向与宗教包容性提供了文献依据。  
关键词：佛教；天神；至上神；业力；轮回  
Title：Devas and Adhidevas in Buddhism  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper explores the early Buddhist understanding of "deva" (god) and "adhideva" (supreme god) by interpreting the Kannakatthala-sutta and Sangarava-sutta of the Majjhima-nikaya. It points out that the Buddha acknowledged the existence of devas, but believed that they are still bound by karma and samsara and not eternal; "adhideva" does not refer to a transcendent creator, but to saints with outstanding practice achievements (such as the Buddha and arhats). The paper corrects the mistranslation of "adhideva" in the scriptures, analyzes its semantic connection with "atideva" (pre-eminent deva), revealing that the early Buddhist view of gods neither denies secular deities nor emphasizes the transcendence of saints, providing textual basis for understanding Buddhism's atheistic tendency and religious inclusiveness.  
Keywords：Buddhism；devas；adhidevas；karma；samsara

# 论文12.  巴利语文学中的幽默
作者：瓦尔波拉·拉胡拉（Walpola Rahula）  
摘要：本文发掘巴利语文学中的幽默元素，反驳佛教文献严肃枯燥的刻板印象。通过列举《法句经》《中部》《注疏》等文献中的案例——包括佛陀对质疑者的机智回应、对婆罗门偏见的讽刺、对修行误区的幽默警示等，展现早期佛教的幽默风格兼具智慧性与教化性。文中分析幽默在巴利语文学中的功能：化解冲突、阐释教义、批判谬误，指出佛教并不排斥愉悦与幽默感，而是将其作为弘法与修行的辅助手段，为全面理解巴利语文学的多元性与佛教的实践智慧提供了新视角。  
关键词：巴利语文学；幽默；佛教教义；弘法方式；修行智慧  
Title：Humour in Pali Literature  
Author：Walpola Rahula  
Abstract：This paper explores the humorous elements in Pali literature, refuting the stereotype that Buddhist literature is serious and boring. By citing examples from literature such as the Dhammapada, Majjhima-nikaya and commentaries—including the Buddha's witty responses to doubters, satire on Brahman prejudices, and humorous warnings against practice misunderstandings—it shows that the humorous style of early Buddhism is both intelligent and educational. The paper analyzes the functions of humour in Pali literature: resolving conflicts, explaining doctrines, and criticizing fallacies, pointing out that Buddhism does not exclude joy and humour, but uses them as auxiliary means for propagating the Dharma and practice, providing a new perspective for a comprehensive understanding of the diversity of Pali literature and the practical wisdom of Buddhism.  
Keywords：Pali literature；humour；Buddhist doctrines；Dharma propagation；practical wisdom

# 论文13.  关于巴利语“gotrabhu”的补充说明

Title：A Further Note on Pali Gotrabhu  
Author：D.S. Seyfort Ruegg

# 论文15.  后期巴利语文学的若干问题

Title：Some Problems of the Later Pali Literature  
Author：A.K. Warder  
Abstract：This paper explores the core issues of later Pali literature (about the 5th-12th centuries), including author dating, textual authenticity and stylistic disputes. Focusing on the attribution of works by the scholar Dhammapala, it points out that his existing more than 30 commentaries and sub-commentaries may be from two authors with the same name, not one person; analyzing the dating and "Sanskritization" disputes of literary works such as the Mahabodhivamsa and Telakatahagatha, refuting the prejudice that later Pali literature is "unorthodox", emphasizing that its vocabulary innovation and use of complex sentences are natural results of literary development.
