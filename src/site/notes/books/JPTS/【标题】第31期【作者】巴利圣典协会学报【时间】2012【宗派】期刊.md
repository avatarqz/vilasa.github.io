# 论文1. 盗窃法：上座部律典与法律注释中的规定
作者：佩特拉·基弗-普尔茨（Petra Kieffer-Pülz）  
摘要：本文聚焦上座部律典中属于波罗夷第二条的盗窃法，探究其文本构成与发展脉络。首先分析律典中盗窃规则的 chronological 层级，包括根本规定、补充条款及相关解释；其次考察《善见律毗婆沙》《岛史》等注释文献对盗窃类型的分类，这类分类整合了早期注释的核心信息；最后探讨早期注释家针对盗窃行为形成的罪业吸收机制。研究指出，盗窃法的规则结构包含序故事、根本规定、词语解析、案例辨析等模块，且存在特殊的补充章节；注释文献将盗窃归纳为二十五种类型，涵盖行为方式、实施手段等多个维度；罪业吸收机制则区分先行行为与同时行为，明确不同阶段罪业的归责逻辑。  
关键词：盗窃法；上座部律典；注释文献；罪业吸收；波罗夷  
Title：The Law of Theft: Regulations in the Theravāda Vinaya and the Law Commentaries  
Author：Petra Kieffer-Pülz  
Abstract：This article focuses on the law of theft, which belongs to the second Pārajika rule in the Theravāda Vinaya, exploring its textual composition and developmental context. Firstly, it analyzes the chronological layers of the theft rules in the Vinaya, including the fundamental provisions, supplementary clauses, and related explanations. Secondly, it investigates the classification of theft types in commentarial literature such as the Samantapāsādikā and the Kaṅkhāvitaraṇī, which integrate core information from early commentaries. Finally, it discusses the mechanism of offence absorption developed by early Aṭṭhakathākāras for theft. The study points out that the structural framework of the theft law includes introductory stories, fundamental regulations, word analysis, casuistry, and other modules, with special supplementary sections. The commentarial literature classifies theft into twenty-five types, covering multiple dimensions such as modes of conduct and means of implementation. The offence absorption mechanism distinguishes between preceding actions and simultaneous actions, clarifying the imputation logic of offences at different stages.  
Keywords：Law of Theft；Theravāda Vinaya；commentarial literature；offence absorption；Pārajika

# 论文2. 从觉音到金刚智的巴利语语法与语法学家：综述
作者：奥勒·霍尔滕·平德（O.H. Pind）  
摘要：本文系统梳理自觉音以来至金刚智时期的巴利语语法传统与语法学家成就。基于现存语法文献及相关注释，考察《迦旃延语法》《灯明语法》等核心著作的内容、传承与影响，分析语法学家对巴利语语音、形态、句法的描述方法。研究指出，早期语法文献多以梵语语法为参照，同时结合巴利语圣典的语言实际；语法学家通过修订旧规则、补充新案例，逐步完善对巴利语特有语言现象的阐释；觉音的注释文献中已包含初步的语法分析，后续语法学家在此基础上构建了系统的巴利语语法体系，且不同时期的语法著作呈现出明显的传承与革新关系。  
关键词：巴利语语法；语法学家；觉音；金刚智；文献传承  
Title：Pāli Grammar and Grammarians from Buddhaghosa to Vajirabuddhi: A Survey  
Author：O.H. Pind  
Abstract：This article systematically sorts out the Pāli grammatical tradition and the achievements of grammarians from the time of Buddhaghosa to Vajirabuddhi. Based on existing grammatical texts and related commentaries, it examines the content, transmission, and influence of core works such as the Kaccāyana Vyākaraṇa and the Mukhamattadīpanī, and analyzes the descriptive methods of grammarians on Pāli phonology, morphology, and syntax. The study points out that early grammatical literature mostly took Sanskrit grammar as a reference while combining the linguistic reality of Pāli canonical texts. Grammarians gradually improved the interpretation of unique linguistic phenomena in Pāli by revising old rules and supplementing new cases. Buddhaghosa's commentarial literature already contained preliminary grammatical analyses, and subsequent grammarians built a systematic Pāli grammatical system on this basis, with obvious inheritance and innovation relationships among grammatical works of different periods.  
Keywords：Pāli grammar；grammarians；Buddhaghosa；Vajirabuddhi；textual transmission

# 论文3. 克恩与印度佛教研究：兼论锡兰达摩鲁支派的推测性笔记
作者：乔纳森·A. 希尔克（Jonathan A. Silk）  
摘要：本文探讨亨德里克·克恩在印度佛教研究领域的贡献，包括其对《妙法莲华经》的校勘与翻译、巴利语词汇研究、阿育王铭文解读等核心成果，分析其研究方法与学术影响。同时，结合锡兰佛教史文献，对达摩鲁支派的历史地位、教义特征及与阿毗耶祇利寺的关联进行推测性考察。研究指出，克恩的著作兼具文献校勘的严谨性与跨文本比较的开阔性，为西方佛教研究奠定重要基础；达摩鲁支派作为与大寺派对立的宗派，其名称可能隐含特殊的教义象征意义，且与印度佛教中的某些思想流派存在潜在关联。  
关键词：亨德里克·克恩；印度佛教研究；达摩鲁支派；锡兰佛教；学术史  
Title：Kern and the Study of Indian Buddhism: With a Speculative Note on the Ceylonese Dhammarucikas  
Author：Jonathan A. Silk  
Abstract：This article explores Hendrik Kern's contributions to the study of Indian Buddhism, including his core achievements such as the collation and translation of the Saddharmapuṇḍarīka-sūtra, research on Pāli vocabulary, and interpretation of Aśokan inscriptions, analyzing his research methods and academic influence. Meanwhile, combined with Ceylonese Buddhist historical documents, it conducts a speculative investigation on the historical status, doctrinal characteristics, and connection with the Abhayagiri Vihāra of the Dhammarucika sect. The study points out that Kern's works combine the rigor of textual collation with the breadth of cross-textual comparison, laying an important foundation for Western Buddhist studies. As a sect opposed to the Mahāvihāra sect, the name of the Dhammarucika sect may imply special doctrinal symbolic significance and have potential connections with certain ideological schools in Indian Buddhism.  
Keywords：Hendrik Kern；Indian Buddhist Studies；Dhammarucika sect；Ceylonese Buddhism；academic history

# 论文4. 雷迪尊者致里斯·戴维斯夫人的两封信（编辑版）
作者：埃里克·布劳恩（Eric Braun）、威廉·普鲁伊特（William Pruitt）（编辑）  
摘要：本文收录并编辑了缅甸著名佛教学者雷迪尊者致巴利圣典协会学者里斯·戴维斯夫人的两封书信，书信分别撰写于1914年和1917年，核心回应了关于阿毗达磨的多个技术问题。内容涵盖记忆的形成机制、意识与对象的关系、意志自由与业力的关联、感官与对象接触的因缘条件等，展现了雷迪尊者对阿毗达磨因果关系、心识活动等核心理论的深刻见解。书信为研究雷迪尊者的学术思想、近代西方与缅甸佛教研究的交流提供了珍贵一手资料。  
关键词：雷迪尊者；里斯·戴维斯夫人；阿毗达磨；书信；佛教研究交流  
Title：Two Letters from Ledi Sayadaw to Mrs Rhys Davids  
Author：Edited by Eric Braun and William Pruitt  
Abstract：This article collects and edits two letters from the renowned Burmese Buddhist scholar Ledi Sayadaw to Mrs Rhys Davids, a scholar of the Pali Text Society. The letters, written in 1914 and 1917 respectively, mainly respond to several technical questions about Abhidhamma. The content covers the formation mechanism of memory, the relationship between consciousness and objects, the connection between free will and karma, and the conditional factors of contact between senses and objects, showing Ledi Sayadaw's profound insights into core Abhidhamma theories such as causal relationships and mental activities. The letters provide precious first-hand materials for studying Ledi Sayadaw's academic thoughts and the exchange between modern Western and Burmese Buddhist studies.  
Keywords：Ledi Sayadaw；Mrs Rhys Davids；Abhidhamma；letters；exchange in Buddhist studies

# 论文5. 《巴利圣典协会学报》第9-31卷索引
作者：（无单独署名，为期刊编辑整理）  
摘要：本文编制了《巴利圣典协会学报》第9卷（1981年）至第31卷（2011年）的论文索引，按作者姓氏排序，收录了各卷发表的学术论文、译文、文献校勘、书评等各类成果。索引标注了每篇文章的标题、作者、发表卷期与页码，涵盖巴利语语法、圣典文献、注释研究、佛教哲学、历史与文化等多个研究领域，为研究者检索该期刊相关文献提供了系统、便捷的工具，助力巴利语研究与佛教研究的学术传承与发展。  
关键词：《巴利圣典协会学报》；索引；第9-31卷；文献检索；佛教研究  
Title：An Index to JPTS, Volumes IX–XXXI  
Author：(No separate author, compiled by journal editors)  
Abstract：This article compiles an index of papers published in the Journal of the Pali Text Society from Volume IX (1981) to Volume XXXI (2011). Arranged by authors' surnames, it includes various achievements such as academic papers, translations, textual collations, and book reviews published in each volume. The index marks the title, author, publication volume, issue, and page number of each article, covering multiple research fields including Pali grammar, canonical literature, commentary studies, Buddhist philosophy, history, and culture. It provides a systematic and convenient tool for researchers to retrieve relevant literature of the journal, facilitating the academic inheritance and development of Pali studies and Buddhist studies.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；index；Volumes IX–XXXI；literature retrieval；Buddhist Studies
