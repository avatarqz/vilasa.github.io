# 【标题】第20期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1994【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 巴利《阿波陀那集》研究
作者：莎莉·梅利克·卡特勒（Sally Mellick Cutler）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利圣典中的《阿波陀那集》（Apadāna），该集由约8000首偈颂构成，包含592则故事，分为佛陀阿波陀那、独觉佛阿波陀那、长老阿波陀那、长老尼阿波陀那四部分，核心主题为业力法则的效力与解脱道追求。研究梳理了文本的结构特征、文体渊源与教义功能，指出其与本生故事、阿毗达磨文献的关联，同时探讨了其在南传佛教经典中的归属争议。通过分析长老僧尼故事中“过去业行-现世成就”的叙事模式，以及佛陀、独觉佛相关文本的独特性，揭示了《阿波陀那集》作为说教文学的核心价值——以具体故事阐释善恶业报与修行目标，既服务于信众教化，也反映了早期佛教向大乘思想过渡的部分特征。  
关键词：阿波陀那集；巴利圣典；业力；南传佛教；说教文学  
Title：The Pali Apadana Collection  
Author：Sally Mellick Cutler  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Apadāna, a collection in the Pali Canon consisting of approximately 8,000 verses and 592 stories, divided into four parts: Buddhapadāna, Paccekabuddhapadāna, Therapadāna, and Theri-apadāna. Its core themes are the efficacy of the law of karma and the pursuit of the path to liberation. The study sorts out the text's structural characteristics, literary origins, and doctrinal functions, points out its connections with Jataka tales and Abhidhamma literature, and discusses the controversy over its attribution in Theravada Buddhist scriptures. By analyzing the narrative pattern of "past karmic actions - present achievements" in the stories of elder monks and nuns, as well as the uniqueness of texts related to the Buddha and Paccekabuddhas, it reveals the core value of the Apadāna as didactic literature—to illustrate good and evil karma and practice goals through specific stories, serving both the edification of believers and reflecting some characteristics of the transition from early Buddhism to Mahayana thought.  
Keywords：Apadana Collection；Pali Canon；karma；Theravada Buddhism；didactic literature

# 论文2. 12世纪巴利语词典《阿毗达磨词汇灯》所反映的南亚植物群
作者：吉纳达萨·利耶纳拉特纳（Jinadasa Liyanaratne）  
摘要：本文以12世纪斯里兰卡僧人摩伽拉那编纂的巴利语词典《阿毗达磨词汇灯》（Abhidhanappadipika）为核心，聚焦其第二部分第五章节“森林篇”（aranavagga）中536-604颂记载的植物词汇。研究结合梵语《阿玛拉科莎》词典、阿育吠陀经典及僧伽罗语注释，对190余种南亚植物的巴利语名称、梵语对应词、僧伽罗语俗称、拉丁学名及用途进行了考证。通过分析植物命名的词源逻辑（形态、药性、栖息地等）与语言学特征，揭示了古代南亚社会对植物的分类认知、药用价值利用及文化象征意义，为佛教文献与南亚自然科学史的交叉研究提供了详实的文本依据。  
关键词：阿毗达磨词汇灯；巴利语词典；南亚植物；阿育吠陀；佛教文献  
Title：South Asian flora as reflected in the twelfth-century Pali lexicon Abhidhanappadipika  
Author：Jinadasa Liyanaratne  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the plant vocabulary recorded in verses 536-604 of the "aranavagga" (Forest Section) in the second part of the 12th-century Pali lexicon Abhidhanappadipika, compiled by the Sri Lankan monk Moggallana. Combining the Sanskrit Amarakosa lexicon, Ayurvedic classics, and Sinhala commentaries, the study conducts textual research on the Pali names, Sanskrit equivalents, Sinhala common names, Latin scientific names, and uses of more than 190 South Asian plants. By analyzing the etymological logic (morphology, medicinal properties, habitat, etc.) and linguistic characteristics of plant naming, it reveals the classification cognition, medicinal value utilization, and cultural symbolic significance of plants in ancient South Asian society, providing detailed textual evidence for the interdisciplinary research of Buddhist literature and the history of South Asian natural sciences.  
Keywords：Abhidhanappadipika；Pali lexicon；South Asian flora；Ayurveda；Buddhist literature

# 论文3. kamaloka：古爪哇语中罕见的巴利语借词？
作者：马克斯·尼霍姆（Max Nihom）  
摘要：本文针对古爪哇语中的“kamaloka”一词展开考证，探讨其是否为罕见的巴利语借词。通过比对巴利语经典（如《清净道论》）、梵语文献及古爪哇语碑铭、文学文本，作者分析了该词的语义演变与语言关联。研究指出，巴利语中“kama”（爱欲）与“loka”（世界）构成的复合词“kamaloka”（欲界），在古爪哇语文本中出现频次极低，且语义与巴利语原义存在细微差异，可能是通过东南亚佛教传播过程中的语言接触形成的借词。文章还讨论了巴利语借词在古爪哇语中的传播路径、本土化特征，为南传佛教文化圈的语言交流史研究提供了个案支撑。  
关键词：kamaloka；巴利语借词；古爪哇语；语言交流；南传佛教  
Title：kamaloka: A rare Pali loan word in Old Javanese?  
Author：Max Nihom  
Abstract：This paper conducts textual research on the Old Javanese word "kamaloka" to explore whether it is a rare Pali loanword. By comparing Pali classics (such as the Visuddhimagga), Sanskrit literature, and Old Javanese inscriptions and literary texts, the author analyzes the semantic evolution and linguistic connections of the word. The study points out that the compound word "kamaloka" (the sensual world) formed by "kama" (sensuality) and "loka" (world) in Pali appears extremely infrequently in Old Javanese texts, and its meaning has slight differences from the original Pali meaning, which may be a loanword formed through language contact during the spread of Buddhism in Southeast Asia. The paper also discusses the transmission path and localization characteristics of Pali loanwords in Old Javanese, providing a case study for the research on the linguistic communication history of the Theravada Buddhist cultural circle.  
Keywords：kamaloka；Pali loanword；Old Javanese；linguistic communication；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文4. 《解脱道论》与阿耨楼陀派：基于《分别善恶业报经》的色蕴研究
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文聚焦南传佛教文献《解脱道论》（Vimuttimagga）与阿耨楼陀派（Abhayagiri）的思想关联，以《分别善恶业报经》（Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya）中关于“色蕴”的论述为核心展开研究。通过比对《解脱道论》与《分别善恶业报经》的平行文本，分析阿耨楼陀派对色蕴的定义、分类及修行观，指出该派在色蕴阐释上既延续了早期佛教的核心教义，又形成了独特的理论侧重。研究还梳理了阿耨楼陀派与上座部正统派（大寺派）的教义分歧，揭示了《解脱道论》在南传佛教哲学体系中的特殊地位，为理解南传佛教内部的思想流派差异提供了关键文本线索。  
关键词：解脱道论；阿耨楼陀派；分别善恶业报经；色蕴；南传佛教哲学  
Title：Vimuttimagga and Abhayagiri: the form-aggregate according to the Samskrtasamskrtaviniscaya  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the ideological connection between the Theravada Buddhist text Vimuttimagga and the Abhayagiri sect, centering on the discussion of the "form-aggregate" (rupa-khandha) in the Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya. By comparing parallel texts of the Vimuttimagga and the Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya, it analyzes the Abhayagiri sect's definition, classification, and practice view of the form-aggregate, pointing out that the sect not only inherited the core doctrines of early Buddhism in interpreting the form-aggregate but also formed unique theoretical emphases. The study also sorts out the doctrinal differences between the Abhayagiri sect and the Theravada orthodox sect (Mahavihara), reveals the special status of the Vimuttimagga in the Theravada Buddhist philosophical system, and provides key textual clues for understanding the ideological school differences within Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Vimuttimagga；Abhayagiri；Samskrtasamskrta-viniscaya；form-aggregate；Theravada Buddhist philosophy

# 论文5. 巴利语词典编纂研究（第十二部分）
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文是“巴利语词典编纂研究”系列的第十二部分，聚焦巴利语词汇的语义辨析、词源考证及词典编纂实践中的核心问题。研究针对巴利语中多义词汇、同源词汇、外来借词等三类词汇展开细致分析，结合巴利圣典原文、注释文献及平行梵语文本，厘清了多个易混淆词汇的核心含义与使用语境。作者还探讨了巴利语词典编纂的基本原则，包括词义优先级排序、引文示例选择、跨语言对应词标注等，针对此前词典中的释义偏差进行了修正补充，为巴利语词典的完善与巴利语语言学研究提供了学术支撑。  
关键词：巴利语；词典编纂；语义辨析；词源考证；语言学研究  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies XII  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper is the twelfth part of the "Pali Lexicographical Studies" series, focusing on the semantic discrimination, etymological research, and core issues in lexicographical practice of Pali vocabulary. The study conducts detailed analysis on three types of Pali vocabulary: polysemous words, cognate words, and loanwords, clarifying the core meanings and usage contexts of several easily confused words by combining Pali canonical texts, commentarial literature, and parallel Sanskrit texts. The author also discusses the basic principles of Pali lexicography, including the priority ordering of word meanings, the selection of citation examples, and the annotation of cross-linguistic equivalents, revising and supplementing the interpretation deviations in previous dictionaries, and providing academic support for the improvement of Pali dictionaries and Pali linguistic research.  
Keywords：Pali；Lexicography；semantic discrimination；etymological research；linguistic studies
