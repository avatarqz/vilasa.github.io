# 【标题】第27期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】2002【宗派】期刊

# 论文1.  斯里兰卡寺庙手稿收藏笔记
作者：安妮·M·布莱克本（Anne M. Blackburn）  
摘要：本文对斯里兰卡康提地区和库鲁内格勒地区六座佛教寺庙的手稿收藏进行了初步介绍，涉及的寺庙包括斯里达拉拉达马拉加瓦寺、马达维拉拉贾马哈维哈拉寺等。研究过程中，作者从寺庙住持的手札中复刻了五座寺庙的手稿清单，对另一座寺庙则进行了为期两天的实地调查。文章指出了现有资料的局限性，强调了寺庙特定手稿收藏对研究南亚和东南亚佛教思想与实践演变的重要价值，分析了手稿收藏的地域、时间和机构差异性所反映的文本实践及佛教实践的稳定性与变化，还探讨了未来值得研究的多个方向。  
关键词：斯里兰卡；佛教寺庙；手稿收藏；佛教研究；文本实践  
Title：Notes on Sri Lankan Temple Manuscript Collections  
Author：Anne M. Blackburn  
Abstract：This paper provides a preliminary account of the manuscripts held in six Sri Lankan Buddhist temples located in the island's Kandyan and Kurunagala regions, including the Sri Dalad Maligava, Madavela Rajamahaviharaya, etc. During the research, the author reproduced the manuscript lists of five temples from the handlists held by the temple's monastic incumbent and conducted a two-day survey of another temple. The paper points out the limitations of the existing materials, emphasizes the important value of temple-specific manuscript collections for studying the evolution of Buddhist thought and practice in South and Southeast Asia, analyzes the stability and changes in textual practice and Buddhist practice reflected by the regional, temporal, and institutional variability of manuscript collections, and discusses several promising areas for future research.  
Keywords：Sri Lanka；Buddhist temples；manuscript collections；Buddhist studies；textual practice

# 论文2.  《Linatthapakasini》与《Saratthamanjusa》：四部尼柯耶的古注释与注释书
作者：普里莫兹·佩岑科（Primoz Pecenko）  
摘要：本文探讨了巴利文献中四部尼柯耶注释的相关问题，指出这些注释在文献中要么被记载为两套完整的注释集（古注释集《Linatthapakasini》和后期注释集《Saratthamanjusa》），要么被记载为一套混合注释集。文章基于巴利文献学资料、手稿及印刷版本展开研究，重点分析了最近发现的缅甸古《Anguttara》注释手稿，通过对比两套注释集中的平行章节，论证了两套完整注释集存在的可能性，还讨论了各类文献资料中关于这些注释的记载差异及原因。  
关键词：巴利文献；四部尼柯耶；注释；Linatthapakasini；Saratthamanjusa  
Title：Linathapakasini and Saratthamaijusa: The Puranatikas and the Tikas on the Four Nikayas  
Author：Primoz Pecenko  
Abstract：This paper explores the issues related to the tikas on the first four nikayas in Pali bibliographical sources. These tikas are either recorded as two more or less complete different sets (the old set of four puranatikas named Linatthapakasini and the later set of four tikas named Saratthamanjusa) or as a single mixed set. Based on Pali bibliographical sources, manuscripts and printed editions, the paper focuses on analyzing a recently discovered Burmese manuscript of the old Anguttara-tika, demonstrates the possibility of the existence of two complete sets of nikaya-tikas through comparing parallel chapters from both sets, and discusses the differences and reasons for the records of these tikas in various literature sources.  
Keywords：Pali literature；Four Nikayas；tikas；Linatthapakasini；Saratthamanjusa

# 论文3.  《Campeyya本生故事》研究（含《Sankhapala本生故事》文本评述）
作者：托马斯·奥伯利斯（Thomas Oberlies）  
摘要：本文针对《Campeyya本生故事》的文本批判存在的历史问题展开研究。该故事虽以44首偈颂流传，却被归入《Visati-nipata》，暗示其原始版本约含20首偈颂，这表明其在传承过程中经过了修订和扩展。文章结合《Mahavastu》版本及与之有共通偈颂的《Sankhapala本生故事》，追溯该那伽本生故事的文本历史，分析了诗节的韵律特征、添加内容及与其他本生故事的关联，还对文本中的疑难问题进行了探讨。  
关键词：Campeyya本生故事；Sankhapala本生故事；文本历史；巴利本生故事；韵律分析  
Title：A Study of the Campeyya Jataka, Including Remarks on the Text of the Sankhapala Jataka  
Author：Thomas Oberlies  
Abstract：This paper addresses the obvious historical problem in the textual criticism of the Campeyya Jataka. Although it has been transmitted in forty-four gaths, it is found in the Visati-nipata, indicating that the original version of the Jataka comprised about twenty gaths, which shows that the Jataka has been revised and extended during its transmission. Combining the Mahavastu version and the Sankhapala Jataka which has various gaths in common with the Campeyya Jataka, the paper traces the textual history of this naga Jataka, analyzes the metrical characteristics, added contents of the gaths and their connections with other Jatakas, and discusses the difficult issues in the text.  
Keywords：Campeyya Jataka；Sankhapala Jataka；textual history；Pali Jatakas；metrical analysis

# 论文4.  缅甸手稿的题跋
作者：海因茨·布劳恩（Heinz Braun）  
摘要：本文指出，在查阅巴利文佛教经典文本版本及上座部佛教国家手稿目录时，缅甸手稿的结尾题跋通常未被提及。基于德国图书馆所藏缅甸手稿目录的详细描述，作者试图对部分程式化的结尾题跋进行梳理，分析了题跋的形式和内容，包括手稿的完成日期、抄写者的表述、宗教功德的表达及相关祈愿等，为理解缅甸手稿题跋的规则和抄写者的创作提供了参考。  
关键词：缅甸手稿；题跋；巴利文手稿；文本结尾；宗教功德  
Title：The Colophons of Burmese Manuscripts  
Author：Heinz Braun  
Abstract：This paper points out that when looking through editions of the texts of Buddhist scriptures in Pali and catalogues of manuscripts from Theravada Buddhist countries, the final remarks (colophons) in Burmese manuscripts are usually not mentioned. Based on the detailed descriptions in the catalogues of Burmese manuscripts in German libraries, the author attempts to bring some order into the series of partly formulaic final remarks, analyzes the form and content of the colophons, including the completion date of the manuscript, the scribe's expressions, the expression of religious merit and related aspirations, providing a reference for understanding the rules of Burmese manuscript colophons and the scribes' creation.  
Keywords：Burmese manuscripts；colophons；Pali manuscripts；text endings；religious merit

# 论文5.  《Syamaratthassa Tepitakatthakatha》新版本评述
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文介绍了1992年在泰国曼谷出版的泰文字体巴利文注释本，该版本为纪念诗丽吉王后五十大寿而刊行，共48卷。新版本遵循严格的编辑原则，基本保留原版文本，仅修正明显的排印错误，并将注释和比较内容置于脚注中，参考了缅甸文版本和巴利圣典协会版本。文章还详细列出了各卷的标题，展示了该版本涵盖的丰富内容。  
关键词：Syamaratthassa Tepitakatthakatha；巴利文注释；新版本；泰国；文本编辑  
Title：On a New Edition of the Syamaratthassa Tepitakatthakatha  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper introduces a Thai-script edition of the Pali commentaries published in Bangkok, Thailand in 1992 to honor the fifth birth cycle of Her Majesty Queen Sirikit. The set consists of forty-eight volumes. Following a strict editorial principle, the new edition retains the text of the original edition without major changes, only correcting obvious typographical errors, and adds comments and comparisons to the footnotes, referring to the Burmese-script edition and the Pali Text Society editions. The paper also lists the titles of each volume in detail, showing the rich content covered by this edition.  
Keywords：Syamaratthassa Tepitakatthakatha；Pali commentaries；new edition；Thailand；text editing

# 论文6.  东南亚的若干引文铭文
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文报道了在柬埔寨吴哥波雷、泰国西贴和越南戈肖艾三个遗址新发现的引文铭文。这些铭文属于铭刻有佛教文本摘录的碑铭或其他镌刻记录，并非原创作品。文章对每处遗址的铭文进行了详细研究，包括文字、语言、内容、年代及相关背景，探讨了这些铭文在东南亚佛教研究中的重要价值，分析了其与佛教仪式、教义传播的关联。  
关键词：东南亚；引文铭文；佛教文本；巴利文；铭文研究  
Title：Some Citation Inscriptions from South-East Asia  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper reports on recently discovered citation inscriptions from three sites: Angkor Borei in Cambodia, Si Thep in Siam, and Go Xoai in Vietnam. These inscriptions are lithic or other engraved records that give excerpts from Buddhist texts and are not original compositions. The paper conducts a detailed study of the inscriptions at each site, including script, language, content, dating and related backgrounds, discusses the important value of these inscriptions in Southeast Asian Buddhist studies, and analyzes their connections with Buddhist rituals and the dissemination of doctrines.  
Keywords：South-East Asia；citation inscriptions；Buddhist texts；Pali；inscription studies
