# 【标题】1889年第3期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1889【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  《论事注》
作者：米纳耶夫教授（Prof. Minayef）  
摘要：本文聚焦《论事注》（Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha），以“皈依佛陀”开篇，系统追溯佛教分裂的历史脉络。核心围绕佛教结集展开，详述第一次至第四次结集的背景、主导者与核心成果，重点剖析第二次结集后跋耆比丘十种邪说引发的僧团分裂，梳理出大众部、上座部等十八个部派的衍生脉络。文本以“人是否实有”等核心议题为切入点，通过正反两方的辩论呈现部派教义分歧，运用“正理”“反理”“反驳”等逻辑框架，阐释五蕴、十二处、十八界、四圣谛等法义，同时探讨禅修方法、清净境界修证等实践路径，兼具史料价值与哲学思辨性。  
关键词：《论事注》；佛教结集；部派分裂；教义辩论；五蕴；四圣谛；南传佛教  
Title：Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha  
Author：Prof. Minayef  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha, starting with "Homage to the Buddha" and systematically tracing the historical context of Buddhist schisms. Centering on the Buddhist Councils, it elaborates on the background, leaders and core achievements of the first to fourth Councils, focusing on the schism of the Sangha caused by the ten heresies of the Vajjiputtaka bhikkhus after the second Council, and sorts out the derivation context of eighteen sects including the Mahasamghika and Theravada. Taking core issues such as "whether the person exists substantially" as the entry point, the text presents the doctrinal differences of various sects through debates between pros and cons, explains Dharma such as the Five Khandhas, Twelve Ayatanas, Eighteen Dhatus and Four Noble Truths by using logical frameworks such as "correct reasoning", "reverse reasoning" and "refutation", and discusses meditation methods and the practice path of pure realms, combining historical value and philosophical speculation.  
Keywords：Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha；Buddhist Councils；Sangha Schism；Doctrinal Debates；Five Khandhas；Four Noble Truths；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文2.  笔记与疑问
作者：莫里斯博士（Dr. Morris）  
摘要：本文为针对南传佛教文献的专题笔记与疑问汇编，聚焦巴利语典籍中的文本校勘、词汇释义、教义阐释等核心问题。内容涵盖对《论事注》等典籍的异文比对，澄清特定术语在不同手稿中的拼写差异与语义演变，探讨部分教义表述的逻辑一致性，同时提出关于文本传承、注释体例等方面的疑问，为后续文献校勘与学术研究提供参考方向。  
关键词：南传佛教文献；文本校勘；术语释义；教义探讨；学术疑问  
Title：Notes and Queries  
Author：Dr. Morris  
Abstract：This paper is a compilation of thematic notes and queries on Theravada Buddhist literature, focusing on core issues such as textual collation, vocabulary explanation and doctrinal interpretation in Pali texts. It includes the comparison of variant readings of texts such as the Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha, clarifies the spelling differences and semantic evolution of specific terms in different manuscripts, discusses the logical consistency of some doctrinal expressions, and raises questions about text transmission and annotation styles, providing reference directions for subsequent textual collation and academic research.  
Keywords：Theravada Buddhist Literature；Textual Collation；Vocabulary Explanation；Doctrinal Discussion；Academic Queries

# 论文3.  米纳耶夫教授《论事注》版本附录
作者：米纳耶夫教授（Prof. Minayef）  
摘要：本文为米纳耶夫教授校勘版《论事注》的补充附录，旨在完善核心文本的学术支撑。内容包含未纳入主文本的手稿异文、校勘说明、部分段落的补充注释，以及与其他版本《论事注》的关键差异比对。附录严格遵循文献校勘规范，为《论事注》的深度研读与版本研究提供了详实的补充材料。  
关键词：《论事注》；版本附录；手稿异文；校勘说明；文本比对  
Title：Appendix to Professor Minayef's Edition of the Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha  
Author：Prof. Minayef  
Abstract：This paper is a supplementary appendix to Prof. Minayef's collated edition of the Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha, aiming to improve the academic support of the core text. It includes manuscript variant readings not included in the main text, collation notes, supplementary annotations for some paragraphs, and comparisons of key differences with other editions of the Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha. Following strict textual collation standards, the appendix provides detailed supplementary materials for the in-depth study and version research of the Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha.  
Keywords：Kathavatthu-ppakarana-Atthakatha；Edition Appendix；Manuscript Variant Readings；Collation Notes；Text Comparison

# 论文4.  巴利圣典协会会员名录
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文收录1889年巴利圣典协会的完整会员名录，按字母顺序排列会员姓名与所属地区/机构。名录涵盖全球各地致力于南传佛教研究的学者、宗教人士及爱好者，直观呈现协会在当时的学术影响力与国际参与度，为研究19世纪末南传佛教研究的学术网络提供重要史料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；会员名录；南传佛教研究；学术网络；19世纪末  
Title：List of the Subscribers to the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper contains the complete list of subscribers to the Pali Text Society in 1889, arranging the names of subscribers and their affiliated regions/institutions in alphabetical order. Covering scholars, religious figures and enthusiasts dedicated to Theravada Buddhist research around the world, the list intuitively presents the society's academic influence and international participation at that time, providing important historical materials for the study of the academic network of Theravada Buddhist research in the late 19th century.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Subscriber List；Theravada Buddhist Research；Academic Network；Late 19th Century

# 论文5.  巴利圣典协会1889年财务报告
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1889年度财务收支明细报告，详细记录该年度的收入来源（含会员会费、捐赠等）与支出项目（含典籍出版、印刷、学术交流等）。报告以精准数据呈现协会的财务运营状况，为研究协会的学术出版规划与机构可持续发展提供量化参考。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；财务报告；收支明细；出版经费；机构运营  
Title：Accounts  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the detailed financial revenue and expenditure report of the Pali Text Society for 1889, recording in detail the society's income sources (including membership dues, donations, etc.) and expenditure items (including text publication, printing, academic exchanges, etc.) for that year. Presenting the society's financial operation status with accurate data, the report provides quantitative reference for the study of the society's academic publication planning and institutional sustainable development.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Financial Report；Revenue and Expenditure Details；Publishing Funds；Institutional Operation

# 论文6.  巴利圣典协会已出版典籍清单
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文系统梳理巴利圣典协会成立以来至1889年已出版的全部巴利语典籍目录，按出版时间排序，详细标注每部典籍的书名、编者、卷次与出版年份。清单涵盖三藏典籍、注疏、语法著作等核心文献，全面呈现协会在南传佛教文献整理出版方面的早期成果，为研究者检索相关文献提供权威指引。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；已出版典籍；文献目录；三藏典籍；南传佛教文献  
Title：Texts Already Published  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the catalog of all Pali texts published by the Pali Text Society from its establishment to 1889, arranged in chronological order of publication, with detailed annotations of the title, editor, volume and publication year of each text. Covering core literatures such as Tipitaka texts, commentaries and grammatical works, the list comprehensively presents the society's early achievements in the collation and publication of Theravada Buddhist literature, providing authoritative guidance for researchers to retrieve relevant literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Published Texts；Literature Catalog；Tipitaka Texts；Theravada Buddhist Literature
