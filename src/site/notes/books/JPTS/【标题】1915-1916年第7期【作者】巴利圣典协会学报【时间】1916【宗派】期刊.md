# 【标题】1915-1916年第7期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1916【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  名色概要
作者：法 Rama（P. Dhammarama）  
摘要：本文编辑整理了南传佛教阿毗达磨文献《名色概要》（Namarupasamasa），该文献由锡兰高僧Khemacariya创作，是九部中世纪阿毗达磨纲要著作之一。文献核心围绕“名”（精神现象）与“色”（物质现象）的分类、特性及相互关系展开，详细阐述了190种心识的分类（善、不善、异熟、业等）、心所法的组合与功能，以及色法的构成要素（四大元素、感官根门、派生色等）。编辑过程中参考了僧伽罗语与缅甸语手稿及相关注释，纠正了文本讹误，以罗马字母转写出版，为阿毗达磨名色理论的研究提供了权威文本依据。  
关键词：名色；阿毗达磨；心识；色法；南传佛教文献  
Title：Namarupasamasa  
Author：P. Dhammarama  
Abstract：This paper edits and collates the Theravada Buddhist Abhidhamma text "Namarupasamasa" (Compendium of Name and Form), which was composed by the Ceylonese elder monk Khemacariya and is one of the nine medieval Abhidhamma compendiums. The core of the text focuses on the classification, characteristics, and interrelationships of "nama" (mental phenomena) and "rupa" (material phenomena), elaborating in detail on the classification of 190 types of consciousness (wholesome, unwholesome, vipaka, kiriya, etc.), the combination and functions of cetasikas (mental factors), and the constituent elements of rupa (the four great elements, sense faculties, derived material phenomena, etc.). During the editing process, Sinhalese and Burmese manuscripts and related commentaries were referenced to correct textual errors, and the text was transcribed and published in Roman script, providing an authoritative textual basis for the study of the Abhidhamma theory of nama and rupa.  
Keywords：Nama and Rupa；Abhidhamma；Consciousness；Material Phenomena；Theravada Buddhist Literature

# 论文2.  关系哲学
作者：雷迪长老（Ledi Sadaw）；翻译：S.Z. Aung；编辑：里斯·戴维斯夫人（Mrs. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文深入探讨南传佛教的“关系”（paccaya）哲学，将其与伯特兰·罗素的关系理论进行对话，指出佛教的关系概念兼具客观性与功能性。核心内容包括二十四种业处关系（如因、所缘、增上缘等）的定义、分类及运作机制，阐明关系在因果规律（缘起法）与关联系统（巴他那体系）中的核心地位。文中区分了“巴他那”（主要因）与“业处”（广义因缘）的差异，分析了心、物、概念、涅槃四类关系主体的特性，强调关系的无常性与普遍性，为理解佛教因果观与认识论提供了深度哲学阐释。  
关键词：关系哲学；业处；巴他那；因果观；南传佛教  
Title：On the Philosophy of Relations  
Author：Ledi Sadaw；Translator: S. Z. Aung；Editor: Mrs. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper delves into the philosophy of "relation" (paccaya) in Theravada Buddhism, engaging in a dialogue with Bertrand Russell's theory of relations, and points out that the Buddhist concept of relation possesses both objectivity and functionality. The core content includes the definition, classification, and operational mechanism of the twenty-four types of paccaya relations (such as hetu, arammana, adhipati, etc.), clarifying the central position of relations in the law of causation (paticcasamuppada) and the system of correlation (Patthana system). The paper distinguishes between the differences between "Patthana" (principal cause) and "paccaya" (general cause), analyzes the characteristics of the four types of relational subjects (mind, matter, concept, Nibbana), and emphasizes the impermanence and universality of relations, providing an in-depth philosophical interpretation for understanding the Buddhist view of causation and epistemology.  
Keywords：Philosophy of Relations；Paccaya；Patthana；View of Causation；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文3.  《名色分别论》勘误
作者：A.P. 佛陀达多（A. P. Buddhadatta）  
摘要：本文针对1913-1914年《巴利圣典协会学报》发表的《名色分别论》（Namarupapariccheda）一文进行文本勘误，列出了多处原文中的拼写错误、用词偏差及语句不通之处，并提供了修正方案。勘误内容涉及经文偈颂的词汇替换、语法调整及语义补正，基于僧伽罗语与缅甸语原始手稿及权威注释，确保文本的准确性与可读性，为该文献的后续研究与引用提供了修正依据。  
关键词：《名色分别论》；文本勘误；巴利语文献；南传佛教；文献校勘  
Title：Corrigenda on Namarupapariccheda (Journal, 1913-1914)  
Author：A. P. Buddhadatta  
Abstract：This paper provides textual corrections for the article "Namarupapariccheda" (Distinction of Name and Form) published in the Journal of the Pali Text Society in 1913-1914, listing numerous spelling errors, wording deviations, and ungrammatical expressions in the original text, and offering revised solutions. The corrections involve vocabulary replacement, grammatical adjustments, and semantic rectification of the verses in the scripture, based on the original Sinhalese and Burmese manuscripts and authoritative commentaries, ensuring the accuracy and readability of the text, and providing a revised basis for the subsequent research and citation of this document.  
Keywords：Namarupapariccheda；textual corrigenda；Pali literature；Theravada Buddhism；textual collation
