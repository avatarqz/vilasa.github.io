# 巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）

# 论文1.  南亚与东南亚巴利语语法传统史（1876）第一部分——基于瓦卡苏瓦·苏布蒂的著作
作者：阿拉斯泰尔·戈纳尔（Alastair Gornall）、阿马尔·古纳塞纳（Amal Gunasena）  
摘要：本文翻译并研究了19世纪斯里兰卡佛教学者僧瓦卡苏瓦·苏布蒂（Vaskaṭuvā Subhūti）撰写的南亚与东南亚巴利语语法传统史。第一部分聚焦卡奇亚纳语法传统（Kaccāyana-vyākaraṇa），详细介绍了17部核心巴利语语法著作的作者、历史背景与核心内容，并摘录大量原文片段以展现其传承脉络。苏布蒂的研究基于手稿题跋、文献引述及《佛教史》（Sāsanavaṃsa），虽部分历史信息被后世研究超越，但保留了诸多未被充分关注的小众语法文本细节，且首次提供了各语法著作中关于“元音连读省略”规则的原文与译文对照，为理解巴利语语法传统的演变提供了关键史料。  
关键词：巴利语语法；卡奇亚纳传统；苏布蒂；语法文献；南亚佛教；东南亚佛教  
Title：A History of the Pali Grammatical Tradition of South and Southeast Asia by Vaskaṭuvā Subhūti (1876), Part I  
Author：Alastair Gornall, Amal Gunasena  
Abstract：This article translates and studies the history of Pali grammatical traditions in South and Southeast Asia written by the 19th-century Sri Lankan Buddhist scholar-monk Vaskaṭuvā Subhūti. The first part focuses on the Kaccāyana-vyākaraṇa tradition, detailing the authors, historical contexts, and core contents of 17 key Pali grammatical works, with extensive excerpts to illustrate their lineage. Subhūti’s research is based on manuscript colophons, textual citations, and the Sāsanavaṃsa. While some historical information has been superseded by later studies, it preserves details of many understudied minor grammatical texts and provides the first parallel original text and translation of the "vowel elision in sandhi" rule across various grammatical works, offering crucial historical materials for understanding the evolution of the Pali grammatical tradition.  
Keywords：Pali grammar；Kaccāyana tradition；Subhūti；grammatical literature；South Asian Buddhism；Southeast Asian Buddhism

# 论文2.  一位阿罗汉临终偈颂——《油罐偈》新译与修订版
作者：阿拉斯泰尔·戈纳尔（Alastair Gornall）、阿莱克斯·鲁伊斯-法尔克斯（Aleix Ruiz-Falqués）  
摘要：本文对匿名诗作《油罐偈》（Telakaṭṭhagāthā）进行了新译与修订校勘。该诗被认为创作于斯里兰卡文学“复兴时期”（公元11-12世纪），受梵语诗学影响深远，采用“春标记”（vasantatilakā）格律，包含100首偈颂。文章梳理了诗歌的历史争议：8世纪泰国铭文显示其部分偈颂早于传统断代，而斯里兰卡传说将其与公元前3世纪国王提萨时期的“沸油试炼”故事关联。通过校勘5份手稿与多个版本，修正了1884年版的 verse 排序错误与注释偏差，并基于18世纪僧团改革时期的僧伽罗语注释，还原其“三宝功德”“死亡观想”“无常”等十大主题分类。该诗兼具宗教修行功能与文学价值，既是中世纪巴利语抒情诗的代表，也是斯里兰卡至今仍在流传的护持文本（paritta）。  
关键词：《油罐偈》；阿罗汉；临终偈颂；巴利语诗歌；斯里兰卡佛教；文本校勘  
Title：Verses of a Dying Arahant: A New Translation and Revised Edition of the Telakaṭṭhagāthā  
Author：Alastair Gornall, Aleix Ruiz-Falqués  
Abstract：This article presents a new translation and revised critical edition of the anonymous poem Telakaṭṭhagāthā ("The Cauldron of Oil Verses"). Believed to have been composed during the "Renaissance period" of Sri Lankan literature (11th-12th centuries CE), the poem is deeply influenced by Sanskrit poetics, adopts the vasantatilakā metre, and consists of 100 verses. The article sorts out historical controversies surrounding the poem: 8th-century Thai inscriptions show some verses predate the traditional dating, while Sri Lankan legends link it to the "boiling oil trial" story during the reign of King Tissa (3rd century BCE). By collating 5 manuscripts and multiple editions, it corrects the verse order errors and annotation biases of the 1884 edition, and restores its ten thematic categories (such as "benefits of the Three Jewels," "reflection on death," "impermanence") based on 18th-century Sinhala commentaries from the monastic reform period. The poem combines religious practice functions with literary value, serving as a representative of medieval Pali lyrical poetry and a paritta text still recited in Sri Lanka today.  
Keywords：Telakaṭṭhagāthā；Arahant；dying verses；Pali poetry；Sri Lankan Buddhism；textual criticism

# 论文3.  《 Manorathapūraṇī 》中关于佛法消亡的注解
作者：克里斯·克拉克（Chris Clark）  
摘要：本文聚焦 Buddhaghosa 对《增支部尼柯耶》的注释《 Manorathapūraṇī 》中关于佛法消亡的段落（Mp I 87–92），纠正了学界长期存在的误读。传统观点认为该文本将佛法消亡划分为五个千年阶段（证悟、修行、经典、僧团标识、佛舍利依次消亡），但原文并未明确时间分期，仅指出“佛陀涅槃后千年内可生起分析智”，且强调“经典消亡是五重消亡的根源”，修行消亡晚于证悟消亡，而各阶段的时间顺序并非固定。文章提供了该段落的完整英译与注解，对比了《 Samantapāsādikā 》等其他注释中的相关预言，指出学界误读源于对科德斯（Cœdès）1956年研究的片面引用，而原文对佛法消亡的描述更侧重逻辑关联而非时间序列。  
关键词：佛法消亡；《 Manorathapūraṇī 》；Buddhaghosa；巴利语注释；佛教预言；文本解读  
Title：A Note on the Disappearance of the Dhamma According to the Manorathapūraṇī  
Author：Chris Clark  
Abstract：This article focuses on the passage about the disappearance of the Dhamma in Buddhaghosa’s commentary on the Aṅguttara-nikāya, the Manorathapūraṇī (Mp I 87–92), correcting long-standing academic misinterpretations. The traditional view holds that the text divides the disappearance of the Dhamma into five one-thousand-year stages (with realisation, practice, scripture, monastic signs, and Buddha relics disappearing in sequence), but the original text does not specify a temporal division. It only states that "beings can produce analytical insights within a thousand years after the Buddha’s parinibbāna" and emphasizes that "the disappearance of scripture is the root of the fivefold disappearance"—practice disappears after partial or complete disappearance of realisation, and the temporal order of each stage is not fixed. The article provides a complete English translation and annotation of this passage, compares related prophecies in other commentaries such as the Samantapāsādikā, and points out that the academic misinterpretation stems from the one-sided citation of Cœdès’ 1956 study, while the original text’s description of the Dhamma’s disappearance focuses more on logical connections than temporal sequence.  
Keywords：Disappearance of the Dhamma；Manorathapūraṇī；Buddhaghosa；Pali commentary；Buddhist prophecy；textual interpretation

# 论文4.  关于巴利语格律的两点注解
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文包含两部分关于巴利语格律的专题研究。第一部分识别出《巴利三藏》中第三处“veṭhas”（韵律散文）文本——用于防蛇咬伤的护持咒语，纠正了此前学界认为仅两处存在该格律的观点，通过校勘《本生经》《律藏》《增支部》的异文，还原其韵律结构与演变。第二部分聚焦《 Sumaṅgalavilāsinī 》的“结语”（Nigamana），该部分在巴利圣典协会版中被遗漏，作者基于暹罗版、缅甸版等异文，修复了其“ Aryā ”格律的完整性，指出抄写者因不熟悉格律导致的文本讹误，并通过与其他尼柯耶注释结语的比对，验证了修复后的韵律一致性。  
关键词：巴利语格律；veṭhas；Sumaṅgalavilāsinī；文本修复；韵律散文；Aryā 格律  
Title：Two Notes on Pali Metre  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This article contains two thematic studies on Pali metre. The first part identifies the third "veṭhas" (rhythmic prose) text in the Pali Tipiṭaka—a paritta text for protection against snake bites—correcting the previous academic view that only two such texts exist. By collating variants from the Jātaka, Vinaya, and Aṅguttara-nikāya, it restores its metrical structure and evolution. The second part focuses on the "Nigamana" (conclusion) of the Sumaṅgalavilāsinī, which was omitted in the Pali Text Society edition. Based on variants from Siamese, Burmese, and other editions, the author restores the integrity of its "Aryā" metre, points out textual corruptions caused by scribes’ unfamiliarity with metre, and verifies the metrical consistency of the restored text by comparing it with the conclusions of other Nikāya commentaries.  
Keywords：Pali metre；veṭhas；Sumaṅgalavilāsinī；textual restoration；rhythmic prose；Aryā metre

# 论文5.  2016年版《 Vimānavatthu-āṭṭhakathā 》校勘补正
作者：佩特拉·基弗-普尔茨（Petra Kieffer-Pülz）  
摘要：本文针对2016年出版的巴利语《天宫事注》（Vimānavatthu-āṭṭhakathā，简称Vv-a）提出系统性校勘补正。内容包括：修正文本中标点符号、拼写错误与页码排版问题；补充遗漏的3处脚注；规范专有名词拼写（如“Dhammapāla”而非“Dhammapala”）；修正 verse 引用格式与参考文献标注错误；统一页眉的文本分类标识，使其与哈代1901年版保持一致。这些补正基于对印度事务部图书馆藏手稿、缅甸版、暹罗版等异文的比对，旨在解决2016年版因文本流传导致的讹误，为巴利语注释文献的精准研究提供基础。  
关键词：《天宫事注》；文本校勘；巴利语注释；Vimānavatthu；文献讹误；版本比对  
Title：Corrections for the 2016 Edition of Vv-a  
Author：Petra Kieffer-Pülz  
Abstract：This article presents systematic corrections for the 2016 edition of the Pali Vimānavatthu-āṭṭhakathā (Vv-a). The corrections include: revising punctuation, spelling errors, and page layout issues; supplementing 3 omitted footnotes; standardizing the spelling of proper nouns (e.g., "Dhammapāla" instead of "Dhammapala"); correcting verse citation formats and reference annotation errors; unifying the textual classification labels in the headers to align with Hardy’s 1901 edition. These corrections are based on the collation of manuscripts from the India Office Library, Burmese, Siamese, and other editions, aiming to resolve corruptions in the 2016 edition caused by textual transmission and provide a foundation for accurate research on Pali commentarial literature.  
Keywords：Vimānavatthu-āṭṭhakathā；textual emendation；Pali commentary；Vimānavatthu；textual corruption；edition collation

# 论文6.  《巴利圣典协会学报》第9-33期索引
作者：（期刊编辑部汇编）  
摘要：本文汇编了1981年学报复刊以来（第9-33期，1981-2018年）发表的所有论文索引，按作者姓氏字母顺序排列。索引包含作者姓名、论文标题及发表期号与页码，涵盖巴利语语法、文献校勘、佛教哲学、历史、考古等多个领域，收录了马克·阿伦、无著等知名学者的研究成果。该索引为巴利语佛教研究领域的文献检索提供了便捷工具，便于学者快速定位相关主题的研究成果。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会学报；论文索引；巴利语研究；佛教研究；文献检索  
Title：An Index to JPTS, Volumes IX–XXXIII  
Author：Compiled by the Editorial Board of the Journal of the Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This article compiles an index of all papers published since the revival of the journal in 1981 (Volumes IX–XXXIII, 1981–2018), arranged alphabetically by author’s surname. The index includes author names, paper titles, and publication volume and page numbers, covering multiple fields such as Pali grammar, textual criticism, Buddhist philosophy, history, and archaeology, and collects research results from renowned scholars such as Mark Allon and Bhikkhu Anālayo. This index provides a convenient tool for literature retrieval in the field of Pali Buddhist studies, facilitating scholars to quickly locate research results on related topics.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；paper index；Pali studies；Buddhist studies；literature retrieval
