# 【标题】第11期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1987【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1. 如车之老身
作者：理查德·冈布里奇（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文聚焦《大般涅槃经》与《相应部》中一处著名文本，核心考证短语“vegha-missakena”的释义与校勘问题。Rhys Davids夫妇将其译为“用皮带捆绑”，但经比对《长老偈》相关段落及注疏，发现“vegha-”存在“vekha-”“vedha-”等异文，注疏中《长部注疏》释为“veghana-”，《相应部注疏》则作“vethana-”。结合K.R.诺曼对《长老偈》的翻译与注释，指出“rakkhe”应作“抵御、防备”解，而非“守护”。最终确认该短语核心义为“如同破旧马车需绳索固定般，佛陀的身体需依靠包扎维系”，并梳理了异文产生的语音与文本传承原因，纠正了此前译本的偏差。  
关键词：巴利语佛典；《大般涅槃经》；文本校勘；短语释义；异文考证  
Title：Old Bodies like Carts  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper focuses on a famous passage in the Mahaparinibbana Sutta and Samyutta Nikaya, focusing on the interpretation and textual emendation of the phrase "vegha-missakena". Mr. and Mrs. Rhys Davids translated it as "kept going by bandaging", but by comparing relevant passages and commentaries in the Thera-gatha, it is found that "vegha-" has variant readings such as "vekha-" and "vedha-". The Digha Nikaya commentary explains it as "veghana-", while the Samyutta Nikaya commentary reads "vethana-". Combined with K.R. Norman's translation and notes on the Thera-gatha, it is pointed out that "rakkhe" should be interpreted as "ward off, guard against" rather than "protect". Finally, it is confirmed that the core meaning of the phrase is "just as a worn-out cart needs to be fixed with thongs, the Buddha's body needs to be maintained by bandaging", and the reasons for the variant readings arising from phonetic and textual transmission are sorted out, correcting the deviations in previous translations.  
Keywords：Pali Buddhist texts；Mahaparinibbana Sutta；textual emendation；phrase interpretation；variant reading research

# 论文2. 《名色合集》英译与研究
作者：哈马拉瓦·萨达蒂萨（Hammalava Saddhatissa）  
摘要：本文首次将10世纪斯里兰卡巴利语阿毗达摩文献《名色合集》（Namarupasamaso）译为英文，并附详细研究。该文本由锡兰长老Khema著，在缅甸被列为“小指手册”（Let-Than）之一，12世纪由Vacissara长老作疏，后传入缅甸被称为《Khemappakarana》。译文以巴塔波拉·达摩波罗依据僧伽罗语手稿修订的版本为底本，参考仰光伯纳德自由图书馆藏缅甸文手稿及相关注疏。文本核心阐释名（精神现象）与色（物质现象）的关系，详细分类89种意识（善、不善、果报、作用意识），并解析心识与物质的缘起关联，是研究南传阿毗达摩心物论的重要文献。  
关键词：《名色合集》；巴利语阿毗达摩；英译；名色关系；南传佛教  
Title：Namarupasamaso: The Summary of Mind and Matter  
Author：Hammalava Saddhatissa  
Abstract：This paper presents the first English translation of the 10th-century Sri Lankan Pali Abhidhamma text Namarupasamaso, accompanied by detailed research. Composed by the Sri Lankan thera Khema, this text was listed as one of the "Let-Than" (little finger manuals) in Burma, commented on by the thera Vacissara in the 12th century, and later introduced to Burma as the Khemappakarana. The translation is based on the version revised by Batapola Dhammapala from Sinhala manuscripts, referring to Burmese manuscripts in the Bernard Free Library in Rangoon and related commentaries. The core of the text explains the relationship between nama (mental phenomena) and rupa (material phenomena), classifies 89 types of consciousness (moral, immoral, resultant, functional consciousness) in detail, and analyzes the dependent origination between consciousness and matter, making it an important document for the study of Theravada Abhidhamma theory of mind and matter.  
Keywords：Namarupasamaso；Pali Abhidhamma；English translation；relationship between nama and rupa；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文3. 巴利语词典学研究IV：十一个巴利语词汇的词源考证
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录、释义错误或词源存疑的十一个巴利语词汇展开考证。包括“akkhi(n)”（赌徒）源自“*aksi(n)”，而非字面义“眼睛”；“ajjhabhava”（输局）与骰子游戏相关，对应梵语“abhibhu”（失败的投掷）；“anugiyanti”（被背诵）应源自“anugiy-”，否定PED认为的“a+griyanti”；“anuvicca”（辨识）与 Ardha-Magadhi语“anuvii”同源，推导自“anu-i-”；“gotra-bhū”（支持或摧毁宗教家族）应释为“成为佛教社群成员”，纠正PED词源与释义矛盾等。研究通过文本语境分析、方言对比及语法验证，补充了词汇语义演变链条，为巴利语词典修订提供关键依据。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源考证；词汇释义；PED；文本语境  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies IV: Eleven Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper conducts etymological research on eleven Pali words that are unincluded, have incorrect meanings, or questionable etymologies in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED). These include "akkhi(n)" (gambler), derived from "*aksi(n)" rather than the literal meaning "eye"; "ajjhabhava" (losing throw), related to dice games and corresponding to Sanskrit "abhibhu" (losing throw); "anugiyanti" (are recited), derived from "anugiy-" and negating PED's view of "a+griyanti"; "anuvicca" (having discerned), cognate with Ardha-Magadhi "anuvii" and derived from "anu-i-"; "gotra-bhū" (supporting or destroying the religious family), which should be interpreted as "becoming a member of the Buddhist community", correcting the contradiction between PED's etymology and definition. Through textual context analysis, dialect comparison and grammatical verification, the study supplements the semantic evolution chain of words and provides key basis for the revision of Pali dictionaries.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；word definition；PED；textual context

# 论文4. 善友与善友性
作者：史蒂文·柯林斯（Steven Collins）  
摘要：本文系统梳理巴利语中“kalyanamitta”（善友）与“kalyanamittata”（善友性）的语法结构、语义层次与文本用法。从语法分析来看，“kalyanamitta”应为“业格复合词”（karmadharaya），义为“对他人有益之友”，或“多财复合词”（bahuvrihi），义为“拥有善友者”，否定此前将其释为“善人之友”的 Tatpurusa 复合词解读。语义层面可分为三个层次：普遍意义上的诚信互惠关系、佛教伦理框架下的善友观、特指助修解脱道的“道友”。通过比对《增支部》《中部》《清净道论》等文献，指出善友在佛教中的核心作用——既是世俗道德的践行者，也是禅修指导者（kammatthanadayaka），善友性更是“圣道之全”，为修行的根本依托。  
关键词：善友；善友性；巴利语语法；南传佛教伦理；修行依托  
Title：Kalyanamitta and Kalyanamittata  
Author：Steven Collins  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the grammatical structure, semantic levels and textual usage of "kalyanamitta" (good friend) and "kalyanamittata" (good friendship) in Pali. Grammatically, "kalyanamitta" should be a karmadharaya compound meaning "a friend beneficial to others" or a bahuvrihi compound meaning "one who has good friends", negating the previous Tatpurusa compound interpretation of "friend of a good man". Semantically, it can be divided into three levels: universal trustworthy and reciprocal relationships, the view of good friends within the framework of Buddhist ethics, and the specific "spiritual friend" who assists in the path of liberation. By comparing texts such as the Anguttara Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya and Visuddhimagga, it is pointed out that the core role of a good friend in Buddhism is not only a practitioner of secular morality but also a meditation instructor (kammatthanadayaka), and kalyanamittata is even "the whole of the holy life", the fundamental reliance for practice.  
Keywords：kalyanamitta；kalyanamittata；Pali grammar；Theravada Buddhist ethics；practice reliance

# 论文5. 一魂、多魂或无魂：一段巴利语文本的阐释困境
作者：理查德·冈布里奇（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文聚焦《转轮圣王狮吼经》中一处争议文本，核心解析短语“ekam attanam damenti ekam attanam samenti ekam attanam parinibbapenti”的阐释矛盾。Rhys Davids夫妇译为“各自掌控自我、平息自我、圆满自我”，但该表述与佛教“无我”教义冲突。经溯源，该短语最早见于《增支部》，实为婆罗门对佛教修行者的批评，意为“仅能掌控、平息、令自身入涅槃，无法利益他人”。后世文本将其误用于赞叹圣弟子，导致教义矛盾。注疏者为调和矛盾，将“attanam”释为“心”，却忽略了短语的批判语境。研究指出，该文本是巴利语佛典中“ pericope（文本片段）”被不当挪用的典型案例，其演变轨迹为：《增支部》（婆罗门批评）→《增支部》另一篇章（佛陀反驳）→《转轮圣王狮吼经》（赞叹圣弟子），反映了佛典汇编过程中的文本层积问题。  
关键词：巴利语文本；无我教义；文本阐释；文本层积；婆罗门批评  
Title：Three Souls, One or None: the Vagaries of a Pali Pericope  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper focuses on a controversial text in the Cakkavatti-sihanada Sutta, focusing on resolving the interpretive contradiction of the phrase "ekam attanam damenti ekam attanam samenti ekam attanam parinibbapenti". Mr. and Mrs. Rhys Davids translated it as "each mastering self, each calming self, each perfecting self", but this expression conflicts with the Buddhist doctrine of anatta (non-self). Through tracing, this phrase first appeared in the Anguttara Nikaya, which was actually a Brahmin criticism of Buddhist practitioners, meaning "only able to master, calm, and enter nibbana by oneself, unable to benefit others". Later texts incorrectly used it to praise noble disciples, leading to doctrinal contradictions. To reconcile the contradiction, commentators interpreted "attanam" as "mind", but ignored the critical context of the phrase. The study points out that this text is a typical case of improper appropriation of "pericope" in Pali Buddhist texts, and its evolution trajectory is: Anguttara Nikaya (Brahmin criticism) → another chapter of Anguttara Nikaya (Buddha's refutation) → Cakkavatti-sihanada Sutta (praising noble disciples), reflecting the textual stratification in the compilation of Buddhist scriptures.  
Keywords：Pali text；anatta doctrine；textual interpretation；textual stratification；Brahmin criticism

# 论文6. 小型巴利语语法文献：《语法之滴》及其“新”疏
作者：弗里德加德·洛特莫泽（Friedgard Lottermoser）  
摘要：本文校勘并研究了缅甸蒲甘王朝国王Kya-cva（1234-1250年）为宫廷女性编撰的小型语法文本《语法之滴》（Saddabindu），及15世纪泰国北部高僧Sad dhammakitti Mahaphussadeva所作的疏释《典籍精要》（Ganthasaro nama Saddabinduvinicchayo）。《语法之滴》仅20节，涵盖语音、名词、格、复合词、派生名词、动词等基础语法范畴，是南传佛教地区罕见的简明语法手册。疏释被称为“全新疏”，补充了早期注疏未及的语法细节，且融合了泰缅两地的语法传统。本文以缅甸1954年版、1937年版等 manuscripts 为底本，校勘异文并附英译，揭示了该文本在南传语法文献传承中的独特地位，及其对研究东南亚巴利语传播的价值。  
关键词：《语法之滴》；巴利语语法；南传佛教；文本校勘；泰缅佛教交流  
Title：Minor Pali Grammar Texts: the Saddabindu and its 'New' Subcommentary  
Author：Friedgard Lottermoser  
Abstract：This paper collates and studies the small grammar text Saddabindu (Drop of Grammar) compiled by King Kya-cva of the Pagan dynasty in Burma (1234-1250 A.D.) for the ladies in the royal palace, and its subcommentary Ganthasaro nama Saddabinduvinicchayo written by the Northern Thai senior monk Sad dhammakitti Mahaphussadeva in the 15th century. The Saddabindu, with only 20 verses, covers basic grammatical categories such as phonetics, nouns, cases, compounds, derivative nouns and verbs, and is a rare concise grammar manual in Theravada Buddhist regions. The subcommentary is called the "brand new subcommentary", supplementing grammatical details not covered in earlier commentaries and integrating the grammatical traditions of Thailand and Burma. Based on manuscripts such as the 1954 and 1937 Burmese editions, this paper collates variant readings and attaches English translations, revealing the unique position of this text in the inheritance of Theravada grammatical literature and its value for the study of Pali transmission in Southeast Asia.  
Keywords：Saddabindu；Pali grammar；Theravada Buddhism；textual collation；Thai-Burmese Buddhist exchange

# 论文7. 《弥兰陀王问经》最古老有纪年手稿
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文报告了泰国北部莱欣寺（Vat Lai Hin）藏《弥兰陀王问经》棕榈叶手稿的发现与研究。该手稿纪年为佛历857年（公元1495年），是目前已知最古老的有纪年《弥兰陀王问经》抄本。手稿原含16个分册（phuk），现存9个分册，缺失第3、8、9、12、13、14分册，内容涵盖原典第1-30、85-420节。通过与Trenckner校勘本比对，发现手稿文本与校勘本高度一致，但存在部分异文（如“rajata”始终作“银”义，元音“i/i”“u/ü”区分不严格），且保留了更完整的结尾段落，补充了Trenckner校勘本中缺失的部分内容。研究指出，该手稿源自锡兰传承，后传入泰国北部，为探讨《弥兰陀王问经》的文本传播与版本演变提供了关键实物证据。  
关键词：《弥兰陀王问经》；巴利语手稿；有纪年手稿；泰国北部；版本学  
Title：The Oldest Dated Manuscript of the Milindapanha  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper reports the discovery and research of a palm leaf manuscript of the Milindapanha preserved at Vat Lai Hin in northern Thailand. Dated to Buddhist Era 857 (1495 A.D.), this manuscript is the oldest dated manuscript of the Milindapanha known to date. Originally consisting of 16 fasciculi (phuk), the manuscript now has 9 surviving fasciculi, missing fasciculi 3, 8, 9, 12, 13, and 14, covering verses 1-30 and 85-420 of the original text. By comparing with Trenckner's critical edition, it is found that the manuscript text is highly consistent with the critical edition, but there are some variant readings (e.g., "rajata" always means "silver", and the vowels "i/i" and "u/ü" are not strictly distinguished), and it retains a more complete concluding paragraph, supplementing some content missing in Trenckner's critical edition. The study points out that this manuscript originated from the Sri Lankan tradition and was later introduced to northern Thailand, providing key physical evidence for exploring the textual transmission and version evolution of the Milindapanha.  
Keywords：Milindapanha；Pali manuscript；dated manuscript；northern Thailand；textual scholarship

# 论文8. 17世纪法语书籍中的巴利语提及
作者：威廉·普鲁伊特（William Pruitt）  
摘要：本文梳理了17世纪下半叶法语书籍中对巴利语（当时写作“Bali”“Baly”）的早期提及，纠正了“Simon de La Loubere的《暹罗王国志》（1691年）是首次提及巴利语”的传统观点。研究发现，早在1680年出版的《法国主教海外传教记》中，就记载传教士Laneau为研究暹罗宗教，学习巴利语并编撰语法与词典，将基督教祷文译为暹罗语，同时用巴利语撰写序言与后记。后续法语书籍如Tachard的《暹罗游记》（1686年）、Choisy的《暹罗旅行日记》（1686年）等，均提及巴利语是暹罗僧侣的学术语言，类似拉丁语在欧洲的地位，用于宗教文本与讲道。本文通过分析这些早期记载，还原了欧洲人对巴利语的初始认知，及其与17世纪法暹宗教文化交流的关联。  
关键词：17世纪；法语书籍；巴利语；法暹交流；早期欧洲佛教研究  
Title：References to Pali in 17th-century French Books  
Author：William Pruitt  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the early mentions of Pali (written as "Bali" or "Baly" at that time) in French books of the second half of the 17th century, correcting the traditional view that "Simon de La Loubere's Du royaume de Siam (1691) is the first mention of Pali". The study finds that as early as the 1680 publication Relation des Missions et des voyages des évesques vicaires apostoliques, it is recorded that the missionary Laneau learned Pali to study Siamese religion, compiled grammar and dictionary, translated Christian prayers into Siamese, and wrote preface and postscript in Pali. Subsequent French books such as Tachard's Voyage de Siam (1686) and Choisy's Journal du voyage de Siam (1686) all mention that Pali was the scholarly language of Siamese monks, similar to the status of Latin in Europe, used for religious texts and sermons. By analyzing these early records, this paper restores Europeans' initial understanding of Pali and its connection with French-Siamese religious and cultural exchanges in the 17th century.  
Keywords：17th century；French books；Pali；French-Siamese exchange；early European Buddhist studies

# 论文9. 《五趣照明论》英译
作者：安·阿普尔比·黑兹尔伍德（Ann Appleby Hazlewood）  
摘要：本文英译了柬埔寨巴利语文本《五趣照明论》（Pancagatidipani），该文本以“五趣”（地狱、畜生、饿鬼、人、天）为核心，阐述善恶业报与轮回流转，是南传佛教“趣论”文献的重要变体。译文以Feer 1884年校勘本为底本，参考巴黎国家图书馆藏柬埔寨文手稿及Mus的研究成果，纠正了底本中的多处异文（如“galagandaka”释为“甲状腺肿大的饿鬼”而非字面义）。文本结构清晰，先礼敬佛陀，再分述八热地狱、次要地狱、畜生道、饿鬼道（含鸠槃荼、夜叉等）、人道、天道的业因与果报，最后总结善恶业的终极归宿。该英译填补了西方学界对柬埔寨系南传“趣论”文献的研究空白，为比较不同地区南传轮回观提供了关键文本。  
关键词：《五趣照明论》；五趣；南传佛教；业报轮回；英译  
Title：A Translation of Pancagatidipani  
Author：Ann Appleby Hazlewood  
Abstract：This paper presents an English translation of the Cambodian Pali text Pancagatidipani (Illumination of the Five Realms of Existence), which focuses on the "five gatis" (naraka, animal, peta, human, deva), expounds on the karmic retribution of good and evil and samsara, and is an important variant of the Theravada Buddhist "gati theory" literature. Based on Feer's 1884 critical edition, the translation refers to Cambodian manuscripts in the Bibliothèque nationale de France and the research results of Mus, correcting many variant readings in the base text (e.g., "galagandaka" is interpreted as "petas with goiter" rather than the literal meaning). The text has a clear structure: first paying homage to the Buddha, then separately describing the karmic causes and retributions of the eight great narakas, secondary narakas, animal realm, peta realm (including kumbhandas, yakkhas, etc.), human realm, and deva realm, and finally summarizing the ultimate destination of good and evil karma. This English translation fills the gap in Western scholarship on Cambodian-style Theravada "gati theory" literature and provides a key text for comparing the concept of samsara in different Theravada regions.  
Keywords：Pancagatidipani；five gatis；Theravada Buddhism；karmic retribution and samsara；English translation
