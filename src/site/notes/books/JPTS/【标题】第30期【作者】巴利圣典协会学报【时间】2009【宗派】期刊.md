# 论文1. 普里莫兹·佩岑科（1947-2007）
作者：马克·艾伦（Mark Allon）  
摘要：本文是对著名巴利语和俗语学者普里莫兹·佩岑科博士（1947-2007）的纪念。佩岑科博士在昆士兰大学担任东方宗教高级讲师及佛教研究中心联合主任，同时是澳大拉西亚佛教研究协会执行委员会成员。他在印度浦那大学获得硕士学位，在澳大利亚国立大学获得博士学位，是澳大利亚佛教研究领域的重要人物。其研究专长为巴利语注释文献，尤其专注于疏释（ṭīkās）这一少有人涉足的领域，核心贡献包括编辑《增支部》疏释（已出版三卷，第四卷待出版），以及发表多篇相关重要论文。他还将多部巴利语文本译为斯洛文尼亚语，并开展缅甸古达乌塔碑铭建筑群研究、佛教禅修理论与实践研究等多个项目。佩岑科博士教学成果丰硕，深受学生尊敬，他的离世是该领域的重大损失。  
关键词：普里莫兹·佩岑科；巴利语研究；注释文献；疏释（ṭīkās）；佛教研究  
Title：Primoz Pecenko, 1947–2007  
Author：Mark Allon  
Abstract：Dr. Primoz Pecenko passed away suddenly from a heart attack on the evening of 1 August 2007 while walking with his family and dog, just a few days before his 60th birthday. He was Senior Lecturer in Eastern Religions and Co-Director of the Centre for Buddhist Studies at the University of Queensland, as well as an Executive Committee member of the Australasian Association of Buddhist Studies (AABS). Having completed a Masters degree at Pune in India and a PhD at the Australian National University, Pecenko was a major figure in Buddhist studies in Australia, and his passing constitutes a great loss to the discipline. His specialization lay in Pali commentarial literature, particularly the sub-commentaries (ṭīkās), a relatively under-researched field. His key contributions include editing the sub-commentary on the Aṅguttara-nikāya (three volumes published, with a fourth in progress) and publishing several important articles. He also translated numerous Pali texts into Slovenian and engaged in research projects such as the study of the Kuthodaw Pagoda Inscriptional Complex in Burma and Buddhist meditation. A dedicated educator, he taught Pali, Sanskrit, and courses on Buddhism, Hinduism, and World Religion, supervising many postgraduate students and earning great respect and affection from his pupils.  
Keywords：Primoz Pecenko；Pali studies；commentarial literature；sub-commentaries (ṭīkās)；Buddhist research

# 论文2. 巴利语文献中的尼柯耶疏释（ṭīkās）史——基于巴利语目录学资料
作者：普里莫兹·佩岑科（Primoz Pecenko）  
摘要：本文探讨传统上座部佛教目录学文献中记载的四部尼柯耶疏释（ṭīkās）的历史。研究表明，存在两套而非单一一套尼柯耶疏释，而现存印刷版仅收录了其中一部分（作为 Chaṭṭhasaṅgāyana 版的一部分）。现代巴利语学术研究通常与上座部传统一致，仅提及一套疏释，但缅甸和斯里兰卡多家图书馆收藏的巴利语手稿目录显示，存在另一套被传统和现代学术忽视、被认为“失传”或“不存在”的疏释。作者在缅甸发现的一份重要“失传”疏释手稿，为重新理解两套疏释的历史发展及巴利语文学史提供了新视角。研究指出，部分被现代学术视为不可靠的早期目录学资料，实际上比后期资料更具可信度，现有巴利语文学史的史料基础需结合早期目录、手稿目录、碑铭及未被研究的手稿重新审视，上座部巴利语文本的传承史也需相应重考。  
关键词：巴利语；尼柯耶疏释（ṭīkās）；目录学资料；手稿；文本传承  
Title：The History of the Nikāya Subcommentaries (ṭīkās) in Pali Bibliographic Sources  
Author：Primoz Pecenko  
Abstract：This article discusses the history of Pali subcommentaries (ṭīkās) on the first four nikāyas as presented in traditional Theravāda bibliographic texts. It demonstrates that there are two sets of nikāya subcommentaries, not just the single set published as part of the Chaṭṭhasaṅgāyana edition. While modern Pali scholarship, aligning with the Theravāda tradition, usually mentions only one set, catalogues of Pali manuscripts held in libraries in Burma and Sri Lanka indicate the existence of another set, which has been ignored and deemed "lost" or "non-existent". The author’s recent discovery of an important manuscript of one of these "lost" subcommentaries in Burma offers a new perspective on the historical development of the two sets and the understanding of Pali literary history. The study reveals that earlier Pali bibliographic sources, often considered less reliable by modern scholarship, are in fact more trustworthy than later ones. Consequently, the sources for the existing history of Pali literature need to be re-examined in light of older bibliographic texts, manuscript catalogues, inscriptions, and unstudied manuscripts, and the history of the traditional Theravāda transmission of Pali texts must also be reconsidered.  
Keywords：Pali；Nikāya subcommentaries (ṭīkās)；bibliographic sources；manuscripts；textual transmission

# 论文3. 苏西马与佛陀的对话：《苏西马经》再研究
作者：菩提比丘（Bhikkhu Bodhi）  
摘要：本文是对《苏西马经》（S 12:70）的后续研究，聚焦经文中“智慧解脱阿罗汉”的相关议题，并结合汉译佛经中的平行文本展开分析。经文中记载，苦行者苏西马以“法贼”身份加入僧团，欲窃取佛陀教法的核心，却发现一群宣称证得阿罗汉果的僧人未具备五通与无色解脱，仅称“以智慧解脱”。作者此前的研究已探讨智慧解脱阿罗汉所需的禅定最低成就，本文进一步分析经文后半部分佛陀对“法住智”与“涅槃智”的阐释，以及四圣谛、十二因缘等教义的宣讲。通过对比巴利语原文与汉译《摩诃僧祇律》《杂阿含经》（第347经）及《阿毗昙毗婆沙论》中的平行版本，作者指出不同文本在“两种智”的表述、苏西马的证悟结果等方面存在差异，并探讨这些差异背后的教义发展与传承脉络，揭示早期佛教不同流派对“智慧解脱”的不同阐释及禅定在解脱道中的地位争议。  
关键词：苏西马经；智慧解脱阿罗汉；法住智；涅槃智；平行文本；巴利语；汉译佛经  
Title：Susīma’s Conversation with the Buddha: A Second Study of the Susīma-sutta  
Author：Bhikkhu Bodhi  
Abstract：This article is a follow-up study of the Susīma-sutta (S 12:70), focusing on the theme of the "wisdom-liberated arahant" and drawing on parallel texts preserved in Chinese Buddhist translations. The sutta recounts how the ascetic Susīma enters the Buddhist monastic order as a "thief of Dhamma" to uncover the core of the Buddha’s teaching, only to find a group of monks who claim to have attained arahantship but lack the five mundane super-knowledges and the formless emancipations, asserting instead that they are "liberated by wisdom". Building on the author’s previous research on the minimum attainment in samādhi required for a wisdom-liberated arahant, this article further analyzes the Buddha’s exposition of "knowledge of the persistence of principles" (dhammaṭṭhitiñāṇa) and "knowledge of nibbāna" (nibbāne ñāṇa) in the latter part of the sutta, as well as his exposition of the Four Noble Truths and dependent origination. By comparing the Pali text with parallel versions in the Mahāsāṅghika Vinaya, Saṃyukta-āgama (No. 347), and Abhidharma-vibhāṣā-śāstra, the author identifies differences in the expression of the "two kinds of knowledge" and the outcome of Susīma’s realization, exploring the doctrinal developments and transmission contexts behind these differences, and revealing disputes among early Buddhist schools regarding the interpretation of "wisdom liberation" and the role of jhānic concentration in the path to liberation.  
Keywords：Susīma-sutta；wisdom-liberated arahant；knowledge of the persistence of principles (dhammaṭṭhitiñāṇa)；knowledge of nibbāna (nibbāne ñāṇa)；parallel texts；Pali；Chinese Buddhist translations

# 论文4. 论直译
作者：肯尼斯·罗伊·诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文围绕巴利语文本的翻译问题展开，重点探讨直译的价值、争议及实践原则。作者指出，《法句经》的英译本已超过五十种，新译本的涌现多因译者对现有译本不满，但部分译者仅关注个别术语的替换，忽视对文本整体含义的把握。翻译的核心目标应是在目标语言中尽可能保留原文特质，包括诗歌体裁、文字游戏等，但双语能力的稀缺使这一理想难以实现。作者主张巴利语文本应采用直译（近乎逐字逐句的散文翻译），理由是散文形式可避免非诗人译者创作劣质诗歌，且直译能清晰呈现译者对原文的理解，为学者和后续译者提供可靠参考。针对学界对直译“缺乏诗意”“晦涩难懂”的批评，作者回应称其直译旨在服务学术研究，而非追求文学性；同时通过评析吉尔·弗伦德尔的《法句经》新译本，指出直译需避免机械僵化，应兼顾准确性与可读性，重视术语翻译的一致性、词源学依据及文本语境，避免过度阐释或随意改写。  
关键词：巴利语翻译；直译；法句经；翻译原则；术语翻译  
Title：On Translating Literally  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This article focuses on the translation of Pali texts, with an emphasis on the value, controversies, and practical principles of literal translation. The author notes that there are now well over fifty English translations of the Dhammapada, and the emergence of new translations is mostly due to translators’ dissatisfaction with existing versions. However, some translators only focus on replacing individual terms without considering the overall meaning of the text. The core goal of translation should be to preserve the peculiarities of the original text in the target language as much as possible, including poetic form and word play, but the scarcity of bilingual competence makes this ideal difficult to achieve. The author advocates for a literal, almost word-for-word prose translation of Pali texts, arguing that prose avoids non-poets producing poor poetry and that literal translation can clearly present the translator’s understanding of the original, providing a reliable reference for scholars and subsequent translators. In response to academic criticisms that literal translation is "lacking poetic flavor" and "incomprehensible", the author argues that his literal translations aim to serve academic research rather than pursue literariness. By analyzing Gil Fronsdal’s new translation of the Dhammapada, the author points out that literal translation should avoid rigidity, balance accuracy and readability, attach importance to the consistency of terminology translation, etymological basis, and textual context, and avoid over-interpretation or arbitrary rewriting.  
Keywords：Pali translation；literal translation；Dhammapada；translation principles；terminology translation

# 论文6. 佛陀真正值得称颂的特质——基于《大萨库鲁达耶经》及其汉译平行文本
作者：无著比丘（Bhikkhu Anālayo）  
摘要：本文以《中部尼柯耶》中的《大萨库鲁达耶经》及其汉译《中阿含经》（第207经）的平行文本为案例，探讨巴利语尼柯耶与汉译阿含经的比较研究价值。作者首先对《中阿含经》相关经文进行带注翻译，随后对比分析两个版本的差异：巴利语版本未明确佛弟子及其他导师的弟子数量，汉译版本则明确佛陀有一千二百五十名弟子，其他导师各有五百名弟子；汉译版本中萨库鲁达耶见到佛陀时起身致敬、合掌问讯，并在经末归依佛陀成为居士，巴利语版本中无此情节；两个版本对“佛陀值得称颂的五种特质”的排序及具体表述也存在不同。研究指出，这些差异反映了口头传承的影响及不同流派提升佛陀地位的倾向，但核心教义一致：佛陀真正值得称颂的并非古代印度推崇的外在苦行表现，而是其高尚德行、无上智慧、超凡知见，以及对四圣谛、解脱道的宣讲。  
关键词：佛陀；值得称颂的特质；大萨库鲁达耶经；平行文本；巴利语；汉译佛经  
Title：The Buddha’s Truly Praiseworthy Qualities: According to the Mahāsakuludāyi-sutta and Its Chinese Parallel  
Author：Bhikkhu Anālayo  
Abstract：This article takes the Mahāsakuludāyi-sutta from the Majjhima-nikāya and its parallel text in the Chinese Madhyama-āgama (No. 207) as a case study to explore the value of comparative research between the Pali Nikāyas and the Chinese Āgamas. The author first provides an annotated translation of the relevant passage in the Madhyama-āgama, then compares and analyzes the differences between the two versions: while the Pali version does not specify the number of the Buddha’s disciples or those of other teachers, the Chinese version clarifies that the Buddha had 1,250 disciples and each of the other teachers had 500 disciples; in the Chinese version, Sakuludāyī rises to greet the Buddha with folded hands when seeing him and takes refuge in the Buddha to become a lay follower at the end of the sutta, which is not recorded in the Pali version; the two versions also differ in the order and specific expressions of the "five praiseworthy qualities of the Buddha". The study points out that these differences reflect the influence of oral transmission and the tendency of different schools to elevate the Buddha’s status, but the core doctrine is consistent: what makes the Buddha truly praiseworthy is not the external ascetic practices valued in ancient India, but his supreme virtue, wisdom, knowledge and vision, as well as his preaching of the Four Noble Truths and the path to liberation.  
Keywords：Buddha；truly praiseworthy qualities；Mahāsakuludāyi-sutta；parallel texts；Pali；Chinese Buddhist translations

# 论文7. 时态与人称的小问题：《法句经》第306颂及其平行文本
作者：乔纳森·A. 希尔克（J.A. Silk）  
摘要：本文聚焦《法句经》第306颂中存在的时态与人称问题，结合多种平行文本展开 philological 分析。该颂巴利语原文为“abhūtavādī nirayaṃ upeti yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha ubho pi te pecca samā bhavanti nihīnakammā manujā parattha”，其中第二句“yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha”的时态（现在时）与人称（第一人称）存在争议，直译“做了某事却说‘我不做’”看似不合逻辑。作者通过对比巴利语注释、帕特纳《法句经》、犍陀罗《法句经》、梵语《优陀那品》及藏译版本，发现平行文本在动词形式上存在差异：帕特纳版本保留第一人称现在时，犍陀罗和梵语版本则为第三人称现在时。研究提出，原初文本可能使用第一人称过去时（aorist），后经口头传承误传为现在时，第三人称形式则是后续误读的结果。作者强调，文本校勘需结合多语言平行材料，兼顾音韵、格律与语境，才能更接近原文本义。  
关键词：法句经第306颂；时态；人称；平行文本；文本校勘；巴利语  
Title：A Small Problem of Tense and Person: Dhammapada 306 and Its Parallels  
Author：J.A. Silk  
Abstract：This article focuses on the problem of tense and person in Dhammapada 306, conducting a philological analysis with reference to various parallel texts. The Pali original of the verse is "abhūtavādī nirayaṃ upeti yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha ubho pi te pecca samā bhavanti nihīnakammā manujā parattha", and the tense (present tense) and person (first person) of the second pada "yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha" are controversial, as the literal translation "one who having done something says 'I do not do it'" seems illogical. By comparing the Pali commentary, the Patna Dharmapada, the Gāndhārī Dharmapada, the Sanskrit Udānavarga, and the Tibetan translation, the author finds that the parallel texts differ in verb form: the Patna version retains the first person present tense, while the Gāndhārī and Sanskrit versions use the third person present tense. The study proposes that the original text may have used the first person aorist (past tense), which was later misrepresented as the present tense through oral transmission, and the third person form is a result of subsequent misreading. The author emphasizes that textual emendation must draw on multilingual parallel materials, considering phonology, meter, and context to better approach the original meaning of the text.  
Keywords：Dhammapada 306；tense；person；parallel texts；textual emendation；Pali

# 论文8. 《论赫尔默·史密斯与冈纳·雅林的通信》补正与后记
作者：西格弗里德·利恩哈德（Siegfried Lienhard）  
摘要：本文是对《巴利圣典协会学报》第29卷（2007年）发表的《论赫尔默·史密斯与冈纳·雅林的通信》一文的补正与后记。补正部分纠正了原文中的多处错误，包括人名、日期、词汇拼写及引文遗漏等问题（如将“父亲”误写为“父亲的妻子”、将“1951年”误写为“1937年”等）。后记中，作者指出，经反复研读史密斯致尼尔斯·西蒙松的信件并与多位学者探讨后确认，著名巴利语学者赫尔默·史密斯在该信中的极端言论并非出于地方爱国主义，而是为了调侃雅林、西蒙松及隆德大学的部分宗教史学家，并无严肃学术意图。  
关键词：赫尔默·史密斯；冈纳·雅林；通信；补正；后记  
Title：Corrigenda et postscriptum for “On the Correspondence of Helmer Smith and Gunnar Jarring”  
Author：Siegfried Lienhard  
Abstract：This article is the corrigenda and postscriptum for the article "On the Correspondence of Helmer Smith and Gunnar Jarring" published in Volume XXIX (2007) of the Journal of the Pali Text Society. The corrigenda section corrects multiple errors in the original text, including mistakes in names, dates, vocabulary spelling, and omitted citations (e.g., mistranslating "father" as "father’s wife", mistaking "1951" for "1937", etc.). In the postscriptum, the author states that after rereading Smith’s letter to Nils Simonsson repeatedly and discussing it with several colleagues, it is confirmed that the extreme remarks made by the renowned Pali scholar Helmer Smith in the letter were not due to local patriotism, but to make fun of Jarring, Simonsson, and certain historians of religion in Lund, with no serious academic intention.  
Keywords：Helmer Smith；Gunnar Jarring；correspondence；corrigenda；postscriptum

# 论文9. 《巴利圣典协会学报》第9-30卷索引
作者：（无单独作者，为期刊整理）  
摘要：本文是《巴利圣典协会学报》第9-30卷（1981-2009年）的文章索引，按作者姓氏字母顺序编排。索引收录了该期间发表的所有学术论文、译文、书评等内容，每条条目包含作者、文章标题及发表卷期页码。此前协会曾于1973年出版第1-8卷索引（由P.D. 拉特纳通加编纂、S.S. 戴维森修订），本索引为后续卷期的补充，全面覆盖了1981年学报重启后至2009年的所有学术成果，为巴利语研究、佛教研究学者检索相关文献提供了便捷工具。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会学报；第9-30卷；索引；学术文献；检索工具  
Title：An Index to JPTS, Volumes IX–XXX  
Author：(Compiled by the Journal)  
Abstract：This article is an index of articles published in Volumes IX–XXX (1981-2009) of the Journal of the Pali Text Society, arranged alphabetically by author’s surname. The index includes all academic papers, translations, book reviews, and other contents published during this period, with each entry containing the author, article title, and volume and page numbers. The Society previously published an index of Volumes I–VIII in 1973 (compiled by P.D. Ratnatunga and revised by S.S. Davidson), and this index is a supplement to the subsequent volumes. It fully covers all academic achievements from the journal’s revival in 1981 to 2009, providing a convenient tool for scholars in Pali studies and Buddhist studies to retrieve relevant literature.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；Volumes IX–XXX；index；academic literature；retrieval tool
