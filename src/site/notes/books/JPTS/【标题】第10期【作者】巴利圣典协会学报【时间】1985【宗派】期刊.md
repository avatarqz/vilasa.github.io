# 【标题】第10期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1985【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1. 曼谷国家图书馆的两份《本生经》手稿
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文梳理了巴利语手稿的编目历史，指出欧洲与亚洲多地的巴利语手稿收藏尚未被全面记录。重点描述了1981-1983年期间在曼谷国家图书馆考察的两份《本生经》手稿：编号6284（佛历2249年，公元1706年）和编号6271（佛历2190年，公元1647年），均为高棉文字体。详细记录了手稿的分册数量、纪年、内容构成、页码特征、文本异文及语法特点，如第一份手稿存在分页混乱与补抄痕迹，第二份手稿的注疏内容具有独特性。两份手稿反映了东南亚巴利语传承的文本特征，为研究《本生经》的版本演变与南传佛教文献传播提供了关键实物证据。  
关键词：《本生经》手稿；曼谷国家图书馆；巴利语；文本校勘；南传佛教文献  
Title：Two Jataka Manuscripts from the National Library in Bangkok  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the cataloging history of Pali manuscripts, pointing out that Pali manuscript collections in many places in Europe and Asia have not been fully recorded. It focuses on describing two Jataka manuscripts inspected at the National Library in Bangkok during 1981-1983: No. 6284 (Buddha Sakkaraja 2249 = AD 1706) and No. 6271 (Buddha Sakkaraja 2190 = AD 1647), both in Khmer script. It details the number of fasciculi, dating, content composition, pagination characteristics, textual variants and grammatical features of the manuscripts. For example, the first manuscript has pagination confusion and traces of supplementary copying, and the commentary content of the second manuscript is unique. These two manuscripts reflect the textual characteristics of the Pali transmission in Southeast Asia, providing key physical evidence for the study of the textual evolution of the Jataka and the dissemination of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Jataka manuscripts；National Library in Bangkok；Pali；textual collation；Theravada Buddhist literature

# 论文2. 巴利语词典学研究III：十个巴利语词源
作者：K. R. 诺曼（K. R. Norman）  
摘要：本文延续巴利语词典学研究，针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录、释义错误或词源存疑的十个巴利语词汇展开考证。包括“asita”（无依附的）、“cunna-pada”（散文）、“nikkhamati”（突出）等，通过比对巴利语原典、梵语对应词、注释文献及方言传承，纠正了PED的词源误判与语义偏差，补充了词汇的使用语境与语义演变脉络，为巴利语词典的修订提供了学术依据。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源研究；文本释义；《巴利语-英语词典》  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies III: Ten Pali Etymologies  
Author：K. R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper continues the Pali lexicographical research, investigating ten Pali words that are unincluded, incorrectly explained, or etymologically questionable in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED). These include "asita" (unattached), "cunna-pada" (prose), "nikkhamati" (to protrude), etc. By comparing Pali original texts, corresponding Sanskrit words, commentarial literature and dialectal traditions, it corrects the etymological misjudgments and semantic deviations in PED, supplements the usage context and semantic evolution of the words, and provides academic basis for the revision of Pali dictionaries.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；word definition；Pali-English Dictionary (PED)

# 论文3. 那部经在哪里？——《增支部》主题索引
作者：坤提帕洛比丘（Phra Khantipalo）  
摘要：本文编制了《增支部》（Anguttara-Nikaya）的主题索引，涵盖主题、比喻、人物、地点等核心要素。以巴利语术语为基础，提供对应的英文翻译，按字母顺序编排条目，标注每条内容在《增支部》中的具体出处（经号、分册等）。索引涵盖佛教伦理、修行方法、四圣谛、八正道、禅定、业报等核心教义，为研究者快速定位《增支部》中相关主题的文献片段提供了便捷工具，助力巴利语佛典的文本检索与学术研究。  
关键词：《增支部》；巴利语佛典；主题索引；文本检索；南传佛教  
Title：Where's that Sutta? A Subject Index to the Anguttara-Nikaya  
Author：Phra Khantipalo  
Abstract：This paper compiles a subject index to the Anguttara-Nikaya, covering core elements such as subjects, similes, persons and places. Based on Pali technical terms, it provides corresponding English translations, arranges entries in alphabetical order, and marks the specific source (sutta number, fascicle, etc.) of each entry in the Anguttara-Nikaya. The index covers core doctrines such as Buddhist ethics, practice methods, Four Noble Truths, Noble Eightfold Path, meditation, and karmic retribution, providing a convenient tool for researchers to quickly locate textual fragments related to relevant themes in the Anguttara-Nikaya and facilitating the textual retrieval and academic research of Pali Buddhist scriptures.  
Keywords：Anguttara-Nikaya；Pali Buddhist scriptures；subject index；text retrieval；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文4. 《究竟义抉择》（Paramatthavinicchaya）
作者：阿努律陀（Anuruddha）  
校勘者：A. P. 佛陀达多（A. P. Buddhadatta）  
摘要：《究竟义抉择》是南传佛教阿毗达磨小型手册之一，由阿努律陀著，A. P. 佛陀达多校勘。文本以诗体（瓦塔 metre）撰写，共分为心法抉择、心所法抉择、色法抉择、涅槃抉择、名言抉择五个主要部分，系统阐释了巴利语阿毗达磨的核心概念，包括心的分类、心所的特性、色法的构成、涅槃的本质及名言的定义。校勘本以仰光伯纳德自由图书馆的缅甸语手稿为底本，比对了僧伽罗语手稿、缅甸语印刷本及僧伽罗语译本，纠正了多处异文，补充了文本的传承线索，为研究南传阿毗达磨的教义体系与文献传承提供了可靠的校勘本。  
关键词：《究竟义抉择》；阿努律陀；巴利语阿毗达磨；文本校勘；南传佛教哲学  
Title：Paramatthavinicchaya  
Author：Anuruddha  
Editor：A. P. Buddhadatta  
Abstract：Paramatthavinicchaya is one of the minor manuals of Theravada Buddhist Abhidhamma, written by Anuruddha and collated by A. P. Buddhadatta. The text is composed in verse (vatta metre) and divided into five main parts: Cittavibhago (Classification of Mind), Cetasikavibhago (Classification of Mental Factors), Rupavibhago (Classification of Material Phenomena), Nibbhanavibhago (Classification of Nibbana), and Pannattivibhago (Classification of Designations). It systematically explains the core concepts of Pali Abhidhamma, including the classification of mind, the characteristics of mental factors, the composition of material phenomena, the nature of Nibbana and the definition of designations. Based on the Burmese manuscript from the Bernard Free Library in Rangoon, the critical edition compares Sinhalese manuscripts, Burmese printed editions and Sinhalese translations, corrects many variant readings, supplements the textual transmission clues, and provides a reliable critical edition for the study of the doctrinal system and textual transmission of Theravada Abhidhamma.  
Keywords：Paramatthavinicchaya；Anuruddha；Pali Abhidhamma；textual collation；Theravada Buddhist philosophy
