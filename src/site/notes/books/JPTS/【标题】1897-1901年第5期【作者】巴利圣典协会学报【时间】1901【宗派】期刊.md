# 【标题】1897-1901年合刊【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1901【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利语译文讨论词汇索引
作者：梅布尔·海恩斯·博德（Mabel Haynes Bode）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会编撰了巴利语词汇索引，旨在为研究者节省检索时间。索引收录了多部巴利语文本译著中带注释的词汇，标注了对应译著的卷次与页码，涵盖历史、语文学、文献学等多维度注释及平行文本参考文献。索引排除专有名词（已有相关索引），聚焦未被《奇尔德斯巴利语词典》收录的词汇，采用贴近《律藏》的词汇收录格式（形容词和阳性名词用原始词干形式，阴性和中性名词多用主格单数形式，动词用现在时第三人称单数形式），虽存在少量不一致性，但以实用性为核心，为巴利语研究提供便捷检索工具。  
关键词：巴利语词汇；索引；译文注释；文献检索；巴利语文本；语文学  
Title：Index to Pali Words Discussed in Translations  
Author：Mabel Haynes Bode  
Abstract：This paper compiles a Pali word index for the Pali Text Society, aiming to save researchers' retrieval time. The index collects annotated words from multiple translated works of Pali texts, marking the corresponding volume and page numbers of the translations, covering annotations in historical, philological, bibliographical and other dimensions as well as references to parallel texts. Excluding proper nouns (for which relevant indices already exist), the index focuses on words not included in Childers' Dictionary, adopting a vocabulary collection format close to the Vinaya (adjectives and masculine nouns in crude stem form, feminines and neuters mostly in the form of nominative singular, verbs in the third person singular of the present indicative). Despite a few inconsistencies, it takes practicality as the core and provides a convenient retrieval tool for Pali studies.  
Keywords：Pali Words；Index；Translation Annotations；Literature Retrieval；Pali Texts；Philology

# 论文2.  赞颂佛陀的若干诗节考
作者：埃德蒙·哈迪（Edmund Hardy）  
摘要：本文基于奥匈帝国前中校冯·比布拉男爵收藏的一份斯里兰卡手稿进行转录与研究。该手稿由冯·西博尔德男爵多年前从斯里兰卡带回，含三张布质叶片，以僧伽罗文书写，语言为梵语变体与巴利语形式的混合体，包含多篇赞颂佛陀的诗节，推测由佛教僧团中虔诚的成员创作。手稿存在诸多文本错误，作者在转录原文（严格还原叶片及行文排列）的基础上提供了修订版文本，仅保留该语言特有的表达，修正了辅音重复等问题。这些诗节未见于其他文献，结尾的“愿成就、愿吉祥、愿健康”公式表明文本完整，其押韵方式或受泰米尔语影响。  
关键词：佛陀赞颂诗；斯里兰卡手稿；僧伽罗文；文本校勘；巴利语；梵语变体  
Title：On Some Stanza in Eulogy of the Buddha  
Author：Edmund Hardy  
Abstract：This paper transcribes and studies a Sri Lankan manuscript collected by Baron von Bibra, a former lieutenant-colonel of the Austro-Hungarian army. The manuscript was brought back from Sri Lanka by Baron von Siebold many years ago, consisting of three cloth leaves written in Sinhalese. The language is a mixture of corrupted Sanskrit and Pali forms, containing several stanzas eulogizing the Buddha, presumably composed by a pious member of the Buddhist Order. There are many textual errors in the manuscript. On the basis of transcribing the original text (strictly restoring the leaves and text arrangement), the author provides a revised text, retaining only the expressions peculiar to the language and correcting the doubling of consonants and other issues. These stanzas are not found in other documents, and the concluding formula "siddhir astu subham astu arogyam astu" (May there be success, may there be auspiciousness, may there be health) indicates the completeness of the text, whose rhyming mode may be influenced by Tamil.  
Keywords：Stanzas in Eulogy of the Buddha；Sri Lankan Manuscript；Sinhalese；Textual Collation；Pali；Corrupted Sanskrit

# 论文3.  佛教兴起时期印度的政治分区考
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文考证了佛教兴起时期（公元前6-7世纪）印度的政治格局，当时印度无至高君主，君主制与贵族共和制并存，且共和制有被邻邦王国逐渐吞并的趋势。文中详细梳理了四大主要君主制王国（摩揭陀、侨萨罗、跋蹉、阿槃底）的疆域、首都、王室联姻及战争史，以及多个共和制部族（释迦、离车、末罗等）的治理模式、行政机构与社会状况。通过分析“十六大国”列表及当时印度主要城市的分布与地位，揭示了该时期印度的政治地理格局，指出婆罗门文献忽视共和制事实，而佛教与耆那教文献为还原这一历史场景提供了关键证据。  
关键词：佛教兴起；印度政治分区；君主制王国；共和制部族；十六大国；城市分布  
Title：Notes on Political Divisions in India When Buddhism Arose  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper investigates the political pattern of India during the rise of Buddhism (6th-7th centuries B.C.). At that time, there was no paramount sovereign in India, and monarchy coexisted with aristocratic republicanism, with the latter gradually being absorbed by neighboring kingdoms. The paper details the territory, capital, royal marriages and war history of the four major monarchical kingdoms (Magadha, Kosala, Vamsa, Avanti), as well as the governance models, administrative institutions and social conditions of several republican clans (Sakya, Licchavi, Malla, etc.). By analyzing the list of "Sixteen Great Countries" and the distribution and status of major cities in India at that time, it reveals the political and geographical pattern of India during this period, pointing out that Brahmin literature ignores the fact of republicanism, while Buddhist and Jain literature provide key evidence for restoring this historical scene.  
Keywords：Rise of Buddhism；Political Divisions in India；Monarchical Kingdoms；Republican Clans；Sixteen Great Countries；Urban Distribution

# 论文4.  最早的攀岩记录考
作者：卡罗琳·A·里斯·戴维斯（Caroline A. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文考证《本生经》中的《六牙象本生》（Chaddanta Jataka）记载了现存最古老的攀岩记录。该故事虽为佛教改编的民间传说（可追溯至亚历山大大帝时期或其后不久），但核心情节源于更古老的民间叙事。故事讲述猎人索努塔拉（Sonuttara）受王后指使，前往喜马拉雅山脉寻找六牙白象王的象牙，途中需穿越多重地形，最终借助攀岩工具（抓钩、绳索、金属支柱等）攀爬悬崖峭壁。作者节选并翻译了攀岩相关段落，分析了猎人使用的装备、攀爬技巧及所处地理环境，指出这一记录虽未涉及冰雪环境，但其攀岩描述具有一定的真实性与研究价值。  
关键词：《本生经》；六牙象本生；攀岩记录；喜马拉雅山脉；民间传说；佛教文献  
Title：The Earliest Rock Climb  
Author：Caroline A. (Foley) Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper verifies that the "Chaddanta Jataka" in the Jataka Tales records the earliest existing rock-climbing record. Although the story is a Buddhist-adapted folk legend (dating approximately to the age of Alexander the Great or shortly thereafter), the core plot originates from older folk narratives. The story tells of the hunter Sonuttara, instructed by the queen, going to the Himalayas to find the tusks of the six-tusked white elephant king. On the way, he had to traverse various terrains and finally climbed cliffs with the help of rock-climbing tools (grappling iron, rope, metal stanchions, etc.). The author excerpts and translates the passages related to rock climbing, analyzes the hunter's equipment, climbing skills and geographical environment, pointing out that although this record does not involve ice and snow environments, its description of rock climbing has certain authenticity and research value.  
Keywords：Jataka Tales；Chaddanta Jataka；Rock-Climb Record；Himalayas；Folk Legend；Buddhist Literature

# 论文5.  三藏目录
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文系统罗列了巴利语三藏（律藏、经藏、小部、论藏）的具体典籍，包含已出版与未出版的文献信息，标注了每部典籍的8开本页数。律藏含《经分别》《犍度》《附随》3类共3部典籍，均已出版，合计1511页；经藏含4部尼柯耶（长部、中部、相应部、增支部），已出版4885页，未出版600页；小部含19部典籍，已出版1124页，未出版1100页；论藏含7部典籍，已出版1089页，未出版2500页。总计已出版8609页，未出版4200页，为巴利语三藏的整理与研究提供了清晰的文献清单。  
关键词：巴利三藏；律藏；经藏；小部；论藏；文献目录  
Title：List of the Pitakas  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically lists the specific texts of the Pali Tipitaka (Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka, Khuddaka Nikaya, Abhidhamma Pitaka), including information on published and unpublished documents, and marks the octavo pages of each text. The Vinaya Pitaka includes 3 categories and 3 texts: Sutta Vibhaṅga, Khandhakas, and Parivāras, all published, totaling 1,511 pages; the Sutta Pitaka includes 4 Nikayas (Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya, Anguttara Nikaya), 4,885 pages published and 600 pages unpublished; the Khuddaka Nikaya includes 19 texts, 1,124 pages published and 1,100 pages unpublished; the Abhidhamma Pitaka includes 7 texts, 1,089 pages published and 2,500 pages unpublished. In total, 8,609 pages have been published and 4,200 pages remain unpublished, providing a clear literature list for the collation and research of the Pali Tipitaka.  
Keywords：Pali Tipitaka；Vinaya Pitaka；Sutta Pitaka；Khuddaka Nikaya；Abhidhamma Pitaka；Literature Catalogue

# 论文6.  巴利圣典协会出版物列表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文以年份排序和字母排序两种方式，梳理了巴利圣典协会1882-1902年的出版物信息。按年份统计，21年间共出版42部文本、51卷，合计15000页，涵盖三藏典籍、注疏、词汇索引等；1903-1905年计划出版《分别论》《大史》《长部》第二、三卷等文献。按字母排序列出了每部文本的出版年份与编者，标注了期刊中收录的文本（以星号标记）。同时明确了订阅政策：年订阅费1畿尼，过刊除1882、1883、1885年（每部2畿尼）外，其余年份均为1畿尼/年。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；出版物列表；出版计划；订阅政策；巴利语文献；文本编者  
Title：Issues of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the publication information of the Pali Text Society from 1882 to 1902 in two ways: chronological order and alphabetical order. According to chronological statistics, 42 texts and 51 volumes have been published in 21 years, totaling 15,000 pages, covering Tipitaka texts, commentaries, vocabulary indices, etc.; the texts planned to be published from 1903 to 1905 include Vibhanga, Mahavamsa, the second and third volumes of Digha Nikaya, etc. Listed alphabetically are the publication year and editor of each text, marking the texts included in the journal (marked with an asterisk). It also clarifies the subscription policy: the annual subscription fee is 1 guinea, and back issues are 1 guinea per year except for 1882, 1883, and 1885 (2 guineas each).  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Publication List；Publishing Plan；Subscription Policy；Pali Literature；Text Editors

# 论文8.  巴利圣典协会1897-1901年报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1897-1901年的工作报告，概述了协会的财务状况、出版物成果及未来计划。期间协会收到捐赠315畿尼，累计捐赠达741英镑17先令，加上斯图迪先生支付的印刷费用，总资助超1000英镑，无债务且资金足以支持2003年前的出版计划。出版成果方面，莱昂·费尔完成《相应部》五卷校勘并将编第六卷索引，埃德蒙·哈迪完成《增支部》五卷校勘，特伦克纳与查尔默斯合作完成《中部》三卷校勘，此外还出版了梅布尔·博德的《教法史》、哈迪校勘的《指导论》等。未来计划出版哈迪校勘的《大史》新译本， Neumann教授暂放弃《无碍解道》校勘，其他多部文献仍在推进中。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；工作报告；财务状况；出版成果；文献校勘；未来计划  
Title：Report of the Pali Text Society, 1897-1901  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the work report of the Pali Text Society from 1897 to 1901, outlining the society's financial situation, publication achievements and future plans. During this period, the society received donations of 315 guineas, with a total donation of £741 17s. Together with the printing fees paid by Mr. Sturdy, the total funding exceeded 1,000 pounds, with no debts and sufficient funds to support the publishing plan up to 2003. In terms of publication achievements, Léon Feer completed the collation of the five volumes of the Samyutta Nikaya and will compile the index for the sixth volume; Edmund Hardy completed the collation of the five volumes of the Anguttara Nikaya; Trenckner and Chalmers collaborated to complete the collation of the three volumes of the Majjhima Nikaya; in addition, Mabel Bode's Sasana Vamsa, Hardy's collation of the Netti Pakarana, etc., were also published. The future plan includes publishing a new edition of the Mahavamsa collated by Hardy; Professor Neumann temporarily abandoned the collation of the Patisambhida; and many other documents are still in progress.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Work Report；Financial Situation；Publication Achievements；Literary Collation；Future Plans
