# 【标题】1884年第1卷【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1884【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  《阿毗达磨摄义论》（《Abhidhammattha-sangaha》）
作者：阿努律陀长老（Anuruddha Thera，原著）；T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids，校勘）  
摘要：本文为12世纪斯里兰卡学者阿努律陀长老所著巴利语佛教学术经典《阿毗达磨摄义论》的校勘本，基于蔡尔德斯手稿、达席尔瓦·古纳塞卡拉转写本及仰光1882年版整理而成。全书分九章，系统梳理阿毗达磨核心教义，涵盖心识分类（善、不善、无记心）、心所法（13种共通心所、14种不善心所等）、杂项分类（受、因、业等）、心路过程、境界分类、色法、总摄分类、因缘分类及修行观行。以简洁的诗体与散文体结合形式，构建了南传佛教心理学与伦理学的理论框架，是研究阿毗达磨文献及南传佛教哲学的关键典籍。  
关键词：《阿毗达磨摄义论》；阿努律陀；阿毗达磨；南传佛教哲学；心识分类；校勘本；巴利语经典  
Title：Abhidhammattha-sangaha  
Author：Anuruddha Thera (Original Author)；T. W. Rhys Davids (Editor)  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali Buddhist academic classic "Abhidhammattha-sangaha" written by the 12th-century Sri Lankan scholar Anuruddha Thera, compiled based on Childers' manuscript, Da Silva Goonesekara's transliterated version and the 1882 Rangoon edition. Divided into nine chapters, the book systematically sorts out the core doctrines of Abhidhamma, covering consciousness classification (wholesome, unwholesome, neutral consciousness), cetasikas (13 universal cetasikas, 14 unwholesome cetasikas, etc.), miscellaneous classifications (vedana, hetu, kamma, etc.), mental processes, sphere classification, rupa, comprehensive classification, paccaya classification and practice contemplation. Combining concise poetic and prose forms, it constructs the theoretical framework of Theravada Buddhist psychology and ethics, being a key text for the study of Abhidhamma literature and Theravada Buddhist philosophy.  
Keywords：Abhidhammattha-sangaha；Anuruddha Thera；Abhidhamma；Theravada Buddhist Philosophy；Consciousness Classification；Collated Edition；Pali Classic

# 论文2.  《油釜伽陀》（《Tela-kataha-gatha》）
作者：埃德蒙·R·古纳拉特纳（Edmund R. Gooneratne，Atapattu Mudaliyar of Galle，校勘）  
摘要：本文为98节巴利语诗歌《油釜伽陀》的校勘本，作者佚名，推测为斯里兰卡佛教僧侣。诗歌以公元前306-207年凯拉尼·提萨王时期的历史事件为背景，讲述一位长老因被怀疑与王后有染，遭判处投入沸油釜处死前的宗教冥想与劝诫。内容融入佛教核心教义，强调无常、苦、无我、不净观等，语言典雅饱含悲悯情怀，既反映了斯里兰卡中世纪巴利语文学的艺术成就，也为研究南传佛教伦理思想与历史传说提供了重要文献。  
关键词：《油釜伽陀》；古纳拉特纳；巴利语诗歌；斯里兰卡佛教；无常观；历史传说；伦理劝诫  
Title：Tela-kataha-gatha  
Author：Edmund R. Gooneratne (Atapattu Mudaliyar of Galle, Editor)  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the 98-stanza Pali poem "Tela-kataha-gatha" by an anonymous author, presumably a Sri Lankan Buddhist monk. Based on a historical event during the reign of King Kelani Tissa (306-207 B.C.), the poem recounts the religious meditations and exhortations of a Thera before being sentenced to death by being thrown into a cauldron of boiling oil on suspicion of having an intrigue with the queen. Integrating core Buddhist doctrines, it emphasizes anicca, dukkha, anatta, asubha-vipassana, etc., with elegant and compassionate language, reflecting the artistic achievements of medieval Sri Lankan Pali literature and providing important materials for the study of Theravada Buddhist ethical thought and historical legends.  
Keywords：Tela-kataha-gatha；Edmund R. Gooneratne；Pali Poem；Sri Lankan Buddhism；Anicca View；Historical Legend；Ethical Exhortation

# 论文3.  巴利语词汇考证与问答
作者：理查德·莫里斯牧师（Rev. Richard Morris, M.A., LL.D.）  
摘要：本文收录莫里斯对巴利语疑难词汇的深度考证与学术问答，聚焦《巴利语词典》未涵盖或释义存疑的术语。内容包括"Atriccha"（极度贪婪）、"Apassena"（枕垫石板）、"Kalasutta"（木匠测量线）等词汇的词源追溯、语义辨析、文献用例梳理及跨语言对比（与梵语、马拉提语等），纠正"Mutthassati"（心不在焉）等术语的传统误读，厘清易混淆词汇的用法差异。考证结合《本生经》《律藏》《弥兰陀王问经》等核心典籍，为巴利语语言学研究与佛教文献准确解读提供关键学术支撑。  
关键词：巴利语；词汇考证；莫里斯；语义辨析；佛教语言学；文献用例；跨语言对比  
Title：Notes and Queries  
Author：Rev. Richard Morris, M.A., LL.D.  
Abstract：This paper collects Rev. Richard Morris' in-depth research and academic questions and answers on difficult Pali vocabulary, focusing on terms not covered or with questionable interpretations in the "Pali Dictionary". The content includes etymological tracing, semantic discrimination, literature example sorting and cross-linguistic comparison (with Sanskrit, Marathi, etc.) of words such as "Atriccha" (excessively covetous), "Apassena" (bolster-slab), "Kalasutta" (carpenter's measuring line), correcting the traditional misreading of terms like "Mutthassati" (inattentive) and clarifying the usage differences of easily confused terms. The research combines core texts such as "Jataka Tales", "Vinaya Pitaka" and "Milinda Panha", providing key academic support for Pali linguistics research and accurate interpretation of Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali；Vocabulary Research；Richard Morris；Semantic Discrimination；Buddhist Linguistics；Literature Examples；Cross-Linguistic Comparison

# 论文4.  《法塔史》（《Datha-vamsa》）
作者：达摩吉帝（Dhammakitti of Pulasti-pura，原著）；T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids，校勘）  
摘要：本文为12世纪斯里兰卡学者达摩吉帝所著巴利语史传文献《法塔史》的校勘本，基于库马拉·斯瓦米的僧伽罗语版、米加穆瓦长老的科伦坡1882年版整理而成。文献以佛陀牙舍利的传承为核心，追溯从佛陀涅槃后舍利分送各地，到斯里兰卡、印度、缅甸等地建造舍利塔的历史，穿插多位国王与高僧护持舍利、弘扬佛法的事迹。文本融合历史叙事与宗教虔诚，反映了南传佛教的舍利崇拜传统，为研究12世纪斯里兰卡佛教史及跨地域佛教文化交流提供重要史料。  
关键词：《法塔史》；达摩吉帝；舍利崇拜；南传佛教史；校勘本；斯里兰卡；文化交流  
Title：Datha-vamsa  
Author：Dhammakitti of Pulasti-pura (Original Author)；T. W. Rhys Davids (Editor)  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali historical text "Datha-vamsa" written by the 12th-century Sri Lankan scholar Dhammakitti of Pulasti-pura, compiled based on Kumara Swamy's Sinhalese version and Migamuwa Unnanse's 1882 Colombo edition. Centered on the transmission of the Buddha's tooth relic, the text traces the history of relic distribution after the Buddha's parinibbana and the construction of stupas in Sri Lanka, India, Burma and other places, interspersed with the deeds of many kings and senior monks protecting the relics and propagating Buddhism. Integrating historical narrative and religious piety, the text reflects the relic worship tradition of Theravada Buddhism, providing important historical materials for the study of 12th-century Sri Lankan Buddhist history and cross-regional Buddhist cultural exchange.  
Keywords：Datha-vamsa；Dhammakitti；Relic Worship；Theravada Buddhist History；Collated Edition；Sri Lanka；Cultural Exchange

# 论文5.  《五趣灯论》（《Panca-gati-dipana》）
作者：莱昂·费尔（M. Léon Feer，校勘）  
摘要：本文为巴利语佛教叙事诗《五趣灯论》的校勘本，基于巴黎国家图书馆藏唯一暹罗梵文手稿整理而成。诗歌分五部分，分别描述地狱、畜生、饿鬼、人类、天道五类众生的生存状态与业报因果：地狱道详述八大热地狱的苦状，畜生道刻画各类动物的相互残杀，饿鬼道展现饥渴折磨，人道阐释善恶业对贫富、寿夭的影响，天道描绘诸天的福报与安乐。以通俗的诗体形式宣扬因果业报思想，是研究南传佛教"五趣"教义及民间佛教信仰的重要文献。  
关键词：《五趣灯论》；莱昂·费尔；五趣；因果业报；南传佛教；叙事诗；校勘本  
Title：Panca-gati-dipana  
Author：M. Léon Feer (Editor)  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali Buddhist narrative poem "Panca-gati-dipana", compiled based on the only Siamese Sanskrit manuscript collected by the Bibliothèque nationale de France. Divided into five parts, the poem describes the living conditions and karmic retribution of beings in the five gatis (hell, animal, hungry ghost, human, deva): the hell gati details the sufferings of the eight great hot hells, the animal gati depicts the mutual killing of various animals, the hungry ghost gati shows the torment of hunger and thirst, the human gati explains the impact of good and bad karma on wealth, poverty, longevity and short life, and the deva gati portrays the blessings and happiness of the gods. Promoting the thought of karmic retribution in a popular poetic form, it is an important text for the study of the Theravada Buddhist doctrine of "five gatis" and folk Buddhist beliefs.  
Keywords：Panca-gati-dipana；M. Léon Feer；Five Gatis；Karmic Retribution；Theravada Buddhism；Narrative Poem；Collated Edition

# 论文6.  巴利圣典协会1884年会员名录
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文收录巴利圣典协会1884年完整会员名单，含捐赠者、六年期会员（五几尼订阅）、年度会员（一几尼订阅）及锡兰地区会员四类，详细标注会员所属地区、机构及通讯地址。会员涵盖全球多地的学者、学术机构、宗教团体及王室成员，包括暹罗国王、大英博物馆、哈佛大学、加尔各答亚洲学会等重要捐赠方与订阅方，锡兰地区会员以寺院高僧与佛教徒为主。名录直观呈现协会在19世纪末南传佛教研究领域的国际影响力与学术网络，为研究近代国际佛教研究共同体的构成提供关键史料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1884年会员名录；学术共同体；南传佛教研究；国际学术网络；捐赠者名单；锡兰会员  
Title：List of Members of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper contains the complete 1884 membership list of the Pali Text Society, including four categories: donors, six-year members (five guineas subscription), annual members (one guinea subscription) and members in Ceylon, with detailed annotations of the members' regions, institutions and correspondence addresses. Members cover scholars, academic institutions, religious groups and royal family members from all over the world, including important donors and subscribers such as the King of Siam, the British Museum, Harvard University and the Asiatic Society of Bengal. Members in Ceylon are mainly senior monks and Buddhists from monasteries. The list intuitively presents the society's international influence and academic network in the field of Theravada Buddhist research at the end of the 19th century, providing key historical materials for the study of the composition of the modern international Buddhist research community.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1884 Membership List；Academic Community；Theravada Buddhist Research；International Academic Network；Donor List；Ceylon Members

# 论文7.  巴利圣典协会1884年财务报告
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1884年度财务收支报告，含英镑与锡兰卢比双币种核算明细。收入项包括1883年余额结转、会员会费、捐赠款、图书销售收入及银行利息；支出项涵盖出版物印刷费、邮资与文具费、手稿采购与装订费、汇率损失等。报告显示年末总余额563英镑14先令3便士，锡兰地区单独列明收支明细，含70名1884年度会员会费及手稿采购支出。报告为研究协会的财务运营模式、学术出版资金来源及成本结构提供量化依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1884年财务报告；收支明细；出版经费；财务运营；双币种核算；成本结构  
Title：Balance Sheet, Etc.  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the 1884 annual financial revenue and expenditure report of the Pali Text Society, including detailed accounts in both British Pounds and Ceylon Rupees. Income items include the carry-over balance from 1883, membership dues, donations, book sales revenue and bank interest; expenditure items cover publication printing fees, postage and stationery fees, manuscript purchase and binding fees, exchange losses, etc. The report shows a total ending balance of 563 Pounds 14 Shillings 3 Pence, with separate revenue and expenditure details listed for Ceylon, including dues from 70 1884 annual members and manuscript purchase expenses. The report provides quantitative basis for the study of the society's financial operation model, academic publishing funding sources and cost structure.

摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会的核心运营章程，明确协会宗旨为整理出版欧洲各图书馆收藏的未刊巴利语早期佛教文献。章程详述文献的历史价值（民俗史、宗教史、语言学）、出版规模（约9000-10000页八开本）、会员订阅标准（年度一几尼或六年五几尼）、捐赠机制等，介绍管理委员会构成及英美、斯里兰卡地区负责人信息，强调协会非营利属性与学术公益定位，提示1885年会费缴纳事宜。章程为了解协会的组织架构、运营规则与学术使命提供权威依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；章程；文献出版；会员制度；组织架构；学术宗旨；非营利组织  
Title：Prospectus of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the core operating charter of the Pali Text Society, clarifying the society's founding purpose as collating and publishing unpublished early Pali Buddhist literature collected in various libraries in Europe. The charter details the historical value of the literature (folk history, religious history, linguistics), publication scale (about 9000-10000 octavo pages), membership subscription standards (one guinea per year or five guineas for six years), donation mechanism, etc., introduces the composition of the management committee and the information of responsible persons in Britain, the United States and Sri Lanka, emphasizing the society's non-profit nature and academic public welfare positioning, and reminding of the 1885 membership dues payment. The charter provides authoritative basis for understanding the society's organizational structure, operating rules and academic mission.

# 论文10.  巴利圣典协会1884年年度报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1884年度工作报告，由主席T·W·里斯·戴维斯撰写。报告回顾协会年度运营情况，会员总数达141人（56名六年期会员、85名年度会员），新增暹罗王室等重要捐赠者；年度出版成果包括《阿毗达磨摄义论》《油釜伽陀》等5部核心典籍，共计464页，另附赠福斯博尔校勘的《小诵》正文230页；介绍《长部》《清净道论》等12部在研项目进展，提及从锡兰获取的19份重要手稿。报告呼吁会员续订会费以支持后续出版，感谢古纳拉特纳等学者为手稿收集提供的支持，为研究协会的学术发展历程与南传佛教文献整理成果提供第一手资料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1884年年度报告；典籍出版；会员发展；学术规划；文献整理；手稿收集  
Title：Report for 1884  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the 1884 annual work report of the Pali Text Society, written by the Chairman T. W. Rhys Davids. The report reviews the society's annual operation, with a total of 141 members (56 six-year members and 85 annual members), adding important donors such as the Siamese royal family; the annual publication achievements include 5 core texts such as "Abhidhammattha-sangaha" and "Tela-kataha-gatha", totaling 464 pages, plus 230 pages of the text of "Sutta Nipata" collated by Fausboll as a bonus; it introduces the progress of 12 ongoing projects such as "Digha Nikaya" and "Visuddhi Magga", and mentions 19 important manuscripts obtained from Ceylon. The report calls on members to renew their dues to support subsequent publications, thanks scholars such as Gooneratne for their support in manuscript collection, providing first-hand materials for the study of the society's academic development process and the achievements of Theravada Buddhist literature collation.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1884 Annual Report；Text Publication；Membership Development；Academic Planning；Literature Collation；Manuscript Collection
