# 【标题】1894-1896年合刊【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1896【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利圣典协会1894-1896年报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1894-1896年的工作报告，详述了协会的财务支持、出版进展及未来计划。期间协会获亨利·C·沃伦等捐赠，爱德华·T·斯图迪先生资助多部论著印刷，虽因印刷厂火灾导致部分文本延迟，但核心出版任务稳步推进。埃德蒙·哈迪接替莫里斯博士完成《增支部》相关校勘，莱昂·费尔推进《相应部》第五卷出版，罗伯特·查尔默斯完成《中部》第二卷部分内容并计划编制完整索引。未来将继续推进《无碍解道》《小诵》等典籍校勘，致力于完善早期佛教核心文献的整理与出版。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；工作报告；文献校勘；出版进展；财务支持；佛教文献  
Title：Report of the Pali Text Society (1894-1896)  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the work report of the Pali Text Society from 1894 to 1896, detailing the society's financial support, publication progress and future plans. During this period, the society received donations from Henry C. Warren and others, and Mr. Edward T. Sturdy funded the printing of several treatises. Although some texts were delayed due to a fire at the printing factory, the core publishing tasks progressed steadily. Edmund Hardy succeeded Dr. Morris in completing the collation related to the Anguttara Nikaya, Léon Feer advanced the publication of the fifth volume of the Samyutta Nikaya, and Robert Chalmers completed part of the second volume of the Majjhima Nikaya and planned to compile a complete index. In the future, it will continue to promote the collation of texts such as the Patisambhida and Niddesa, committed to improving the collation and publication of core early Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Work Report；Textual Collation；Publication Progress；Financial Support；Buddhist Literature

# 论文2.  印度事务部图书馆曼德勒手稿目录（原属曼德勒王宫图书馆）
作者：V·福斯博尔教授（Professor V. Fausböll）  
摘要：本文整理了印度事务部图书馆收藏的原曼德勒王宫图书馆手稿目录，该批手稿约1150部，现仅存300余部，其余或遭劫掠或遗失。手稿分为纯巴利语、纯缅甸语及巴利语-缅甸语混合三类（含巴利语文本注疏），以缅甸文字书写于棕榈叶，多有9行/页的版式，部分带烫金装帧。目录按律藏、经藏、论藏、教义、历史、语法修辞等分类，详细记录了每部手稿的编号、页数、内容概要及版本特征，校正了手稿中常见的书写错误，为南传佛教文献研究提供了珍贵的原始文献线索。  
关键词：曼德勒手稿；印度事务部图书馆；目录整理；巴利语手稿；缅甸语手稿；南传佛教文献  
Title：Catalogue of the Mandalay MSS. in the India Office Library (Formerly part of the King's Library at Mandalay)  
Author：Professor V. Fausböll  
Abstract：This paper compiles the catalogue of manuscripts from the former Mandalay Royal Library collected by the India Office Library. The collection originally included about 1,150 manuscripts, but only over 300 survive today, with the rest looted or lost. The manuscripts are divided into three categories: pure Pali, pure Burmese, and mixed Pali-Burmese (including commentaries on Pali texts), written in Burmese script on palm leaves, mostly with a format of 9 lines per page, and some with gilt bindings. The catalogue is classified by Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka, Doctrine, History, Grammar and Rhetoric, etc., detailing the number, page count, content summary and version characteristics of each manuscript, correcting common writing errors in the manuscripts, and providing valuable original literature clues for the study of Theravada Buddhist texts.  
Keywords：Mandalay MSS.；India Office Library；Catalogue Compilation；Pali Manuscripts；Burmese Manuscripts；Theravada Buddhist Literature

# 论文3.  《香史》索引
作者：梅布尔·博德（Mabel Bode）  
摘要：本文为米纳耶夫校勘本《香史》（Gandhavamsa）编制索引，以1886年巴利圣典协会学报刊载的文本页码为参照，分为作者与典籍、地名与国王、无名著作三类。索引收录了《香史》中提及的佛教典籍、作者、创作背景等关键信息，涵盖三藏文献、注疏、语法著作等，标注了各典籍的出版情况及馆藏信息，厘清了不同作者的地域归属与作品关联，为研究南传佛教文献传承史提供了便捷的检索工具。  
关键词：《香史》；索引；南传佛教；文献传承；作者；典籍  
Title：Index to the Gandhavamsa  
Author：Mabel Bode  
Abstract：This paper compiles an index to Minayeff's collated edition of the Gandhavamsa, with references to the page numbers of the text published in the Journal of the Pali Text Society in 1886, divided into three categories: Authors and Books, Places and Kings, and Works without Names. The index collects key information such as Buddhist texts, authors, and creation backgrounds mentioned in the Gandhavamsa, covering Tipitaka texts, commentaries, grammatical works, etc., marks the publication status and collection information of each text, clarifies the regional affiliation and work connections of different authors, and provides a convenient retrieval tool for the study of the transmission history of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Gandhavamsa；Index；Theravada Buddhism；Literary Transmission；Authors；Texts

# 论文4.  印度佛教徒遭受的迫害
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文对印度佛教徒是否遭受系统性迫害的历史记载进行考证。通过分析玄奘游记、《大事》《岛史》等文献，指出所谓“迫害”多为战争劫掠、政治冲突或传说演绎，如米希拉库拉入侵犍陀罗、商羯罗的宗教竞争等，均缺乏明确的宗教迫害证据。早期巴利文献中未提及宗教迫害，佛教与婆罗门教等长期和平共存。印度佛教遗址的毁坏多与战争、世俗冲突相关，而非宗教迫害。文中还补充了尼泊尔及南印度本土学者的相关观点，但认为其缺乏实证支持，难以改变“印度佛教未遭大规模宗教迫害”的核心结论。  
关键词：印度佛教；宗教迫害；历史考证；文献分析；宗教共存；佛教遗址  
Title：Persecution of Buddhists in India  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper examines historical records of whether Buddhists in India suffered systematic persecution. By analyzing texts such as Xuanzang's Travels, the Divyavadana, and the Dipavamsa, it points out that the so-called "persecution" was mostly war looting, political conflicts or legendary interpretations. For example, Mihirakula's invasion of Gandhara and Sankara's religious competition all lack clear evidence of religious persecution. Early Pali texts do not mention religious persecution, and Buddhism coexisted peacefully with Brahmanism for a long time. The destruction of Buddhist sites in India was mostly related to wars and secular conflicts, not religious persecution. The paper also supplements the relevant views of native scholars in Nepal and South India, but argues that they lack empirical support and are difficult to change the core conclusion that "Indian Buddhism did not suffer large-scale religious persecution."  
Keywords：Indian Buddhism；Religious Persecution；Historical Research；Literary Analysis；Religious Coexistence；Buddhist Sites

# 论文5.  阿育王巴布拉敕令中若干头衔注释
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文对阿育王巴布拉敕令中提及的若干佛教文献头衔进行考释。通过比对《大事》与巴利语三藏文献，指出敕令中的“Sailagatha”对应巴利语《塞拉经》（Sela Sutta），“Munigatha”对应《牟尼经》（Muni Sutta），“Atthakavaggiyani”为《八颂集》相关文本。文中还考证了敕令推荐的七部佛教文献片段，确认其中《未来怖畏经》（Anagatabhayani）源自《增支部》，并附上 Buddhaghosa对该经的注疏文本，为解读阿育王时期的佛教文献传承及敕令内涵提供了关键依据。  
关键词：阿育王；巴布拉敕令；文献头衔；巴利语三藏；注疏；佛教传承  
Title：Note on Some of the Titles used in the Bhabra Edict of Asoka  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper interprets several Buddhist text titles mentioned in Asoka's Bhabra Edict. By comparing the Divyavadana with the Pali Tipitaka, it points out that "Sailagatha" in the edict corresponds to the Pali Sela Sutta, "Munigatha" corresponds to the Muni Sutta, and "Atthakavaggiyani" refers to texts related to the Atthaka-vagga. The paper also examines seven Buddhist text excerpts recommended in the edict, confirms that the Anagatabhayani among them is derived from the Anguttara Nikaya, and attaches Buddhaghosa's commentary on this sutta, providing key basis for interpreting the transmission of Buddhist literature and the connotation of the edict during Asoka's reign.  
Keywords：Asoka；Bhabra Edict；Text Titles；Pali Tipitaka；Commentary；Buddhist Transmission

# 论文6.  Buddhaghosa对《未来怖畏经》的注疏
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文收录了 Buddhaghosa对《未来怖畏经》（Anagatabhayani）的完整注疏文本。注疏将“未来怖畏”分为外在与内在两类，外在怖畏包括蛇害、疾病、野兽、盗贼等十种威胁隐士修行的因素，内在怖畏则涉及教义与戒律败坏、懈怠奢华等十种僧团隐患。注疏详细阐释了每种怖畏的内涵及应对之道，强调修行者应以外在怖畏为动力、以内在怖畏为警示，坚守禅定与观智修行。该注疏为理解《未来怖畏经》的教义核心及阿育王推崇该经的原因提供了权威解读。  
关键词：Buddhaghosa；《未来怖畏经》；注疏；佛教修行；怖畏；禅定观智  
Title：Buddhaghosa's Commentary on the Anagata Bhayani  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper collects the complete commentary text of Buddhaghosa on the Anagata Bhayani. The commentary divides "future fears" into external and internal categories. External fears include ten factors threatening hermits' practice such as snake harm, disease, wild beasts, and robbers; internal fears involve ten monastic hidden dangers such as the corruption of doctrine and discipline, and laziness and luxury. The commentary elaborates on the connotation and response methods of each fear in detail, emphasizing that practitioners should take external fears as motivation and internal fears as warnings, and adhere to samatha and vipassana practice. This commentary provides an authoritative interpretation for understanding the core doctrine of the Anagata Bhayani and the reason why Asoka advocated this sutta.  
Keywords：Buddhaghosa；Anagata Bhayani；Commentary；Buddhist Practice；Fears；Samatha and Vipassana

# 论文7.  巴利语典籍标题缩写规范
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文提出一套统一的巴利语典籍标题缩写规范，旨在为词典编纂、文本注释等学术工作提供便捷。规范遵循“三藏典籍单字母缩写、后期文献三字母缩写”的原则，对核心三藏文献（如《长部》《中部》《律藏》）及重要注疏、语法著作等进行缩写命名，并明确了每种典籍的引用格式（按卷页、偈颂、章节等分类）。该规范有助于统一学术表达，减少不同学者使用不同缩写导致的混淆，推动南传佛教文献研究的标准化。  
关键词：巴利语典籍；标题缩写；学术规范；引用格式；南传佛教；文献研究  
Title：Abbreviations of Titles of Pali Books  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper proposes a set of unified abbreviation norms for Pali text titles, aiming to provide convenience for academic work such as dictionary compilation and text annotation. Following the principle of "single-letter abbreviations for Tipitaka texts and three-letter abbreviations for later texts," the norms name core Tipitaka texts (such as Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, Vinaya Pitaka) and important commentaries, grammatical works, etc., with abbreviations, and clarify the citation format of each text (classified by volume and page, verse, chapter, etc.). This set of norms helps unify academic expression, reduce confusion caused by different scholars using different abbreviations, and promote the standardization of Theravada Buddhist literature research.  
Keywords：Pali Books；Title Abbreviations；Academic Norms；Citation Format；Theravada Buddhism；Literary Research

# 论文8.  印度佛教徒遭受迫害的补充注释
作者：约翰内斯·布勒（Johannes Bühler）  
摘要：本文为“印度佛教徒遭受的迫害”一文提供补充考证，收录了布勒博士关于公元800年后西印度及南印度佛教徒生存状况的铭文证据。铭文显示，9-12世纪期间，坎赫里、丹巴尔等地的佛教僧团仍获官方与民间资助，科尔哈普尔首领甚至为佛教造像捐赠土地，表明佛教在该时期仍有一定影响力。文中还引用中国文献中关于米希拉库拉迫害佛教徒的记载，指出其可能存在宗教迫害行为，但整体仍缺乏系统性迫害的实证，进一步支持了“印度佛教衰亡非主要因宗教迫害”的观点。  
关键词：印度佛教；迫害；补充考证；铭文证据；佛教生存状况；文献补充  
Title：Further Note on Persecutions of Buddhists in India  
Author：Johannes Bühler  
Abstract：This paper provides supplementary research for the article "Persecution of Buddhists in India," collecting epigraphic evidence from Dr. Bühler on the living conditions of Buddhists in Western and Southern India after 800 A.D. The inscriptions show that during the 9th-12th centuries, Buddhist monastic communities in Kanheri, Dambal and other places still received official and private funding, and the ruler of Kolhapur even donated land for Buddhist statues, indicating that Buddhism still had a certain influence during this period. The paper also quotes records from Chinese texts about Mihirakula's persecution of Buddhists, pointing out that he may have engaged in religious persecution, but overall there is still a lack of empirical evidence for systematic persecution, further supporting the view that "the decline of Indian Buddhism was not mainly due to religious persecution."  
Keywords：Indian Buddhism；Persecution；Supplementary Research；Epigraphic Evidence；Buddhist Living Conditions；Literary Supplement

# 论文9.  三藏目录

Title：List of the Pitakas  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically lists the catalogues and publication status of the Pali Tipitaka (Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka, Khuddaka Nikaya, Abhidhamma Pitaka). The catalogue clarifies the printed pages and unpublished pages of each text, providing a clear quantitative basis for the collation and publication planning of the Pali Tipitaka.

Title：Issues of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper sorts out the publication information of the Pali Text Society from 1882 to 1896 in two ways: chronological order and alphabetical order. According to chronological statistics, 40 texts and 45 volumes have been published in 15 years, totaling 13,000 pages, covering Tipitaka texts, commentaries, indices, etc.; the texts planned to be published from 1897 to 1899 include Attha Salini, Sasana Vamsa, the fifth volume of the Samyutta Nikaya, etc. Listed alphabetically are the publication year and editor of each text, marking the texts included in the journal (marked with an asterisk), providing a convenient tool for researchers to retrieve the publication achievements of the Pali Text Society.
