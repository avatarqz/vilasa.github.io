# 【标题】1883年第1卷【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1883【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  佛教：其起源、历史、教义、经典及语言（巴利语）
作者：詹姆斯·阿尔维斯（James Alwis）  
摘要：本文收录詹姆斯·阿尔维斯于1861年在科伦坡发表的两场演讲，系统梳理佛教核心内容。第一讲聚焦佛教起源与教义，追溯公元前6世纪印度宗教环境，介绍六位外道思想家的主张，详述乔达摩·佛陀的生平、弃世修行历程及佛教传播的社会背景，阐释佛教“无常、苦、无我”核心教义、涅槃概念及对种姓制度的突破；第二讲围绕佛教经典与巴利语，探讨经典的口传与成文历史、文本传承脉络，考证巴利语的起源与性质，辨析其与梵语、摩揭陀语的关系，强调其作为佛教圣典语言的重要性。演讲结合大量史料与文献引证，为理解佛教的历史发展与文化价值提供全面视角。  
关键词：佛教；起源；教义；巴利语；佛教经典；种姓制度；涅槃；演讲  
Title：Buddhism: Its Origin; History; and Doctrines; Its Scriptures; and Their Language, the Pali  
Author：James Alwis  
Abstract：This paper collects two lectures delivered by James Alwis in Colombo in 1861, systematically sorting out the core content of Buddhism. The first lecture focuses on the origin and doctrines of Buddhism, tracing the religious environment in India in the 6th century B.C., introducing the views of six heterodox thinkers, detailing the life of Gotama Buddha, his journey of renouncing the world and practicing asceticism, and the social background of Buddhism's spread, explaining the core Buddhist doctrines of "anicca, dukkha, anatta", the concept of nibbana, and its breakthrough against the caste system; the second lecture centers on Buddhist scriptures and the Pali language, discussing the oral and written history of the scriptures, the context of text transmission, researching the origin and nature of Pali, distinguishing its relationship with Sanskrit and Magadhi, and emphasizing its importance as the sacred language of Buddhist scriptures. The lectures combine a large number of historical materials and literary citations, providing a comprehensive perspective for understanding the historical development and cultural value of Buddhism.  
Keywords：Buddhism；Origin；Doctrines；Pali Language；Buddhist Scriptures；Caste System；Nibbana；Lectures

# 论文2.  纪念已故肯久·笠原
作者：马克斯·穆勒（Max Müller）  
摘要：本文是马克斯·穆勒为已故日本佛教学者肯久·笠原撰写的讣告及补充笔记，最初发表于《泰晤士报》。讣告详述笠原的学术生涯：1876年受日本寺院派遣赴英，在牛津学习梵语与巴利语，专注佛教经典研究，治学勤勉、品格高尚，后因肺结核病逝于日本。补充笔记中，穆勒整理笠原未竟的《法集论》（Dharmasaṅgraha）翻译手稿，考证龙树菩萨相关文献的作者归属、年代及核心内容，探讨《龙树菩萨劝诫王颂》的文本流传与汉语、藏语译本差异，为佛教文献学研究提供珍贵一手资料。  
关键词：肯久·笠原；马克斯·穆勒；佛教研究；《法集论》；龙树菩萨；文献考证；讣告  
Title：The Late Kenjiu Kasawara  
Author：Max Müller  
Abstract：This paper is an obituary and supplementary notes written by Max Müller for the late Japanese Buddhist scholar Kenjiu Kasawara, originally published in The Times. The obituary details Kasawara's academic career: he was sent to Britain by a Japanese monastery in 1876, studied Sanskrit and Pali at Oxford, focused on Buddhist scripture research, was diligent in scholarship and noble in character, and later passed away in Japan due to pulmonary tuberculosis. In the supplementary notes, Müller collates Kasawara's unfinished translation manuscript of "Dharmasaṅgraha", researches the author attribution, age and core content of documents related to Nagarjuna Bodhisattva, discusses the text transmission of "Arya-Nagarjuna-bodhisattva-suhṛllekha" and the differences between Chinese and Tibetan translations, providing valuable first-hand materials for Buddhist philology research.  
Keywords：Kenjiu Kasawara；Max Müller；Buddhist Research；Dharmasaṅgraha；Nagarjuna Bodhisattva；Textual Research；Obituary

# 论文3.  《大品》段落考释与问答
作者：塞西尔·本德尔（Cecil Bendall）  
摘要：本文针对奥登伯格校勘的《律藏》（Vinaya Pitaka）中《大品》（Mahavagga）的特定段落展开深度考释。聚焦“Brahmayacana katha”章节结尾的偈颂，辨析“pamuccantu saddham”等核心词句的语义争议，对比巴利语与梵语译本（如《方广大庄严经》相关段落）、藏语译本的差异，引用巴黎国家图书馆藏缅甸文、僧伽罗文注释本及暹罗文手稿进行佐证。同时，对“yoniso manasikara”“vijanavatam”等术语的词源与用法提出新解，为《律藏》的精准解读与巴利语语言学研究提供学术参考。  
关键词：《大品》；《律藏》；巴利语；段落考释；术语辨析；译本对比；文献佐证  
Title：Notes and Queries on Passages in the Mahavagga  
Author：Cecil Bendall  
Abstract：This paper conducts in-depth research and explanation on specific passages in the Mahavagga of the Vinaya Pitaka collated by Oldenberg. Focusing on the gatha at the end of the chapter "Brahmayacana katha", it analyzes the semantic disputes of core words and phrases such as "pamuccantu saddham", compares the differences between Pali and Sanskrit translations (such as relevant passages in the Lalita-vistara), and Tibetan translations, citing Burmese, Sinhalese commentaries and Siamese manuscripts collected by the Bibliothèque nationale de France for evidence. At the same time, it puts forward new interpretations on the etymology and usage of terms such as "yoniso manasikara" and "vijanavatam", providing academic reference for the accurate interpretation of the Vinaya Pitaka and Pali linguistics research.  
Keywords：Mahavagga；Vinaya Pitaka；Pali；Passage Exegesis；Term Discrimination；Translation Comparison；Literary Evidence

# 论文4.  《小戒经》与《根本戒经》
作者：爱德华·米勒（Edward Müller）  
摘要：本文为巴利语佛教律典《小戒经》（Khuddasikkha）与《根本戒经》（Mulasikkha）的校勘本，两部文献以诗体为主、散文为辅，是《律藏》的简明概要。米勒基于缅甸文手稿及12世纪僧伽罗文注释本进行整理，考证文献年代（推测为公元6-7世纪或更早），辨析其语言特征与巴利语经典的差异。内容涵盖比丘戒的核心规范，包括四波罗夷、僧残、舍堕等戒律类别，附设与奥登伯格校勘《律藏》的段落对照表，为研究斯里兰卡佛教戒律传承与律典文本演变提供重要文献。  
关键词：《小戒经》；《根本戒经》；律藏；巴利语；校勘本；佛教戒律；文本对照  
Title：Khuddasikkha and Mulasikkha  
Author：Edward Müller  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali Buddhist vinaya texts Khuddasikkha and Mulasikkha, which are concise summaries of the Vinaya Pitaka, mainly in verse with supplementary prose. Müller compiled them based on Burmese manuscripts and 12th-century Sinhalese commentaries, researched the age of the documents (speculated to be the 6th-7th century A.D. or earlier), and distinguished their linguistic characteristics from other Pali classics. The content covers the core norms of bhikkhu precepts, including four parajika, sanghadisesa, nissaggiya, etc., with a comparative table of passages with Oldenberg's collated Vinaya Pitaka, providing important documents for the study of the inheritance of Buddhist precepts in Sri Lanka and the evolution of vinaya texts.  
Keywords：Khuddasikkha；Mulasikkha；Vinaya Pitaka；Pali；Collated Edition；Buddhist Precepts；Text Comparison

# 论文5.  大英博物馆藏巴利语手稿目录
作者：赫宁博士（Dr. Hoerning）  
摘要：本文系统梳理大英博物馆东方手稿部收藏的巴利语手稿，按《律藏》《经藏》《论藏》及语法文献分类，详细记录每部手稿的标题、叶数、书写字体（僧伽罗文、缅甸文、高棉文等）、载体（棕榈叶、象牙、铜版等）及馆藏编号。手稿涵盖《铜鍱律》《长部》《中部》《相应部》《阿毗达磨集论》等核心典籍，以及注疏、语法著作，为研究者检索巴利语原始文献提供权威指引，反映19世纪末欧洲对南传佛教文献的收藏与整理现状。  
关键词：大英博物馆；巴利语手稿；目录；南传佛教；文献收藏；核心典籍；检索指引  
Title：List of Pali Manuscripts in the British Museum  
Author：Dr. Hoerning  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the Pali manuscripts collected by the Department of Oriental MSS. of the British Museum, classifying them into Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka and grammatical documents, and detailing the title, number of leaves, writing character (Sinhalese, Burmese, Khmer, etc.), material (palm leaves, ivory, copper plates, etc.) and collection number of each manuscript. The manuscripts include core texts such as the Culavaggo, Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya, Dhammasangani, as well as commentaries and grammatical works, providing authoritative guidance for researchers to retrieve original Pali documents and reflecting the collection and collation status of Theravada Buddhist documents in Europe at the end of the 19th century.  
Keywords：British Museum；Pali Manuscripts；Catalogue；Theravada Buddhism；Literary Collection；Core Texts；Retrieval Guide

Title：List of Pali Manuscripts in the Cambridge University Library  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：Compiled by T. W. Rhys Davids, this paper collects the list of Pali manuscripts held by the Cambridge University Library, arranged in alphabetical order, marking the title, writing character (Sinhalese, Burmese, etc.), number of leaves and collection number of each manuscript. # 论文8.  斯德哥尔摩藏巴利语与僧伽罗语手稿目录
作者：E·W·达尔格伦（E. W. Dahlgren，整理）  
摘要：本文整理斯德哥尔摩瑞典人类学与地理学会收藏的巴利语及僧伽罗语手稿，这些手稿由诺登舍尔德男爵在锡兰收集。清单收录11部核心文献，包括《梵网经》《大念处经》《大沙门果经》等巴利语经典（附僧伽罗语注释）、《辞汇灯论》（Abhidhânappadîpikâ）、语法著作《Pada-rûpa-siddhi》及僧伽罗语散文作品，详细记录每部手稿的载体（棕榈叶）、叶数、每页行数及页码标识。目录填补了北欧巴利语文献收藏的记录空白，为跨区域文献研究提供重要线索。  
关键词：斯德哥尔摩；巴利语手稿；僧伽罗语手稿；目录；锡兰收集；经典注释；跨区域研究  
Title：List of Pali and Sinhalese Manuscripts at Stockholm  
Author：E. W. Dahlgren (Arranger)  
Abstract：This paper arranges the Pali and Sinhalese manuscripts collected by the Swedish Society of Anthropology and Geography in Stockholm, which were collected in Ceylon by Baron Nordenskiold. The list includes 11 core documents, including Pali classics such as Brahmajala Sutta, Mahasatipatthana Sutta, Mahasamaya Sutta (with Sinhalese commentaries), Abhidhânappadîpikâ, grammatical work Pada-rûpa-siddhi and Sinhalese prose works, detailing the material (palm leaves), number of leaves, number of lines per page and page number marking of each manuscript. The catalogue fills the gap in the records of Pali literature collections in Northern Europe, providing important clues for cross-regional literary research.  
Keywords：Stockholm；Pali Manuscripts；Sinhalese Manuscripts；Catalogue；Ceylon Collection；Classic Commentaries；Cross-Regional Research

Title：Members of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society

The report calls on members to actively develop new subscribers, responds to feedback from Sri Lankan scholars on text abbreviations, and emphasizes the society's core role in the collation and dissemination of Theravada Buddhist documents.

# 论文11.  巴利圣典协会章程
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）

Title：Prospectus of the Society

# 论文12.  巴利圣典协会1883年财务报告
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1883年度财务收支报告，详细列明英镑收支明细。收入项包括1882年余额结转、会员会费、捐赠款及图书销售收入；支出项涵盖出版物印刷费、邮资与文具费、手稿采购与装订费等。报告显示年度总余额稳定增长，锡兰地区单独列明收支情况，为研究协会的财务健康状况、学术出版资金保障及成本结构提供量化数据，体现协会透明的财务管理模式。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1883年财务报告；收支明细；出版经费；财务状况；成本结构；财务管理  
Title：Balance Sheet  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the 1883 annual financial revenue and expenditure report of the Pali Text Society, detailing the revenue and expenditure in British Pounds. Income items include the carry-over balance from 1882, membership dues, donations and book sales revenue; expenditure items cover publication printing fees, postage and stationery fees, manuscript purchase and binding fees, etc. The report shows a steady growth in the annual total balance, with separate revenue and expenditure details listed for Ceylon, providing quantitative data for the study of the society's financial health, academic publishing funding guarantee and cost structure, reflecting the society's transparent financial management mode.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1883 Financial Report；Revenue and Expenditure Details；Publishing Funds；Financial Status；Cost Structure；Financial Management

# 论文13.  巴利圣典协会已出版与在研典籍清单（至1883年）
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文分为两部分，第一部分梳理协会至1883年已出版的8部巴利语典籍，按出版年份排序，标注每部典籍的书名、编者与出版时间，涵盖经藏、律藏、论藏及史传文献；第二部分列出12部在研项目，包括《长部》《相应部》《清净道论》等核心典籍，明确每部著作的编者与进展情况，同时标注待招募编者的典籍清单及预估篇幅。清单全面呈现协会的学术出版规划与成果，为研究者检索巴利语典籍版本信息提供权威指引。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；已出版典籍；在研项目；文献清单；南传佛教；出版规划；版本信息  
Title：Works Published and in Progress  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is divided into two parts. The first part sorts out 8 Pali texts published by the society up to 1883, arranged by publication year, marking the title, editor and publication time of each text, covering Sutta Pitaka, Vinaya Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka and historical texts; the second part lists 12 ongoing projects, including core texts such as Digha Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya and Visuddhi Magga, specifying the editor and progress of each work, and marking the list of texts for which editors are to be recruited and their estimated length. The list comprehensively presents the society's academic publishing planning and achievements, providing authoritative guidance for researchers to retrieve version information of Pali texts.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Published Works；Ongoing Projects；Literature List；Theravada Buddhism；Publishing Planning；Version Information

# 论文14.  佛陀
作者：A·C·本森（A. C. Benson）  
摘要：本文是A·C·本森创作的一首宗教抒情诗，最初发表于《观察家报》。诗歌以优美典雅的语言歌颂佛陀的慈悲精神与教义价值，聚焦佛陀“为众生流泪、承受痛苦”的利他品格，将其教义比作“通往不朽的门户”，赞美佛教跨越两千余年、影响无数信众的深远力量。诗歌融合宗教感悟与文学审美，从西方视角表达对佛陀与佛教的崇敬之情，为理解佛教在西方的文化接受提供文学例证。  
关键词：佛陀；抒情诗；佛教；慈悲精神；西方视角；文化接受；文学表达  
Title：Buddha  
Author：A. C. Benson  
Abstract：This paper is a religious lyric poem written by A. C. Benson, originally published in the Spectator. With elegant and graceful language, the poem eulogizes the compassionate spirit and doctrinal value of Buddha, focusing on his altruistic character of "weeping for all beings and bearing pain", comparing his teachings to "the door to immortality", and praising the profound power of Buddhism that has influenced countless believers for more than 2,000 years. Integrating religious perception and literary aesthetics, the poem expresses reverence for Buddha and Buddhism from a Western perspective, providing a literary example for understanding the cultural acceptance of Buddhism in the West.  
Keywords：Buddha；Lyric Poem；Buddhism；Compassionate Spirit；Western Perspective；Cultural Acceptance；Literary Expression
