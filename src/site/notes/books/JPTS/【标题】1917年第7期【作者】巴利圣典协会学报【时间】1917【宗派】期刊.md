# 【标题】1917年第7期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报【时间】1917【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 中文题目：《真理概要》（Sacca-Sankhepa）
作者：达磨波罗（Dhammapala）；编者：法 Rama 比丘（P. Dhammaramā, Bhikkhu）  
摘要：本文首次以罗马字母出版《真理概要》，该作品是缅甸归类的九部中世纪早期阿毗达磨小手册（Let-thaw手册）之一，位列经典。作者并非著名注释家达磨波罗，而是其弟子小达磨波罗（Culla-Dhammapala）。文本分为五章，分别探讨色法分析、受等蕴分析、心的生起、杂集分析及涅槃相关内容，为填补中世纪巴利文学研究空白提供了重要文献。编者整理了僧伽成员提供的多种手稿与印刷版本，经校勘后呈现，虽仍有部分晦涩文句未完全厘清，但力求保留原文风貌。  
关键词：真理概要；巴利文学；阿毗达磨；中世纪佛教；文本校勘  
Title：Sacca-Sankhepa  
Author：Dhammapala; Edited by P. Dhammaramā, Bhikkhu  
Abstract：This paper publishes the Sacca-Sankhepa in Roman script for the first time. It is one of the nine early medieval Abhidhamma manuals (Let-thaw manuals) categorized in Burma and ranks as a classic. The author is not the famous commentator Dhammapala, but his disciple Culla-Dhammapala. The text is divided into five chapters, discussing the analysis of rūpa (form), the analysis of vedanā (feeling) and other khandhas (aggregates), the arising of citta (mind), the analysis of miscellaneous collections, and content related to nibbāna (nirvana), providing important literature for filling the gaps in the study of medieval Pali literature. The editor collated various manuscripts and printed editions provided by members of the Sangha and presented them after textual criticism. Although some obscure sentences remain unclear, efforts were made to preserve the original style of the text.  
Keywords：Sacca-Sankhepa; Pali Literature; Abhidhamma; Medieval Buddhism; Textual Criticism

# 论文2. 中文题目：《古代思想中的宇宙法则》
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文挑战了泰勒《原始文化》中将宗教起源单一归因于万物有灵论（Animism）的观点，提出“常态论”（Normalism）这一平行思想体系。常态论指人类对自然与社会中固有规则、秩序的信仰，无需神灵或灵魂干预，如中国的“道”、印度的“梨多”（Rta）、希腊哲学中对立元素互动理论等。作者通过对比中国、印度、两河流域等文明的文献，论证常态论与万物有灵论在古代长期并存、相互影响，指出宗教定义需纳入这一非神灵中心的信仰维度，丰富了对古代宗教思想多样性的认知。  
关键词：宇宙法则；万物有灵论；常态论；古代宗教；比较宗教学  
Title：Cosmic Law in Ancient Thought  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper challenges Tylor's view in "Primitive Culture" that attributes the origin of religion solely to Animism, proposing the "Normalism" as a parallel ideological system. Normalism refers to human belief in inherent rules and order in nature and society, independent of the intervention of gods or souls, such as the Chinese "Tao", the Indian "Rta", and the theory of interaction between opposing elements in Greek philosophy. By comparing documents from civilizations such as China, India, and Mesopotamia, the author argues that Normalism and Animism coexisted and interacted for a long time in ancient times, pointing out that the definition of religion needs to include this non-theocentric belief dimension, enriching the understanding of the diversity of ancient religious thought.  
Keywords：Cosmic Law; Animism; Normalism; Ancient Religion; Comparative Religion

# 论文3. 中文题目：《词典学注释》
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）、W. 施泰德（W. Stede）  
摘要：本文收录巴利语词典编纂过程中的关键注释，聚焦多个核心巴利语词汇的词源、语义演变、用法及文献例证。涵盖“无始”（Anamatagga）、“近”（Abhi）、“殊胜智”（Abhinna）、“阿毗达磨”（Abhidhamma）、“洪流”（Ogha）等术语，对比了柴尔德斯（Childers）等前辈学者的研究成果，补充了大量新文献依据与方言变体分析，为巴利语词汇的精准解读与词典完善提供了重要支撑。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词汇注释；语义演变；文献例证  
Title：Lexicographical Notes  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids, W. Stede  
Abstract：This paper collects key notes from the compilation process of the Pali Dictionary, focusing on the etymology, semantic evolution, usage, and literary examples of several core Pali words. It covers terms such as "Anamatagga" (without beginning), "Abhi" (near), "Abhinna" (supernormal knowledge), "Abhidhamma", and "Ogha" (flood), compares the research results of previous scholars such as Childers, supplements a large number of new literary evidence and dialectal variation analyses, and provides important support for the accurate interpretation of Pali words and the improvement of the dictionary.  
Keywords：Pali; Lexicography; Lexical Notes; Semantic Evolution; Literary Examples

# 论文4. 中文题目：《解脱道》——巴利语《清净道论》的中文对应文本
作者：永井正（M. Nagai）  
摘要：本文介绍了公元505年由柬埔寨僧人僧伽婆罗翻译的中文佛典《解脱道论》（Vimutti-magga），指出其与巴利语《清净道论》（Visuddhi-magga）在形式与内容上高度关联。作者考证《解脱道论》的原作者为公元1世纪斯里兰卡高僧优波底沙（Upatissa），早于觉音（Buddhaghosa）的《清净道论》，推测觉音编纂《清净道论》时参考了该文本。通过对比两书的篇章结构（戒、定、慧、解脱）与具体内容，分析了二者的异同，揭示了早期佛教文献在南亚、东南亚及中国的传播与演变轨迹。  
关键词：解脱道论；清净道论；优波底沙；觉音；佛教文献传播  
Title：The Vimutti-magga: The Chinese Counterpart of the Pali Visuddhi-magga  
Author：M. Nagai  
Abstract：This paper introduces the Chinese Buddhist text "Vimutti-magga" (The Way to Deliverance) translated by the Cambodian monk Sanghapala in 505 AD, pointing out its high relevance to the Pali "Visuddhi-magga" (The Path of Purification) in form and content. The author textualizes that the original author of the "Vimutti-magga" is the Sri Lankan high monk Upatissa in the 1st century AD, earlier than Buddhaghosa's "Visuddhi-magga", and speculates that Buddhaghosa referred to this text when compiling the "Visuddhi-magga". By comparing the chapter structures (sila, jhāna, paññā, vimutti) and specific contents of the two books, the similarities and differences are analyzed, revealing the dissemination and evolution of early Buddhist literature in South Asia, Southeast Asia, and China.  
Keywords：Vimutti-magga; Visuddhi-magga; Upatissa; Buddhaghosa; Dissemination of Buddhist Literature
