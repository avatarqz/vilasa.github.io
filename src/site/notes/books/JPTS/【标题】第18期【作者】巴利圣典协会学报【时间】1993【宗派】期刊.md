# 【标题】第18期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1993【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 《布拉·马列亚德瓦长老传》导论、原文与译文
作者：史蒂文·柯林斯（Steven Collins）、尤金·丹尼斯（Eugène Denis S.J.）  
摘要：本文聚焦南传佛教文献《布拉·马列亚德瓦长老传》（Brah Maleyyadevattheravatthum），包含导论、原文校勘与英文译文三部分。导论梳理了该文献的版本源流、斯里兰卡源头与东南亚文本发展，探讨其在泰国佛教中的实践影响与语言文学价值；原文基于5份柬埔寨、泰国、巴黎馆藏手稿校勘而成，保留了东南亚巴利语的独特特征；译文则完整呈现了长老游历天堂地狱、见证未来佛弥勒事迹的核心叙事。研究指出，该文本虽非正统巴利三藏文献，却通过业力故事与弥勒信仰的结合，成为东南亚佛教通俗教化的重要载体，其语言特征为研究后期巴利语演变提供了关键实例。  
关键词：马列亚德瓦长老传；巴利语手稿；东南亚佛教；弥勒信仰；业力叙事  
Title：Brah Maleyyadevattheravatthum: Introduction, Text, The Story of the Elder Maleyyadeva  
Author：Steven Collins, Eugène Denis S.J.  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Theravada Buddhist text Brah Maleyyadevattheravatthum, consisting of three parts: introduction, edited text, and English translation. The introduction sorts out the text's textual origin, Sri Lankan sources, and development in Southeast Asian versions, exploring its practical influence in Thai Buddhism and linguistic-literary value; the edited text is based on five manuscripts from Cambodia, Thailand, and Paris, preserving the unique characteristics of Southeast Asian Pali; the translation fully presents the core narrative of the elder's travels to heaven and hell and his witness to the deeds of Metteyya, the future Buddha. The study points out that although the text is not part of the orthodox Pali Tipitaka, it has become an important carrier of popular Buddhist education in Southeast Asia through the combination of karmic stories and Metteyya belief, and its linguistic features provide key examples for the study of later Pali evolution.  
Keywords：Brah Maleyyadevattheravatthum；Pali manuscripts；Southeast Asian Buddhism；Metteyya belief；karmic narrative

# 论文2. 《涅槃经》：一篇据称非三藏的涅槃大城喻经
作者：查尔斯·哈利西（Charles Hallisey）  
摘要：本文对据称非巴利三藏的《涅槃经》（Nibbanasutta）进行了初步校勘与翻译，该文本以“涅槃为大城”为核心隐喻，通过旅人赴城的叙事串联佛教修行、解脱与轮回教义。研究基于巴黎远东学院馆藏的柬埔寨棕榈叶手稿，分析其文本结构与隐喻体系，指出该经虽未被纳入正统三藏，却继承了南传佛教以喻释法的传统，将涅槃城的城墙、城门、街道等要素分别对应戒律、智慧、禅定等修行阶位。文本通过整合《中部尼柯耶》《长老偈》等经典素材，成为阐释涅槃教义的通俗教化文本，其东南亚巴利语特征为研究后期南传佛教文献的创作与传播提供了重要线索。  
关键词：涅槃经；非三藏文献；涅槃隐喻；南传佛教；巴利语手稿  
Title：Nibbanasutta: an allegedly non-canonical sutta on Nibbāna as a Great City  
Author：Charles Hallisey  
Abstract：This paper presents a preliminary edition and translation of the Nibbanasutta, an allegedly non-canonical Pali text centered on the metaphor of "Nibbāna as a great city". Through the narrative of a traveler journeying to a city, it connects Buddhist practices, liberation, and samsara doctrines. Based on a Cambodian palm-leaf manuscript in the collection of the École Française d'Extrême-Orient in Paris, the study analyzes the text's structure and metaphorical system, pointing out that although the sutta is not included in the orthodox Tipitaka, it inherits the Theravada tradition of explaining the Dhamma through metaphors, equating elements such as the city's walls, gates, and streets with the stages of practice like sila, pañña, and samadhi. By integrating materials from canonical texts such as the Majjhima Nikaya and Theragatha, the text becomes a popular educational work explaining Nibbāna doctrine, and its Southeast Asian Pali characteristics provide important clues for the study of the composition and dissemination of later Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Nibbanasutta；non-canonical text；Nibbāna metaphor；Theravada Buddhism；Pali manuscript

# 论文3. 剑桥大学图书馆藏斯里兰卡巴利语手稿叙录
作者：吉纳达萨·利耶纳拉特纳（Jinadasa Liyanaratne）  
摘要：本文对剑桥大学图书馆馆藏的42份斯里兰卡巴利语手稿进行了系统叙录，涵盖棕榈叶与纸质两种载体，以19世纪抄本为主，包含阿毗达磨、律藏、本生故事、词典等各类文献。叙录详细记录了每份手稿的馆藏编号、载体形制、抄写时间与地点、内容概要及学术价值，重点梳理了T.W.里斯·戴维斯旧藏手稿的来源与特征，其中包括未被此前目录收录的22份珍贵文献。研究指出，这些手稿不仅为巴利语文献校勘提供了重要异文，其抄写者信息、来源地分布还反映了19世纪斯里兰卡佛教文献的传承状况，部分手稿的跨语种注释（僧伽罗语、罗马语）为研究巴利语的传播与本土化提供了实物证据。  
关键词：剑桥大学图书馆；斯里兰卡；巴利语手稿；文献叙录；佛教文献传承  
Title：Pali Manuscripts of Sri Lanka in the Cambridge University Library  
Author：Jinadasa Liyanaratne  
Abstract：This paper systematically catalogs 42 Sri Lankan Pali manuscripts in the Cambridge University Library, covering two carriers (palm-leaf and paper), mainly 19th-century copies, including various types of literature such as Abhidhamma, Vinaya, Jataka tales, and dictionaries. The catalog records in detail each manuscript's collection number, carrier form, copying time and location, content summary, and academic value, focusing on sorting out the source and characteristics of the manuscripts from T.W. Rhys Davids' collection, including 22 precious texts not included in previous catalogs. The study points out that these manuscripts not only provide important variant readings for the collation of Pali literature but also their copyist information and source distribution reflect the inheritance of Buddhist literature in Sri Lanka in the 19th century. The multilingual annotations (Sinhala, Roman script) of some manuscripts provide physical evidence for the study of the dissemination and localization of Pali.  
Keywords：Cambridge University Library；Sri Lanka；Pali manuscripts；literature catalog；Buddhist literature inheritance

# 论文4. 巴利语词典编纂研究（第十一部分）：六个巴利语词源考
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文为“巴利语词典编纂研究”系列第十一部分，聚焦六个巴利语词汇的词源与语义辨析，包括kaddhati（拖拽）、tiracchānakatha（闲谈）、pacchabandha（船舵）、matang araiia（象林）、vidha（带扣）、setthi（渣滓）。通过比对梵语、俗语平行文本及巴利语注释文献，作者修正了《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中的部分释义偏差，厘清了词汇的构词理据与语义演变。研究指出，这些词汇的词源考证不仅有助于完善巴利语词典编纂，其语言变异特征还为揭示巴利语与中古印度-雅利安语的关联提供了语言学依据。  
关键词：巴利语；词典编纂；词源考证；语义辨析；中古印度-雅利安语  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies XI: Six Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper is the eleventh part of the "Pali Lexicographical Studies" series, focusing on the etymology and semantic discrimination of six Pali words: kaddhati (to drag), tiracchānakatha (gossip), pacchabandha (rudder), matang araiia (elephant forest), vidha (buckle), and setthi (dregs). By comparing parallel texts in Sanskrit and Prakrit as well as Pali commentarial literature, the author corrects some interpretive deviations in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED) and clarifies the word-formation motivation and semantic evolution of the words. The study points out that the etymological research of these words not only helps improve Pali lexicography but also their linguistic variation characteristics provide linguistic evidence for revealing the connection between Pali and Middle Indo-Aryan.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；semantic discrimination；Middle Indo-Aryan

# 论文5. 阿耨楼陀派《佛种姓经》引文考
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文对藏文译本《 tarkajvala》中引用的阿耨楼陀派（Abhayagiri）《佛种姓经》（*Buddhavamsa）片段进行了校勘与研究。该引文共四节偈颂，强调菩萨应受世间敬奉，提及十波罗蜜与佛陀功德，是目前已知唯一明确的阿耨楼陀派文献引文。研究梳理了阿耨楼陀派与大寺派的教义分歧及文献传承状况，指出该引文虽简短，却印证了阿耨楼陀派存在独立的巴利语经典传承，其对菩萨信仰的重视反映了该派与大乘佛教的思想关联，为研究南传佛教内部流派差异及思想交流提供了关键文本证据。  
关键词：阿耨楼陀派；佛种姓经；藏文译本；南传佛教；菩萨信仰  
Title：A Citation from the *Buddhavamsa of the Abhayagiri School  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper collates and studies a fragment of the *Buddhavamsa of the Abhayagiri school cited in the Tibetan translation of the Tarkajvala. The citation consists of four verses, emphasizing that bodhisattvas should be revered by the world, mentioning the ten paramis and the Buddha's merits, and is the only known explicit citation of Abhayagiri literature. The study sorts out the doctrinal differences and literary inheritance between the Abhayagiri school and the Mahavihara school, pointing out that although the citation is brief, it confirms that the Abhayagiri school had an independent Pali canonical tradition. Its emphasis on bodhisattva belief reflects the ideological connection between the school and Mahayana Buddhism, providing key textual evidence for the study of sectarian differences and ideological exchanges within Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Abhayagiri school；*Buddhavamsa；Tibetan translation；Theravada Buddhism；bodhisattva belief

# 论文6. 《巴利圣典协会学报》第九至十八卷索引
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文为《巴利圣典协会学报》（JPTS）第九至十八卷（1981-1993）的文献索引，按作者姓氏字母顺序编排，收录了该时期发表的论文、译文、手稿叙录、词典研究等各类文献。每条索引包含作者、文献标题与卷期页码，涵盖南传佛教文献校勘、巴利语语言学、佛教哲学、考古与手稿研究等多个领域。该索引为巴利语与南传佛教研究提供了便捷的文献检索工具，系统反映了20世纪80-90年代相关领域的研究热点与学术脉络。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会学报；文献索引；南传佛教研究；巴利语研究；学术史  
Title：An Index to JPTS Volumes IX-XVIII  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper is a literature index for Volumes IX to XVIII (1981-1993) of the Journal of the Pali Text Society (JPTS), arranged in alphabetical order by authors' surnames, including various types of literature published during this period such as papers, translations, manuscript catalogs, and lexicographical studies. Each index entry includes the author, literature title, and volume-issue page numbers, covering multiple fields such as Theravada Buddhist text collation, Pali linguistics, Buddhist philosophy, archaeology, and manuscript research. The index provides a convenient literature retrieval tool for Pali and Theravada Buddhist studies, systematically reflecting the research hotspots and academic context of related fields in the 1980s and 1990s.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；literature index；Theravada Buddhist studies；Pali studies；academic history
