# 【标题】1887年第2期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1887【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  佛陀赞歌（《 Pajjamadhu》）
作者：埃德蒙·R·古纳拉特纳（Edmund R. Gooneratne，Mudaliyar）  
摘要：本文收录由高僧 Buddhappiya 创作的巴利语赞歌《Pajjamadhu》，全文共104节，采用梵语化巴利语撰写。前69节描绘佛陀的身相之美，后35节赞颂佛陀的智慧，结尾以对僧团与涅槃的礼赞收束。作者 Buddhappiya 为 Ananda 的弟子，亦著有巴利语语法著作《Rūpa Siddhi》，学界推测该诗创作于公元1100年左右的锡兰地区，附有僧伽罗语注释。经文通过丰富比喻与庄严意象，展现佛陀的圆满功德与佛教的核心教义，为巴利语宗教诗歌研究提供重要范本。  
关键词：巴利语诗歌；佛陀赞歌；《Pajjamadhu》；Buddhappiya；南传佛教；僧伽罗语注释；宗教文学  
Title：The Pajjamadhu. A Poem in Praise of Buddha  
Author：Edmund R. Gooneratne (Mudaliyar)  
Abstract：This paper includes the Pali poem "Pajjamadhu" composed by the venerable monk Buddhappiya, consisting of 104 stanzas written in Sanskritized Pali. The first 69 stanzas describe the beauty of the Buddha's physical form, the latter 35 praise the Buddha's wisdom, and the conclusion ends with an eulogy to the Sangha and Nirvana. The author Buddhappiya, a disciple of Ananda, also wrote the Pali grammar work "Rūpa Siddhi". Academic circles speculate that the poem was composed in Ceylon around 1100 AD, with a Sinhalese commentary. Through rich metaphors and solemn imagery, the text shows the perfect merits of the Buddha and the core doctrines of Buddhism, providing an important model for the study of Pali religious poetry.  
Keywords：Pali Poem；Buddha Praise Poem；Pajjamadhu；Buddhappiya；Theravada Buddhism；Sinhalese Commentary；Religious Literature

# 论文2.  界争裁决论（《Sima-Vivada-Vinicchaya-Katha》）
作者：J·P·米纳耶夫教授（Professor J. P. Minayeff）  
摘要：本文为南传佛教戒律专题文献《Sima-Vivada-Vinicchaya-Katha》的校勘本，聚焦僧团“界”（修行活动区域）的界定争议与裁决规则。文献结合律藏注释与历史案例，系统阐释“共界”与“不共界”的划分标准、水域与陆地界域的界定方法，以及界域争议的解决流程。文中提及佛陀涅槃后佛教部派分裂背景下的僧团规范演变，收录锡兰与印度两地僧团的交流案例，为研究南传佛教戒律实践与僧团管理提供关键史料。  
关键词：南传佛教；戒律；界域界定；僧团管理；米纳耶夫；《Sima-Vivada-Vinicchaya-Katha》；戒律争议  
Title：Sima-Vivada-Vinicchaya-Katha  
Author：Professor J. P. Minayeff  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Theravada Buddhist Vinaya thematic text "Sima-Vivada-Vinicchaya-Katha", focusing on the disputes and ruling rules regarding the definition of "Sima" (practice area) of the Sangha. Combining Vinaya commentary and historical cases, the document systematically explains the division standards of "Sabhaga Sima" (common boundary) and "Visabhaga Sima" (uncommon boundary), the definition methods of water and land boundaries, and the resolution process of boundary disputes. It mentions the evolution of Sangha norms under the background of Buddhist sect division after the Buddha's Nirvana, and includes exchange cases between Sanghas in Ceylon and India, providing key historical materials for the study of Theravada Buddhist Vinaya practice and Sangha management.  
Keywords：Theravada Buddhism；Vinaya；Boundary Definition；Sangha Management；J. P. Minayeff；Sima-Vivada-Vinicchaya-Katha；Vinaya Disputes

# 论文3.  善法入道论（《Saddhammopayana》）
作者：理查德·莫里斯博士（Dr. Richard Morris）  
摘要：本文为巴利语佛教典籍《Saddhammopayana》的校勘与注释本，全文共19章，以诗歌体阐释佛教核心教义。内容涵盖十不善道的过患、饿鬼与畜生道的苦状、涅槃的功德、布施持戒的果报等，通过寓言与譬喻强调善法修行对脱离轮回的重要性。典籍融合经藏与论藏思想，语言简洁凝练，附有莫里斯博士的详细注释，对关键术语、引文出处及教义背景进行考证，为理解南传佛教的伦理观与修行体系提供重要参考。  
关键词：《Saddhammopayana》；善法修行；南传佛教教义；理查德·莫里斯；佛教伦理；轮回与涅槃；巴利语典籍  
Title：Saddhammopayana  
Author：Dr. Richard Morris  
Abstract：This paper is a collated and annotated edition of the Pali Buddhist text "Saddhammopayana", consisting of 19 chapters that explain the core Buddhist doctrines in poetic form. The content covers the drawbacks of the ten unwholesome paths, the sufferings of the hungry ghost and animal realms, the merits of Nirvana, and the karmic fruits of giving and keeping precepts, emphasizing the importance of wholesome practice for escaping samsara through fables and metaphors. Integrating the ideas of Sutta Pitaka and Abhidhamma Pitaka, the text is concise and concise, with detailed annotations by Dr. Richard Morris, verifying key terms, citation sources and doctrinal backgrounds, providing an important reference for understanding the ethical outlook and practice system of Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Saddhammopayana；Wholesome Practice；Theravada Buddhist Doctrines；Richard Morris；Buddhist Ethics；Samsara and Nirvana；Pali Texts

Title：Notes on Saddhammopayana  
Author：Dr. Richard Morris

# 论文7.  “挑棍游戏”考（Spellicans）
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为戴维斯对巴利语典籍中记载的古代游戏“Santikam”的考证研究，认为该游戏与欧洲传统“挑棍游戏”（Spellicans）同源。文中引用《长部》注释中对“Santikam”的描述——将小木块或陶器碎片堆叠，玩家用指甲抽取碎片而不扰动整体，扰动者为负——对比现代挑棍游戏规则，推测该游戏起源于公元前500年左右的印度，后传播至欧洲。研究为跨文化游戏传播史与古代印度社会生活研究提供独特视角。  
关键词：Santikam；挑棍游戏；跨文化传播；古代印度游戏；T·W·里斯·戴维斯；佛教文献；社会生活  
Title：Spellicans  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is Davis's research on the ancient game "Santikam" recorded in Pali texts, arguing that the game is homologous to the European traditional "Spellicans". Citing the description of "Santikam" in the commentary on Digha Nikaya—stacking small wooden blocks or pottery fragments, players use their nails to extract fragments without disturbing the whole, and the disturber loses—comparing with modern Spellicans rules, it is speculated that the game originated in India around 500 BC and later spread to Europe. The research provides a unique perspective for the history of cross-cultural game dissemination and the study of ancient Indian social life.  
Keywords：Santikam；Spellicans；Cross-Cultural Dissemination；Ancient Indian Games；T. W. Rhys Davids；Buddhist Literature；Social Life
