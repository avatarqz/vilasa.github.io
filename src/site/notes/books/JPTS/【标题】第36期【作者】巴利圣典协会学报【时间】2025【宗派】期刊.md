# 论文1.  森祖道（1934-2025）
作者：马场纪寿（Norihisa Baba）  
摘要：本文追忆了日本著名巴利语注释文献与斯里兰卡大乘佛教研究学者森祖道教授的生平与学术贡献。森祖道1934年生于东京，经历二战疏散后返京求学，曾接受曹洞宗禅修训练，先后就读于驹泽大学与东京大学，师从水野弘元、中村元等学者。他长期在城西大学、爱知学院大学任教，获多项学术奖项，担任巴利圣典协会日本地区代表，主编《佛教研究》期刊四十期。其学术研究涵盖巴利语注释文献考证、斯里兰卡大乘佛教（弥勒菩萨、观音菩萨、密教）研究，代表作包括《巴利佛教注释文献研究》《斯里兰卡的大乘佛教》，强调实证性与文献学严谨性，拒绝将个人宗教信仰融入学术研究。文章还收录了他的主要中英文著作目录，展现其对国际巴利语研究与佛教文化交流的深远影响。  
关键词：森祖道；巴利语注释文献；斯里兰卡大乘佛教；佛教研究；巴利圣典协会  
Title：Sodo Mori (1934-2025)  
Author：Norihisa Baba  
Abstract：This paper commemorates the life and academic contributions of Professor Sodo Mori, a renowned Japanese scholar of Pali commentarial literature and Mahāyāna Buddhism in Sri Lanka. Born in Tokyo in 1934, Mori was evacuated during World War II and later returned to pursue his studies. He received Sōtō Zen training, studied at Komazawa University and the University of Tokyo, and was mentored by scholars such as Kogen Mizuno and Hajime Nakamura. He taught at Jōsai University and Aichi Gakuin University for a long time, won numerous academic awards, served as the Regional Representative of the Pali Text Society for Japan, and edited the journal Bukkyō Kenkyū (Buddhist Studies) for forty issues. His academic research covers the textual criticism of Pali commentaries and the study of Mahāyāna Buddhism in Sri Lanka (including Maitreya Bodhisattva, Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva, and Esoteric Buddhism), with representative works such as Pāri Bukkyō chūshaku bunken no kenkyū (A Study of the Pali Commentaries) and Suriranka no Daijōbukkyō (Mahāyāna Buddhism in Sri Lanka). He emphasized empirical rigor and philological precision, rejecting the integration of personal religious beliefs into academic research. The paper also includes a list of his major works in Chinese and English, demonstrating his profound influence on international Pali studies and Buddhist cultural exchange.  
Keywords：Sodo Mori; Pali commentarial literature; Mahāyāna Buddhism in Sri Lanka; Buddhist studies; Pali Text Society

# 论文2.  为最胜教法之繁荣与光辉：缅甸僧王难陀毗旺萨致兰卡僧团书
作者：延斯·W·博格朗德（Jens W. Borgland）  
摘要：本文聚焦1801年缅甸僧王难陀毗旺萨（Ñāṇābhivaṃsa Dhammasenāpati）致斯里兰卡僧团的《书信记述》（Sandesakathā），该文献是阿玛拉普拉教派（Amarapura nikāya）在兰卡建立的关键史料。文章指出，书信核心并非单纯为低种姓僧人受戒提供佐证，而是围绕缅甸“僧袍穿着争议”（ekāṃsika-pārupaṇa），推广“双肩覆袍”的正统戒律解释。难陀毗旺萨通过援引巴利语律典、注释书及副注释书，构建法律论证，反驳“单肩覆袍”与“胸巾束缚”等做法，强调沙弥应遵循与比丘一致的戒律。文章还分析了书信的历史背景，包括斯里兰卡佛教传承的多次中断与复兴、缅甸与兰卡的宗教交流，以及僧袍争议背后的教派竞争与王权影响，并提供了《书信记述》的英文译文与详细校注。  
关键词：难陀毗旺萨；《书信记述》；阿玛拉普拉教派；僧袍争议；巴利语律典；缅甸-斯里兰卡佛教交流  
Title：'For the Sake of the Prosperity and Splendour of the Most Excellent Teaching: The Burmese Saṃgharāja Ñāṇābhivaṃsa's Letter to the Monastic Community of Lanka  
Author：Jens W. Borgland  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Sandesakathā (Letter Account) written by the Burmese Saṃgharāja Ñāṇābhivaṃsa Dhammasenāpati in 1801 to the monastic community of Lanka, a key document for the establishment of the Amarapura nikāya in Sri Lanka. The article argues that the core of the letter is not merely to provide evidence for the ordination of low-caste monks, but to promote the orthodox interpretation of vinaya rules regarding "covering both shoulders" in response to Burma's "robe-wearing controversy" (ekāṃsika-pārupaṇa). By citing Pali vinaya texts, commentaries, and sub-commentaries, Ñāṇābhivaṃsa constructs a legal argument refuting practices such as "wearing the robe over one shoulder" and "binding a chest cover", emphasizing that novices should follow the same discipline as bhikkhus. The paper also analyzes the historical context of the letter, including the repeated interruptions and revivals of Buddhist transmission in Sri Lanka, religious exchanges between Burma and Lanka, and the sectarian competition and royal influence behind the robe controversy. An English translation and detailed annotations of the Sandesakathā are provided.  
Keywords：Ñāṇābhivaṃsa; Sandesakathā; Amarapura nikāya; robe-wearing controversy; Pali Vinaya; Burma-Sri Lanka Buddhist exchange

# 论文3.  卓越之德：巴利藏经中头陀行的新视角
作者：奥利弗·弗赖伯格（Oliver Freiberger）  
摘要：本文重新审视巴利藏经中的“头陀行”（dhutaṅgas/dhutaguṇas），挑战将其简单定义为“上座部佛教苦行实践”的传统观点。通过梳理巴利语经、律、论中的相关记载，作者指出头陀行在文本中呈现出显著的复杂性与矛盾性：列表长度与内容不一致、部分实践与律典规定差异细微、文本对其价值的评价相互冲突。文章分析了头陀行的多元形态（从轻度自律到极端苦行），探讨了其在早期佛教中的双重定位——既被批评为与提婆达多相关的“异端实践”，又被推崇为佛陀弟子马哈咖沙巴等所践行的“解脱助缘”。作者提出，头陀行最初可能是描述僧人独特品质的松散概念，而非固定体系，其标准化（十三头陀行）是后世文本编纂的结果，反映了早期佛教内部对苦行、戒律与精神实践关系的多元思考。  
关键词：头陀行；巴利藏经；早期佛教；苦行实践；僧团戒律；佛教修行  
Title：Qualities of Distinction: A New Perspective on the dhutaṅgas in the Pāli Canon  
Author：Oliver Freiberger  
Abstract：This paper re-examines the "dhutaṅgas" (or dhutaguṇas) in the Pāli Canon, challenging the traditional view of them as simply "ascetic practices in Theravāda Buddhism". By sorting through relevant records in Pāli suttas, vinayas, and abhidhammas, the author points out that the dhutaṅgas exhibit significant complexity and contradictions in the texts: inconsistent lengths and contents of lists, subtle differences between some practices and vinaya regulations, and conflicting evaluations of their value in the texts. The paper analyzes the diverse forms of dhutaṅgas (from mild self-discipline to extreme asceticism) and explores their dual positioning in early Buddhism—criticized as "heretical practices" associated with Devadatta, yet praised as "aids to liberation" practiced by the Buddha's disciples such as Mahākassapa. The author argues that the dhutaṅgas were initially a loose concept describing the distinctive qualities of monks rather than a fixed system, and their standardization (the thirteen dhutaṅgas) was a result of later textual compilation, reflecting diverse thinking within early Buddhism on the relationship between asceticism, monastic discipline, and spiritual practice.  
Keywords：dhutaṅgas; Pāli Canon; early Buddhism; ascetic practice; monastic discipline; Buddhist practice

# 论文4.  巴利文献中的世界构想——2022年第十九届I·B·霍纳纪念讲座
作者：阿拉斯泰尔·戈纳尔（Alastair Gornall）  
摘要：本文基于2022年第十九届I·B·霍纳纪念讲座内容，系统梳理了巴利语文献中宇宙观的发展脉络。作者指出，巴利藏经中并无统一的宇宙体系，早期文本以“此世-彼世”的二元划分为主，阿毗达摩首次构建了“欲界-色界-无色界”的三界三十一层天模型，但缺乏空间与时间维度的详细描述。 Buddhaghosa在《清净道论》中拓展了佛陀的“世界认知”，提出“行法界（saṅkhāraloka）-有情界（sattaloka）-器界（okāsaloka）”的三界新模型，整合了物理宇宙（须弥山、四大洲等）与时间周期（劫）的理论。文章进一步考察了中世纪巴利语宇宙学文献（如《世间分别论》《五趣照明论》《轮围照明论》等），分析其对梵语佛教与印度教宇宙观的吸收，以及从“描述性宇宙学”到“解释性宇宙学”的转变，揭示上座部佛教宇宙观如何通过文本编纂不断丰富，成为理解佛教世界观与修行实践的重要维度。  
关键词：巴利文献；宇宙观；三界模型；Buddhaghosa；清净道论；上座部佛教  
Title：Conceptualising the World in Pali Literature—Nineteenth I. B. Horner Memorial Lecture, 2022  
Author：Alastair Gornall  
Abstract：Based on the content of the Nineteenth I. B. Horner Memorial Lecture in 2022, this paper systematically sorts out the development context of cosmology in Pali literature. The author points out that there is no unified cosmic system in the Pāli Canon; early texts mainly adopt a dualistic division of "this world (ayaṃloko) - the other world (paro loko)". The Abhidhamma first constructed the model of the "three worlds (kāmaloka-rūpaloka-arūpaloka)" with thirty-one realms, but lacked detailed descriptions of spatial and temporal dimensions. Buddhaghosa expanded the Buddha's "knowledge of the world" in the Visuddhimagga, proposing a new three-world model of "saṅkhāraloka (world of formations) - sattaloka (world of living beings) - okāsaloka (world of space)", integrating theories of the physical universe (Mount Sineru, four great continents, etc.) and time cycles (kappa). The paper further examines medieval Pali cosmological texts (such as Lokapaññatti, Pañcagatidīpanī, Cakkavāḷadīpanī, etc.), analyzes their absorption of Sanskrit Buddhist and Hindu cosmological views, and the transformation from "descriptive cosmology" to "interpretative cosmology", revealing how Theravāda Buddhist cosmology was continuously enriched through textual compilation, becoming an important dimension for understanding the Buddhist worldview and spiritual practice.  
Keywords：Pali literature; cosmology; three-world model; Buddhaghosa; Visuddhimagga; Theravāda Buddhism

# 论文5.  老挝早期宗教与历史传统——阿里耶旺萨的《增福佛造像缘起》
作者：哈维尔·施纳克（Javier Schnake）  
摘要：本文首次对老挝巴利语文献《增福佛造像缘起》（Aḍḍhabhāgabuddharūpanidāna）进行校勘与研究，该文本由阿里耶旺萨（Ariyavaṃsa）撰写，是兰纳王国（Lān Xāng）早期宗教与历史的核心史料。文章指出，文本以“增福佛”（即老挝国宝“帕邦佛”）的起源与流转为主线，分为两部分：第一部分叙述佛像在斯里兰卡的神话性制作（由古那长老发起，融合金银铜等贵金属），后传入柬埔寨吴哥；第二部分详细记载佛像从柬埔寨传入老挝的过程，串联起兰纳王国的建国史、国王法昂（Pabbhāradeva）的征服与佛教传播、历代国王与佛像的关联，以及佛像在老挝的最终安置。文章通过校勘泰、法两国馆藏手稿，纠正了文本流传中的讹误，揭示其对老挝编年史（如《Phongsāvadān》）的影响，填补了老挝早期佛教史与文化交流研究的空白。  
关键词：《增福佛造像缘起》；阿里耶旺萨；老挝佛教；帕邦佛；兰纳王国；巴利语文献  
Title：An Early Religious and Historical Tradition of Laos: The Aḍḍhabhāgabuddharūpanidāna of Ariyavaṃsa  
Author：Javier Schnake  
Abstract：This paper presents the first critical edition and study of the Pali text Aḍḍhabhāgabuddharūpanidāna (Account of the Buddha image made of different shares of wealth) composed by Ariyavaṃsa, a core historical source for the early religious and historical traditions of the Lān Xāng kingdom. The paper points out that the text centers on the origin and circulation of the "Aḍḍhabhāgabuddha" (i.e., the Phra Bang Buddha, Laos' national treasure), divided into two parts: the first part narrates the mythical creation of the Buddha image in Sri Lanka (initiated by the Thera Cūḷanāga, integrating precious metals such as gold, silver, and bronze), which later spread to Angkor, Cambodia; the second part details the process of the Buddha image's transmission from Cambodia to Laos, connecting the founding history of the Lān Xāng kingdom, King Fa Ngum's (Pabbhāradeva) conquests and Buddhist propagation, the association between successive kings and the Buddha image, and the final placement of the image in Laos. By collating manuscripts collected in Thailand and France, the paper corrects errors in the text's transmission, reveals its influence on Lao chronicles (such as the Phongsāvadān), and fills the gap in the study of early Lao Buddhist history and cultural exchange.  
Keywords：Aḍḍhabhāgabuddharūpanidāna; Ariyavaṃsa; Lao Buddhism; Phra Bang Buddha; Lān Xāng kingdom; Pali literature

# 论文6.  巴利语尼柯耶中的叙事：小品词kho与病僧文本循环
作者：埃维亚塔尔·舒尔曼（Eviatar Shulman）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语尼柯耶文献中的小品词“kho”，探讨其在叙事结构与宗教意义传递中的核心作用。通过分析“病僧”相关文本循环（如《阿含经》中病僧向佛陀请法、佛陀开示无常与观身法门的叙事），作者发现“kho”不仅承担语法功能（强调主语、连接上下文），更通过在关键叙事节点的使用，引导读者关注佛法核心教义。文章指出，“kho”在病僧叙事中常出现在佛陀回应、教义阐释及僧众领悟等场景，强化了“苦-集-灭-道”的逻辑脉络，同时构建了“佛陀-病僧-僧团”的宗教社群联结。通过对比不同尼柯耶中同类叙事的“kho”使用差异，作者揭示了巴利语叙事文学的编纂技巧与教义传播策略，为理解早期佛教文本的形成与宗教实践的关联提供了新视角。  
关键词：巴利语尼柯耶；小品词kho；病僧叙事；文本循环；佛教叙事文学；教义传播  
Title：Storytelling in the Pali Nikayas: the Particle kho and the Textual Cycle of the Sick Monk  
Author：Eviatar Shulman  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the particle "kho" in the Pali Nikayas, exploring its core role in narrative structure and the transmission of religious meaning. By analyzing the textual cycle related to "the sick monk" (such as narratives in the Āgamas where sick monks seek teachings from the Buddha, and the Buddha expounds the Dharma of impermanence and body contemplation), the author finds that "kho" not only fulfills grammatical functions (emphasizing the subject, connecting context) but also guides readers to focus on the core Buddhist teachings through its use at key narrative nodes. The paper points out that in the sick monk narratives, "kho" often appears in scenes such as the Buddha's responses, doctrinal explanations, and the monastic community's realization, strengthening the logical thread of "dukkha-samudaya-nirodha-magga" and constructing a religious community connection of "Buddha-sick monk-saṅgha". By comparing the differences in the use of "kho" in similar narratives across different Nikayas, the author reveals the compilation techniques and doctrinal propagation strategies of Pali narrative literature, providing a new perspective for understanding the formation of early Buddhist texts and their connection to religious practice.  
Keywords：Pali Nikayas; particle kho; sick monk narrative; textual cycle; Buddhist narrative literature; doctrinal propagation

# 论文7.  巴利语中的muttba、mussati、mosa及相关词汇
作者：马丁·斯特劳贝（Martin Straube）  
摘要：本文对巴利语中的一组相关词汇——muttba、mussati、mosa及衍生词进行了词源学、语义学与文本实证研究。通过梳理巴利藏经、注释书及中世纪文献中的用法，作者追溯了这些词汇的印欧语系根源，分析其语义演变轨迹：muttba最初与“释放、解脱”相关，后在律典中特指“放弃戒律”；mussati核心义为“遗忘、忽略”，常与宗教实践中的“正念缺失”关联；mosa则有“愚蠢、迷惑”之意，多用于描述烦恼与认知障碍。文章通过对比不同文本语境中的词汇使用差异，探讨了它们在佛教教义（如正念、戒律、烦恼论）中的具体应用，澄清了学界对部分词汇的误读，并揭示了巴利语词汇语义与佛教思想发展的互动关系，为巴利语语言学与佛教哲学研究提供了基础参考。  
关键词：巴利语；词汇研究；muttba；mussati；mosa；词源学；佛教语义学  
Title：Pali muttba, mussati, mosa, and Related Words  
Author：Martin Straube  
Abstract：This paper conducts an etymological, semantic, and textual empirical study of a group of related Pali words—muttba, mussati, mosa, and their derivatives. By sorting through their usage in the Pāli Canon, commentaries, and medieval texts, the author traces the Indo-European roots of these words and analyzes their semantic evolution: muttba was initially related to "release, liberation" and later specifically referred to "abandoning the precepts" in the Vinaya; mussati's core meaning is "forget, neglect", often associated with "lack of mindfulness" in religious practice; mosa means "foolish, confused", mostly used to describe defilements and cognitive obstacles. By comparing the differences in the usage of these words in different textual contexts, the paper explores their specific applications in Buddhist doctrines (such as mindfulness, precepts, and defilement theory), clarifies academic misinterpretations of some words, and reveals the interactive relationship between Pali vocabulary semantics and the development of Buddhist thought, providing a basic reference for Pali linguistics and Buddhist philosophy research.  
Keywords：Pali; vocabulary study; muttba; mussati; mosa; etymology; Buddhist semantics

# 论文8.  关于达摩难陀·高善必《新珍宝疏》的标题与年代考

Title：A Note on the Title and Date of Dharmananda Kosambi's Navanitatika  
Author：Anonymous (or in line with journal conventions)  
Abstract：This paper investigates the title and composition date of Navanitatika, a Pali work by the renowned Indian Buddhist scholar Dharmananda Kosambi. Keywords：Dharmananda Kosambi; Navanitatika; title verification; composition date; Pali commentary
