# 【标题】1908年第6期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1908【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利圣典协会1907年度报告
作者：T. W. 里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文概述了巴利圣典协会1907年的发展状况与核心工作。协会保持稳步发展并实现自给自足，订阅费与旧刊销售收入足以支撑次年出版及资金积累。核心成果包括推进巴利语词典编撰筹备工作，明确由多位欧洲顶尖学者按字母分工编写，计划分四卷出版，规模与质量均超蔡尔德斯词典；巴伐利亚科学院从哈代基金拨款支持该项目，恩斯特·库恩教授接替已故的哈代教授负责元音部分编撰。同时提及将出版面向学生的简明词典，由仰光的杜瓦塞尔教授协助完成，且次年将推进《长部》第三卷及《增支部》五卷索引的出版工作。此外，作者呼吁重视学者劳动价值，主张为学术工作提供合理报酬。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1907年度报告；巴利语词典编撰；学术出版；资金状况；学者报酬  
Title：Report of the Society for the Year 1907  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper outlines the development and core work of the Pali Text Society in 1907. The Society maintained steady progress and achieved self-sufficiency, with subscription fees and sales revenue from back issues sufficient to support the next year's publications and fund accumulation. Key achievements include advancing the preparatory work for the compilation of the Pali Dictionary, which will be compiled by several top European scholars divided by letters, planned to be published in four volumes, surpassing Childers's Dictionary in both scale and quality. The Bavarian Academy of Sciences allocated funds from the Hardy Fund to support the project, and Professor Ernst Kuhn took over from the late Professor Hardy to compile the vowel part. It also mentions that a concise dictionary for students will be published with the assistance of Professor Charles Duroiselle from Rangoon, and the third volume of the Digha Nikaya and the index to the five volumes of the Anguttara Nikaya will be promoted next year. In addition, the author calls for attention to the value of scholars' labor and advocates for reasonable remuneration for academic work.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1907 Annual Report；Pali Dictionary Compilation；Academic Publishing；Financial Status；Scholar Remuneration

# 论文2.  《律藏·小品》第十一、十二章所述的王舍城与吠舍离佛教结集
作者：鲁道夫·奥托·弗兰克教授（Professor R. Otto Franke）  
摘要：本文聚焦《律藏·小品》第十一、十二章记载的王舍城与吠舍离佛教结集，对其历史真实性展开深入考证。通过比对《长部·大般涅槃经》《岛史》《大史》及觉音注疏等文献，指出《岛史》的可信度存在缺陷，其与《大史》《善见律毗婆沙》均无古老僧伽罗语注疏作为史料支撑。结合经典文本内部矛盾（如不同尼柯耶中关于佛陀晚年及弟子事迹的记载冲突），质疑结集记载的真实性。重点分析《小品》相关章节与《大般涅槃经》的文学依赖关系，指出前者多处借鉴后者文本，且通过调整对话顺序、强化僧团礼仪规范等方式构建叙事。同时探讨结集记载中关于戒律修订、僧团争议等内容的文学建构特征，认为这两章实为以“僧团礼仪典范”为核心的文学创作，而非真实历史记录。  
关键词：佛教结集；王舍城；吠舍离；《律藏·小品》；《大般涅槃经》；历史真实性；文本依赖  
Title：The Buddhist Councils at Rajagaha and Vesali, as Alleged in Cullavagga XI., XII.  
Author：Professor R. Otto Franke  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Buddhist Councils at Rajagaha and Vesali recorded in Chapters XI. and XII. of the Cullavagga, conducting in-depth research on their historical authenticity. By comparing texts such as the Digha Nikaya's Mahaparinibbana Suttanta, Dipavansa, Mahavansa, and Buddhaghosa's commentaries, it points out the flaws in the credibility of the Dipavansa, which, along with the Mahavansa and Samantapasadika, has no ancient Sinhalese commentaries as historical support. Combining internal contradictions in canonical texts (such as conflicting records about the Buddha's later years and his disciples' deeds in different Nikayas), it questions the authenticity of the Council records. It focuses on analyzing the literary dependence of the relevant chapters in the Cullavagga on the Mahaparinibbana Suttanta, pointing out that the former borrows extensively from the latter's text and constructs the narrative by adjusting the order of dialogues and strengthening monastic etiquette norms. At the same time, it explores the literary construction characteristics of content such as the revision of precepts and monastic disputes in the Council records, arguing that these two chapters are actually literary works centered on "models of monastic etiquette" rather than real historical records.  
Keywords：Buddhist Councils；Rajagaha；Vesali；Cullavagga；Mahaparinibbana Suttanta；Historical Authenticity；Textual Dependence

# 论文3.  缅甸早期巴利语语法学家
作者：梅布尔·博德博士（Dr. Mabel Bode）  
摘要：本文系统梳理缅甸早期巴利语语法学的发展脉络与核心学者成果。指出缅甸巴利语文学起源于11世纪，受下缅甸孟族（得楞族）佛教文化影响深远，阿奴律陀王征服直通后将巴利三藏及学者带回蒲甘，为语法研究奠定基础。重点介绍12至14世纪的核心语法学家及其著作：阿迦梵萨的《 Saddaniti》（现存最全面的巴利语语法著作）、其弟子查帕塔（法灯）的《Suttaniddesa》《Sankhepavannana》等，以及达摩塞纳帕蒂、萨达马西里、维玛拉 Buddha 等学者的语法研究成果。分析缅甸巴利语语法学的特点，包括融合梵语语法元素、注重经典文本注释、服务于佛教教义传播等，同时探讨僧团宗派分歧对学术传承的影响，以及蒲甘王朝覆灭对语法研究发展的冲击。  
关键词：缅甸；巴利语语法学家；巴利语文学；蒲甘王朝；语法著作；佛教文化传播  
Title：Early Pali Grammarians in Burma  
Author：Dr. Mabel Bode  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the development context and core scholars' achievements of early Pali grammar in Burma. It points out that Burmese Pali literature originated in the 11th century and was profoundly influenced by the Buddhist culture of the Mon (Talaing) people in Lower Burma. King Anawrahta brought the Pali Tipitaka and scholars back to Pagan after conquering Thaton, laying the foundation for grammatical research. It focuses on introducing core grammarians and their works from the 12th to the 14th centuries: Aggavansa's Saddaniti (the most comprehensive existing Pali grammar work), his disciple Chapata (Saddhammajotipala)'s Suttaniddesa, Sankhepavannana, etc., as well as the grammatical research achievements of scholars such as Dhammasenapati, Saddhammasiri, and Vimalabuddhi. It analyzes the characteristics of Burmese Pali grammar, including integrating Sanskrit grammatical elements, focusing on canonical text annotation, and serving the dissemination of Buddhist doctrines. At the same time, it explores the impact of monastic sectarian divisions on academic inheritance and the impact of the fall of the Pagan Dynasty on the development of grammatical research.  
Keywords：Burma；Pali Grammarians；Pali Literature；Pagan Dynasty；Grammatical Works；Buddhist Culture Dissemination

# 论文4.  特伦克纳《弥兰陀王问经》及《中部》注释索引
作者：迪内斯·安德森教授（Professor Dines Andersen）（修订与编辑）；V. 特伦克纳（V. Trenckner）（原作者）  
摘要：本文是对V. 特伦克纳关于《弥兰陀王问经》第一章（外在论）的语文学注释及《中部》相关注释的修订、编辑与索引整理。特伦克纳的注释基于大量巴利语手稿及平行文本，涵盖词汇考证、语音变化分析、语法结构解析、文本异文比对等核心内容，涉及巴利语与梵语的词源关联、时态用法、复合词构词规则等关键问题。安德森教授对注释进行校订增补，补充文献引用出处，修正部分解读偏差，并编制详细的词汇、主题及文献引用索引，包括巴利语词汇、语法术语、专有名词等分类索引，为《弥兰陀王问经》及《中部》的文本校勘、语文学研究提供了系统的参考工具。  
关键词：《弥兰陀王问经》；《中部》；特伦克纳；语文学注释；索引；巴利语校勘  
Title：Index to Trenckner's Notes on the Milinda and Majjhima-Nikaya  
Author：Professor Dines Andersen (Revised and Edited)；V. Trenckner (Original Author)  
Abstract：This paper is the revision, editing, and index compilation of V. Trenckner's philological notes on the first chapter (Bahirakatha) of the Milinda-panha and his related notes on the Majjhima Nikaya. Trenckner's notes are based on a large number of Pali manuscripts and parallel texts, covering core contents such as vocabulary research, phonetic change analysis, grammatical structure interpretation, and textual variant comparison, involving key issues such as etymological connections between Pali and Sanskrit, tense usage, and compound word formation rules. Professor Dines Andersen revised and supplemented the notes, added literature citation sources, corrected some interpretation deviations, and compiled detailed indexes of words, subjects, and literature citations, including classified indexes of Pali words, grammatical terms, proper nouns, etc., providing a systematic reference tool for the textual collation and philological research of the Milinda-panha and the Majjhima Nikaya.  
Keywords：Milinda-panha；Majjhima Nikaya；Trenckner；Philological Notes；Index；Pali Textual Collation

# 论文5.  《无碍解道》索引
作者：梅布尔·亨特小姐（Miss Mabel Hunt）  
摘要：本文编制了巴利语经典《无碍解道》的系统索引，涵盖核心术语、概念、文本段落及相关主题分类。索引按巴利语词汇字母顺序排列，收录了经文中涉及的佛教教义（如四圣谛、八正道、禅那等）、修行阶段、心所法、色法、术语定义等关键内容，详细标注每个条目在《无碍解道》中的具体出处（章节、页码）。该索引为研究者快速查阅《无碍解道》中的核心概念、文本引用及主题关联内容提供了便捷工具，助力对这部巴利语论藏经典的深入研究与文本梳理。  
关键词：《无碍解道》；巴利语经典；索引；佛教术语；文本研究；论藏  
Title：Index to the Patisambhidamagga  
Author：Miss Mabel Hunt  
Abstract：This paper compiles a systematic index to the Pali canonical text Patisambhidamagga, covering core terms, concepts, textual passages, and related thematic classifications. Arranged in alphabetical order of Pali words, the index includes key contents such as Buddhist doctrines (e.g., Four Noble Truths, Noble Eightfold Path, Jhana), practice stages, mental factors, material phenomena, and term definitions in the text, with detailed annotations of the specific sources (chapters, page numbers) of each entry in the Patisambhidamagga. This index provides a convenient tool for researchers to quickly retrieve core concepts, textual citations, and theme-related contents in the Patisambhidamagga, facilitating in-depth research and textual sorting of this Pali Abhidhamma classic.  
Keywords：Patisambhidamagga；Pali Canon；Index；Buddhist Terms；Textual Research；Abhidhamma Pitaka

# 论文6.  尼柯耶中的比喻增补与修正
作者：里斯·戴维斯夫人（Mrs. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文是对尼柯耶经典中比喻修辞的补充与修正研究，基于过去十二个月的文献研读及劳斯博士翻译的《本生经》最后一卷整理而成。新增多个尼柯耶文献中未被收录的比喻案例，涵盖自然现象（如火焰、星辰、河流）、动植物（如蛇、鸟、莲花）、日常器物（如容器、布料、工具）等类别，补充了原比喻列表的遗漏条目。同时修正部分原有比喻的文本出处标注错误，明确每个比喻在《长部》《中部》《相应部》《增支部》及《本生经》中的具体位置，分析比喻的修辞功能与教义阐释作用，为尼柯耶文献的文学性研究与教义解读提供了更完整的参考资料。  
关键词：尼柯耶；比喻；修辞；《本生经》；文本增补；教义阐释  
Title：Addenda to Similes in the Nikayas  
Author：Mrs. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is a supplementary and corrective study on the similes in the Nikaya canon, compiled based on the perusal of literature over the past twelve months and Dr. Rouse's translation of the concluding volume of the Jataka. It adds multiple previously unincluded similes from the Nikaya texts, covering categories such as natural phenomena (e.g., flame, star, river), animals and plants (e.g., snake, bird, lotus), and daily utensils (e.g., container, cloth, tool), supplementing the missing entries in the original list of similes. At the same time, it corrects the errors in the textual source annotations of some existing similes, clarifies the specific positions of each simile in the Digha Nikaya, Majjhima Nikaya, Samyutta Nikaya, Anguttara Nikaya, and Jataka, analyzes the rhetorical functions and doctrinal interpretation roles of the similes, and provides more complete reference materials for the literary research and doctrinal interpretation of the Nikaya texts.  
Keywords：Nikayas；Similes；Rhetoric；Jataka；Textual Addenda；Doctrinal Interpretation
