# 【标题】1885-1887年第2卷【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1885-1887【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  布朗大学图书馆藏巴利语手稿目录
作者：亨利·C·沃伦（Henry C. Warren）  
摘要：本文系统梳理美国罗德岛州普罗维登斯市布朗大学图书馆收藏的22份巴利语手稿信息，手稿由浸信会传教士J·N·库欣博士捐赠。内容涵盖律藏（如《经分别》《大品》《小品》）、经藏注疏（如《善吉祥注》）、论藏（如《法集论》《分别论》）及语法著作（如《迦旃延语法》），详细标注每份手稿的载体（棕榈叶/铜板）、叶数、行数、抄写年代（ Sakkaraj纪年）及文本完整性。手稿以缅甸文字书写，部分含巴缅双语内容，为研究南传佛教文献传承、版本流变及19世纪巴利语手稿收藏史提供重要目录学依据。  
关键词：布朗大学；巴利语手稿；图书馆目录；南传佛教文献；沃伦；缅甸文字；版本学  
Title：Pali MSS. in the Brown University Library at Providence, R.I., U.S.  
Author：Henry C. Warren  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the information of 22 Pali manuscripts collected by the Brown University Library in Providence, Rhode Island, USA, which were donated by the Baptist missionary Dr. J. N. Cushing. The content includes Vinaya Pitaka (such as Sutta-Vibhaiga, Mahavagga, Cullavagga), Sutta Pitaka commentaries (such as Sumaigalavilasini), Abhidhamma Pitaka (such as Dhammasaigani, Vibhaiga) and grammatical works (such as Kaccayana's Grammar), with detailed annotations of each manuscript's carrier (palm leaf/copper plate), number of leaves, lines per page, copying era (Sakkaraj calendar) and text integrity. The manuscripts are written in Burmese script, some with mixed Pali and Burmese content, providing important bibliographical basis for the study of Theravada Buddhist literature transmission, version evolution and the history of Pali manuscript collection in the 19th century.  
Keywords：Brown University；Pali Manuscripts；Library Catalog；Theravada Buddhist Literature；Henry C. Warren；Burmese Script；Textual Criticism

# 论文2.  《发爪舍利史》（《Cha-Kesa-Dhatu-Vamsa》）
作者：J·米纳耶夫教授（Professor J. Minayeff）  
摘要：本文为缅甸匿名作者的巴利语佛教史传文献《发爪舍利史》校勘本，基于两份缅甸文手稿整理而成。文献以佛陀在王舍城竹林精舍的开示为开篇，记载阿那律等六位阿罗汉奉佛陀嘱托，携带佛发、佛爪舍利前往南印度弘法，联合天帝释、龙王等众生建造舍利塔的事迹。文本融合 prose 与诗歌体，详述六座舍利塔的建造缘起、选址及庄严形制，穿插佛教伦理劝诫，反映南传佛教的舍利崇拜与弘法传统，为研究缅甸佛教文献创作及南传佛教信仰实践提供核心史料。  
关键词：《发爪舍利史》；米纳耶夫；舍利崇拜；南传佛教史传；巴利语校勘；缅甸手稿；弘法传统  
Title：The Cha-Kesa-Dhatu-Vamsa  
Author：Professor J. Minayeff  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali Buddhist historical text "Cha-Kesa-Dhatu-Vamsa" by an anonymous Burmese author, compiled based on two Burmese manuscripts. Beginning with the Buddha's teachings at the Veluvana Kalandakanivape in Rajagaha, the text records the deeds of six Arahats including Anuruddha, who were entrusted by the Buddha to carry the Buddha's hair and nail relics to South India for Dharma propagation, and jointly built stupas with Sakra Devaraja, Naga kings and other beings. Integrating prose and poetic forms, the text details the origin, location selection and solemn form of the six stupas, interspersed with Buddhist ethical exhortations, reflecting the relic worship and Dharma propagation tradition of Theravada Buddhism, providing core historical materials for the study of Burmese Buddhist literature creation and Theravada Buddhist faith practice.  
Keywords：Cha-Kesa-Dhatu-Vamsa；J. Minayeff；Relic Worship；Theravada Buddhist Historical Text；Pali Collation；Burmese Manuscripts；Dharma Propagation Tradition

# 论文3.  《信笺故事》（《Sandesa-Katha》）
作者：J·米纳耶夫教授（Professor J. Minayeff）  
摘要：本文为巴利语佛教叙事文献《信笺故事》的校勘本，基于缅甸文手稿整理而成。文献以斯里兰卡国王与高僧的书信往来为线索，追溯南传佛教在锡兰（斯里兰卡）的传承历程，记载多次外侮导致佛法衰微后，国王遣使从印度、缅甸迎请比丘复兴佛法的史实。内容涵盖僧团戒律、出家受戒仪式、佛教节日庆典等实践细节，提及阿马拉普拉、康提等佛教圣地及多座著名寺院，兼具历史叙事与宗教劝诫属性，为研究18世纪斯里兰卡佛教复兴运动及南传佛教跨地域交流提供重要文献支撑。  
关键词：《信笺故事》；米纳耶夫；斯里兰卡佛教；佛法复兴；南传佛教交流；巴利语叙事；戒律实践  
Title：The Sandesa-Katha  
Author：Professor J. Minayeff  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali Buddhist narrative text "Sandesa-Katha", compiled based on Burmese manuscripts. Taking the correspondence between the Sri Lankan king and senior monks as the clue, the text traces the transmission history of Theravada Buddhism in Ceylon (Sri Lanka), and records the historical facts that after the decline of Buddhism caused by multiple foreign invasions, the king sent envoys to invite monks from India and Burma to revive Buddhism. The content covers practical details such as monastic precepts, ordination ceremonies and Buddhist festivals, mentioning Buddhist sacred sites such as Amarapura and Kandy as well as many famous monasteries, with both historical narrative and religious exhortatory attributes, providing important literary support for the study of the 18th-century Sri Lankan Buddhist revival movement and cross-regional communication of Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Sandesa-Katha；J. Minayeff；Sri Lankan Buddhism；Buddhism Revival；Theravada Buddhist Communication；Pali Narrative；Precept Practice

# 论文4.  巴利语词汇考证与问答
作者：理查德·莫里斯牧师（Rev. Richard Morris, M.A., LL.D.）  
摘要：本文收录莫里斯对巴利语疑难词汇的考证研究与学术问答，聚焦《巴利语词典》未收录或释义存疑的术语。内容涵盖"Akkhanavedhi"（闪电般的穿透力）、"Atthiminja"（骨髓）、"Aragatta"（波斯轮）等词汇的词源追溯、语义辨析、文献用例梳理及跨语言对比（与梵语、耆那教普拉卡利特语），纠正部分词汇的传统误读，厘清易混淆术语的用法差异。考证结合《本生经》《律藏》《论藏》等核心典籍，同时探讨"Ekodi-Bhava"（一心专注）等哲学概念的教义内涵，为巴利语语言学研究与佛教文献准确解读提供重要学术参考。  
关键词：巴利语；词汇考证；莫里斯；语义辨析；佛教语言学；文献用例；跨语言对比  
Title：Notes and Queries  
Author：Rev. Richard Morris, M.A., LL.D.  
Abstract：This paper collects Rev. Richard Morris's research and academic questions and answers on difficult Pali vocabulary, focusing on terms not included in the "Pali Dictionary" or with questionable interpretations. The content includes etymological tracing, semantic discrimination, literature example sorting and cross-linguistic comparison (with Sanskrit and Jaina Prakrit) of words such as "Akkhanavedhi" (lightning-like penetration), "Atthiminja" (bone marrow) and "Aragatta" (Persian wheel), correcting the traditional misreading of some words and clarifying the usage differences of easily confused terms. The research combines core texts such as "Jataka Tales", "Vinaya Pitaka" and "Abhidhamma Pitaka", and explores the doctrinal connotation of philosophical concepts such as "Ekodi-Bhava" (single-minded concentration), providing important academic reference for Pali linguistics research and accurate interpretation of Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali；Vocabulary Research；Richard Morris；Semantic Discrimination；Buddhist Linguistics；Literature Examples；Cross-Linguistic Comparison

# 论文5.  巴利圣典协会1885年会员名录
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文收录巴利圣典协会1885年完整会员名单，含捐赠者、六年期会员（五几尼订阅）、年度会员（一几尼订阅）及锡兰地区会员四类，详细标注会员所属地区、机构及通讯地址。会员涵盖全球多地的学者、学术机构、宗教团体及王室成员，包括暹罗国王、哈佛大学、大英博物馆、加尔各答大学等重要捐赠方与订阅方，锡兰地区会员以寺院高僧与佛教徒为主。名录直观呈现协会在19世纪末南传佛教研究领域的国际影响力与学术网络，为研究近代国际佛教研究共同体的构成提供关键史料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1885年会员名录；学术共同体；南传佛教研究；国际学术网络；捐赠者名单；锡兰会员  
Title：List of Members of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper contains the complete 1885 membership list of the Pali Text Society, including four categories: donors, six-year members (five guineas subscription), annual members (one guinea subscription) and members in Ceylon, with detailed annotations of the members' regions, institutions and correspondence addresses. Members cover scholars, academic institutions, religious groups and royal family members from all over the world, including important donors and subscribers such as the King of Siam, Harvard University, the British Museum and the University of Calcutta. Members in Ceylon are mainly senior monks and Buddhists from monasteries. The list intuitively presents the society's international influence and academic network in the field of Theravada Buddhist research at the end of the 19th century, providing key historical materials for the study of the composition of the modern international Buddhist research community.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1885 Membership List；Academic Community；Theravada Buddhist Research；International Academic Network；Donor List；Ceylon Members

# 论文6.  巴利圣典协会1885年财务报告
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1885年度财务收支报告，含英镑与锡兰卢比双币种核算明细。收入项包括1884年余额结转、会员会费、捐赠款及银行利息；支出项涵盖出版物印刷费、邮资与文具费、编辑薪酬、手稿与书籍采购费、汇率损失等。报告显示年末英镑余额216英镑10先令11便士，锡兰卢比余额1043卢比45分，详细列明每笔收支的金额与用途，同时附锡兰地区会员会费收支专项明细。报告为研究协会的财务运营模式、学术出版资金来源及成本结构提供量化依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1885年财务报告；收支明细；出版经费；财务运营；双币种核算；成本结构  
Title：Balance Sheet, Etc.  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the 1885 annual financial revenue and expenditure report of the Pali Text Society, including detailed accounts in both British Pounds and Ceylon Rupees. Income items include the carry-over balance from 1884, membership dues, donations and bank interest; expenditure items cover publication printing fees, postage and stationery fees, editor salaries, manuscript and book purchase fees, exchange losses, etc. The report shows an ending balance of 216 Pounds 10 Shillings 11 Pence in British Pounds and 1043 Rupees 45 Cents in Ceylon Rupees, detailing the amount and purpose of each revenue and expenditure, with a special breakdown of membership dues revenue and expenditure in Ceylon. The report provides quantitative basis for the study of the society's financial operation model, academic publishing funding sources and cost structure.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1885 Financial Report；Revenue and Expenditure Details；Publishing Funds；Financial Operation；Dual-Currency Accounting；Cost Structure

作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文分为两部分，第一部分系统梳理协会至1885年已出版的19部巴利语典籍，按出版年份排序，标注每部典籍的书名、编者与出版时间，涵盖经藏（如《相应部·伽陀品》）、律藏、论藏及注疏文献；第二部分列出21部在研项目，包括《长部》《中部》《善吉祥注》等核心典籍，明确每部著作的编者与进展情况。清单全面呈现协会的学术出版规划与成果，反映19世纪末南传佛教核心文献的整理现状，为研究者检索巴利语典籍版本信息提供权威指引。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；已出版典籍；在研项目；文献清单；南传佛教；出版规划；版本信息  
Title：Works Published and in Progress  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is divided into two parts. The first part systematically sorts out 19 Pali texts published by the society up to 1885, arranged by publication year, marking the title, editor and publication time of each text, covering Sutta Pitaka (such as Sagatha-vagga of the Samyutta), Vinaya Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka and commentary literature; the second part lists 21 ongoing projects, including core texts such as "Digha Nikaya", "Majjhima Nikaya" and "Sumaigala Vilasini", specifying the editor and progress of each work. The list comprehensively presents the society's academic publishing planning and achievements, reflects the collation status of core Theravada Buddhist texts at the end of the 19th century, providing authoritative guidance for researchers to retrieve version information of Pali texts.

# 论文8.  巴利圣典协会章程
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会的核心运营章程，明确协会宗旨为整理出版欧洲各图书馆收藏的未刊巴利语早期佛教文献。章程详述文献的历史价值（民俗史、宗教史、语言学）、出版规模（约9000-10000页八开本）、会员订阅标准（年度一几尼或六年五几尼）、捐赠机制等，介绍管理委员会构成及英美、斯里兰卡地区负责人信息，强调协会非营利属性与学术公益定位，提示1886年会费缴纳事宜。章程为了解协会的组织架构、运营规则与学术使命提供权威依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；章程；文献出版；会员制度；组织架构；学术宗旨；非营利组织  
Title：Prospectus of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the core operating charter of the Pali Text Society, clarifying the society's founding purpose as collating and publishing unpublished early Pali Buddhist literature collected in various libraries in Europe. The charter details the historical value of the literature (folk history, religious history, linguistics), publication scale (about 9000-10000 octavo pages), membership subscription standards (one guinea per year or five guineas for six years), donation mechanism, etc., introduces the composition of the management committee and the information of responsible persons in Britain, the United States and Sri Lanka, emphasizing the society's non-profit nature and academic public welfare positioning, and reminding of the 1886 membership dues payment. The charter provides authoritative basis for understanding the society's organizational structure, operating rules and academic mission.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Charter；Literature Publication；Membership System；Organizational Structure；Academic Purpose；Non-Profit Organization

# 论文9.  巴利圣典协会1885年年度报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1885年度工作报告，由主席T·W·里斯·戴维斯撰写。报告回顾协会年度运营情况，会员总数达157人（58名六年期会员、99名年度会员），欧洲、美洲及印度会员数量稳步增长，锡兰地区会员虽有减少但不影响整体运营；年度出版成果包括《发爪舍利史》《信笺故事》等5部核心典籍，共计约850页；介绍《长部》《善吉祥注》等21部在研项目进展，提及从锡兰获取的11份重要手稿。报告呼吁会员续订会费以支持后续出版，感谢捐赠者与编者的学术支持，为研究协会的学术发展历程与南传佛教文献整理成果提供第一手资料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1885年年度报告；典籍出版；会员发展；学术规划；文献整理；手稿收集  
Title：Report for 1885  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the 1885 annual work report of the Pali Text Society, written by the Chairman T. W. Rhys Davids. The report reviews the society's annual operation, with a total of 157 members (58 six-year members and 99 annual members), and the number of members in Europe, America and India is steadily growing. Although the number of members in Ceylon has decreased, it does not affect the overall operation; the annual publication achievements include 5 core texts such as "Cha-Kesa-Dhatu-Vamsa" and "Sandesa-Katha", totaling about 850 pages; it introduces the progress of 21 ongoing projects such as "Digha Nikaya" and "Sumaigala Vilasini", and mentions 11 important manuscripts obtained from Ceylon. The report calls on members to renew their dues to support subsequent publications, thanks donors and editors for their academic support, providing first-hand materials for the study of the society's academic development process and the achievements of Theravada Buddhist literature collation.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1885 Annual Report；Text Publication；Membership Development；Academic Planning；Literature Collation；Manuscript Collection
