# 【标题】1924-1927年第8期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1927【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  温迪施的研究与当代研究
作者：弗里德里希·韦勒（Friedrich Weller）  
摘要：本文指出莱比锡当下的巴利语研究与温迪施时代存在差异。温迪施的核心是理解巴利圣典中的佛教，而当代研究虽仍以巴利圣典为中心，但更注重通过文本比较与批判，检验圣典的适用范围。研究一方面剖析巴利圣典的编纂历史与核心结构，另一方面通过文本批判确立文献年代顺序，进而验证圣典中关于其起源与部派分裂记载的可靠性。同时探讨巴利圣典在佛教整体传统中的地位，提出《论事》是圣典断代的关键，且需重新审视锡兰传承中关于该作品及阿毗达磨的可信度。此外，指出战争及社会贫困严重阻碍了研究进展，年轻学者需涉足缅甸、西藏、中国的佛教考古与文献传统，以解决佛教研究中的诸多难题。  
关键词：巴利圣典；文本批判；温迪施；佛教研究；部派分裂  
Title：Windisch's Work and the Work of To-Day  
Author：Friedrich Weller  
Abstract：This paper points out that the current Pali studies in Leipzig are different from those in Windisch's time. Windisch's core was to understand Buddhism as preserved in the Pali Canon, while contemporary research, though still centered on the Pali Canon, focuses more on testing the applicability of the Canon through textual comparison and criticism. On the one hand, the research analyzes the compilation history and core structure of the Pali Canon; on the other hand, it establishes the chronological order of the texts through textual criticism, thereby verifying the reliability of the records in the Canon regarding its origin and sectarian schisms. It also explores the position of the Pali Canon in the overall Buddhist tradition, proposing that the Kathavatthu is the key to dating the Canon, and the credibility of this work and the Abhidhamma in the Ceylonese tradition needs to be re-examined. In addition, it is pointed out that war and social poverty have severely hindered research progress, and young scholars need to engage in the Buddhist archaeology and textual traditions of Burma, Tibet, and China to solve many problems in Buddhist studies.  
Keywords：Pali Canon；textual criticism；Windisch；Buddhist studies；sectarian schism

# 论文2.  《长老偈》与《长老尼偈》的半句诗索引
作者：威廉·施泰德（W. Stede）  
摘要：本文编纂了《长老偈》（Thera-gatha）与《长老尼偈》（Therigatha）的半句诗（padas）索引，旨在填补早期巴利诗歌历史与发展系统研究的空白。索引分为两部分，第一部分为半句诗索引，经修订、纠错及补充后，对长复合词进行拆分，方便学者对比最小文本单位；第二部分为重复半句诗的概要索引。文中指出《长老偈》原文准确性较高，而米勒编纂的《长老尼偈》存在较多文本异读问题。索引可为两部文献的修订版提供参考，同时揭示了早期巴利诗歌中重复与叠句的重要文学功能，反映出正统佛教诗歌的固定短语体系，以及部分原创诗歌的艺术价值。此外，还讨论了诗歌的韵律划分难点及特殊文本案例的处理方式。  
关键词：《长老偈》；《长老尼偈》；半句诗索引；巴利语诗歌；文本编纂  
Title：The Padas of Thera- and Theri-gatha  
Author：W. Stede  
Abstract：This paper compiles an index of padas (half-lines) from the Thera-gatha and Therigatha, aiming to fill the gap in the systematic study of the history and development of early Pali poetry. The index is divided into two parts: the first part is an index of padas, which, after revision, correction and supplementation, splits long compounds to facilitate scholars' comparison of the smallest textual units; the second part is a synoptical index of repeated padas. The paper points out that the text of the Thera-gatha is relatively accurate, while the Therigatha edited by Miller has many textual variant issues. The index can provide reference for the revised editions of the two texts, and at the same time reveals the important literary functions of repetition and refrain in early Pali poetry, reflecting the fixed phrase system of orthodox Buddhist poetry and the artistic value of some original poems. In addition, it discusses the difficulties in prosodic division of poetry and the handling of special textual cases.  
Keywords：Thera-gatha；Therigatha；pada index；Pali poetry；text compilation

# 论文3.  锡兰回忆
作者：威廉·盖格尔（W. Geiger）  
摘要：本文为盖格尔博士关于锡兰的回忆性文章，分享了其在锡兰的亲身经历与观察。内容围绕锡兰的佛教文化场景、宗教实践传统、当地学者交流等方面展开，展现了20世纪初期锡兰佛教的生存状态与文化氛围，为研究巴利语佛教在锡兰的传承与实践提供了第一手的感性资料与背景参考。  
关键词：锡兰；佛教文化；回忆；巴利语传承；宗教实践  
Title：Memories of Ceylon  
Author：W. Geiger  
Abstract：This paper is a memoir of Ceylon by Dr. W. Geiger, sharing his personal experiences and observations in Ceylon. Focusing on Ceylon's Buddhist cultural scenes, religious practice traditions, and exchanges with local scholars, it presents the living conditions and cultural atmosphere of Ceylon Buddhism in the early 20th century, providing first-hand emotional materials and background reference for the study of the inheritance and practice of Pali Buddhism in Ceylon.  
Keywords：Ceylon；Buddhist culture；memories；Pali inheritance；religious practice

# 论文4.  《法句经》第207节的新解读
作者：V.莱斯尼（V. Lesny）  
摘要：本文针对《法句经》第207节提出了新的解读观点。通过对经文文本的细致校勘与语义分析，纠正了以往解读中的偏差，深入阐释了该节经文的原始含义与宗教内涵，为《法句经》的精准理解与研究提供了新的学术视角。  
关键词：《法句经》；第207节；文本解读；巴利语经文；语义分析  
Title：A New Reading of Dhammapada 207  
Author：V. Lesny  
Abstract：This paper proposes a new interpretation of Dhammapada 207. Through detailed textual collation and semantic analysis of the scripture, it corrects the deviations in previous interpretations, deeply explains the original meaning and religious connotation of this verse, and provides a new academic perspective for the accurate understanding and research of the Dhammapada.  
Keywords：Dhammapada；Verse 207；textual interpretation；Pali scriptures；semantic analysis

# 论文5.  佛教与否定性
作者：里斯·戴维斯夫人（Mrs. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文探讨了佛教中的“否定性”概念，深入分析佛教教义中否定性表述的哲学基础与宗教意义。从佛教的认知论、修行观出发，阐释否定性思维在破除执念、体悟实相过程中的作用，对比其他思想体系中的否定性表述，揭示佛教否定性概念的独特性与实践价值。  
关键词：佛教；否定性；哲学基础；修行观；实相体悟  
Title：Buddhism and the Negative  
Author：Mrs. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper explores the concept of "negativity" in Buddhism, and deeply analyzes the philosophical basis and religious significance of negative expressions in Buddhist doctrines. Starting from Buddhism's epistemology and view of practice, it explains the role of negative thinking in eliminating attachments and realizing the true nature, compares negative expressions in other ideological systems, and reveals the uniqueness and practical value of the concept of negativity in Buddhism.  
Keywords：Buddhism；negativity；philosophical basis；view of practice；realization of true nature

# 论文6.  希腊纸草文献中的“幻”（续篇）
作者：O.斯坦因（O. Stein）  
摘要：本文是希腊纸草文献中“幻”（maya）概念研究的续篇。通过对新发现的希腊纸草文献进行文本释读与跨文化比较，探讨“幻”概念在希腊文化语境中的表述与内涵，并与佛教中的“幻”概念进行关联分析，揭示不同文化间思想交流与互渗的可能，为比较宗教学与跨文化研究提供了重要案例。  
关键词：希腊纸草文献；幻（maya）；跨文化比较；思想交流；比较宗教学  
Title：Maya in a Greek Papyrus (2)  
Author：O. Stein  
Abstract：This paper is a continuation of the study on the concept of "maya" in Greek papyri. Through textual interpretation and cross-cultural comparison of newly discovered Greek papyri, it explores the expression and connotation of the concept of "maya" in the Greek cultural context, and conducts a comparative analysis with the concept of "maya" in Buddhism, revealing the possibility of ideological exchange and mutual infiltration between different cultures, and providing an important case for comparative religion and cross-cultural studies.  
Keywords：Greek papyri；maya；cross-cultural comparison；ideological exchange；comparative religion
