# 【标题】1882年第1卷【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1882【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  巴利圣典协会1882年年度报告
作者：T·W·里斯·戴维斯（T. W. Rhys Davids）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会1882年度工作报告，回顾协会创立初期的运营与发展。协会于1881年5月成立，初期订阅增长缓慢，得益于欧洲、美国及锡兰佛教僧侣的支持得以稳步发展，锡兰70余名比丘提前订阅为出版提供重要资金。报告调整初期出版计划，聚焦锡兰保存的巴利语典籍，回应锡兰学者对文献分类、文本校勘的建议，明确优先出版三藏文献及注疏。详细列出已出版与在研典籍清单，包括《长部》《中部》《相应部》等核心经典，说明手稿收集需求与欧洲各图书馆馆藏情况，分析财务收支与会员构成，展望协会在南传佛教文献整理、跨文化学术交流中的核心价值。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1882年年度报告；巴利语典籍；出版计划；手稿收集；会员构成；学术交流  
Title：Report of the Pali Text Society for 1882  
Author：T. W. Rhys Davids  
Abstract：This paper is the 1882 annual work report of the Pali Text Society, reviewing the operation and development of the society in its early establishment. Founded in May 1881, the society initially experienced slow subscription growth but steadily developed with the support of Orientalists in Europe, America, and Buddhist monks in Ceylon. More than 70 Bhikkhus in Ceylon subscribed in advance, providing important funds for publication. The report adjusts the initial publishing plan, focusing on Pali texts preserved in Ceylon, responds to suggestions from Sri Lankan scholars on literature classification and text collation, and clarifies the priority of publishing the Tripiṭaka and its commentaries. It details the list of published and ongoing texts, including core classics such as the Digha Nikāya, Majjhima Nikāya, and Samyutta Nikāya, explains the need for manuscript collection and the collection status of major European libraries, analyzes financial revenue and expenditure as well as membership composition, and looks forward to the society's core value in the collation of Theravada Buddhist literature and cross-cultural academic exchanges.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1882 Annual Report；Pali Texts；Publishing Plan；Manuscript Collection；Membership Composition；Academic Exchange

# 论文2.  锡兰高僧来信
作者：皮亚拉塔纳·提萨长老（Piyaratana Tissa Thera）、斯里·萨达南达长老（Sri Saddhànanda Thera）、帕纳南达长老（Pannānanda Thera）、斯里·苏马纳·提萨长老（Sri Sumana Tissa Thera）  
摘要：本文收录四位锡兰高僧致巴利圣典协会的信件（三篇僧伽罗语、一篇巴利语），表达对协会出版巴利语典籍的支持与期待。皮亚拉塔纳·提萨长老建议区分三藏文本、注疏与相关论著，强调文献分类的重要性；斯里·萨达南达长老推荐出版《阿毗达磨摄义论》及相关注疏，愿提供手稿支持；帕纳南达长老强调优质手稿对文本准确性的意义，建议区分佛教与耆那教文献；斯里·苏马纳·提萨长老指出锡兰手稿的版本优越性，建议欧洲学者在翻译与校勘中寻求锡兰高僧的协助，并列出多位资深佛教学者名单。信件为协会的出版方向、文本校勘与国际合作提供重要参考。  
关键词：锡兰高僧；巴利圣典协会；巴利语典籍；手稿；文本校勘；出版建议；学术合作  
Title：Letters from Theras in Ceylon  
Author：Piyaratana Tissa Thera, Sri Saddhànanda Thera, Pannānanda Thera, Sri Sumana Tissa Thera  
Abstract：This paper collects four letters from senior Buddhist monks in Ceylon to the Pali Text Society (three in Sinhalese and one in Pali), expressing their support and expectations for the society's publication of Pali texts. Ven. Piyaratana Tissa suggests distinguishing the Tripiṭaka texts, commentaries, and related treatises, emphasizing the importance of literature classification; Ven. Sri Saddhànanda recommends publishing the Abhidhammattha-saṅgaha and its commentaries and is willing to provide manuscript support; Ven. Pannānanda emphasizes the significance of high-quality manuscripts for text accuracy and suggests distinguishing Buddhist and Jain literature; Ven. Sri Sumana Tissa points out the superiority of Sri Lankan manuscripts, recommends that European scholars seek assistance from senior Sri Lankan monks in translation and collation, and lists the names of many senior Buddhist scholars. The letters provide important references for the society's publishing direction, text collation, and international cooperation.  
Keywords：Sri Lankan Theras；Pali Text Society；Pali Texts；Manuscripts；Text Collation；Publishing Suggestions；Academic Cooperation

# 论文3.  牛津大学博德利图书馆藏巴利语手稿目录
作者：弗兰克福特博士（Dr. Frankfurter）  
摘要：本文系统梳理牛津大学博德利图书馆收藏的巴利语手稿，按文献类型分类编目。涵盖《律藏》相关的《羯磨文》（Kammavaca）手稿、《经藏》中的《中部》《小部》片段（如《法句经》）、《本生经》选段（如《维桑塔本生》）、《论藏》的《法集论》及注疏，还有非三藏文献如 Buddhaghosa 的《善见律毗婆沙》（Samanta Pasadikā）、《大史》（Mahāvaṃsa）等。详细记录每部手稿的篇章、载体、书写字体及馆藏标识，为巴利语文献研究者提供精准的馆藏检索指引，反映该图书馆在南传佛教文献收藏中的重要地位。  
关键词：牛津大学博德利图书馆；巴利语手稿；目录；三藏文献；非三藏文献；馆藏检索；文献收藏  
Title：List of Pali MSS. in the Bodleian Library, Oxford  
Author：Dr. Frankfurter  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the Pali manuscripts collected by the Bodleian Library of the University of Oxford and catalogues them by document type. It covers Kammavaca manuscripts related to the Vinaya Piṭaka, fragments of the Majjhima Nikāya and Khuddaka Nikāya in the Sutta Piṭaka (such as the Dhammapada), Jataka selections (such as the Vessantara Jataka), the Dhammasaṅgani of the Abhidhamma Piṭaka and its commentaries, as well as extra-canonical works such as Buddhaghosa's Samanta Pasadikā and the Mahāvaṃsa. It details the chapters, materials, writing scripts, and collection identifiers of each manuscript, providing accurate collection retrieval guidance for Pali literature researchers and reflecting the library's important status in the collection of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Bodleian Library, University of Oxford；Pali Manuscripts；Catalogue；Tripiṭaka；Extra-Canonical Works；Collection Retrieval；Literary Collection

# 论文4.  巴黎国家图书馆藏巴利语手稿目录
作者：莱昂·菲尔（Léon Feer）  
摘要：本文由莱昂·菲尔编纂，全面收录巴黎国家图书馆馆藏巴利语手稿，按“三藏文献及注疏”“非三藏著作”“语法与辞书类著作”三类划分。三藏文献涵盖《律藏》的《波罗提木叉》《大品》《小品》，《经藏》四部尼柯耶及《小部》各经典，《论藏》七论及注疏，手稿书写字体包括僧伽罗文、缅甸文、高棉文；非三藏著作包含《小戒经》《清净道论》《弥兰陀王问经》等；语法类著作涵盖 Kaccayana 语法及注疏、辞书《阿毗达磨词汇灯论》等。详细记录每部手稿的完整性、书写字体及馆藏信息，为国际巴利语文献研究提供关键馆藏参考。  
关键词：巴黎国家图书馆；巴利语手稿；目录；三藏文献；非三藏著作；语法著作；馆藏参考  
Title：List of Pali MSS. in the Bibliothèque Nationale, Paris  
Author：Léon Feer  
Abstract：Compiled by Léon Feer, this paper comprehensively collects the Pali manuscripts in the Bibliothèque Nationale de France, divided into three categories: "Tripiṭaka Texts and Commentaries", "Extra-Canonical Works", and "Grammars and Lexicons". The Tripiṭaka texts include the Patimokkha, Mahāvagga, Cūlavagga of the Vinaya Piṭaka, the four Nikāyas and various classics of the Khuddaka Nikāya in the Sutta Piṭaka, the seven treatises of the Abhidhamma Piṭaka and their commentaries, with manuscripts written in Sinhalese, Burmese, and Khmer scripts; extra-canonical works include the Khuddasikkha, Visuddhi Magga, Milinda Pañha, etc.; grammatical works cover Kaccayana's grammar and its commentaries, the lexicon Abhidhānappadīpikā, etc. It details the completeness, writing script, and collection information of each manuscript, providing key collection reference for international Pali literature research.  
Keywords：Bibliothèque Nationale de France；Pali Manuscripts；Catalogue；Tripiṭaka；Extra-Canonical Works；Grammatical Works；Collection Reference

# 论文5.  康提东方图书馆藏僧伽罗语、巴利语及梵语书籍目录
作者：H·C·P·贝尔（H. C. P. Bell）  
摘要：本文由H·C·P·贝尔整理，收录康提东方图书馆收藏的僧伽罗语、巴利语及梵语书籍，按语言分类编目。僧伽罗语书籍涵盖语法、辞书、文学、历史、医学等领域，包括《词形正确论》（Rūpa-siddhi）、《岛史》（Dīpavaṃsa）选段等；巴利语书籍以三藏文献为主，含《律藏》《中部》《本生经》及注疏、语法著作如《巴利语词典》（Palinighandu）；梵语书籍包括《罗摩衍那》（Raghuvamśa）、《摩奴法论》（Manusmṛti）、吠陀文献等。详细记录每部书籍的标题、载体及馆藏位置，填补锡兰地方图书馆巴利语及相关文献收藏的记录空白，为区域文献研究提供支持。  
关键词：康提东方图书馆；僧伽罗语书籍；巴利语书籍；梵语书籍；目录；区域文献；馆藏记录  
Title：List of Sinhalese, Pali, and Sanskrit Books in the Oriental Library, Kandy  
Author：H. C. P. Bell  
Abstract：Compiled by H. C. P. Bell, this paper collects the Sinhalese, Pali, and Sanskrit books held by the Oriental Library in Kandy, catalogued by language. Sinhalese books cover grammar, lexicons, literature, history, medicine, etc., including Rūpa-siddhi and excerpts from the Dīpavaṃsa; Pali books are mainly Tripiṭaka texts, including the Vinaya Piṭaka, Majjhima Nikāya, Jataka and their commentaries, and grammatical works such as Palinighandu; Sanskrit books include Raghuvamśa, Manusmṛti, Vedic literature, etc. It details the title, material, and collection location of each book, filling the gap in the records of Pali and related literature collections in local Sri Lankan libraries and providing support for regional literature research.  
Keywords：Oriental Library, Kandy；Sinhalese Books；Pali Books；Sanskrit Books；Catalogue；Regional Literature；Collection Records

# 论文6.  科伦坡博物馆藏巴利语、僧伽罗语及梵语手稿目录
作者：路易斯·德·佐伊萨（Louis de Zoysa）  
摘要：本文基于1876年科伦坡博物馆（原锡兰政府东方图书馆）官方目录编纂，收录馆藏188卷（含209部独立著作）手稿，按“佛教三藏典籍”“宗教杂著”“历史著作”“语言学著作”“诗歌”“杂项（科学、医学等）”分类。三藏典籍含缅甸文完整版本及僧伽罗文抄本，涵盖四部尼柯耶、《论藏》七论等；宗教杂著包括注疏、戒本相关著作；历史著作含《大史》《岛史》《佛牙史》等；语言学著作涵盖语法、辞书；医学著作含《药物集》（Bhaisajya Manjusa）等。详细说明每部手稿的来源、完整性及核心内容，为南传佛教文献的版本比对与传承研究提供重要依据。  
关键词：科伦坡博物馆；巴利语手稿；僧伽罗语手稿；梵语手稿；目录；佛教三藏；版本研究  
Title：List of Pali, Sinhalese, and Sanskrit Manuscripts in the Colombo Museum  
Author：Louis de Zoysa  
Abstract：Compiled based on the 1876 official catalogue of the Colombo Museum (formerly the Ceylon Government Oriental Library), this paper collects 188 volumes (including 209 independent works) of manuscripts in the collection, classified into "Buddhist Tripiṭaka Texts", "Miscellaneous Religious Works", "Historical Works", "Philological Works", "Poetry", and "Miscellaneous (Scientific, Medical, etc.)". The Tripiṭaka texts include complete Burmese versions and Sinhalese copies, covering the four Nikāyas, the seven treatises of the Abhidhamma Piṭaka, etc.; miscellaneous religious works include commentaries and works related to precepts; historical works include the Mahāvaṃsa, Dīpavaṃsa, Dalada Sirita, etc.; philological works cover grammar and lexicons; medical works include Bhaisajya Manjusa, etc. It details the source, completeness, and core content of each manuscript, providing important basis for the version comparison and inheritance research of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Colombo Museum；Pali Manuscripts；Sinhalese Manuscripts；Sanskrit Manuscripts；Catalogue；Buddhist Tripiṭaka；Version Research

Title：Catalogue of the Pali Manuscripts in the India Office Library  
Author：Hermann Oldenberg  
Abstract：Compiled by Hermann Oldenberg, this paper systematically sorts out the Pali manuscripts in the India Office Library, detailing the catalogue into three categories: "Sacred Literature with Commentaries", "Grammatical and Lexical Works", and "Historical and Miscellaneous Works". The sacred literature includes a complete set of Tripiṭaka manuscripts (29 volumes) donated by the King of Burma, covering the Vinaya Piṭaka, the four Nikāyas of the Sutta Piṭaka, various classics of the Khuddaka Nikāya, and the seven treatises of the Abhidhamma Piṭaka, with commentaries such as Samanta Pasadikā and Dhammapada Atthakatha; grammatical works include Kaccayana's grammar and its commentaries, Rūpa-siddhi, etc.; historical and miscellaneous works include the Mahāvaṃsa, Milinda Pañha, Visuddhi Magga, etc. It details the volume, number of leaves, writing script, age, and core content of each manuscript, providing authoritative collection retrieval resources for international Pali literature research and reflecting the library's core status in the collection of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：India Office Library；Pali Manuscripts；Catalogue；Tripiṭaka；Grammatical Works；Historical Works；Collection Retrieval

作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文收录巴利圣典协会1882年完整会员名单，按“捐赠者”“五年期会员（六年间五几尼订阅）”“年度会员（一几尼订阅）”“锡兰地区会员”四类划分。捐赠者含个人与机构捐赠；五年期会员涵盖欧美学术机构、学者及王室代表（如暹罗皇家博物馆）；年度会员包括全球多地大学图书馆、学术团体、学者；锡兰地区会员以寺院高僧、地方官员为主。详细标注会员姓名、所属机构、通讯地址及订阅类型，直观呈现协会的国际学术影响力与会员构成，为研究近代南传佛教研究共同体的形成提供关键史料。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；1882年会员名录；学术共同体；国际会员；锡兰会员；订阅机制；史料价值  
Title：Members of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper contains the complete 1882 membership list of the Pali Text Society, divided into four categories: "Donors", "Subscribers of Five Guineas (for Six Years)", "Subscribers of One Guinea (Annual)", and "Subscribers in Ceylon". Donors include individual and institutional donations; five-guinea subscribers cover European and American academic institutions, scholars, and royal representatives (such as the Bangkok Royal Museum); annual subscribers include university libraries, academic groups, and scholars from all over the world; subscribers in Ceylon are mainly senior monks and local officials from monasteries. It details the members' names, affiliated institutions, correspondence addresses, and subscription types, intuitively presenting the society's international academic influence and membership composition, providing key historical materials for the study of the formation of the modern Theravada Buddhist research community.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；1882 Membership List；Academic Community；International Members；Ceylon Members；Subscription Mechanism；Historical Value

# 论文9.  巴利圣典协会章程
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会的核心运营章程，明确协会宗旨为整理出版欧洲各图书馆收藏的未刊巴利语早期佛教文献，强调这些文献在民俗史、宗教史、语言学领域的重要价值。章程规定会员订阅标准（年度一几尼或六年五几尼，提前预付）、捐赠机制及管理委员会构成，说明出版物免费寄送给会员的权益。呼吁各界捐赠支持学术出版，提醒1883年会费缴纳事宜，体现协会非营利性与学术公益定位，为理解协会的组织架构与运营规则提供权威依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；章程；巴利语文献；出版宗旨；会员权益；捐赠机制；组织架构  
Title：Prospectus of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the core operating charter of the Pali Text Society, clarifying the society's founding purpose as collating and publishing unpublished early Pali Buddhist literature collected in various libraries in Europe, emphasizing the important value of these documents in the fields of folk history, religious history, and linguistics. The charter stipulates membership subscription standards (one guinea per year or five guineas for six years, payable in advance), donation mechanism, and the composition of the management committee, and explains the right of members to receive publications free of charge by post. It calls on all sectors to donate to support academic publishing, reminds of the 1883 membership dues payment, reflects the society's non-profit nature and academic public welfare positioning, and provides authoritative basis for understanding the society's organizational structure and operating rules.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Charter；Pali Literature；Publishing Purpose；Member Rights；Donation Mechanism；Organizational Structure
