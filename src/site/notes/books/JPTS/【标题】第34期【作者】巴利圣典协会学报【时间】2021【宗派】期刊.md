# 巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）
【标题】第34期【编者】奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）、鲁柏·葛汀（Rupert Gethin）【时间】2021【宗派】巴利佛教研究期刊

# 论文1. 史蒂文·柯林斯（1951–2018）
作者：鲁柏．葛汀（Rupert Gethin）  
摘要：本文缅怀了于2018年意外离世的佛教学者史蒂文·柯林斯（Steven Collins）。柯林斯享年66岁，对巴利佛教文献研究贡献卓著，曾两度长期担任巴利圣典协会理事会成员。文章追溯其学术生涯，从牛津大学本科学习拉丁语、希腊语及哲学、政治学、经济学起步，在理查德·冈布里奇指导下完成博士论文《上座部佛教中的个人连续性》，后修订出版为《无私之人：上座部佛教中的意象与思想》。他的研究融合文本学、哲学、社会学、人类学等多学科视角，注重通过巴利文本中的意象解读系统教义，后续著作《涅槃与其他佛教福祉》探索了佛教苦行主义的文化逻辑，晚年还关注上座部佛教与性别议题及跨文化哲学比较。文章还梳理了其主要学术成果与学术影响，凸显其对佛教研究领域的重要价值。  
关键词：史蒂文·柯林斯；巴利佛教文献；上座部佛教；佛教研究；学术生平  
Title：Steven Collins, 1951–2018  
Author：Rupert Gethin  
Abstract：This article commemorates the Buddhist scholar Steven Collins, who passed away unexpectedly in 2018. At the age of 66, Collins made outstanding contributions to the study of Pali Buddhist literature and served as a member of the Council of the Pali Text Society for two extended periods. The article traces his academic career, starting from his undergraduate studies in Latin, Greek, Philosophy, Politics, and Economics at the University of Oxford. He completed his doctoral dissertation "Personal Continuity in Theravada Buddhism" under the supervision of Richard Gombrich, which was later revised and published as "Selfless Persons: Imagery and Thought in Theravada Buddhism". His research integrates multidisciplinary perspectives such as textual scholarship, philosophy, sociology, and anthropology, focusing on interpreting systematic doctrines through imagery in Pali texts. His subsequent work "Nirvana and Other Buddhist Felicities" explores the cultural logic of Buddhist asceticism, and in his later years, he also focused on issues of Theravada Buddhism and gender as well as cross-cultural philosophical comparisons. The article also sorts out his main academic achievements and influences, highlighting his important value in the field of Buddhist studies.  
Keywords：Steven Collins；Pali Buddhist literature；Theravada Buddhism；Buddhist studies；academic biography

# 论文2. 奥莱·霍尔滕·平德博士（1945–2018）
作者：约根·波德曼·索伦森（Jørgen Podemann Sørensen）  
摘要：本文悼念2018年逝世的佛教学者奥莱·霍尔滕·平德（Ole Holten Pind）。平德自1967年起在奥胡斯大学研习宗教史，精通希伯来语、梵语、巴利语、藏语等多种语言，秉持“每年学习一门新语言”的原则深耕语言学与佛学研究。他曾参与《批判性巴利语词典》第三卷的编纂，在巴利语语法研究领域成就斐然，通过一系列论文及对《迦旃延语法》的校勘确立国际权威地位。其研究核心聚焦佛教哲学，尤其关注中观派“空性”概念及法称等佛教认识论学者的思想，曾基于梵语手稿重构法称《集量论注》第五章相关内容并获维也纳大学博士学位。此外，他还将多部巴利语及梵语佛教经典译为丹麦语，为丹麦的佛教研究教学奠定基础，其学术成果兼具专业性与普及价值。  
关键词：奥莱·霍尔滕·平德；巴利语研究；佛教哲学；佛教认识论；语言翻译  
Title：Dr phil. Ole Holten Pind, 1945–2018  
Author：Jørgen Podemann Sørensen  
Abstract：This article mourns the Buddhist scholar Ole Holten Pind, who passed away in 2018. Pind began studying the history of religions at Aarhus University in 1967, proficient in multiple languages such as Hebrew, Sanskrit, Pali, and Tibetan, and adhered to the principle of "learning a new language every year" to delve into linguistics and Buddhist studies. He participated in the compilation of the third volume of "A Critical Pali Dictionary" and achieved remarkable success in the field of Pali grammar research, establishing an international authority through a series of papers and the edition of Kaccayana's grammar. His research focuses on Buddhist philosophy, especially the concept of "emptiness" in the Madhyamaka school and the ideas of Buddhist epistemological scholars such as Dignāga. He reconstructed the relevant content of the fifth chapter of Dignāga's "Pramāasamuccayavtti" based on Sanskrit manuscripts and obtained a doctorate from the University of Vienna. In addition, he translated many Pali and Sanskrit Buddhist classics into Danish, laying the foundation for Buddhist studies and teaching in Denmark, and his academic achievements combine professionalism and popularization value.  
Keywords：Ole Holten Pind；Pali language studies；Buddhist philosophy；Buddhist epistemology；language translation

# 论文3. 阿毗达磨研究第四辑：《真理概要》及其注释
作者：L.S. 库辛斯（L.S. Cousins）  
摘要：本文是L.S. 库辛斯身后出版的第四篇阿毗达磨研究论文，聚焦《真理概要》（Saccasaṅkhepa）及其注释的相关问题。文章指出，该著作的作者归属长期存在争议，传统文献将其归于阿难或达摩波罗，但经文本内容考证，排除了这两位学者的可能性，推测可能为乔提婆（Jotipāla）所作，但尚无确凿证据。文中梳理了现存的两部注释——《真理概要广释》（Saccasaṅkhepavivaraṇa）与《要义明灯》（Sāratthasālinī），考证前者虽被部分手稿标注为“古注释”，实则晚于后者，且后者成书不晚于13世纪。通过分析文本中涉及的“生是否生起”“心所生声与交流”等核心议题，探讨了《真理概要》在阿毗达磨注释文献史上的定位，其作者采用兼容并蓄的态度，同时呈现了注释传统与疏释传统的观点。此外，文章还讨论了著作开篇的致敬传统等文本特征，为阿毗达磨文献研究提供重要参考。  
关键词：阿毗达磨研究；《真理概要》；文本注释；作者考证；巴利文献  
Title：Abhidhamma Studies IV: The Saccasaṅkhepa and Its Commentaries  
Author：L.S. Cousins  
Abstract：This article is the fourth posthumously published Abhidhamma study by L.S. Cousins, focusing on the Saccasaṅkhepa and its commentaries. The article points out that the authorship of this work has long been controversial. Traditional documents attribute it to Ānanda or Dhammapāla, but textual research excludes these two scholars and speculates that it may be the work of Jotipāla, though there is no conclusive evidence. The article sorts out the two existing commentaries - Saccasaṅkhepavivaraṇa and Sāratthasālinī, verifying that although the former is labeled as an "old commentary" in some manuscripts, it is actually later than the latter, and the latter was completed no later than the 13th century. By analyzing core issues such as "whether birth is born" and "mind-born sound and communication" involved in the text, it explores the position of the Saccasaṅkhepa in the history of Abhidhamma exegetical literature. Its author adopts an eclectic attitude, presenting the views of both the commentary tradition and the subcommentary tradition. In addition, the article discusses textual features such as the homage tradition at the beginning of the work, providing important references for the study of Abhidhamma literature.  
Keywords：Abhidhamma Studies；Saccasaṅkhepa；textual commentaries；authorship research；Pali literature

# 论文4. 16世纪兰那（泰国北部）的巴利语文本创作：西里玛加拉的生平与著作（第一部分）
作者：格雷戈里·库里尔斯基（Gregory Kourilsky）  
摘要：本文聚焦16世纪泰国北部兰那王国的巴利语文本创作，重点研究僧侣学者西里玛加拉（Sirimaṅgala）的生平与著作。西里玛加拉活跃于16世纪初清迈，著有《维桑塔罗往世书释》《吉祥经释》等多部重要巴利语著作，其作品在泰国、老挝、柬埔寨、缅甸及斯里兰卡广泛流传，对东南亚上座部佛教影响深远。文章将其置于15-16世纪兰那巴利语文学“黄金时代”的背景下，梳理兰那佛教的三大派系及巴利语文学的发展脉络，指出西里玛加拉的作品体现了对巴利语的娴熟掌握。通过分析其著作的题跋、内容及流传情况，考证其生平细节、修行场所及学术传承，探讨其作品中体现的兰那佛教特色与周边佛教文化的交流互动。本文作为第一部分，为后续深入研究西里玛加拉的学术贡献及兰那巴利语文学的整体图景奠定基础。  
关键词：16世纪兰那；巴利语文本；西里玛加拉；兰那佛教；东南亚佛教  
Title：Writing Pali Texts in 16th century Lan Na (Northern Thailand): The Life and Work of Sirimaṅgala (Part I)  
Author：Gregory Kourilsky  
Abstract：This article focuses on the composition of Pali texts in the 16th-century Lan Na kingdom in northern Thailand, with an emphasis on the life and works of the monk-scholar Sirimaṅgala. Active in Chiang Mai in the early 16th century, Sirimaṅgala authored many important Pali works such as the Vessantaradīpanī and the Maṅgalatthadīpanī. His works have been widely circulated in Thailand, Laos, Cambodia, Myanmar, and Sri Lanka, exerting a profound influence on Theravada Buddhism in Southeast Asia. Placing him in the context of the "golden age" of Lanna Pali literature in the 15th-16th centuries, the article sorts out the three major sects of Lanna Buddhism and the development context of Pali literature, pointing out that Sirimaṅgala's works reflect a masterful grasp of Pali. By analyzing the colophons, contents, and circulation of his works, it researches the details of his life, practice sites, and academic inheritance, and explores the characteristics of Lanna Buddhism reflected in his works and the exchange and interaction with surrounding Buddhist cultures. As the first part, this article lays the foundation for the subsequent in-depth study of Sirimaṅgala's academic contributions and the overall picture of Lanna Pali literature.  
Keywords：16th-century Lan Na；Pali texts；Sirimaṅgala；Lan Na Buddhism；Southeast Asian Buddhism

# 论文5. 重审巴利语单音节词
作者：哈维尔·施纳克（Javier Schnake）  
摘要：本文重新探讨巴利语中的单音节词（ekakkharas）相关问题。巴利语词典编纂传统虽不及梵语丰富，但单音节词的研究仍具有重要意义。文章追溯单音节词在巴利语文献中的发展脉络，从早期经典中的零星使用，到后期注释文献中通过语义词源学方法阐释术语含义，再到专门词典的编纂。重点分析《阿毗达磨词汇》《 Saddanīti》等著作中对单音节词的整理与阐释，探讨其在语言游戏、密宗实践及诗歌创作中的应用。以16世纪缅甸学者萨达马基蒂（Saddhammakitti）编纂的《单音节词词典》（Ekakkharakosa）为例，分析其编纂背景、内容特色及学术价值，揭示单音节词研究与佛教教义传承、语言保护之间的关联。文章还讨论了单音节词在兰那等东南亚地区巴利语文学中的应用，展现其跨地域的学术影响。  
关键词：巴利语；单音节词；词典编纂；语义词源学；佛教语言  
Title：Pali ekakkharas Revisited  
Author：Javier Schnake  
Abstract：This article re-examines issues related to monosyllables (ekakkharas) in Pali. Although the Pali lexicographic tradition is not as rich as that of Sanskrit, the study of monosyllables is still of great significance. The article traces the development context of monosyllables in Pali literature, from sporadic use in early classics, to the interpretation of term meanings through semantic etymological methods in later commentarial literature, and then to the compilation of specialized dictionaries. It focuses on the collation and interpretation of monosyllables in works such as the Abhidhānappadīpikā and the Saddanīti, exploring their application in language games, Tantric practices, and poetry creation. Taking the Ekakkharakosa compiled by the 16th-century Burmese scholar Saddhammakitti as an example, it analyzes its compilation background, content characteristics, and academic value, revealing the connection between monosyllable research and the inheritance of Buddhist doctrines and language protection. The article also discusses the application of monosyllables in Pali literature in Southeast Asian regions such as Lan Na, demonstrating their cross-regional academic influence.  
Keywords：Pali；monosyllables；lexicography；semantic etymology；Buddhist language

# 论文6. 《长老偈》第168首
作者：马丁·斯特劳贝（Martin Straube）  
摘要：本文对《长老偈》（Theragāthā）第168首偈颂进行文本校勘与解读。该偈颂原文为“tvañ ca me maggam akkhīhi añjasaṃ amatogadhaṃ; ahaṃ monena monissaṃ Gaṅgāsoṭo va sāgaraṃ ti”，传统注释将“monissaṃ”源于动词“munīti”（知晓），译为“我将以智慧知晓它，如恒河之流汇入大海”，但此解读在语境与语法上存在矛盾。作者提出新的校勘观点，认为“monissaṃ”应读作“mon’ issaṃ”，即“monaṃ issaṃ”的连声形式，“issaṃ”为动词“eti”（前往、抵达）的第一人称未来时，据此将后半句译为“以修行圣者之道，我将抵达圣者之境，如恒河之流汇入大海”。该解读不仅使比喻更契合语境，且与早期巴利语偈颂中“mona”与“muni”的语义关联及修辞传统相符，为《长老偈》的文本解读提供了新的视角。  
关键词：《长老偈》；文本校勘；巴利语偈颂；语义解读；佛教诗歌  
Title：Theragāthā 168  
Author：Martin Straube  
Abstract：This article conducts textual emendation and interpretation of the 168th verse of the Theragāthā. The original text of the verse is "tvañ ca me maggam akkhīhi añjasaṃ amatogadhaṃ; ahaṃ monena monissaṃ Gaṅgāsoṭo va sāgaraṃ ti". Traditional commentaries derive "monissaṃ" from the verb "munīti" (to know), translating it as "By sage-hood I shall know it, as the stream of Ganges will eventually know the sea", but this interpretation has contradictions in context and grammar. The author proposes a new emendation view, arguing that "monissaṃ" should be read as "mon’ issaṃ", i.e., the sandhi form of "monaṃ issaṃ", where "issaṃ" is the first-person future tense of the verb "eti" (to go, to reach). Based on this, the second half of the verse is translated as "By living the way of life of a muni I will reach the state of a muni, as the stream of the Ganges will reach the sea". This interpretation not only makes the metaphor more consistent with the context but also conforms to the semantic connection between "mona" and "muni" and the rhetorical tradition in early Pali verses, providing a new perspective for the textual interpretation of the Theragāthā.  
Keywords：Theragāthā；textual emendation；Pali verses；semantic interpretation；Buddhist poetry

# 论文7. 固都陶佛塔石碑镌刻的巴利三藏版本及其与其他缅甸版本的关联
作者：罗伊斯·怀尔斯（Royce Wiles）、克里斯·克拉克（Chris Clark）、塔玛拉·迪特里希（Tamara Ditrich）、马克·艾伦（Mark Allon）  
摘要：本文探讨了缅甸曼德勒固都陶佛塔石碑上镌刻的巴利三藏版本及其与其他缅甸版本的关联。1860-1868年，敏东王下令将完整的巴利三藏镌刻于729块大理石石碑上，该版本经1871年第五次结集审定，成为缅甸佛教传统中重要的文本典范。文章梳理了该版本的历史背景、西方学者的相关记载、第五次结集的文本校勘过程及后续印刷出版情况，重点分析了1893-1939年汉达瓦底出版社等基于石碑文本的印刷版本。通过对比石碑文本、19世纪手稿、第五次与第六次结集印刷本及巴利圣典协会版本的四个经典片段，揭示了固都陶版本的文本特征及在缅甸巴利三藏传承中的核心地位。此外，文章还探讨了第六次结集对固都陶版本的继承与修订，为理解缅甸巴利三藏的文本传承脉络提供关键参考。  
关键词：固都陶佛塔；巴利三藏；文本版本；缅甸佛教；第五次结集；第六次结集  
Title：The Pāli Tipiṭaka Recension Carved on the Kuthodaw Pagoda Stelae and Its Relation to Other Burmese Versions  
Author：Royce Wiles, Chris Clark, Tamara Ditrich, Mark Allon  
Abstract：This article explores the Pāli Tipiṭaka recension carved on the stelae of the Kuthodaw Pagoda in Mandalay, Myanmar, and its relation to other Burmese versions. From 1860 to 1868, King Mindon ordered the complete Pāli Tipiṭaka to be carved on 729 marble stelae. This version was authenticated by the Fifth Buddhist Council in 1871, becoming an important textual model in the Burmese Buddhist tradition. The article sorts out the historical background of this version, relevant records by Western scholars, the textual emendation process of the Fifth Buddhist Council, and subsequent printing and publication. It focuses on analyzing the printed versions based on the stelae text by presses such as the Hanthawaddy Press from 1893 to 1939. By comparing four canonical excerpts from the stelae text, 19th-century manuscripts, printed versions of the Fifth and Sixth Buddhist Councils, and the Pali Text Society version, it reveals the textual characteristics of the Kuthodaw version and its core position in the transmission of the Pāli Tipiṭaka in Myanmar. In addition, the article discusses the inheritance and revision of the Kuthodaw version by the Sixth Buddhist Council, providing key references for understanding the textual transmission context of the Pāli Tipiṭaka in Myanmar.  
Keywords：Kuthodaw Pagoda；Pāli Tipiṭaka；textual recension；Burmese Buddhism；Fifth Buddhist Council；Sixth Buddhist Council

# 论文8. 边界与领域：理解布达皮亚《色法成就论》中的选择性
作者：阿莱克斯·鲁伊斯-法尔克斯（Aleix Ruiz-Falqués）  
摘要：本文聚焦布达皮亚（Buddhappiya）的《色法成就论》（Rūpasiddhi），旨在理解其中的“选择性”（optionality）概念及其涉及的边界与领域问题。《色法成就论》是一部重要的巴利语佛教论著，探讨色法相关的理论与实践，其中“选择性”议题涉及佛教教义中色法分类、修行路径等方面的灵活阐释。文章通过文本细读，分析布达皮亚对色法边界的界定、不同修行领域中选择性的表现形式，以及这一概念与上座部佛教传统教义的关联与创新。结合相关注释文献与学术研究，探讨“选择性”在佛教认识论、修行论中的理论意义，为深入理解《色法成就论》的思想体系及上座部佛教色法理论的发展提供新的研究视角。  
关键词：布达皮亚；《色法成就论》；选择性；色法理论；上座部佛教  
Title：Boundaries and Domains: Understanding Optionality in Buddhappiya’s Rūpasiddhi  
Author：Aleix Ruiz-Falqués  
Abstract：This article focuses on Buddhappiya’s Rūpasiddhi, aiming to understand the concept of "optionality" and the related issues of boundaries and domains. The Rūpasiddhi is an important Pāli Buddhist treatise that explores theories and practices related to materiality (rūpa). The issue of "optionality" involves the flexible interpretation of materiality classification and practice paths in Buddhist doctrines. Through close textual reading, the article analyzes Buddhappiya’s definition of the boundaries of materiality, the manifestations of optionality in different practice domains, and the connection and innovation of this concept with traditional Theravada Buddhist doctrines. Combining relevant commentarial literature and academic research, it explores the theoretical significance of "optionality" in Buddhist epistemology and practice theory, providing a new research perspective for an in-depth understanding of the ideological system of the Rūpasiddhi and the development of Theravada Buddhist materiality theory.  
Keywords：Buddhappiya；Rūpasiddhi；optionality；rūpa theory；Theravada Buddhism

# 论文9. 《巴利语派生动词注释索引》
作者：蒂莫西·P. 莱特希泽（Timothy P. Lighthiser）  
摘要：本文介绍了《巴利语派生动词注释索引》（Nāmadhātusūcī）的编纂背景、结构与价值。派生动词（denominative verbs）是巴利语动词体系的重要组成部分，由名词或形容词派生而来，在巴利语文献中应用广泛。该索引对巴利语中的派生动词进行系统整理，包含动词的词形、词源、语义解释及文献用例，并附上详细注释。文章阐述了索引的编纂方法，包括文献来源的选取、动词的筛选与分类、注释的撰写原则等，强调其对巴利语语法研究、文本解读及词典编纂的重要参考价值。通过该索引，学者可更便捷地查询巴利语派生动词的相关信息，深入理解其语义演变与用法特征，为巴利语语言研究及佛教文献解读提供实用工具。  
关键词：巴利语；派生动词；索引；语法研究；文献解读  
Title：Nāmadhātusūcī: An Annotated Index of Denominative Verbs in Pāli  
Author：Timothy P. Lighthiser  
Abstract：This article introduces the compilation background, structure, and value of the Nāmadhātusūcī: An Annotated Index of Denominative Verbs in Pāli. Denominative verbs are an important part of the Pali verb system, derived from nouns or adjectives, and widely used in Pali literature. This index systematically collates denominative verbs in Pali, including their forms, etymologies, semantic explanations, and literary examples, with detailed annotations. The article elaborates on the compilation methods of the index, including the selection of literary sources, the screening and classification of verbs, and the principles for writing annotations, emphasizing its important reference value for Pali grammar research, textual interpretation, and lexicography. Through this index, scholars can more conveniently query relevant information about Pali denominative verbs, gain an in-depth understanding of their semantic evolution and usage characteristics, and provide a practical tool for Pali language research and Buddhist literature interpretation.  
Keywords：Pali；denominative verbs；index；grammar research；textual interpretation

# 论文10. 巴利语咒语与咒语持诵者研究
作者：马丁·斯特劳贝（Martin Straube）  
摘要：本文研究了巴利文中的“mantā”（咒语）与“mantabhāvin”（持咒者）的概念，分析了它们在巴利佛教文学中的重要性。作者探讨了咒语在佛教仪式和修行中的作用，特别是在冥想和精神修行中的功能。同时，论文也讨论了持咒者这一概念如何在佛教的修行和文化中具有特殊意义。
关键词：巴利语；咒语；咒语持诵者；佛教密宗；文献研究  
Title：Pali mantras and mantabhājini
Author：Martin Straube  
Abstract：This paper examines the concepts of "mantā" (mantras) and "mantabhāvin" (mantra practitioners) in Pāli, discussing their significance in Pāli Buddhist literature. The paper explores the role of mantras in Buddhist rituals and practices, especially in meditation and spiritual exercises. It also addresses the unique importance of the concept of mantra practitioners in the context of Buddhist religious and cultural practices.
Keywords：Pali；mantras；mantabhājini；Buddhist Tantrism；literature research
