# 【标题】第23期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1997【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 巴利语、帕尼尼语法与“通俗”梵语
作者：托马斯·奥伯利斯（Thomas Oberlies）  
摘要：本文聚焦巴利语、史诗与往世书梵语（即“通俗”梵语）及帕尼尼语法的关联，指出学界此前未系统探究三者的语言学联系。通过分析格范畴替换（工具格代处所格）、动词形态、词汇构词等句法与词汇现象，论证三者的交叉研究对理解印度-雅利安语言发展及文本语义的重要价值。研究修正了传统语法中关于巴利语元音省略、特殊过去时形式等问题的解读，结合帕尼尼语法规则与梵语、巴利语平行文本，厘清了多个疑难语言现象的演变脉络。  
关键词：巴利语；帕尼尼语法；通俗梵语；印度-雅利安语言；语言学对比  
Title：Pali, Panini and "Popular" Sanskrit  
Author：Thomas Oberlies  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the connections between Pali, Epic and Puranic Sanskrit (i.e., "Popular" Sanskrit), and Panini's grammar, pointing out that academic circles have not systematically explored the linguistic links between the three. By analyzing syntactic and lexical phenomena such as case substitution (instrumental in place of locative), verb morphology, and word formation, it demonstrates the important value of cross-research on the three for understanding the development of Indo-Aryan languages and textual semantics. The study revises the interpretations of issues such as vowel syncopation and special preterite forms in Pali in traditional grammar, and clarifies the evolutionary context of several difficult linguistic phenomena by combining Panini's grammatical rules with parallel texts in Sanskrit and Pali.  
Keywords：Pali；Panini；Popular Sanskrit；Indo-Aryan languages；linguistic comparison

# 论文2. 《Paramatthajotikadipani》：《Paramatthajotika II》中〈小诵〉的疏释残篇
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文介绍了1532年抄写的泰国北部兰那地区棕榈叶手稿残篇，该残篇为《Paramatthajotika II》（《小诵》注释）的疏释文本，填补了巴利语藏外文献中《小诵》疏释的空白。手稿涵盖《小诵》“爱欲经”至“前分经”的注释内容，包含大量文献学术语与语法分析，引用了《尼达萨》等早期注释。研究整理了手稿的文本特征、抄写错误及修正情况，分析了其对理解兰那地区巴利文献传承及注释传统的学术价值，指出手稿存在约160页的文本缺漏，推测其与底本的对应关系存在特殊性。  
关键词：Paramatthajotikadipani；小诵；巴利语疏释；兰那手稿；文献学  
Title：The Paramatthajotikadipani, a Fragment of the Sub-commentary to the Paramatthajotika II on the Suttanipata  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper introduces a palm-leaf manuscript fragment copied in 1532 from the Lan Na region in northern Thailand. The fragment is a sub-commentary on the Paramatthajotika II (a commentary on the Suttanipata), filling the gap of sub-commentaries on the Suttanipata in extra-canonical Pali literature. The manuscript covers the commentary content from the "Kamasutta" to the "Purabhedasutta" of the Suttanipata, containing a large number of philological terms and grammatical analyses, and citing early commentaries such as the Niddesa. The study sorts out the textual characteristics, scribal errors and corrections of the manuscript, analyzes its academic value for understanding the transmission of Pali literature and the commentarial tradition in the Lan Na region, and points out that the manuscript has a textual gap of about 160 pages, speculating that its corresponding relationship with the original text is special.  
Keywords：Paramatthajotikadipani；Suttanipata；Pali sub-commentary；Lan Na manuscript；philology

# 论文3. 兰那佛教史相关兰那佛教文献
作者：汉斯·彭特（Hans Penth）  
摘要：本文系统梳理了兰那地区（泰国北部）与佛教史相关的本土文献，将其分为通用宗教编年史、佛像编年史、宗教场所编年史、铭文、题跋及其他六大类。这些文献以孟语、巴利语、兰那泰语撰写，载体涵盖石碑、棕榈叶等，核心聚焦本地佛教事件、佛像传承、寺庙历史等。研究详细列出各类文献的代表作、成书年代、版本流传及出版情况，分析了兰那文献“以地域为中心、以事件为导向”的特征，指出其虽缺乏批判性比较与哲学思辨，但因忠实传承原文而具有重要史料价值，为重构兰那佛教发展史提供了核心文本支撑。  
关键词：兰那；佛教文献；佛教史；文献分类；兰那泰语  
Title：Buddhist Literature of Lān Nā on the History of Lān Nā's Buddhism  
Author：Hans Penth  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the local literature related to the history of Buddhism in the Lan Na region (northern Thailand), dividing it into six categories: general religious chronicles, chronicles of Buddha images, chronicles of religious sites, inscriptions, colophons, and others. These documents are written in Mon, Pali, and Lan Na Thai, with carriers including stone steles and palm leaves, focusing on local Buddhist events, the inheritance of Buddha images, and the history of temples. The study details the representative works, compilation dates, version dissemination, and publication status of various types of literature, analyzes the "region-centered and event-oriented" characteristics of Lan Na literature, and points out that although it lacks critical comparison and philosophical speculation, it has important historical value due to its faithful inheritance of the original text, providing core textual support for reconstructing the development history of Lan Na Buddhism.  
Keywords：Lan Nā；Buddhist literature；Buddhist history；literature classification；Lan Nā Thai

# 论文4. “巴特那《法句经》”的教派归属考
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文针对11-12世纪印度巴特那地区出土的《法句经》手稿（PDhp）的教派归属问题展开考证。该手稿语言为独特的中期印度雅利安语，既非巴利语也非已知佛教混合梵语，内容与上座部、说一切有部等现存教派的《法句经》差异显著。通过梳理文献学、语言学证据及藏文史料中关于印度佛教教派的记载，排除了大众部-说出世部、（根本）说一切有部、上座部等可能，论证该手稿最可能归属正量部。研究结合正量部在中印度的传播历程、语言特征及相关铭文佐证，指出巴特那《法句经》是正量部经典的重要遗存，填补了该教派文献传承的空白。  
关键词：巴特那《法句经》；正量部；教派归属；佛教文献；中期印度雅利安语  
Title：On the School-affiliation of the "Patna Dhammapada"  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper conducts textual research on the school-affiliation of the Dhammapada manuscript (PDhp) unearthed in the Patna region of India in the 11th-12th centuries. The language of this manuscript is a unique Middle Indo-Aryan language, neither Pali nor known Buddhist Hybrid Sanskrit, and its content is significantly different from the Dhammapada of existing schools such as the Theravada and Sarvastivada. By sorting out philological, linguistic evidence and records of Indian Buddhist schools in Tibetan historical materials, it excludes the possibilities of the Mahasamghika-Lokottaravadin, (Mula)Sarvastivadin, Sthavira, etc., and argues that the manuscript most likely belongs to the Sammatiya school. Combining the dissemination history of the Sammatiya school in Madhyadesa, linguistic characteristics, and relevant epigraphic evidence, the study points out that the Patna Dhammapada is an important relic of Sammatiya canonical texts, filling the gap in the literary transmission of this school.  
Keywords：Patna Dhammapada；Sammatiya；school-affiliation；Buddhist literature；Middle Indo-Aryan language

# 论文5. 东南亚新发现的巴利语铭文
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文报道了在东南亚新发现的多篇巴利语引文铭文，这些铭文出自柬埔寨、泰国、越南等遗址，内容为佛教经典摘录，非原创作品。铭文涵盖巴利圣典、注释文献中的偈颂与散文片段，涉及因果业报、修行解脱等核心教义。研究对铭文的文字形态、语言特征、成书年代及文化背景进行了详细考证，分析了其与当地佛教仪式、教义传播的关联，指出这些铭文是研究东南亚佛教与印度佛教文本传承关系的重要实物证据，反映了巴利文献在东南亚的广泛传播与本土化过程。  
关键词：东南亚；巴利语铭文；佛教经典；文本传承；铭文考证  
Title：New Pali Inscriptions from South-east Asia  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This paper reports on several newly discovered Pali citation inscriptions in Southeast Asia. These inscriptions are from sites in Cambodia, Thailand, Vietnam, etc., and their content is excerpts from Buddhist scriptures, not original works. The inscriptions include verses and prose fragments from the Pali Canon and commentarial literature, involving core doctrines such as karma and rebirth, and practice and liberation. The study conducts detailed textual research on the script form, linguistic characteristics, compilation date, and cultural background of the inscriptions, analyzes their connections with local Buddhist rituals and the dissemination of doctrines, and points out that these inscriptions are important physical evidence for studying the textual inheritance relationship between Southeast Asian Buddhism and Indian Buddhism, reflecting the wide dissemination and localization of Pali literature in Southeast Asia.  
Keywords：South-east Asia；Pali inscriptions；Buddhist scriptures；textual transmission；epigraphic research

# 论文6. 舍利弗及其著作
作者：普里莫兹·佩岑科（Primoz Pecenko）  
摘要：本文聚焦早期佛教重要论师舍利弗的生平事迹与著作传承，梳理了巴利圣典、梵文文献及相关注释中关于舍利弗的记载，还原其作为佛陀十大弟子之一的核心地位。研究重点考证了相传为舍利弗所著的《集异门足论》《法蕴足论》等阿毗达磨文献的归属问题，分析了这些著作的思想特征与文本流传脉络。通过对比不同教派文献中对舍利弗思想的阐释差异，探讨了其教义对后世上座部、说一切有部等流派的影响，指出舍利弗的著作是早期佛教哲学体系构建的重要基石。  
关键词：舍利弗；阿毗达磨；佛教论师；文本传承；教义影响  
Title：Sariputta and his works  
Author：Primoz Pecenko  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the life deeds and literary inheritance of Sariputta, an important master of early Buddhism, sorting out records about Sariputta in the Pali Canon, Sanskrit literature, and related commentaries, restoring his core status as one of the Buddha's ten principal disciples. The study focuses on textual research on the attribution of Abhidhamma texts traditionally attributed to Sariputta, such as the Vibhanga and Dhamma-sangani, and analyzes the ideological characteristics and textual transmission context of these works. By comparing the differences in the interpretation of Sariputta's thoughts in the literature of different schools, it explores the influence of his doctrines on later schools such as the Theravada and Sarvastivada, pointing out that Sariputta's works are an important cornerstone for the construction of the early Buddhist philosophical system.  
Keywords：Sariputta；Abhidhamma；Buddhist master；textual transmission；doctrinal influence

# 论文7. 《巴利圣典协会学报》第九至二十二卷索引
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文为《巴利圣典协会学报》第九至二十二卷（1981-1996年出版）编制了系统索引，是对该学报早期索引的补充。索引按作者姓氏字母顺序排列，收录各卷发表的论文、译文、书评、文献考证等各类成果，明确标注每篇文献的卷次、页码及核心主题，涵盖巴利语语言学、佛教文献学、教义研究、手稿整理等多个领域。该索引为巴利学研究者快速检索学报往期成果提供了高效工具，助力相关主题的系统性研究与文献梳理。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会学报；索引；巴利学研究；文献检索；学术成果整理  
Title：An Index to JPTS Volumes IX-XXII  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper compiles a systematic index for Volumes IX to XXII of the Journal of the Pali Text Society (published from 1981 to 1996), supplementing the early index of the journal. Arranged in alphabetical order by authors' surnames, the index includes various achievements published in each volume, such as papers, translations, book reviews, and philological research, clearly marking the volume, page number, and core theme of each document, covering multiple fields including Pali linguistics, Buddhist philology, doctrinal research, and manuscript collation. This index provides an efficient tool for Pali studies researchers to quickly retrieve past achievements of the journal, facilitating systematic research and literature sorting on related topics.  
Keywords：Journal of the Pali Text Society；index；Pali studies；literature retrieval；academic achievement collation
