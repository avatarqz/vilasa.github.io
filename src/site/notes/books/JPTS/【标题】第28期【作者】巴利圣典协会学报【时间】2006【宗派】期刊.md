# 论文1. 《未来史》再探
作者：肯尼斯·罗伊·诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文旨在修订并出版《未来史》（Anāgatavaṃsa）的新版本及译本，该文本记载了未来佛弥勒的相关传说。新版本以米纳耶夫（Minayeff）1886年的版本为基础，参考了洛伊曼（Leumann）1919年的修订本，修正了明显讹误，沿用了米纳耶夫的诗节划分以契合《批判巴利语词典》的引用规范，同时采用了洛伊曼的标点与词法划分。译本修订自普鲁伊特（Pruitt）1988年的译文，并吸纳了柯林斯（Collins）1998年译本及注释的核心观点。作者原本计划与普鲁伊特合作编辑《未来史》的注释，但因健康原因未能实现，希望此次修订本能为后续注释编辑工作提供基础。  
关键词：《未来史》；弥勒；巴利语文本；文本修订；佛传文献  
Title：The Anāgatavaṃsa Revisited  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This article aims to revise and publish a new edition and translation of the Anāgatavaṃsa, a text recording legends about the future Buddha Metteyya. The new edition is based on Minayeff's 1886 version, refers to Leumann's 1919 revised edition, corrects obvious errors, follows Minayeff's verse division to conform to the citation standards of the Critical Pāli Dictionary, and adopts Leumann's punctuation and word division. The translation is revised from Pruitt's 1988 translation and incorporates core insights from Collins' 1998 translation and notes. The author originally planned to collaborate with Pruitt on editing the commentary on the Anāgatavaṃsa but was unable to do so due to health reasons, hoping that this revised edition will lay the foundation for subsequent commentary editing work.  
Keywords：Anāgatavaṃsa；Metteyya；Pāli text；textual revision；Buddhist biographical literature

# 论文2. 斯里兰卡文献学
作者：吉纳达萨·利亚纳拉特纳（Jinadasa Liyanaratne）  
摘要：本文是对安妮·M.布莱克本《斯里兰卡寺庙手稿收藏札记》的评论，聚焦斯里兰卡贝叶经的价值、整理现状及存在的问题。斯里兰卡贝叶经是20世纪前该国知识遗产的核心载体，涵盖佛教、语法、医学等多领域内容，以僧伽罗语、巴利语、梵语及泰米尔语书写，但其面临宗教冲突、气候侵蚀、文物贩卖等破坏风险。19世纪中叶起已有手稿编目工作，但布莱克本的目录存在分类不合理、转录错误、语言学问题等缺陷。作者强调，编目需具备多语言知识与文献学训练，呼吁开展全国性斯里兰卡手稿普查，肯定布莱克本聚焦文化遗产价值的努力。  
关键词：斯里兰卡；贝叶经；文献编目；手稿保护；文献学评论  
Title：Sri Lankan Manuscriptology  
Author：Jinadasa Liyanaratne  
Abstract：This article is a review of Anne M. Blackburn's "Notes on Sri Lankan Temple Manuscript Collections", focusing on the value, current status of collation, and existing problems of Sri Lankan palm-leaf manuscripts. Sri Lankan palm-leaf manuscripts are the core carrier of the country's intellectual heritage before the 20th century, covering Buddhism, grammar, medicine and other fields, written in Sinhala, Pāli, Sanskrit and Tamil, but they face destruction risks such as religious conflicts, climate erosion, and cultural relic trafficking. Cataloguing work has been carried out since the mid-19th century, but Blackburn's inventory has flaws such as unreasonable classification, transcription errors, and linguistic issues. The author emphasizes that cataloguing requires multilingual knowledge and philological training, calls for a nationwide census of Sri Lankan manuscripts, and affirms Blackburn's efforts in highlighting the value of cultural heritage.  
Keywords：Sri Lanka；palm-leaf manuscripts；textual cataloguing；manuscript preservation；philological review

# 论文3. 舍利弗关于《善见律毗婆沙》的三部著作
作者：凯特·克罗斯比（Kate Crosby）  
摘要：12世纪波隆纳鲁沃国王帕拉克拉马巴胡一世推动佛教改革，强调戒律的正确理解与践行，学者僧舍利弗受国王委托撰写了多部关于觉音《善见律毗婆沙》的著作，包括《隐藏要义阐释》（Linasaratthadipani）、《戒律裁决汇编》（Palimuttakavinayavinicchayasangaha）及《戒律裁决汇编注释》（Palimuttakavinayavinicchayasangahatika）。本文补充了这三部律典著作的相关信息，通过梳理手稿与印刷版本（如1908年科伦坡版、大英图书馆藏手稿Or. 4957），考证了《戒律裁决汇编注释》的作者归属、文本结构与核心内容，指出其本质是对《善见律毗婆沙》注释文献的重新编排，服务于戒律实践，并探讨了其与缅甸17世纪《戒律装饰注释》的传承关系。  
关键词：舍利弗；《善见律毗婆沙》；上座部律典；注释文献；戒律研究  
Title：Sariputta's Three Works on the Samantapasadika  
Author：Kate Crosby  
Abstract：In the 12th century, King Parakramabahu I of Polonnaruwa promoted a Buddhist reform, emphasizing the correct understanding and practice of the Vinaya. The monk-scholar Sariputta was commissioned by the king to write several works on Buddhaghosa's Samantapasadika, including the Linasaratthadipani, Palimuttakavinayavinicchayasangaha, and Palimuttakavinayavinicchayasangahatika. This article supplements information about these three Vinaya works, verifies the authorship, textual structure, and core content of the Palimuttakavinayavinicchayasangahatika by sorting out manuscripts and printed editions (such as the 1908 Colombo edition and British Library manuscript Or. 4957), points out that it is essentially a rearrangement of the commentarial literature on the Samantapasadika serving Vinaya practice, and discusses its inheritance relationship with the 17th-century Burmese Vinayalankaratika.  
Keywords：Sariputta；Samantapasadika；Theravāda Vinaya；commentarial literature；Vinaya studies

# 论文4. 《指导论》及其他著作的经典地位
作者：彼得·杰克逊（Peter Jackson）  
摘要：本文反驳了杜鲁瓦塞尔（Duroiselle）关于缅甸传统从未将《指导论》（Netti）、《解说道论》（Petakopadesa）等四部著作归入《小部》经典的观点。通过援引觉音《妙义疏》中对第一次结集时小部经典范围的记载，以及缅甸学者那纳比万萨（Nanabhivamsa）在《吉祥喜宴疏新注》中的阐释，指出那纳比万萨明确将《指导论》与《解说道论》视为与《行藏》《譬喻藏》《佛种姓》同等地位的经典，只是将其归属于《解说》（Niddesa）或《无碍解道》（Patisambhidamagga）范畴。这一结论证明缅甸传统中存在将上述著作纳入经典体系的观点，并非如杜鲁瓦塞尔所言的“无 educated 缅甸人认同”。  
关键词：《指导论》；经典地位；上座部；小部经典；缅甸佛教传统  
Title：The Canonicity of the Netti and Other Works  
Author：Peter Jackson  
Abstract：This article refutes Duroiselle's view that the Burmese tradition never included four works such as the Netti and Petakopadesa in the Khuddakanikaya. By citing Buddhaghosa's account in the Sumangalavilasini of the scope of the Khuddakanikaya during the First Council, and the interpretation of the Burmese scholar Nanabhivamsa in his Silakkhandhavagga-abhinavatika, it points out that Nanabhivamsa explicitly regarded the Netti and Petakopadesa as canonical works of the same status as the Cariyapitaka, Apadana, and Buddhavamsa, but classified them under the Niddesa or Patisambhidamagga. This conclusion proves that there was a view in the Burmese tradition that incorporated the above works into the canonical system, not as Duroiselle claimed that "no educated Burman recognized them".  
Keywords：Netti；canonicity；Theravāda；Khuddakanikaya；Burmese Buddhist tradition

# 论文5. 神话即冥想：从《大善见经》到《阿弥陀经》
作者：鲁珀特·盖辛（Rupert Gethin）  
摘要：本文探讨《长部》第17经《大善见经》的神话特质与宗教功能，该经讲述佛陀前世作为大善见王在 Kusavatī 城的生平，其华丽的城市与宫殿描写、超现实的叙事风格常被现代学者视为非历史文本。作者反驳了19-20世纪学界将神话视为“非核心教义”的偏见，提出该经本质是“冥想性叙事”：城市与宫殿的细节描写构成可视化冥想的模板，国王从世俗王权到出家修行的历程对应佛教修行路径（布施、持戒、禅定、观无常），其与《阿弥陀经》中净土描写的相似性，揭示了早期佛教与大乘佛教在冥想实践上的传承关系。经文中对无常、离欲的强调，使其成为佛陀涅槃叙事的重要补充，传递宗教情感与修行指引。  
关键词：《大善见经》；神话；冥想；可视化修行；大乘佛教渊源  
Title：Mythology as Meditation: From the Mahāsudassana Sutta to the Sukhāvatīvyūha Sūtra  
Author：Rupert Gethin  
Abstract：This article explores the mythic characteristics and religious functions of the 17th sutta of the Dīgha-nikāya, the Mahāsudassana Sutta, which narrates the life of the Buddha's past life as King Mahāsudassana in the city of Kusavatī. Its elaborate descriptions of cities and palaces and surreal narrative style are often regarded as non-historical texts by modern scholars. The author refutes the prejudice of 19th-20th century academia that regarded myths as "non-core doctrines", proposing that the sutta is essentially a "meditative narrative": the detailed descriptions of cities and palaces form a template for visual meditation, and the king's journey from secular kingship to monastic practice corresponds to the Buddhist path (dāna, sīla, samādhi, vipassanā). Its similarity to the pure land descriptions in the Sukhāvatīvyūha Sūtra reveals the inheritance relationship in meditation practice between early Buddhism and Mahāyāna Buddhism. The emphasis on impermanence and renunciation in the sutta makes it an important supplement to the narrative of the Buddha's parinibbāna, conveying religious emotion and practice guidance.  
Keywords：Mahāsudassana Sutta；myth；meditation；visualization practice；Mahāyāna Buddhist origins

# 论文6. 东南亚的《本生经》与《五百本生经》
作者：彼得·斯基林（Peter Skilling）  
摘要：本文系统梳理《本生经》在东南亚的传播与发展，包括“经典本生”（巴利语《本生经集》及其注释）与“非经典本生”（区域化创作的本生故事）两类。经典本生通过铭文、艺术表现（如佛塔浮雕）、讲经仪式（如《维桑塔本生》宣讲）深度融入东南亚佛教文化；非经典本生以《五百本生经》（Pannasa-jataka）为代表，是各地汇编的本土叙事集，内容涵盖布施、伦理、浪漫故事等，虽未被纳入巴利三藏，却通过地方语言（泰语、老挝语、高棉语等）的手稿与译本广泛流传，影响了区域文学、戏剧与艺术。文章考证了不同版本《五百本生经》的内容差异、文本传承与地域特色，强调其在东南亚佛教民间实践与文化认同中的核心地位。  
关键词：本生经；《五百本生经》；东南亚佛教；非经典文献；文化传播  
Title：Jataka and Pannasa-jataka in South-East Asia  
Author：Peter Skilling  
Abstract：This article systematically sorts out the spread and development of the Jataka in Southeast Asia, including two categories: "classical Jataka" (the Pāli Jātaka collection and its commentaries) and "non-classical Jataka" (localized created Jataka stories). Classical Jataka has been deeply integrated into Southeast Asian Buddhist culture through inscriptions, artistic expressions (such as stupa reliefs), and preaching rituals (such as the preaching of the Vessantara Jataka); non-classical Jataka, represented by the Pannasa-jataka (Five Hundred Jataka), is a collection of local narratives compiled in various regions, covering dāna, ethics, romantic stories, etc. Although not included in the Pāli Tipitaka, it has been widely circulated through manuscripts and translations in local languages (Thai, Lao, Khmer, etc.), influencing regional literature, drama, and art. The article examines the content differences, textual inheritance, and regional characteristics of different versions of the Pannasa-jataka, emphasizing its core status in the folk practice and cultural identity of Southeast Asian Buddhism.  
Keywords：Jataka；Pannasa-jataka；Southeast Asian Buddhism；non-canonical literature；cultural transmission
