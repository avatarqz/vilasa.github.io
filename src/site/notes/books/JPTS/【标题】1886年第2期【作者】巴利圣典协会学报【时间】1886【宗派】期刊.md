# 【标题】1886年第2期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1886【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  龙树《友善书简》（《Bces Pai Phrin Yig》）
作者：海因里希·温策尔博士（Dr. Heinrich Wenzel）  
摘要：本文收录龙树菩萨致乌达衍那王（King Udayana）的巴利语诗歌体文献《友善书简》的藏译本译文，全文共123节。内容系统阐释佛教核心教义，涵盖六念处、十善业、六度、四梵住、四禅等基础法门，详解八热地狱、畜生道、饿鬼道等轮回苦状，重申十二因缘、四圣谛与八正道等核心教理。文献语言简洁直白，兼具劝诫性与哲理性，虽属大乘佛教文献，却保留大量原始佛教教义元素，与巴利语三藏文本存在诸多文本关联。译文附有详细注释，考证术语源流、文本异文及文化背景，为研究龙树思想与早期佛教向大乘佛教过渡的教义演变提供重要文献支撑。  
关键词：龙树；《友善书简》；藏译巴利语；大乘佛教；佛教教义；轮回与涅槃；文本考证  
Title：Nagarjuna's "Friendly Epistle" (Bces Pai Phrin Yig)  
Author：Dr. Heinrich Wenzel  
Abstract：This paper includes the Tibetan translation of the Pali poetic text "Friendly Epistle" (Bces Pai Phrin Yig) written by Nagarjuna Bodhisattva to King Udayana, consisting of 123 stanzas. It systematically explains core Buddhist doctrines, including basic practices such as the Six Anusmrtayas, Ten Wholesome Actions, Six Paramitas, Four Brahmaviharas and Four Dhyanas, details the sufferings of samsara such as the Eight Hot Hells, Animal Realm and Hungry Ghost Realm, and reaffirms core teachings like the Twelve Nidanas, Four Noble Truths and Noble Eightfold Path. The text is concise and straightforward, with both exhortatory and philosophical qualities. Though belonging to Mahayana Buddhist literature, it retains many elements of early Buddhist doctrines and has numerous textual connections with the Pali Tipitaka. The translation is accompanied by detailed annotations, researching the origin of terms, textual variants and cultural background, providing important literary support for the study of Nagarjuna's thought and the doctrinal evolution from early Buddhism to Mahayana Buddhism.  
Keywords：Nagarjuna；Friendly Epistle；Tibetan-translated Pali；Mahayana Buddhism；Buddhist Doctrines；Samsara and Nirvana；Textual Research

# 论文2.  《未来史》（《Anagata-Vamsa》）
作者：J·米纳耶夫教授（Professor J. Minayeff）  
摘要：本文为巴利语预言文献《未来史》的校勘本，基于曼德勒、仰光等地收藏的4份缅甸文与僧伽罗文手稿整理而成。文献以佛陀对舍利弗尊者的开示为框架，预言未来弥勒佛（Metteyya Buddha）降世的时代背景、社会风貌及弘法历程，详细描述佛法衰微的五个阶段——证悟衰微、修行衰微、闻法衰微、相状衰微、传承衰微，同时记载27位过去佛的事迹与未来十佛的化现。文本融合 prose 与诗歌体，兼具宗教预言与历史叙事属性，为研究南传佛教的弥勒信仰、末世观及文献传承提供核心史料，校勘注释部分梳理了不同手稿的异文与传承脉络。  
关键词：《未来史》；弥勒信仰；南传佛教预言；文献校勘；米纳耶夫；佛法衰微；巴利语手稿  
Title：Anagata-Vamsa  
Author：Professor J. Minayeff  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Pali prophetic text "Anagata-Vamsa", compiled based on 4 Burmese and Sinhalese manuscripts collected in Mandalay, Rangoon and other places. Framed by the Buddha's teachings to Venerable Sariputra, the text predicts the historical background, social scene and Dharma propagation process of the future Metteyya Buddha's descent, details the five stages of the decline of Buddhism—decline of realization, decline of practice, decline of hearing the Dharma, decline of signs and decline of transmission, and records the deeds of 27 past Buddhas and the manifestation of 10 future Buddhas. Integrating prose and poetic forms, the text has both religious prophetic and historical narrative attributes, providing core historical materials for the study of Theravada Buddhist Metteyya belief, eschatology and literary transmission. The collation and annotation part sorts out the variants and transmission context of different manuscripts.  
Keywords：Anagata-Vamsa；Metteyya Belief；Theravada Buddhist Prophecy；Text Collation；J. Minayeff；Decline of Buddhism；Pali Manuscripts

# 论文3.  《香史》（《Gandha-Vamsa》）
作者：J·米纳耶夫教授（Professor J. Minayeff）  
摘要：本文为南传佛教文献目录学著作《香史》的校勘本，基于普罗美地区的两份缅甸文手稿整理而成。文献系统梳理佛教三藏典籍的分类与传承，详细列举律藏、经藏、论藏的核心典籍及分类标准，收录从古代论师到近现代学者的重要巴利语著作目录，涵盖注疏、词典、语法书等辅助文献。书中记载印度、锡兰、爪哇等地的学者传承谱系，梳理《清净道论》《摄阿毗达磨义论》等重要文献的编纂背景与流传情况，为研究南传佛教文献学、学术传承及典籍版本流变提供详实的目录学资料。  
关键词：《香史》；南传佛教文献学；三藏典籍；文献目录；米纳耶夫；学术传承；版本流变  
Title：Gandha-Vamsa  
Author：Professor J. Minayeff  
Abstract：This paper is a collated edition of the Theravada Buddhist bibliographical work "Gandha-Vamsa", compiled based on two Burmese manuscripts from the Prome region. The text systematically sorts out the classification and transmission of the Buddhist Tipitaka, details the core texts and classification standards of Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka and Abhidhamma Pitaka, and includes a catalog of important Pali works from ancient masters to modern scholars, covering commentaries, dictionaries, grammar books and other auxiliary literature. It records the academic inheritance lineage of scholars in India, Ceylon, Java and other places, and sorts out the compilation background and circulation of important documents such as "Visuddhimagga" and "Abhidhammattha Sangaha", providing detailed bibliographical materials for the study of Theravada Buddhist philology, academic inheritance and the evolution of text versions.  
Keywords：Gandha-Vamsa；Theravada Buddhist Philology；Tipitaka Texts；Literature Catalog；J. Minayeff；Academic Inheritance；Version Evolution

# 论文4.  《Divyavadana》诗句索引
作者：海因里希·温策尔博士（Dr. Heinrich Wenzel）  
摘要：本文为梵语佛教故事集《Divyavadana》（《天业譬喻经》）的诗句索引，按梵语字母顺序编排，收录典籍中全部诗歌类文本的关键词、诗句片段及对应页码。索引条目标注诗句与巴利语《法句经》《自说经》等核心文献的文本关联，对比同源教义表述的语言差异，为研究者检索《Divyavadana》中的诗歌文献、考证文本源流及跨文本传播提供便捷工具，同时为巴利语与梵语佛教文献的对比研究提供基础数据支撑。  
关键词：《Divyavadana》；诗句索引；文本检索；佛教文献对比；温策尔；梵语佛教；巴利语文献  
Title：Index to Verses in the Divyavadana  
Author：Dr. Heinrich Wenzel  
Abstract：This paper is an index of verses from the Sanskrit Buddhist story collection "Divyavadana", arranged in Sanskrit alphabetical order, including keywords, verse fragments and corresponding page numbers of all poetic texts in the collection. Index entries mark the textual connections between verses and core Pali texts such as "Dhammapada" and "Udana", compare linguistic differences of homologous doctrinal expressions, providing a convenient tool for researchers to retrieve poetic literature in "Divyavadana", verify textual origin and cross-text transmission, and at the same time provide basic data support for the comparative study of Pali and Sanskrit Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Divyavadana；Verse Index；Text Retrieval；Buddhist Literature Comparison；Heinrich Wenzel；Sanskrit Buddhism；Pali Texts

# 论文5.  巴利语词汇考证与问答
作者：理查德·莫里斯博士（Dr. Richard Morris）  
摘要：本文收录莫里斯博士关于巴利语词汇的考证研究与学术问答，聚焦《巴利语词典》未收录或释义存疑的词汇。内容涵盖“Akkula”“Agginikasi”“Aghavi”等词汇的词源考证、语义辨析、文献用例梳理及语法功能解析，结合《本生经》《律藏》《论藏》等核心典籍，纠正部分词汇的传统误读，补充多语境下的语义维度，厘清易混淆词汇的用法差异，为巴利语语言学研究与佛教文献解读提供重要学术参考。  
关键词：巴利语；词汇考证；语义辨析；莫里斯；佛教语言学；文献用例；语法解析  
Title：Notes and Queries  
Author：Dr. Richard Morris  
Abstract：This paper collects Dr. Richard Morris's research and academic questions and answers on Pali vocabulary, focusing on words not included in the "Pali Dictionary" or with questionable interpretations. The content includes etymological research, semantic discrimination, literature example sorting and grammatical function analysis of words such as "Akkula", "Agginikasi" and "Aghavi", correcting the traditional misreading of some words, supplementing semantic dimensions in multiple contexts, clarifying the usage differences of easily confused words by combining core texts such as "Jataka Tales", "Vinaya Pitaka" and "Abhidhamma Pitaka", providing important academic reference for Pali linguistics research and Buddhist literature interpretation.  
Keywords：Pali；Vocabulary Research；Semantic Discrimination；Richard Morris；Buddhist Linguistics；Literature Examples；Grammatical Analysis

Title：List of Members of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper contains the 1886 membership list of the Pali Text Society, including three categories: donors, six-year members (five guineas subscription) and annual members (one guinea subscription), with annotations of the members' regions, institutions and correspondence addresses. Members cover scholars, academic institutions, religious groups and royal family members from Europe, America, Asia and other places, including important donors and subscribers such as the King of Siam, Harvard University and the British Museum. The list reflects the society's influence and academic network in the global field of Theravada Buddhist research, providing important historical materials for the study of the composition of the international Theravada Buddhist research community in the late 19th century.

Title：Accounts  
Author：Pali Text Society

Income items include the carry-over balance from 1885, membership dues, donations, etc.; expenditure items cover document mailing fees, manuscript purchase fees, editor salaries, exchange losses, advertising fees, etc. The report uses Ceylon Rupees as the accounting unit, detailing the date, amount and purpose of each revenue and expenditure, with an ending balance of 449.95 Rupees, and attached supplementary explanation of revenue and expenditure related to 1885 publications. # 论文8.  巴利圣典协会已出版典籍清单（至1886年）
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文系统梳理巴利圣典协会成立以来至1886年已出版的22部巴利语典籍目录，按出版年份排序，详细标注每部典籍的书名、编者与出版时间。清单涵盖经藏（如《相应部》第一部分）、律藏、论藏及注疏文献，其中《未来史》《香史》《善吉祥注》第一部分等为1886年新增出版成果。清单全面呈现协会的学术出版规划与成果，为研究者检索南传佛教核心文献的版本信息提供权威指引。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；已出版典籍；文献目录；三藏典籍；南传佛教；出版规划；版本信息  
Title：Works Already Published  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the catalog of 22 Pali texts published by the Pali Text Society from its establishment to 1886, arranged by publication year, with detailed annotations of the title, editor and publication time of each text. The list covers Sutta Pitaka (such as Part I of Samyutta Nikaya), Vinaya Pitaka, Abhidhamma Pitaka and commentary literature, among which "Anagata-Vamsa", "Gandha-Vamsa" and Part I of "Sumaigala Vilasini" are new publications in 1886. The list comprehensively presents the society's academic publishing planning and achievements, providing authoritative guidance for researchers to retrieve version information of core Theravada Buddhist literature.

Title：Report for 1886

Abstract：This paper is the 1886 annual work report of the Pali Text Society, written by the Chairman T. W. Rhys Davids. The report calls on members to renew their dues to support subsequent publications, thanks donors and editors for their academic support, providing first-hand materials for the study of the society's academic development process and the achievements of Theravada Buddhist literature collation.

# 论文10.  巴利圣典协会章程
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文为巴利圣典协会的核心运营章程，明确协会宗旨为整理出版欧洲各图书馆收藏的未刊巴利语早期佛教文献。章程详述文献的历史价值（民俗史、宗教史、语言学）、出版规模（约9000-10000页八开本）、会员订阅标准（年度一几尼或六年五几尼）、捐赠机制等，介绍管理委员会构成及英美、斯里兰卡地区负责人信息，强调协会非营利属性与学术公益定位，提示1887年会费缴纳事宜。章程为了解协会的组织架构、运营规则与学术使命提供权威依据。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；章程；文献出版；会员制度；组织架构；学术宗旨；非营利组织  
Title：Prospectus of the Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper is the core operating charter of the Pali Text Society, clarifying the society's founding purpose as collating and publishing unpublished early Pali Buddhist literature collected in various libraries in Europe. The charter details the historical value of the literature (folk history, religious history, linguistics), publication scale (about 9,000-10,000 octavo pages), membership subscription standards (one guinea per year or five guineas for six years), donation mechanism, etc., introduces the composition of the management committee and the information of responsible persons in Britain, the United States and Sri Lanka, emphasizing the society's non-profit nature and academic public welfare positioning, and reminding of the 1887 membership dues payment. The charter provides authoritative basis for understanding the society's organizational structure, operating rules and academic mission.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Charter；Literature Publication；Membership System；Organizational Structure；Academic Purpose；Non-Profit Organization
