# 【标题】1910-1912年第6期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1912【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  关于早期印度年代学与文学的两篇论文
作者：赫尔曼·奥尔登堡（Hermann Oldenberg）  
摘要：本文包含两篇核心论文，聚焦早期印度年代学与文学研究。第一篇《迦腻色伽时代考》深入探讨贵霜王朝迦腻色伽王的纪年问题，批判将其归于塞迦纪元（公元78年）或毗克拉摩纪元（公元前57年）的观点，结合钱币学、碑铭学及中文史料，论证迦腻色伽纪元应在公元1世纪末。第二篇《叙事散文与韵文类型及本生故事》围绕印度古代“ Akhyana ”（叙事文体）展开，反驳学界对本生故事非“ Akhyana ”类型的质疑，指出本生故事以“散文框架+核心韵文”为典型结构，韵文为传承核心，散文为后世补全，且该文体与吠陀文献、史诗文学中的叙事传统一脉相承，为理解印度古代叙事文学的起源与发展提供关键支撑。  
关键词：早期印度；年代学；迦腻色伽；本生故事；Akhyana 文体；叙事文学  
Title：Two Essays on Early Indian Chronology and Literature  
Author：Hermann Oldenberg  
Abstract：This paper consists of two core essays focusing on the study of early Indian chronology and literature. The first essay "On the Era of Kaniska" delves into the chronological issue of King Kaniska of the Kusana Dynasty, criticizing the views that assign him to the Saka era (A.D. 78) or the Vikrama era (57 B.C.). Combining numismatic, epigraphic, and Chinese historical sources, it argues that the Kaniska era should be at the end of the first century A.D. The second essay "The Prose-and-Verse Type of Narrative and the Jatakas" centers on the ancient Indian narrative genre "Akhyana", refuting academic doubts that the Jatakas are not of the "Akhyana" type. It points out that the Jatakas have a typical structure of "prose framework + core verses", with verses as the core of transmission and prose supplemented by later generations. Moreover, this genre is inherently connected to the narrative traditions in Vedic literature and epic literature, providing key support for understanding the origin and development of ancient Indian narrative literature.  
Keywords：Early India；Chronology；Kaniska；Jatakas；Akhyana Genre；Narrative Literature

# 论文2.  《双对论》注疏（摘自考伽罗摩的《五论注疏》，归于觉音）
作者：觉音（Buddhaghosa，归于）；编辑：里斯·戴维斯夫人（C. A. F. Rhys Davids）；转录：M. C. 福利（M. C. Foley）  
摘要：本文是南传佛教阿毗达磨藏第四部《双对论》（Yamaka）的首部欧洲语编辑本注疏，依据僧伽罗语棕榈叶手稿与缅甸语版本校勘整理而成。注疏系统阐释《双对论》的十大核心主题（根本双对、蕴双对、处双对、界双对、谛双对、行双对、随眠双对、心双对、法双对、根双对），每个主题均包含“列举分”“阐释分”“通达分”三层结构，通过“成对设问+正反论证”的方式解析阿毗达磨核心概念。重点厘清“双对”（Yamaka）的本质是对概念的二元辩证分析，涵盖概念定义、运作机制、分类逻辑等，为理解南传佛教的认识论与辩证法提供权威文本支撑。  
关键词：《双对论》；觉音；阿毗达磨；注疏；南传佛教辩证法  
Title：The Yamaka Commentary from the Pancappakarana-Atthakatha  
Author：Attributed to Buddhaghosa；Editor: C. A. F. Rhys Davids；Transcriber: M. C. Foley  
Abstract：This paper is the first European edition commentary on the "Yamaka" (Pair Treatise), the fourth book of the Theravada Buddhist Abhidhamma Pitaka, collated and edited based on Sinhalese palm-leaf manuscripts and Burmese editions. The commentary systematically explains the ten core themes of the "Yamaka" (Mula-Yamaka, Khandha-Yamaka, Ayatana-Yamaka, Dhatu-Yamaka, Sacca-Yamaka, Sankhara-Yamaka, Anusaya-Yamaka, Citta-Yamaka, Dhamma-Yamaka, Indriya-Yamaka). Each theme includes three layers: "enumeration section", "explanation section", and "attainment section", analyzing core Abhidhamma concepts through the method of "paired questioning + positive and negative argumentation". It focuses on clarifying that the essence of "Yamaka" (pair) is the dual dialectical analysis of concepts, covering concept definition, operational mechanism, classification logic, etc., providing an authoritative textual basis for understanding the epistemology and dialectics of Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Yamaka；Buddhaghosa；Abhidhamma；Commentary；Theravada Buddhist Dialectics

# 论文3.  《佛所行赞》札记二则
作者：渡边海旭（K. Watanabe）  
摘要：本文包含两则关于马鸣《佛所行赞》（Buddha-Carita）的专题研究。第一则考证鸠摩罗什译《佛垂般涅槃略说教诫经》实为《佛所行赞》“大般涅槃分”的散文译本，通过文本比对，梳理其与梵文原典、法护译本、宝云译本的对应关系，揭示该经在中日佛教中的传播与影响。第二则探讨《佛所行赞》与《马克兰达往世书》（Markandeya-Purana）的关联，指出经中引用的“阿里与瓦卡争斗”典故源自往世书中毗湿婆与极裕仙人的斗法传说，分析婆罗门教文学对佛教叙事文学的影响，为跨宗教文学交流研究提供实例。  
关键词：《佛所行赞》；马鸣；鸠摩罗什；往世书；文本考证；文学关联  
Title：Two Notes on the Buddha-Carita  
Author：K. Watanabe  
Abstract：This paper consists of two thematic studies on Asvaghosa's "Buddha-Carita" (Acts of the Buddha). The first note verifies that Kumarajiva's translation "Sutra of Teaching Spoken Briefly by the Buddha Just Before His Attaining Parinirvana" is actually a prose translation of the "Mahaparinirvana Section" of the "Buddha-Carita". Through textual comparison, it sorts out its corresponding relations with the Sanskrit original, Dharmaraksa's translation, and Pao-yun's translation, revealing the dissemination and influence of this sutra in Chinese and Japanese Buddhism. The second note explores the connection between the "Buddha-Carita" and the "Markandeya-Purana", pointing out that the allusion of "the dispute between Ari and Vaka" quoted in the sutra originates from the legend of the duel between Siva and Visvamitra in the Purana. It analyzes the influence of Brahminical literature on Buddhist narrative literature, providing an example for the study of cross-religious literary exchanges.  
Keywords：Buddha-Carita；Asvaghosa；Kumarajiva；Purana；Textual Research；Literary Connection

# 论文4.  缅甸的阿毗达磨文献
作者：施韦赞·昂（Shwe Zan Aung, B.A.）  
摘要：本文系统梳理缅甸阿毗达磨文献的传承、分类与研究现状。核心内容包括四类文献：一是经典文献（七部阿毗达磨论），详述各部论著的核心思想与缅甸学界的研究重点；二是注疏与复注文献，介绍觉音、法护等学者的注疏及缅甸高僧的复注成果；三是“小指手册”（Lethan）类纲要文献，如阿努律陀《阿毗达磨摄义论》等九部中世纪纲要著作；四是缅甸语译本与本土著作，包括经典译著、研究论文及教学手册。文中强调缅甸阿毗达磨研究以“传承经典、阐释义理”为核心，虽本土原创巴利文著作较少，但缅甸语阐释文献丰富，且与斯里兰卡、泰国的阿毗达磨传统一脉相承，展现了缅甸在南传佛教文献传承中的重要地位。  
关键词：缅甸；阿毗达磨文献；南传佛教；文献传承；注疏；纲要著作  
Title：Abhidhamma Literature in Burma  
Author：Shwe Zan Aung, B.A.  
Abstract：This paper systematically sorts out the inheritance, classification, and research status of Abhidhamma literature in Burma. The core content includes four categories of literature: first, canonical literature (the seven Abhidhamma treatises), detailing the core ideas of each treatise and the research focus of Burmese academic circles; second, commentaries and sub-commentaries, introducing the commentaries by scholars such as Buddhaghosa and Dharmapala, and the sub-commentary achievements by Burmese senior monks; third, "Lethan" (Little-finger Manuals) compendiums, such as the nine medieval compendiums including Anuruddha's "Abhidhammattha-Sangaha"; fourth, Burmese translations and indigenous works, including canonical translations, research papers, and teaching manuals. The paper emphasizes that Abhidhamma research in Burma focuses on "inheriting classics and interpreting doctrines". Although there are few original Pali works by local scholars, there is a wealth of Burmese explanatory literature, which is inherently connected to the Abhidhamma traditions of Sri Lanka and Thailand, demonstrating Burma's important position in the inheritance of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Burma；Abhidhamma Literature；Theravada Buddhism；Literary Inheritance；Commentary；Compendium Works

# 论文5.  僧伽罗文字体印刷的斯里兰卡巴利语书籍目录
作者：W. A. 德·席尔瓦（W. A. de Silva）  
摘要：本文是首部系统整理斯里兰卡以僧伽罗文字体印刷的巴利语书籍目录，收录1737年荷兰传入印刷术至1910年间的232部著作。目录按书名首字母排序，详细记录每部书籍的编者、出版机构、出版年份、页数及版本信息，涵盖阿毗达磨论著、经藏文献、律藏文献、注疏、语法书、史书等类别。文中追溯斯里兰卡巴利语书籍的印刷史，指出早期印刷以基督教文献为主，佛教巴利语书籍自1862年兰卡奥帕卡拉出版社成立后逐渐增多，且存在文本印刷不系统、部分著作未完成等问题。该目录为研究斯里兰卡巴利语文献的传播、保存与版本学提供了珍贵的一手资料。  
关键词：斯里兰卡；巴利语书籍；僧伽罗文字体；印刷史；文献目录；版本学  
Title：A List of Pali Books Printed in Ceylon in Singhalese Characters  
Author：W. A. de Silva  
Abstract：This paper is the first systematic catalog of Pali books printed in Singhalese script in Ceylon, collecting 232 works from the introduction of printing by the Dutch in 1737 to 1910. The catalog is sorted by the first letter of the book title, detailing the editor, publishing institution, publication year, page count, and version information of each book, covering categories such as Abhidhamma treatises, Sutta Pitaka literature, Vinaya Pitaka literature, commentaries, grammar books, and historical books. The paper traces the printing history of Pali books in Ceylon, pointing out that early printing mainly focused on Christian literature, and Buddhist Pali books gradually increased after the establishment of the Lankopakara Press in 1862. It also notes problems such as unsystematic text printing and incomplete publication of some works. This catalog provides valuable first-hand materials for the study of the dissemination, preservation, and textual criticism of Pali literature in Ceylon.  
Keywords：Ceylon；Pali Books；Singhalese Script；Printing History；Literary Catalog；Textual Criticism
