# 【标题】1904-1905年合刊【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（Journal of the Pali Text Society）【时间】1905【宗派】南传佛教研究期刊

# 论文1.  佛传（Jina-Carita）
作者：瓦纳拉塔纳·梅丹卡拉（Vanaratana Medhankara），编校与翻译：W. H. D. 劳斯（W. H. D. Rouse）  
摘要：本文收录并翻译了巴利语佛传文献《Jina-Carita》，文本基于僧伽罗语手稿、1886年科伦坡印刷版等三类材料编校而成。内容以诗歌体记述佛陀从过去生苏梅达（Sumedha）供养燃灯佛发愿成佛，到今生悉达多太子放弃王位、苦行修道、战胜魔军、菩提树下证悟，再到转法轮、度化众生、回归故国等完整生平，详尽呈现佛陀修行历程中的关键事件与教义阐释，是研究南传佛教佛传传统的重要文献。  
关键词：《Jina-Carita》；佛传；巴利语文献；佛陀生平；南传佛教；文本校勘  
Title：Jina-Carita  
Author：Vanaratana Medhankara；Edited and Translated by W. H. D. Rouse  
Abstract：This paper collects and translates the Pali Buddhist biography text "Jina-Carita", compiled based on three types of materials including a Sinhalese manuscript and the 1886 Colombo printed edition. In poetic form, it records the complete life of the Buddha: from Sumedha in his past life making a vow to become a Buddha by offering alms to Dipankara Buddha, to Prince Siddhattha renouncing the throne in his present life, practicing asceticism, defeating Mara's army, attaining enlightenment under the Bodhi tree, then turning the Dharma wheel, converting sentient beings, and returning to his hometown. It elaborates on key events and doctrinal explanations in the Buddha's practice journey, serving as an important document for the study of the Buddhist biography tradition in Theravada Buddhism.  
Keywords：Jina-Carita；Buddhist Biography；Pali Literature；Life of the Buddha；Theravada Buddhism；Textual Collation

# 论文2.  说一切有部的阿毗达磨文献
作者：高楠顺次郎（Professor Takakusu）  
摘要：本文系统研究说一切有部的阿毗达磨文献体系，该宗派是佛教十八部派中最古老的宗派之一，核心教义为“一切有”，地理传播范围曾涵盖印度、中国、东南亚等地。文章梳理该宗派七部核心阿毗达磨著作（《发智论》《品类足论》等）的作者、内容与传承，分析《大毗婆沙论》《俱舍论》等重要哲学文献的思想脉络，详解“法”“界”“蕴”等核心概念的阐释，揭示说一切有部在佛教哲学史上的重要地位及其对后世佛教思想的影响。  
关键词：说一切有部；阿毗达磨文献；佛教哲学；核心教义；文献传承；部派佛教  
Title：The Abhidharma Literature of the Sarvastivadins  
Author：Professor Takakusu  
Abstract：This paper systematically studies the Abhidharma literature system of the Sarvastivadin school, one of the oldest among the eighteen Buddhist schools, with the core doctrine of "sarvastitva" (existence of all things), and its geographical spread once covered India, China, Southeast Asia and other regions. The paper sorts out the authors, contents and inheritance of the school's seven core Abhidharma works (such as "Jñānaprasthāna" and "Prakaranapāda"), analyzes the ideological context of important philosophical documents like "Mahāvibhāṣā" and "Abhidharmakośa", elaborates on the interpretation of core concepts such as "dharma", "dhātu" and "skandha", and reveals the important position of the Sarvastivadin school in the history of Buddhist philosophy and its influence on later Buddhist thought.  
Keywords：Sarvastivadins；Abhidharma Literature；Buddhist Philosophy；Core Doctrines；Literary Inheritance；Buddhist Schools

# 论文3.  《饿鬼事》（Petavatthu）校勘记
作者：埃德蒙·哈迪（Edmund Hardy）  
摘要：本文对巴黎国家图书馆藏棕榈叶缅甸文字体《饿鬼事》手稿与米纳耶夫教授的已出版版本进行校勘比对。校勘聚焦经文偈颂的用字差异、语句增减、语序调整等细节，发现该手稿与费尔收藏的缅甸语手稿亲缘关系最为密切，同时记录了诸多独特异文。校勘结果补充了《饿鬼事》的文本传承线索，为南传佛教饿鬼相关文献的版本学研究提供了重要参考。  
关键词：《饿鬼事》；文本校勘；缅甸语手稿；版本差异；南传佛教；异文研究  
Title：Collation of the 'Petavatthu'  
Author：Edmund Hardy  
Abstract：This paper collates the Burmese-script palm-leaf manuscript of the "Petavatthu" in the Bibliothèque Nationale de France with the published edition by Professor Minayeff. The collation focuses on detailed textual variants such as word usage differences, additions and deletions of sentences, and adjustments of word order, finding that this manuscript has the closest affinity with the Burmese manuscript in the Phayre Collection, while recording many unique variants. The collation results supplement the textual inheritance clues of the "Petavatthu" and provide important reference for the textual research of Theravada Buddhist texts related to petas (hungry ghosts).  
Keywords：Petavatthu；Textual Collation；Burmese Manuscript；Version Differences；Theravada Buddhism；Variant Research

# 论文4.  涅槃问题探析
作者：F. 奥托·施拉德（F. Otto Schrader, Ph.D.）  
摘要：本文围绕佛教核心概念“涅槃”展开深度探讨，重点回应“无余涅槃是否为绝对 annihilation”的争议。作者反驳涅槃虚无主义观点，认为佛陀“无我”教义否定的是恒常不变的“我”，而非超越时空与因果的绝对实在；通过分析《阿含经》中火焰熄灭等比喻、佛陀对涅槃相关问题的回应，论证涅槃是超越“有”“无”二元对立的终极实在，是摆脱五蕴束缚后的真实存在状态，而非彻底消亡。  
关键词：涅槃；无我；佛教哲学；终极实在；无余涅槃；教义阐释  
Title：Nirvana  
Author：F. Otto Schrader, Ph.D.  
Abstract：This paper conducts an in-depth discussion on the core Buddhist concept of "Nirvana", focusing on responding to the controversy over whether "anupādisesa-nibbāna" is absolute annihilation. The author refutes the nihilistic view of Nirvana, arguing that the Buddha's doctrine of "anatta" (non-self) negates the eternal and unchanging "atta" (self), not the absolute reality transcending time, space and causality; by analyzing metaphors such as the extinguishing of fire in the Agamas and the Buddha's responses to Nirvana-related questions, it demonstrates that Nirvana is the ultimate reality beyond the dualistic opposition of "existence" and "non-existence", a state of true existence after being freed from the bondage of the five skandhas, rather than complete extinction.  
Keywords：Nirvana；Anatta；Buddhist Philosophy；Ultimate Reality；Anupādisesa-nibbāna；Doctrinal Interpretation

# 论文5.  巴利圣典协会出版物列表
作者：巴利圣典协会（Pali Text Society）  
摘要：本文按年份系统罗列巴利圣典协会1882-1905年的出版物，包括期刊、经藏校勘本（如《相应部》《中部》）、论藏文献（如《法集论》《分别论》）、注释著作等，共计44部文本、57卷，超16500页。同时预告1906-1908年计划出版的文献，包括《长部》第三卷、《法句经注》《Petakopadesa》等。列表详细标注每部出版物的名称、卷次，全面呈现协会在巴利语佛教文献整理与传播中的核心成就。  
关键词：巴利圣典协会；出版物列表；巴利语经典；文献整理；校勘本；出版计划  
Title：Issues of the Pali Text Society  
Author：Pali Text Society  
Abstract：This paper systematically lists the publications of the Pali Text Society from 1882 to 1905 by year, including journals, collated editions of the Sutta Pitaka (such as "Samyutta Nikaya" and "Majjhima Nikaya"), Abhidharma texts (such as "Dhamma Saṅgaṇi" and "Vibhaṅga"), commentaries, etc., totaling 44 texts and 57 volumes, exceeding 16,500 pages. It also previews the literature planned for publication from 1906 to 1908, including the third volume of the "Digha Nikaya", the "Dhammapada Commentary", and the "Petakopadesa". The list details the name and volume of each publication, comprehensively presenting the core achievements of the Society in the collation and dissemination of Pali Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Pali Text Society；Publication List；Pali Canon；Literary Collation；Collated Editions；Publishing Plan
