# 【标题】第15期【作者】巴利圣典协会学报（JOURNAL OF THE PALI TEXT SOCIETY）【时间】1990【宗派】期刊

# 论文1. 《Namacaradipaka》（心之作用）校注与译文
作者：哈马拉瓦·萨达提萨（Hammalawa Saddhatissa）  
摘要：本文校注并呈现了缅甸上座部佛教论著《Namacaradipaka》（心之作用）的完整文本，该著作传统归属于11-12世纪缅甸高僧查帕塔（Chapata，又名萨达哈马乔提帕拉），是南传佛教九部“小指手册”（Let-than）之一。文本共分七章，系统阐释了南传阿毗达磨核心范畴：地界分类（Bhumivibhago）、众生分类（Puggalavibhago）、前后世分类（Pubbaparavibhago）、功能分类（Kiccatthanavibhago）、所缘分类（Arammanavibhago）、物质描述（Rupaniddeso）、因缘分类（Paccayavibhago）。内容涵盖28种物质形态的属性与成因、89种心识状态的分类（善、恶、果报、无记）、52种心所法的组合规律，以及涅槃的终极特质。校注以伦敦印度事务部图书馆藏1911年缅甸印刷本为底本，厘清了文本中的韵律瑕疵与抄写异文，考证了作者归属争议，指出该文本或为查帕塔传承而非原创，为南传阿毗达磨文献研究提供了权威校勘本。  
关键词：《Namacaradipaka》；南传阿毗达磨；查帕塔；心识分类；物质观；因缘论  
Title：Namacaradipaka (The Action of Mind) - Edited and Translated  
Author：Hammalawa Saddhatissa  
Abstract：This paper edits and presents the complete text of the Theravada Buddhist treatise Namacaradipaka (The Action of Mind), traditionally attributed to the 11th-12th century Burmese高僧 Chapata (also known as Saddhammajotipala) and one of the nine Theravada "Let-than" (Little-finger Manuals). Divided into seven chapters, the text systematically explains core Theravada Abhidhamma categories: Classification of Realms (Bhumivibhago), Classification of Individuals (Puggalavibhago), Classification of Previous and Following Births (Pubbaparavibhago), Classification of Functions (Kiccatthanavibhago), Classification of Objects (Arammanavibhago), Description of Matter (Rupaniddeso), and Classification of Conditions (Paccayavibhago). It covers the attributes and causes of 28 types of material forms, the classification of 89 states of mind (good, bad, resultant, inoperative), the combinatorial rules of 52 mental properties, and the ultimate characteristics of Nibbana. Based on the 1911 Burmese printed edition in the India Office Library, London, the edition clarifies metrical flaws and scribal variants, examines the controversy over authorship, and argues that the text was likely transmitted rather than composed by Chapata, providing an authoritative critical edition for the study of Theravada Abhidhamma literature.  
Keywords：Namacaradipaka；Theravada Abhidhamma；Chapata；mind classification；matter theory；conditionality

# 论文2. 巴利语尼柯耶经的文本分类及其对佛教教义与文献研究的启示
作者：乔伊·曼内（Joy Manné）  
摘要：本文通过分析《长部尼柯耶》（DN）与《中部尼柯耶》（MN）的开篇格式、结尾公式及内部结构，将巴利语经藏文本划分为“布道文（Sermons）”“辩论文（Debates）”“咨询文（Consultations）”三大核心类别。布道文以简洁或扩展式开篇公式为标志，面向僧团传授教义，内部结构为单向阐释；辩论文包含挑战者、反驳、认输等戏剧化元素，多为佛陀与外道或王公的公开论争；咨询文以问答对话为核心，涵盖僧俗向佛陀或高僧请教教义、修行等问题。统计显示，《长部尼柯耶》中辩论文占比52.94%，《中部尼柯耶》中布道文与咨询文占比分别达36.8%和29.94%，表明前者最初旨在吸引信众、扩大影响，后者侧重僧团教化与修行指导。该分类为辨析早期佛教文献的功能差异、还原教义传播场景提供了关键框架。  
关键词：巴利语尼柯耶；文本分类；布道文；辩论文；咨询文；南传佛教文献  
Title：Categories of sutta in the Pali Nikayas and their Implications for Our Appreciation of the Buddhist Teaching and Literature  
Author：Joy Manné  
Abstract：By analyzing the introductory formulas, concluding formulas, and internal structures of the Digha Nikaya (DN) and Majjhima Nikaya (MN), this paper classifies Pali Sutta Pitaka texts into three core categories: "Sermons", "Debates", and "Consultations". Sermons are marked by simple or expanded introductory formulas, delivered to the Sangha to teach the Dharma with a one-way explanatory structure. Debates include dramatic elements such as challengers, refutations, and admissions of defeat, mostly public disputes between the Buddha and non-Buddhists or nobles. Consultations center on question-and-answer dialogues, covering monastics and laypeople seeking advice on the Dharma and practice from the Buddha or senior monks. Statistics show that Debates account for 52.94% of DN, while Sermons and Consultations account for 36.8% and 29.94% of MN respectively, indicating that DN was initially intended to attract followers and expand influence, while MN focused on monastic education and practice guidance. This classification provides a key framework for distinguishing the functional differences of early Buddhist literature and restoring the context of Dharma transmission.  
Keywords：Pali Nikayas；text classification；Sermons；Debates；Consultations；Theravada Buddhist literature

# 论文3. 论巴利语圣典的本质——南传佛教圣典的形成与历史角色
作者：史蒂文·柯林斯（Steven Collins）  
摘要：本文从外部学术视角重新审视巴利语圣典（Pali Canon）的形成与角色，提出核心观点：巴利语圣典并非早于上座部佛教存在的“原始佛教文本基础”，而是公元初几个世纪斯里兰卡大寺派（Mahavihara）为确立正统性而构建的“封闭性经典名录”。通过辨析“canon”的“包容性经典”与“排他性名录”双重含义，考证巴利语中“pali”“tipitaka”“buddhavacana”等术语的语义演变，指出早期佛教并无封闭圣典概念，直到12世纪帕拉卡马巴胡一世统一僧团后，大寺派的经典名录才成为正统标准。研究结合锡兰编年史（Vamsa）与碑铭资料，揭示圣典的形成与大寺派和无畏山寺派（Abhayagiri）的宗派竞争密切相关，其核心功能是通过文本权威合法化教派传承，而非单纯保存“原始佛教教义”。  
关键词：巴利语圣典；上座部佛教；大寺派；圣典形成；宗派竞争；文本权威  
Title：On the Very Idea of the Pali Canon  
Author：Steven Collins  
Abstract：From an external academic perspective, this paper re-examines the formation and role of the Pali Canon, proposing a core argument: the Pali Canon is not a "textual foundation of early Buddhism" pre-dating Theravada, but a "closed list of scriptures" constructed by the Mahavihara lineage in Ceylon in the early centuries CE to establish orthodoxy. By distinguishing the dual meanings of "canon" (inclusive scriptures vs. exclusive list), investigating the semantic evolution of Pali terms such as "pali", "tipitaka", and "buddhavacana", it argues that there was no concept of a closed canon in early Buddhism. It was not until King Parakkamabahu I unified the Sangha in the 12th century that the Mahavihara's list of scriptures became the orthodox standard. Combining Ceylonese chronicles (Vamsa) and epigraphic data, the study reveals that the formation of the Canon was closely related to the sectarian competition between the Mahavihara and Abhayagiri lineages, with its core function being to legitimize sectarian transmission through textual authority rather than merely preserving "original Buddhist teachings".  
Keywords：Pali Canon；Theravada Buddhism；Mahavihara；canon formation；sectarian competition；textual authority

# 论文4. 犍度瓦塔（Khandhakavatta）——巴利语律藏存在文本缺失吗？
作者：奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文回应格雷戈里·肖彭（Gregory Schopen）关于“巴利语律藏犍度部分缺失佛塔供养相关戒律”的观点，通过校勘《清净道论》与12世纪《帕拉卡马巴胡法典》（Katikavata）的相关文本，结合律藏注疏（如《萨曼塔帕萨迪卡》）的阐释，指出所谓“缺失”实为误解。研究表明，《犍度》（Khandaka）中的“瓦塔”（vatta，意为“义务”）特指14种僧团日常规范（如接待僧、斋堂使用等），而佛塔、菩提树相关礼仪属于“非律藏核心戒律”，是后世僧团根据实践发展的补充规范，并非原始律藏的缺失部分。律藏注疏中从未将佛塔礼仪归入犍度范畴，其与律藏规范的差异是佛教教团法的自然发展，而非文本传承中的遗漏，进而佐证了巴利语律藏文本的完整性。  
关键词：巴利语律藏；犍度（Khandaka）；佛塔供养；文本缺失；教团法；律藏注疏  
Title：Khandhakavatta: Loss of text in the Pali Vinayapitaka?  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper responds to Gregory Schopen's view that the Khandaka section of the Pali Vinaya lacks precepts related to stupa worship. By collating relevant texts from the Visuddhimagga and the 12th-century Mahaparakramabahu Katikavata, combined with interpretations from Vinaya commentaries (such as the Samantapasadika), it argues that the so-called "loss" is a misunderstanding. The study shows that "vatta" (meaning "duty") in the Khandaka specifically refers to 14 daily norms of the Sangha (e.g., receiving monks, using the refectory), while rituals related to stupas and bodhi trees are "non-core Vinaya precepts", supplementary norms developed by later Sanghas based on practice, not missing parts of the original Vinaya. Vinaya commentaries never classified stupa rituals under the Khandaka; their difference from Vinaya norms is the natural development of Buddhist ecclesiastical law, not omissions in textual transmission, thus confirming the integrity of the Pali Vinaya text.  
Keywords：Pali Vinaya；Khandaka；stupa worship；text loss；ecclesiastical law；Vinaya commentaries

# 论文5. 安巴帕利的机锋——《长老尼偈》第270节的双关语解析
作者：理查德·贡布里希（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文聚焦《长老尼偈》（Therigatha）中安巴帕利（Ambapali）所作第270节偈颂，揭示其中被注疏者忽略的双关语修辞。偈颂“ediso ahu ayam samussayo jajjaro bahudukhanam alayo so'palepapatito jaragharo saccavadivacanam anahhatha”中，“apalepa-patito”既指“灰泥脱落”（喻身体衰老），又通“avalepa”（傲慢），构成“美貌如屋，灰泥（傲慢）脱落”的双重隐喻。安巴帕利以曾为妓女的身份，通过这一双关表达对衰老的坦然与对“诸法无常”的体悟，既体现了诗歌的文学性，又深化了教义内涵。研究纠正了以往译本对该术语的单一解读，为理解南传佛教诗歌的修辞艺术与思想表达提供了新视角。  
关键词：安巴帕利；《长老尼偈》；双关语；南传佛教诗歌；修辞解析；无常观  
Title：A note on Ambapali's wit  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper focuses on verse 270 of the Therigatha attributed to Ambapali, revealing a pun overlooked by commentators. In the verse "ediso ahu ayam samussayo jajjaro bahudukhanam alayo so'palepapatito jaragharo saccavadivacanam anahhatha", the term "apalepa-patito" means both "plaster fallen off" (metaphor for physical aging) and is homophonous with "avalepa" (pride), forming a dual metaphor of "beauty as a house, whose plaster (pride) has fallen off". As a former courtesan, Ambapali expresses her acceptance of aging and realization of "anicca" (impermanence) through this pun, embodying both the literariness of the poem and the depth of doctrinal meaning. The study corrects the single interpretation of the term in previous translations, providing a new perspective for understanding the rhetorical art and ideological expression of Theravada Buddhist poetry.  
Keywords：Ambapali；Therigatha；pun；Theravada Buddhist poetry；rhetorical analysis；anicca

# 论文6. 小题大做：所谓“缺失的佛塔”——对《巴利语律藏与佛塔崇拜》一文的回应
作者：理查德·贡布里希（Richard Gombrich）  
摘要：本文反驳格雷戈里·肖彭（Gregory Schopen）关于“巴利语律藏犍度部分缺失佛塔相关戒律”的核心论点，指出其关键错误在于误译12世纪《帕拉卡马巴胡法典》中的连词“du”（意为“和”），导致将“佛塔礼仪”与“犍度义务”错误归为同一范畴。研究表明，巴利语律藏《分别》（Vibhanga）部分已包含佛塔崇拜的相关规范，而《犍度》未专门列出是因为其侧重僧团内部管理，佛塔礼仪属于“补充性实践”而非核心戒律。结合斯里兰卡僧团墓葬传统与早期佛教碑铭，指出佛塔崇拜是僧俗共同实践，律藏未单独分类记录是文本功能差异导致，而非“系统性删除”，进一步佐证巴利语律藏的完整性与连贯性。  
关键词：巴利语律藏；佛塔崇拜；文本解读；肖彭；犍度；戒律分类  
Title：Making mountains without molehills: the case of the missing stupa  
Author：Richard Gombrich  
Abstract：This paper refutes Gregory Schopen's core argument that the Khandaka section of the Pali Vinaya lacks precepts related to stupas, pointing out that his key error lies in mistranslating the conjunction "du" (meaning "and") in the 12th-century Mahaparakramabahu Katikavata, incorrectly classifying "stupa rituals" and "Khandaka duties" as the same category. The study shows that the Vibhanga section of the Pali Vinaya already contains relevant norms for stupa worship, and the Khandaka does not list them separately because it focuses on internal Sangha management, while stupa rituals are "supplementary practices" rather than core precepts. Combining the monastic burial tradition in Sri Lanka and early Buddhist epigraphs, it argues that stupa worship was a common practice of monastics and laypeople, and the failure of the Vinaya to record it separately is due to textual function differences, not "systematic deletion", further confirming the integrity and coherence of the Pali Vinaya.  
Keywords：Pali Vinaya；stupa cult；text interpretation；Schopen；Khandaka；precept classification

# 论文7. 巴利语词典学研究（八）——七个巴利语词汇的词源与释义考证
作者：K.R. 诺曼（K.R. Norman）  
摘要：本文针对《巴利语-英语词典》（PED）中未收录或释义、词源有误的七个巴利语词汇展开考证：1. “aharita”（带来），实为古巴利语独立分词形式，而非《巴利语词典》（CPD）推测的“ahariya”变体；2. “chandaso”（自愿地），与“chanda”（意愿）相关，而非“韵律、吠陀语”；3. “vac'-uggata”（口头习得的），“uggata”源自“avagata”（习得），释义应为“通过口头传承习得”，而非“发音优美”；4. “vyasanna”（下沉），是“vasanno”的误写，喻“陷入困境”；5. “vyamhita”（恐惧的），由“vyadheti”（惊吓）衍生，而非“vimhita”的韵律变体；6. “sihavasa”（狮子油），喻“珍贵之物”，常用于比喻修行需谨慎护持；7. “Sugata/Tathagata”（佛陀），“Sugata”指“善去者”，“Tathagata”指“如实去者”，均为佛陀的专属称谓，而非普通“善趣众生”。研究纠正了词典疏漏，补充了词汇的语境用法与语义演变。  
关键词：巴利语；词典学；词源考证；词汇释义；PED；CPD  
Title：Pali Lexicographical Studies VIII: Seven Pali Etymologies  
Author：K.R. Norman  
Abstract：This paper conducts etymological and definitional research on seven Pali words that are either unincluded or have incorrect meanings/etymologies in the Pali-English Dictionary (PED): 1. "aharita" (having brought) is an independent participle form in Old Pali, not a variant of "ahariya" as conjectured by the Critical Pali Dictionary (CPD); 2. "chandaso" (willingly) is related to "chanda" (will), not "metre, Vedic language"; 3. "vac'-uggata" (learnt orally) derives from "uggata" (avagata, learnt), meaning "acquired through oral transmission" rather than "well-intoned"; 4. "vyasanna" (sunk down) is a miswriting of "vasanno", metaphorically "falling into trouble"; 5. "vyamhita" (frightened) derives from "vyadheti" (to frighten), not a metrical variant of "vimhita"; 6. "sihavasa" (lion's oil) metaphorically "precious thing", often used to imply that practice requires careful protection; 7. "Sugata/Tathagata" (Buddha): "Sugata" means "well-gone", "Tathagata" means "thus-gone", both exclusive titles of the Buddha, not ordinary "beings in good realms". The study corrects dictionary omissions and supplements the contextual usage and semantic evolution of the words.  
Keywords：Pali；lexicography；etymological research；word definition；PED；CPD

# 论文8. 《Tundilovada》——一篇所谓的非圣典经文
作者：查尔斯·哈利西（Charles Hallisey）  
摘要：本文校勘并研究了未被收入巴利语圣典的南传佛教文本《Tundilovada》（《对通迪拉的教诲》），该文本以散文与偈颂结合的形式，记载佛陀在居士通迪拉（Tundila）供养僧团时的开示，核心阐释“布施-持戒-禅修-涅槃”的渐修路径。文本虽未被列入五尼柯耶，但内容符合南传佛教正统教义，包含五戒、十戒的功德阐释、天界福报的描述，以及以“涅槃之城”为核心的终极境界隐喻。研究基于伦敦大英图书馆与亚非学院藏19世纪手稿，梳理了文本的流传脉络，指出其可能形成于康提时期（Kandyan period），是斯里兰卡佛教“次圣典文献”的代表。文本的存在揭示了巴利语圣典的“开放性”，为理解南传佛教文献的多元传承提供了重要案例。  
关键词：《Tundilovada》；非圣典经文；南传佛教；渐修路径；次圣典文献；斯里兰卡佛教  
Title：Tundilovada: An allegedly non-canonical Sutta  
Author：Charles Hallisey  
Abstract：This paper edits and studies the Theravada Buddhist text Tundilovada (The Advice to Tundila), which is not included in the Pali Canon. Combining prose and verses, the text records the Buddha's teaching to the layman Tundila when he offered alms to the Sangha, focusing on the gradual path of "dana (giving) - sila (morality) - bhavana (meditation) - Nibbana". Although not included in the five Nikayas, its content conforms to orthodox Theravada teachings, including explanations of the merits of the Five Precepts and Ten Precepts, descriptions of heavenly blessings, and a core metaphor of "the city of Nibbana" for the ultimate state. Based on 19th-century manuscripts in the British Library and the School of Oriental and African Studies, London, the study sorts out the text's transmission context, suggesting it may have been composed during the Kandyan period and is a representative of Sri Lankan Buddhist "deutero-canonical literature". The existence of the text reveals the "openness" of the Pali Canon, providing an important case for understanding the diverse transmission of Theravada Buddhist literature.  
Keywords：Tundilovada；non-canonical sutta；Theravada Buddhism；gradual path；deutero-canonical literature；Sri Lankan Buddhism

# 论文9. 作为历史文献的巴利语律藏——对格雷戈里·肖彭的回应
作者：查尔斯·哈利西（Charles Hallisey）  
摘要：本文回应格雷戈里·肖彭（Gregory Schopen）关于“巴利语律藏缺失佛塔相关戒律”的观点，通过分析律藏注疏（如萨里普塔的《Saratthadipani》）对“犍度瓦塔”（Khandhakavatta）的阐释，指出“瓦塔”特指14种僧团日常义务，佛塔礼仪属于“补充性实践”，并非律藏原始内容的缺失。研究认为，肖彭的核心误读在于将“犍度义务”与“后世发展的礼仪”混为一谈，巴利语律藏的价值不在于记录所有佛教实践，而在于体现特定历史时期的僧团思想与规范。结合斯里兰卡僧团的实际实践与碑铭资料，指出律藏是“思想世界的表达”，需与考古证据、注疏文献结合，才能全面还原早期佛教僧团的历史 reality。  
关键词：巴利语律藏；历史文献；佛塔戒律；肖彭；律藏注疏；僧团规范  
Title：Apropos the Pali Vinaya as a historical document: a reply to Gregory Schopen  
Author：Charles Hallisey  
Abstract：This paper responds to Gregory Schopen's view that the Pali Vinaya lacks precepts related to stupas. By analyzing the interpretation of "Khandhakavatta" in Vinaya commentaries (such as Sariputta's Saratthadipani), it points out that "vatta" specifically refers to 14 daily duties of the Sangha, and stupa rituals are "supplementary practices" rather than missing parts of the original Vinaya. The study argues that Schopen's core misunderstanding lies in confusing "Khandaka duties" with "rituals developed later". The value of the Pali Vinaya does not lie in recording all Buddhist practices, but in reflecting the ideological world and norms of the Sangha in a specific historical period.
