# 论文1. 耆那教-佛教对话：来自巴利圣典的材料
作者：纳利尼·巴尔比尔（Nalini Balbir）  
摘要：本文探讨佛教徒对耆那教的认知这一重要问题，梳理了从雅可比1895年的研究到近现代相关学者的研究成果，包括R.冈布里奇与J.布龙霍斯特的学术争论。作者基于巴利圣典，从修行实践、教义思想、论证方式等方面分析相关内容，注重文本的时代性，区分了巴利圣典与后世注释书对耆那教的记载差异。文章指出巴利文献中常用“尼乾陀”（nigantha）指代耆那教徒，追溯了该术语的词源及使用语境，探讨了耆那教领袖摩诃毗罗在巴利圣典中的形象，分析了巴利文献中耆那教出现的两类核心语境（与佛陀的辩论、作为非佛教教派代表），并深入研究了耆那教的“四重约束”、苦行实践、非暴力理念、业力观等核心教义在巴利圣典中的呈现与解读。  
关键词：耆那教；佛教；巴利圣典；宗教对话；教义比较  
Title：Jain-Buddhist Dialogue: Material from the Pali Scriptures  
Author：Nalini Balbir  
Abstract：This paper explores the important question of what Buddhists knew about the Jains, tracing research results from Jacobi's study in 1895 to relevant scholars in modern times, including the academic debate between R. Gombrich and J. Bronkhorst. Based on the Pali Scriptures, the author analyzes relevant content from aspects such as practice, doctrinal thought, and argumentation methods, emphasizing the temporality of texts and distinguishing the differences in records of Jainism between the Pali Scriptures and later commentaries. The paper points out that the term "nigantha" was commonly used in Pali literature to refer to Jains, traces the etymology and usage context of this term, explores the image of Mahavira, the leader of Jainism, in the Pali Scriptures, analyzes the two core contexts in which Jainism appears in Pali literature (debates with the Buddha, as representatives of non-Buddhist sects), and conducts an in-depth study of the presentation and interpretation of core Jain doctrines such as the "fourfold restraint", ascetic practices, ahimsa, and karma theory in the Pali Scriptures.  
Keywords：Jainism；Buddhism；Pali Scriptures；religious dialogue；doctrinal comparison

# 论文2. 上座部律藏中佛教尼僧团建立的传说
作者：乌特·许斯肯（Ute Hüsken）  
摘要：本文聚焦上座部《律藏》小部《毗尼母经》第十章第一节，探讨早期佛教中女性的地位及尼僧团建立的相关问题。文章详细梳理了佛陀养母摩诃波阇波提·乔达弥三次请求佛陀允许女性出家、阿难从中斡旋的过程，重点分析了佛陀为尼僧制定的八项特殊规则（garudhammas），包括尼僧对僧团的从属关系、雨季安居规定、半月参请制度等。通过对比不同佛教流派文献中关于尼僧团建立的记载，探讨了这些规则的来源、内容及影响，指出佛陀既承认女性有获得觉悟的能力，又在社会文化语境下对尼僧团施加了诸多限制，反映了早期佛教对女性出家问题的复杂态度。  
关键词：上座部；律藏；尼僧团；八项特殊规则；女性地位  
Title：The Legend of the Establishment of the Buddhist Order of Nuns in the Theravada Vinaya-Pitaka  
Author：Ute Hüsken  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the first section of Chapter Ten of the Cullavagga in the Theravada Vinaya-Pitaka, exploring the status of women in early Buddhism and issues related to the establishment of the Buddhist Order of Nuns. The paper details the process of Mahapajapati Gotami, the Buddha's foster mother, requesting the Buddha three times to allow women to become monks and Ananda mediating between them, focusing on analyzing the eight special rules (garudhammas) formulated by the Buddha for nuns, including the subordination of nuns to the sangha, regulations on the rainy season retreat, and the twice-monthly consultation system. By comparing records of the establishment of the Order of Nuns in documents of different Buddhist schools, the paper explores the origin, content, and influence of these rules, pointing out that while the Buddha recognized women's ability to attain enlightenment, he imposed many restrictions on the Order of Nuns in the social and cultural context, reflecting the complex attitude of early Buddhism towards women's monasticism.  
Keywords：Theravada；Vinaya-Pitaka；Buddhist Order of Nuns；garudhammas；status of women

# 论文3. 再论Tuvattati/tuvatteti
作者：奥奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文重新探讨巴利语中罕见的本土词汇tuvattati/tuvatteti（及相关形式）的含义与词源。此前拉斯·阿尔斯多夫在阿育王法敕中发现该词，将其与耆那教文献结合解读，W.B.博莱补充了上座部文献的相关证据，但二者均未明确其词源。H.提肯试图将其源于梵语ati-vrt-，主张意为“过度活动”，但存在语义与语音上的矛盾。作者通过分析巴利语律藏中该词的使用语境，指出其核心含义应为“躺下”，并反驳了提肯将其解读为“性行为相关活动”的观点。文章强调，阿育王法敕中该词的语境虽存在争议，但 Jain 和佛教文献中丰富的证据足以支撑“躺下”这一核心含义，不应被单一碑铭文献的模糊解读所否定。  
关键词：巴利语；tuvattati/tuvatteti；词源；语义解读；佛教文献  
Title：Tuvattati/tuvatteti Again  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper re-examines the meaning and etymology of the rare desi-word tuvattati/tuvatteti (and related forms) in Pali. Previously, Ludwig Alsdorf discovered this word in the Asokan edicts and interpreted it in combination with Jain texts, and W.B. Bollée supplemented relevant evidence from Theravada literature, but neither of them clarified its etymology. H. Tieken attempted to derive it from Sanskrit ati-vrt-, advocating that it means "to move excessively", but there are semantic and phonetic contradictions. By analyzing the usage context of this word in the Pali Vinaya, the author points out that its core meaning should be "to lie down" and refutes Tieken's view that it refers to "activities related to sexual behavior". The paper emphasizes that although the context of this word in the Asokan edicts is controversial, the abundant evidence in Jain and Buddhist literature is sufficient to support the core meaning of "to lie down", which should not be negated by the ambiguous interpretation of a single epigraphic text.  
Keywords：Pali；tuvattati/tuvatteti；etymology；semantic interpretation；Buddhist literature

# 论文4. 玩火：从吠陀思想视角看缘起法
作者：乔安娜·尤雷维奇（Joanna Jurewicz）  
摘要：本文跳出佛教文本与传统视角，从早期吠陀思想出发解读佛教缘起法（pratityasamutpada）。作者指出，缘起法与吠陀宇宙生成论在整体结构、阶段概念等方面存在显著相似性，均以火为核心意象描述主客体认知的产生、过程与本质，但二者存在根本差异——吠陀宇宙生成论被视为合意的创造过程，而缘起法被认为导向痛苦。文章通过对比十二支缘起与吠陀宇宙生成论的对应环节，分析了佛陀如何通过重新定义婆罗门教概念，以缘起法批判吠陀思想的核心假设（如阿特曼的存在），将吠陀的创造过程重构为人类陷入经验存在的痛苦链条，揭示了缘起法作为对吠陀宇宙观的辩论性回应的深层内涵。  
关键词：缘起法；吠陀思想；佛教；宇宙生成论；火意象  
Title：Playing with Fire: The pratityasamutpada from the Perspective of Vedic Thought  
Author：Joanna Jurewicz  
Abstract：This paper interprets the Buddhist doctrine of dependent origination (pratityasamutpada) from the perspective of early Vedic thought, breaking away from Buddhist texts and traditional perspectives. The author points out that there are significant similarities between the pratityasamutpada and the Vedic cosmogony in terms of overall structure and stage concepts, both using fire as the core image to describe the generation, process, and nature of subject-object cognition, but there is a fundamental difference between them—the Vedic cosmogony is regarded as a desirable creative process, while the pratityasamutpada is considered to lead to suffering. By comparing the corresponding links between the twelvefold dependent origination and the Vedic cosmogony, the paper analyzes how the Buddha redefined Brahmanic concepts, criticized the core assumptions of Vedic thought (such as the existence of atman) through the pratityasamutpada, and reconstructed the Vedic creative process into a painful chain of human entanglement in empirical existence, revealing the profound connotation of the pratityasamutpada as a polemical response to the Vedic worldview.  
Keywords：pratityasamutpada；Vedic thought；Buddhism；cosmogony；fire imagery

# 论文5. 佛陀的死因
作者：梅塔南多比丘（Mettanando Bhikkhu）、奥奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文基于《长部·大般涅槃经》，结合医学视角探讨佛陀的死因。经文记载佛陀晚年健康状况不佳，在接受铁匠纯陀供养的特殊食物“sikaramaddava”后突发疾病，出现剧烈腹痛、便血等症状，最终涅槃。学者对“sikaramaddava”的具体所指存在争议，包括野猪肉、竹笋、蘑菇等解读，但均缺乏确凿证据。通过对症状的医学分析，作者排除了食物中毒、化学中毒、寄生虫感染、消化性溃疡等可能性，提出肠系膜梗死（mesenteric infarction）是最可能的死因——该疾病常见于老年人，常由进食引发，症状与经文中记载的急性腹痛、大量便血、口渴等高度吻合，且病程发展与佛陀从发病到涅槃的时间线一致。文章同时指出，佛陀的死亡是原有慢性疾病与食物诱因共同作用的结果，而非单纯由食物导致。  
关键词：佛陀；死因；大般涅槃经；医学分析；肠系膜梗死  
Title：The Cause of the Buddha's Death  
Author：Mettanando Bhikkhu, Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：Based on the Mahaparinibbanasuttanta in the Digha Nikaya, this paper explores the cause of the Buddha's death from a medical perspective. The sutra records that the Buddha's health deteriorated in his later years, and after accepting the special food "sikaramaddava" offered by the smith Cunda, he suddenly fell ill, presenting symptoms such as severe abdominal pain and hematochezia, and finally attained parinirvana. Scholars have disputes over the specific meaning of "sikaramaddava", including interpretations such as wild boar meat, bamboo shoots, and mushrooms, but none have conclusive evidence. Through medical analysis of the symptoms, the authors rule out the possibilities of food poisoning, chemical poisoning, parasitic infection, peptic ulcer, etc., and propose that mesenteric infarction is the most likely cause of death—this disease is common in the elderly, often triggered by eating, and its symptoms are highly consistent with those recorded in the sutra such as acute abdominal pain, massive hematochezia, and thirst, and the course of the disease is consistent with the timeline from the Buddha's onset to his parinirvana. The paper also points out that the Buddha's death was the result of the combined effect of an existing chronic disease and a food inducement, rather than being caused solely by food.  
Keywords：Buddha；cause of death；Mahaparinibbanasuttanta；medical analysis；mesenteric infarction

# 论文6. 15世纪晚期作为巴利文学中心的兰那
作者：奥奥斯卡·封兴伯（Oskar von Hinüber）  
摘要：本文聚焦泰国北部的兰那地区，探讨其在15世纪晚期作为巴利文学中心的发展状况。长期以来，学界对巴利文学的研究多集中于印度、斯里兰卡和缅甸，而兰那地区的相关遗产未受足够重视。作者通过梳理兰那地区现存的巴利手稿、铭文及刻经佛像等资料，指出15世纪晚期至16世纪早期是兰那文化的“黄金时代”，这一时期涌现出大量巴利文献，包括经典注释、语法著作等。文章重点介绍了拉塔纳帕尼亚、西里曼加拉、纳纳基蒂等当地学者的代表作，分析了兰那巴利文学的特点及与周边佛教文化圈的交流，指出1558年缅甸征服兰那后，当地的巴利文学传统逐渐被缅甸的注释传统吸收，但其短暂的繁荣仍是巴利文学发展史上的重要阶段。  
关键词：兰那；巴利文学；15世纪晚期；佛教文献；学术交流  
Title：Lan₂ Na as a Centre of Pali Literature During the Late 15th Century  
Author：Oskar von Hinüber  
Abstract：This paper focuses on the Lan₂ Na region in northern Thailand, exploring its development as a center of Pali literature in the late 15th century. For a long time, academic research on Pali literature has focused on India, Sri Lanka, and Burma, while the relevant heritage of the Lan₂ Na region has not received sufficient attention. By sorting out existing Pali manuscripts, inscriptions, and Buddha images with inscribed scriptures in the Lan₂ Na region, the author points out that the late 15th century to the early 16th century was the "golden age" of Lan₂ Na culture, during which a large number of Pali documents emerged, including canonical commentaries and grammatical works. The paper focuses on introducing the representative works of local scholars such as Ratanapania, Sirimangala, and Nanakitti, analyzes the characteristics of Lan₂ Na Pali literature and its exchanges with surrounding Buddhist cultural circles, and points out that after Burma conquered Lan₂ Na in 1558, the local Pali literary tradition was gradually absorbed by Burma's commentarial tradition, but its short-term prosperity remains an important stage in the development of Pali literature.  
Keywords：Lan₂ Na；Pali literature；late 15th century；Buddhist documents；academic exchange

# 论文7. 梵蒂冈图书馆的九部巴利手稿
作者：杰奎琳·菲利奥扎（Jacqueline Filliozat）  
摘要：本文基于1986年对梵蒂冈图书馆的考察，详细整理了馆内收藏的九部巴利手稿的基本信息。这些手稿分属博尔贾收藏（Borgia）和梵蒂冈-印度收藏（Vaticana-Indiana），涵盖缅甸文字、暹罗卡姆文字、僧伽罗文字三种书写形式，抄写时间集中于18世纪至19世纪中叶，部分手稿来源可追溯至早期天主教传教士与缅甸僧侣的交流。文章逐一记录了每部手稿的内容（包括《波罗提木叉》《本生经》片段、《迦玛瓦卡》等）、形制、保存状况及相关文献学信息，分析了手稿的学术价值，尤其是对研究巴利文献在欧洲的收藏与传播、早期东西方佛教研究交流具有重要意义。  
关键词：梵蒂冈图书馆；巴利手稿；文献学；佛教文献收藏；书写形式  
Title：Nine Pali Manuscripts in the Vatican Library  
Author：Jacqueline Filliozat  
Abstract：Based on a survey of the Vatican Library in 1986, this paper details the basic information of the nine Pali manuscripts collected in the library. These manuscripts belong to the Borgia Collection and the Vaticana-Indiana Collection, covering three writing forms: Burmese script, Siamese Kham script, and Sinhalese script. The copying time is concentrated from the 18th century to the mid-19th century, and some manuscripts can be traced back to the exchanges between early Catholic missionaries and Burmese monks. The paper records the content (including the Patimokkha, Jataka fragments, Kammavaca, etc.), form, preservation status, and relevant philological information of each manuscript one by one, and analyzes the academic value of the manuscripts, especially their important significance for studying the collection and dissemination of Pali literature in Europe and the early exchange of Buddhist studies between the East and the West.  
Keywords：Vatican Library；Pali manuscripts；philology；Buddhist literature collection；writing forms
